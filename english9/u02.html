Inspiring People

榜样人物 · 被动语态 · 人物描述

📖 话题：Role models and inspiring figures💬 功能：Describe people's achievements, express admiration📚 语法：被动语态 (一般现在/过去) · be known/famous for📝 写作：介绍一个榜样人物

🎯 我能做到

📖 课文精讲 · Who is your hero?
场景：Li Ming 和 Sarah 在谈论各自心目中的英雄人物。
Li Ming:Sarah, who is your hero?
Sarah:I admire Yuan Longping. He is known as the Father of Hybrid Rice. His work helped feed millions of people.
Li Ming:That's amazing! My hero is Tu Youyou. She discovered artemisinin and won the Nobel Prize. She is regarded as one of the greatest scientists in China.
Sarah:I also admire Malala. She fought for girls' education and was awarded the Nobel Peace Prize at a very young age.
Li Ming:These people are truly inspiring. They made great contributions to society. Their stories are known all over the world.
Sarah:I agree. Their courage and determination encourage me to work hard and make a difference.
📖 查看翻译

Li Ming: Sarah，你心目中的英雄是谁？

Sarah: 我敬佩袁隆平。他被称为杂交水稻之父。他的工作帮助养活了数百万人。

Li Ming: 太了不起了！我的英雄是屠呦呦。她发现了青蒿素并获得了诺贝尔奖。她被认为是中国最伟大的科学家之一。

Sarah: 我也敬佩马拉拉。她为女孩的教育权利而战，并在很小的时候就获得了诺贝尔和平奖。

Li Ming: 这些人真的很鼓舞人心。他们为社会做出了巨大贡献。他们的故事闻名于世。

Sarah: 我同意。他们的勇气和决心激励我努力学习，做出改变。

🔑 重点讲解：

被动语态 be known as 表示「被称为」 — He is known as the Father of Hybrid Rice.

被动语态 be regarded as 表示「被认为」 — She is regarded as one of the greatest scientists.

被动语态 be awarded 表示「被授予」 — She was awarded the Nobel Peace Prize.

make a difference 表示「有所作为；产生影响」

🔤 重点句型 · Sentence Patterns
be known as / be known for
be known as 表示「作为……而知名」；be known for 表示「因……而知名」。
Yuan Longping is known as the Father of Hybrid Rice. / He is known for his great contribution to agriculture.
be regarded as
表示「被认为是……」
She is regarded as one of the most influential scientists. / He is regarded as a hero by his people.
被动语态(过去)：was/were + pp
表示「被……」，用于描述过去发生的被动动作。
The vaccine was developed by Chinese scientists. / Malala was awarded the Nobel Peace Prize.
被动语态(现在)：is/are + pp
表示「被……」，用于描述一般事实。
Rice is grown in many parts of China. / Heroes are remembered by their people forever.
📚 语法聚焦 · 被动语态 (Passive Voice)

被动语态的基本结构

时态结构例句
一般现在时is/am/are + 过去分词The story is known by everyone.
一般过去时was/were + 过去分词The medicine was discovered by Tu Youyou.
主动变被动宾语变主语，动词变 ppPeople grow rice. → Rice is grown (by people).

by + 动作执行者

结构用法例句
by + 名词/代词引出动作的执行者The book was written by Mo Yan.
by 可省略执行者不重要或显而易见English is spoken all over the world.
be + pp + by强调动作的执行者The award was given by the president.

重要短语

短语用法例句
be known as作为……而知名He is known as a great leader.
be known/famous for因……而知名She is famous for her scientific work.
be regarded as被认为是He is regarded as a pioneer.
used to + V过去常常（现在不）She used to struggle with math.

被动语态中，过去分词（pp）不能单独作谓语，必须和 be 动词一起使用。

by + 执行者放在句末，如果不需要强调执行者可以省略 by 短语。

区分 used to do（过去常常）和 be used to doing（习惯做）。

Well begun is half done. 良好的开端是成功的一半。

📖 阅读专项 · Heroes Among Us

Throughout history, there have been many inspiring people whose stories still touch our hearts today. They are known for their courage, determination, and great contributions to society.

One such hero is Yuan Longping, the Father of Hybrid Rice. He was born in 1930 and dedicated his whole life to solving the food problem. His hybrid rice was planted in many countries and helped feed millions of people. He was awarded many honors for his achievements, but he remained humble. He once said, "I have always dreamt that one day hybrid rice could be grown all over the world."

Another inspiring figure is Tu Youyou. She is known for discovering artemisinin, a medicine that has saved millions of lives from malaria. In 2015, she was awarded the Nobel Prize in Physiology or Medicine. She was the first Chinese woman to win a Nobel Prize in science. Her success shows that perseverance and hard work can lead to great breakthroughs.

Malala Yousafzai is also a young hero. She fought for girls' right to education. Despite great danger, she never gave up. In 2014, she was awarded the Nobel Peace Prize at the age of 17, making her the youngest Nobel laureate. Her courage inspires young people around the world to stand up for what they believe in.

These heroes teach us that ordinary people can achieve extraordinary things. Their stories remind us that one person can make a difference. They are not just heroes of their time — their legacy will live on forever.

✍️ 写作进阶 · My Hero / An Inspiring Person
📋 写作题目
你心中一定有一位令你敬佩的人物——可能是一位科学家、一位运动员、一位历史人物，或者就是你身边的人。请写一篇短文介绍这位人物，内容包括他/她的成就以及你为什么敬佩他/她。尽量使用被动语态和本单元学到的词汇。
开头：介绍你要写的人物是谁
主体1：人物背景和主要成就（包含被动语态）
主体2：人物的品质（courage, determination 等）
主体3：你从他/她身上学到了什么
结尾：总结人物对你的影响
My Hero — Yuan Longping Yuan Longping is my hero. He is known as the Father of Hybrid Rice. He was born in 1930 and dedicated his life to agriculture. His greatest achievement was the development of hybrid rice, which was planted in many countries and helped feed millions of people. For his contributions, he was awarded many honors both at home and abroad. What I admire most is his determination and perseverance. He never stopped his research, even when he was very old. His courage to solve the world's food problem inspires me greatly. From him, I learned that one person can make a difference. I hope one day I can also contribute to society like he did. Yuan Longping will always be remembered as a true hero.
我的英雄——袁隆平 袁隆平是我的英雄。他被誉为杂交水稻之父。他出生于1930年，一生致力于农业。 他最大的成就是培育了杂交水稻，这种水稻在许多国家被种植，帮助养活了数百万人。由于他的贡献，他被授予了国内外许多荣誉。 我最敬佩的是他的决心和毅力。即使到了晚年，他也从未停止研究。他解决世界粮食问题的勇气极大地激励了我。 从他身上，我学到一个人可以有所作为。我希望有一天我也能像他一样为社会做贡献。袁隆平将永远被人们铭记为一位真正的英雄。
