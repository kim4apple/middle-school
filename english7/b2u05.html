七下 · Unit 5

Here and Now

现在进行时 · 日常活动 · 此时此刻

📖 话题：What people are doing right now 💬 功能：Describe ongoing actions & activities 📚 语法：现在进行时 (am/is/are + doing) 📝 写作：描述此刻的场景

🎯 我能做到

📖 课文精讲 · Phone Call on Saturday
场景：周六上午，Tom 打电话找 Li Ming。
Tom: Hello! May I speak to Li Ming?
Mum: Hold on, please. Li Ming, you have a phone call!
Li Ming: Thanks, Mum! Hi, Tom! What are you doing?
Tom: I'm reading a book at home. What are you doing?
Li Ming: I'm doing my homework. But I'm almost finished!
Tom: Great! Is your brother at home? What is he doing?
Li Ming: He is playing computer games in his room.
Tom: I'm calling because I want to go to the park. Are you free?
Li Ming: Yes! Let's meet at 3 o'clock at the park.
📖 查看翻译

逐句翻译：

Tom: 你好！请找一下李明接电话好吗？

妈妈: 请稍等。李明，你的电话！

李明: 谢谢妈妈！嗨，Tom！你正在做什么？

Tom: 我正在家里看书。你正在做什么？

李明: 我正在做作业。但我快做完了！

Tom: 太好了！你弟弟在家吗？他正在做什么？

李明: 他正在房间里玩电脑游戏。

Tom: 哈哈！我打电话是因为我想去公园。你有空吗？

李明: 有！我们下午3点在公园见。

🔑 重点讲解：

1. What are you doing? → "你正在做什么？" 现在进行时特殊疑问句。

2. I'm reading / I'm doing → am/is/are + 动词-ing 表示正在做某事。

3. May I speak to ...? → 电话用语"请找……接电话"。

4. Hold on, please. → 请稍等，别挂电话。

5. Is your brother at home? → 询问某人是否在家。

📖 Reading: What Are They Doing?

It's Sunday morning. Everyone is busy at home.

My father is reading a newspaper in the living room.

My mother is cooking in the kitchen. She is making dumplings!

My sister is cleaning her room. She is listening to music at the same time.

My little brother is playing with his toy car.

What am I doing? I am writing in my diary. I am recording this happy moment!

🔤 重点句型 · Sentence Patterns
What + am/is/are + 主语 + doing?
询问某人正在做什么。
✅ What are you doing? — I'm reading.
✅ What is she doing? — She is cooking.
✅ What are they doing? — They are playing.
主语 + am/is/are + V-ing.
描述某人正在做某事。现在进行时肯定句。
✅ I am reading a book.
✅ He is playing computer games.
✅ They are watching TV.
Is/Are + 主语 + V-ing?
现在进行时一般疑问句，"某人正在做某事吗？"
✅ Is he sleeping? — Yes, he is. / No, he isn't.
✅ Are you watching TV? — Yes, I am. / No, I'm not.
✅ Are they playing? — Yes, they are.
May I speak to ...? / Hold on, please.
电话常用语。
✅ May I speak to Li Ming?
✅ Hold on, please.
✅ Is Tom there? — 电话中问"……在吗？"
📚 语法聚焦 · 现在进行时

1️⃣ 现在进行时结构

表示"此时此刻"正在进行的动作或发生的事情。

主语be 动词动词-ing例句
IamreadingI am reading a book.
He / She / ItisplayingHe is playing games.
We / You / TheyarewatchingThey are watching TV.

2️⃣ -ing 变化规则

规则动词原形-ing 形式
直接加 -ingplay, read, watch, do, cook, cleanplaying, reading, watching, doing, cooking, cleaning
去 e 加 -ingwrite, take, make, havewriting, taking, making, having
双写最后一个字母 + -ingrun, swim, get, sit, putrunning, swimming, getting, sitting, putting

3️⃣ 现在进行时一般疑问句

结构例句肯定回答否定回答
Is/Are + 主语 + V-ing?Is he reading?Yes, he is.No, he isn't.
Are you + V-ing?Are you doing homework?Yes, I am.No, I'm not.

4️⃣ 现在进行时时间状语

时间状语含义例句

right now就在此刻He is watching TV right now.

Look! / Listen!看！/ 听！Look! He is running!

5️⃣ 电话用语汇总

英语中文用法
May I speak to ...?请找……接电话打电话找某人
Hold on, please.请稍等让对方别挂电话
Is ... there?……在吗？询问某人在不在
You have a phone call.有电话找你转达有电话
⚠️ 中式英语纠错：
❌ What you doing?（缺少 be 动词 am/is/are）
✅ What are you doing?

❌ He is read a book.（be 动词后要用 V-ing，不是原形）
✅ He is reading a book.

❌ She is writting a letter.（write → writing，t 不双写）
✅ She is writing a letter.
📝 三级闯关 · 实战练习

🥉 基础关 · 概念识记

基础 ★☆☆
He ____ (is / am / are) reading a book now.
📝 查看答案
答案：is
解析：主语 He 是第三人称单数，be 动词用 is。
基础 ★☆☆
写出 listen 的 -ing 形式：____
📝 查看答案
答案：listening
解析：listen 直接加 -ing。
基础 ★☆☆
"请稍等"用英语怎么说？
📝 查看答案
答案：Hold on, please.
解析：电话常用语，礼貌地让对方稍等。

🥈 提升关 · 语境运用

提升 ★★☆
改错：He is writting a letter.
📝 查看答案
答案：He is writing a letter.
解析：write 以 e 结尾，去 e 再加 -ing。
提升 ★★☆
连词成句：doing / what / you / are / ?
📝 查看答案
答案：What are you doing?
解析：疑问词 What + be + 主语 + V-ing?
提升 ★★☆
They are playing basketball. (改为一般疑问句)
📝 查看答案
答案：Are they playing basketball?
解析：将 be 动词提到主语前。

🥇 挑战关 · 综合运用

挑战 ★★★
汉译英：你弟弟正在做什么？他正在玩电脑游戏。
📝 查看答案
答案：What is your brother doing? He is playing computer games.
解析：第三人称单数用 is，play 直接加 -ing。
挑战 ★★★
补全对话：A: Hello! May I ____ (找) Tom? B: ____ (稍等), please.
📝 查看答案
答案：speak to; Hold on
解析：May I speak to...? "请找……接电话"；Hold on "稍等"。
挑战 ★★★
写出下列动词的 -ing 形式：run, swim, make, get
📝 查看答案
答案：running, swimming, making, getting
解析：重读闭音节双写+ing (run/swim/get)；去e+ing (make)。
挑战 ★★★
翻译：现在是晚上7点。我爸爸正在看电视。我妈妈正在看书。我正在写日记。
📝 查看答案
答案：It's 7 o'clock in the evening. My father is watching TV. My mother is reading a book. I am writing in my diary.
解析：注意 be 动词的搭配：father/mother 用 is，I 用 am。
