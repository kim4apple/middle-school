Unit 2

We're Family!

家庭成员 · 介绍家人 · 描述外貌性格

📖 话题：Family members and descriptions 💬 功能：Introduce family members · Describe people 📚 语法：名词复数 · have/has · 形容词描述 📝 写作：介绍你的家庭

🎯 我能做到

📖 课文精讲 · Peter visits Chen Jie's home
场景：Peter 到陈洁家做客，陈洁向他介绍家人。
Chen Jie: Mum, this is my friend, Peter.
Mum: Hello, Peter! Welcome to our home!
Peter: Thank you, Auntie! Your home is nice!
Chen Jie: Peter, this is my dad. And this is my little sister, Xiaomei.
Dad: Hi, Peter! Nice to meet you!
Peter: Nice to meet you too, Uncle!
Xiaomei: Hi, Peter! I'm Xiaomei!
Chen Jie: Xiaomei is 6 years old. She has big eyes and long hair.
Peter: She's cute! Do you have any pets, Chen Jie?
Chen Jie: Yes, we have a dog. His name is Doudou. He's black and white.
Peter: Wow! I love dogs!
📖 查看翻译

逐句翻译：

陈洁：妈妈，这位是我的朋友 Peter。

妈妈：你好 Peter！欢迎来到我们家！

Peter：谢谢阿姨！你们家真漂亮！

陈洁：Peter，这是我爸爸。这是我的小妹妹小美。

爸爸：嗨 Peter！很高兴认识你！

Peter：我也很高兴认识您，叔叔！

小美：嗨 Peter！我是小美！

陈洁：小美6岁了。她有大眼睛和长头发。

Peter：她好可爱！你有宠物吗，陈洁？

陈洁：是的，我们有一只狗。他叫豆豆。他是黑白相间的。

Peter：哇！我喜欢狗！

🔑 重点讲解：

1. This is ... → "这是……" 介绍人用 this is + 人名
e.g. This is my friend, Peter.

2. Welcome to ... → "欢迎来到……" 固定搭配。
e.g. Welcome to our home!

3. She has big eyes. → have/has 表示"拥有"。第三人称单数用 has。
e.g. I have a dog. / She has long hair.

4. cute → "可爱的"，形容人或动物。

5. Do you have ... ? → "你有……吗？" 一般疑问句，用 Do 开头。
肯定回答：Yes, I do. / 否定回答：No, I don't.

6. 家庭成员的称呼：mum, dad, brother, sister, grandpa, grandma

📖 Reading: My Family

Hello! My name is Wang Fang. I have a happy family.

There are four people in my family: my dad, my mum, my brother and me.

My dad is tall and strong. He is a teacher.

My mum is kind and beautiful. She has long black hair.

My brother is 8 years old. He has small eyes and a big mouth. He is very funny!

🔤 重点句型 · Sentence Patterns
This is + 人名.
用来介绍人。指近处的人用 this，远处用 that。
✅ This is my mum.
✅ This is my friend, Peter.
➡ That is my grandpa.（远处指）
主语 + have / has + 物品/特征
have/has 表示"拥有"。I/You/We/They 用 have；He/She/It 用 has。
✅ I have a brother.
✅ She has big eyes.
➡ He has small eyes.
Do you have + 名词?
询问对方是否有某物/某人。
✅ Do you have any pets?
✅ Do you have a sister?
➡ 回答：Yes, I do. / No, I don't.
主语 + be + 形容词
用形容词描述人的外貌或性格。
✅ My dad is tall and strong.
✅ My mum is kind.
➡ She is cute. / He is funny.
How many + 复数 + do you have?
询问数量。
✅ How many people do you have in your family?
➡ I have four people in my family.
📚 语法聚焦 · 名词复数 & have/has

1️⃣ 名词复数（Plural Nouns）

英语中，表示两个或以上的事物时，名词要变成复数形式。

规则单数复数
一般情况 + sbookbooks
以 s/x/sh/ch 结尾 + esbusbuses
以"辅音+y"结尾 → 变 y 为 i + esfamilyfamilies
以 f/fe 结尾 → 变 f/fe 为 v + esknifeknives

📌 I have two brothers. / She has three sisters.

2️⃣ have / has 的用法

have 和 has 都表示"有"，根据主语的人称选择使用。

主语动词例句
I / You / We / TheyhaveI have a sister.
He / She / IthasShe has a brother.
💡 口诀：I/You 用 have, He/She 用 has；单数用 has，复数用 have。
⚠️ 常见错误：
❌ She have a dog.
✅ She has a dog.

❌ I has a brother.
✅ I have a brother.

3️⃣ 形容词描述外貌（Adjectives）

形容词用来描述人或事物的特征。在句中放在 be 动词之后，或修饰名词。

用法结构例句
be + 形容词主语 + be + adj.My dad is tall.
have/has + adj. + 名词主语 + have/has + adj. + n.She has big eyes.
💡 常见形容词：tall / short, big / small, long / short, kind, cute, funny, beautiful, strong

4️⃣ How many ... do you have?

用来询问数量，后面跟可数名词复数形式。

✅ How many people do you have in your family?
✅ How many sisters do you have?
➡ I have one sister. / I have two brothers.

📝 三级闯关 · 实战练习

🥉 基础关 · 概念识记

基础 ★☆☆
My mum ____ long hair. (have / has)
📝 查看答案
答案：has
解析：Mum 是第三人称单数，用 has。
基础 ★☆☆
I ____ (有) a happy family.
📝 查看答案
答案：have
解析：I 后用 have。
基础 ★☆☆
写出复数：book → ____ ; bus → ____ ; family → ____
📝 查看答案
答案：books; buses; families
解析：book+s；bus+es；family→变y为i+es。

🥈 提升关 · 语境运用

提升 ★★☆
改错：She have two sister.
📝 查看答案
答案：She has two sisters.
解析：She 后用 has；two 后用复数 sisters。
提升 ★★☆
连词成句：have / do / a / you / brother / ?
📝 查看答案
答案：Do you have a brother?
解析：一般疑问句：Do + 主语 + have + 名词?
提升 ★★☆
用 has 改写：My parents ____ (有) a dog.
📝 查看答案
答案：have
解析：My parents 是复数，用 have。

🥇 挑战关 · 综合运用

挑战 ★★★
汉译英：我有一个妹妹。她有大眼睛和长头发。
📝 查看答案
答案：I have a sister. She has big eyes and long hair.
解析：第一句 I have；第二句 she 用 has。
挑战 ★★★
补全对话：
A: ____ you ____ any brothers?
B: No, I ____. But I ____ a sister. ____ is 5 years old.
📝 查看答案
答案：Do; have; don't; have; She
解析：Do you have 问"你有……吗"；否定回答用 don't；She 指代 sister。
挑战 ★★★
如何问"你家有几口人"？💡 用 How many 造句。
📝 查看答案
答案：How many people are there in your family? / How many people do you have in your family?
解析：How many + 复数 + do you have / are there ...?
挑战 ★★★
读短文判断正误(T/F)：
Wang Fang has a happy family. There are three people in her family.
📝 查看答案
答案：F (False)
解析：文中说 There are four people in my family: my dad, my mum, my brother and me. 所以是4口人。
