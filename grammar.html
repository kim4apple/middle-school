S1 ⏱ 时态系统
一般过去时 八上U1 🔥 高频
▾
规则动词visited · watched · studied · stopped
不规则必备go→went · eat→ate · see→saw · take→took · buy→bought · have→had
否定/疑问didn't + 动词原形 / Did + 主 + 动词原形?
🚨 didn't + 过去式 ❌ I didn't went to Beijing. → ✅ I didn't go to Beijing.
be going to 将来时 八上U1 · U6 🔥 高频
▾
句式结构例句
肯定am/is/are + going to + doI am going to study computer science.
否定am/is/are + not + going to + doHe is not going to be a teacher.
疑问Be + 主语 + going to + do?Are you going to find a job?
🚨 be 不能丢 ❌ He going to be a pilot. → ✅ He is going to be a pilot.
will 将来时 八上U7 🔥 高频
▾
结构例句
will + 动词原形People will have robots.
won't + 动词原形Kids won't go to school.
There will beThere will be more pollution.
more / less / fewer
more更多（可数/不可数）
less + 不可数less water / time / pollution
fewer + 可数复数fewer cars / people / trees
🚨 There will have ❌ ❌ There will have more robots. → ✅ There will be more robots.
过去进行时 八下U5 🔥🔥 必考
▾
结构例句
was/were + doingI was watching TV at 8 pm.
when + 一般过去时（短动作打断）I was sleeping when the phone rang.
while + 过去进行时（两动作同时）While I was sleeping, my mom was cooking.
when vs while（完形填空中考必杀题）
when后接一般过去时（短动作）
I was reading when he came in.
while后接过去进行时（长动作）
While I was reading, he was watching TV.
现在完成时 八下U8 · 九全U12深化 🔥🔥 必考
▾
句式结构例句
肯定句have/has + 过去分词I have visited the museum.
否定句haven't/hasn't + 过去分词She hasn't read the book yet.
疑问句Have/Has + 主语 + 过去分词?Have you ever been to Beijing?
been to / gone to / been in 辨析
have been to去过（已回）
I have been to Beijing.
have gone to去了（未回）
He has gone to Shanghai.
have been in在某地待了多久
She has been in Beijing for 3 years.
三大过去时间对比矩阵 ★
一般过去时"过去某时发生的动作"
I saw the movie yesterday.
关注"时间点"
现在完成时"过去动作与现在有关"
I have seen the movie.
关注"对现在的影响"
过去完成时"过去的过去"
I had seen the movie before she told me.
需要过去参照点
过去完成时 九全U12 🔥🔥 必考
▾
结构例句
had + 过去分词I had finished my homework.
By the time + 过, 主 had doneBy the time I got there, he had left.
Before + 过, 主 had doneWhen I arrived, the bus had already left.
🚨 必须有过去参照点 ❌ I had finished my homework.（无参照点，应用一般过去时）
✅ I had finished my homework before my mom came home.
S2 🔒 被动语态
一般现在时被动 九全U5 🔥 高频
▾
结构主动 → 被动
am/is/are + 过去分词 ± byPeople grow tea. → Tea is grown in China.
be made + 介词辨析
be made of看得出原材料
Chair is made of wood.
be made from看不出原材料
Paper is made from trees.
be made in产地

一般过去时被动 九全U6 🔥 高频
▾
结构例句
was/were + 过去分词The car was invented in 1885.
was/were + 过去分词 + byIt was invented by Karl Benz.
🚨 be 动词时态随语境变 ❌ The car is invented in 1885. → ✅ The car was invented in 1885.
情态动词被动 九全U7 🔥🔥 必考
▾
结构例句
情态动词 + be + 过去分词Homework should be done on time.
情态动词 + not + be + 过去分词Phones shouldn't be used in class.
allow 搭配链
allow sb. to do · be allowed to do · should be allowed to do
❌ This problem must solved. → ✅ This problem must be solved.
关键公式：情态动词 + be + 过去分词，缺一不可。
进行时被动 · 综合复习 九全U13 🔥 高频
▾
一般现在时被动is/are + 过去分词
The river is polluted.（强调状态）
进行时被动is/are + being + 过去分词
The river is being polluted.（强调正在被破坏）
U13 综合复习例句
现在进行时We are trying to save the earth.
used toThe river used to be clean.
should + 被动Something must be done.
S3 📎 三大从句
宾语从句（that / if / whether） 九全U2 🔥🔥 必考
▾
引导词来源从句语序例句
that陈述句陈述语序I think that mooncakes are delicious.
if / whether一般疑问句陈述语序I wonder if he likes the festival.
特殊疑问词特殊疑问句陈述语序Can you tell me where the library is?
if vs whether（什么时候只能用 whether？）
if 可以一般问句转化
I don't know if he will come.
必须用 whether① or not 连用 ② 介词后 ③ 不定式前
whether to go / depend on whether
🚨 从句语序：疑问词后接陈述句 ❌ I wonder where is the post office.
✅ I wonder where the post office is.
口诀：疑问词后跟陈述，be 动词放主语后。
宾语从句语序 · 礼貌请求 九全U3 🔥 高频
▾
原问句变为宾语从句
Where is the library?Could you tell me where the library is?
How can I get there?I wonder how I can get there.
Is there a bank?Do you know if there is a bank?
🚨 Could you tell me + 陈述语序 ❌ Could you tell me where is the restroom?
✅ Could you tell me where the restroom is?
定语从句（that / who / which） 九全U9 🔥🔥 必考
▾
关系代词指代例句
that人 / 物I like music that makes me happy.
who人I like singers who sing well.
which物I like movies which are funny.
只能用 that 的 3 种情况（中考单选杀手）
① 序数词/最高级后the first book that I read
② 先行词为 all/everything/nothingAll that I want is peace.
③ 人和物混合先行词the people and things that I like
🚨 关系代词作宾语可省略 ✅ This is the movie (that) I watched last night.
that/which 在从句中作宾语时可省略，作主语不可省。
S4 🎯 情态动词
情态动词表推测 九全U8 🔥🔥 必考
▾
情态动词推测程度含义例句
must~95%一定It must be Carla's.
could~70%可能It could be Wang Fang's.
might~50%也许It might belong to Li Ming.
can't~99% 否定不可能It can't be his.
🚨 mustn't ≠ "一定不"（它是"禁止"） ❌ He mustn't be at home. → ✅ He can't be at home.
口诀：肯定推测用 must，否定推测用 can't。mustn't 是 "禁止"。
could 表礼貌请求 八下U3 📌 中频
▾
句式用法回答
Could you please + do?请求对方Sure. / I'm afraid I can't.
Could I + do?请求允许Of course. / Sorry, you can't.
🚨 否定回答不用 couldn't ❌ Could I go out? — No, you couldn't.
✅ Could I go out? — Sorry, you can't.
could 在请求句中表礼貌，不是过去时，回答用 can/can't。
S5 📊 比较与最高级
形容词比较级 八上U3 🔥 高频
▾
构成例词
单音节 + ertall→taller, fast→faster
辅音+y → ierheavy→heavier, happy→happier
重读闭音节双写+erbig→bigger, hot→hotter, thin→thinner
多音节 + moreoutgoing→more outgoing
特殊变化good/well→better, bad→worse
同级比较
as + 原级 + as 和……一样 · not as/so + 原级 + as 不如……
🚨 more 与 -er 不共用 ❌ She is more taller than me. → ✅ She is taller than me.
形容词最高级 八上U4 🔥 高频
▾
构成例词
单音节 + estcheap→the cheapest
最高级标志in + 范围 / of + 群体
❌ She is most popular girl. → ✅ She is the most popular girl.
最高级前必须加 the。
数字 + 比较/最高级 八下U7 🔥 高频
▾
结构例句
数字 + 形容词It's 8,844 meters high.
最高级 + inThe highest mountain in the world.
any other + 单数名词deeper than any other lake
❌ any other lakes → ✅ any other lake
any other 后接可数名词单数。
S6 🔗 连词专题
until / although / so that 八下U4 🔥 高频
▾
连词含义要点例句
until直到not…until "直到……才"He didn't leave until his mom came back.
although虽然❌ 不与 but 连用Although he's angry, he cares.
so that以便引导目的状语Talk nicely so that she can understand.
🚨 although 和 but 不连用 ❌ Although he was tired, but he kept working.
✅ Although he was tired, he kept working.
✅ He was tired, but he kept working.
unless / as soon as / so…that 八下U6 🔥 高频
▾
连词含义等价例句
unless除非= if…notYou won't pass unless you study.
as soon as一……就……= the momentAs soon as he saw her, he smiled.
so…that如此……以至于so + 形/副 + thatSo high that few could climb.
so…that vs such…that
so + 形容词 + thatso hot that we didn't go out.
such (a/an) + 形 + 名 + thatsuch a hot day that we didn't go out.
❌ It was so a hot day that we didn't go out.
✅ It was such a hot day that we didn't go out.
S7 ✨ 特殊句式
感叹句（What / How） 九全U2 🔥🔥 必考
▾
句式结构例句
What + 名词短语What (a/an) + adj. + n. + (主+谓)!What a bright moon!
How + 形容词/副词How + adj./adv. + 主 + 谓!How bright the moon is!
❌ How a beautiful day it is!
✅ What a beautiful day it is! 或 How beautiful the day is!
used to / be used to 辨析 九全U4 🔥 高频
▾
"used 四姐妹"（完形填空经典题）
used to + do过去常常（现在不做了）
be used to + doing习惯做（to 是介词）
be used to + do被用来做（不定式）
be used for + doing被用来做（for 表用途）
句式结构
肯定主语 + used to + do
否定主语 + didn't use to + do
疑问Did + 主语 + use to + do?
❌ I used to getting up early now.（"习惯"应用 be used to + doing）
✅ I am used to getting up early.
be supposed to 九全U10 📌 中频
▾
句式例句
be supposed to + doYou are supposed to shake hands.
be not supposed to + doYou are not supposed to kiss on the cheek.
be expected to + doYou are expected to arrive on time.
❌ You are suppose to shake hands. → ✅ You are supposed to shake hands.
注意 supposed 的 d 不可漏。
make + 宾语 + 宾补 九全U11 📌 中频
▾
结构例句
make + sb. + adj.Sad movies make me sad.
make + sb. + doThe joke made me laugh.
make + sb. + feel + adj.It makes me feel relaxed.
🚨 make sb. do（无 to）→ 变被动要还原 to 主动：The boss made him work all day.
被动：He was made to work all day.
祈使句 · 提建议 八上U8 📌 中频
▾
功能句式例句
共同行动Let's + 动词原形Let's communicate.
建议How about + doing?How about making a plan?
禁止Don't + 动词原形Don't shout.
❌ Let's don't argue. → ✅ Let's not argue.
❌ How about take a break? → ✅ How about taking a break?
S8 📝 非谓语动词
by + doing · 动名词作主语 九全U1 🔥 高频
▾
结构例句
by + doing（通过……方式）I study by making flashcards.
Doing… + 三单谓语Reading aloud helps a lot.
🚨 by + doing vs for + doing ❌ I study for listening to songs.（手段用 by）
✅ I study by listening to songs.
注意动名词作主语，谓语用三单：Reading helps ✓
不定式 · 反身代词 八下U1 🔥 高频
▾
结构例句
It's + adj. + to doIt's important to relax.
want/hope/need to doI want to take a break.
反身代词 myself / yourself / himself / herself / itself / ourselves / yourselves / themselves
常用：enjoy oneself · by oneself · teach oneself
❌ He enjoyed myself at the party. → ✅ He enjoyed himself at the party.
remember/forget + doing vs to do 九全U14 🔥 高频
▾
动词+ doing（做过）+ to do（要做）
rememberI remember meeting you.Remember to call me.
forgetI forgot locking the door.Don't forget to write.
tryTry eating healthy food.Try to finish on time.
stopStop smoking.Stop to rest.
🚨 意思完全不同 ❌ I remember to meet you before.（"记得去见面" ≠ "记得见过"）
✅ I remember meeting you before.
口诀：doing 做过，to do 要做。
S9 📖 词法与短语
频率副词 · How often 八上U2 🔥 高频
▾
副词含义频率
always总是100%
usually通常80%
often经常60%
sometimes有时40%
hardly ever几乎从不~5%
never从不0%
How often / How long / How soon
How often多久一次（频率）
— Twice a week.
How long多长时间（时长）
— For two hours.
How soon多久以后
— In two days.
❌ I hard ever exercise. → ✅ I hardly ever exercise.
动词短语（短语动词） 八下U2 📌 中频
▾
短语含义例句
clean up打扫干净Let's clean up the park.
give away赠送Let's give away old clothes.
put up张贴Let's put up signs.
cheer up使振奋Let's cheer up the sick kids.
🚨 代词放中间 ✅ clean up the park = clean the park up
✅ clean it up ✓   ❌ clean up it ✗
