七下 · Unit 8

Is there a post office near here?

问路指路 · 街区设施 · There be 句型

📖 话题：Neighborhood & Locations 💬 功能：Ask for and give directions · Describe locations 📚 语法：There is/are · 方位介词 across from / next to / between 📝 写作：描述你的街区/社区

🎯 我能做到

📖 课文精讲 · Asking for directions
场景：一个游客在街上问路，想去附近的邮局。
Visitor: Excuse me. Is there a post office near here?
Linda: Yes, there is. Just go straight and turn left on Bridge Street.
Visitor: Is it far from here?
Linda: No, it's not far. It's across from the library.
Visitor: Thank you very much. Also, is there a restaurant near the post office?
Linda: Yes. There's a Chinese restaurant between the post office and the hotel.
Visitor: Great! Thanks for your help.
Linda: You're welcome. Have a nice day!
📖 查看翻译

逐句翻译：

游客: 打扰一下，这附近有邮局吗？

Linda: 是的，有。直走，然后在桥街左转。

游客: 离这里远吗？

Linda: 不远。就在图书馆对面。

游客: 非常感谢。还有，邮局附近有餐馆吗？

Linda: 有的。邮局和酒店之间有一家中餐馆。

游客: 太好了！谢谢你的帮助。

Linda: 不客气。祝你有美好的一天！

🔑 重点讲解：

1. Is there a post office near here? → There be 句型的一般疑问句：Is/Are there...?

2. Go straight → "直走"；turn left / turn right → "左转 / 右转"。

3. Across from → "在……对面"；between...and... → "在……和……之间"。

4. Is it far from here? → "离这里远吗？" far from = 离……远。

📖 Reading: My Neighborhood

I live in a nice neighborhood. There is a supermarket near my house.

Go out of my house and turn right. Then go straight along Center Street.

You will see a park on your left. The supermarket is across from the park.

There is also a bank next to the supermarket. And a hotel is behind the bank.

I love my neighborhood because there are many nice places to go.

🔤 重点句型 · Sentence Patterns
Is there a ... near here?
询问某地是否有某物/某场所。
✅ Is there a post office near here?
✅ Are there any restaurants near here?
➡ 肯定回答：Yes, there is/are.
➡ 否定回答：No, there isn't/aren't.
Where is the ...? / How can I get to ...?
更具体的问路方式。
✅ Where is the library?
✅ How can I get to the hospital?
➡ It's on Center Street.
➡ Go straight and turn left.
Go straight / Turn left / Turn right
指路的核心指令。
✅ Go straight along Main Street.
✅ Turn left at the first crossing.
✅ Turn right on Bridge Road.
➡ 注意：Go 跟副词，Turn 跟方向词。
方位介词：across from / next to / between
描述两个地点之间的位置关系。
✅ A is across from B. （在……对面）
✅ A is next to B. （在……旁边）
✅ A is between B and C. （在B和C之间）
✅ A is in front of B. （在……前面）
✅ A is behind B. （在……后面）
📚 语法聚焦 · There be & Prepositions

1️⃣ There be 句型：表示"某地有某物"

There is/are + 名词 + 地点 表示"在……地方有……"。

形式主语例句
There is (+ a/an + 单数名词)单数There is a bank near here.
There are (+ 复数名词)复数There are two hotels on this street.
否定There isn't a supermarket near here.
一般疑问句Is there a post office near here?

📌 注意：There be 遵循"就近原则"——如果紧挨着的是单数名词用 is，复数名词用 are。
There is a book and two pens. / There are two pens and a book.

2️⃣ 方位介词图示

介词含义示例
in在……里面The pen is in the pencil case.
on在……上面The book is on the desk.
under在……下面The cat is under the table.
next to在……旁边The hotel is next to the bank.
across from在……对面The park is across from the library.
between在……之间My school is between the park and the bank.
in front of在……前面The car is in front of the house.
behind在……后面The garden is behind the house.
⚠️ 中式英语纠错：
❌ The bank is across the street.（缺少 from）
✅ The bank is across from the street.

❌ In my school have a library.（中文直译"有"用 have）
✅ There is a library in my school.
📝 三级闯关 · 实战练习

🥉 基础关 · 概念识记

基础 ★☆☆
____ there a bank near here? (Is / Are / Am)
📝 查看答案
答案：Is
解析：a bank 是单数名词，There be 句型中用 Is there。
基础 ★☆☆
The supermarket is ____ (在……对面) the post office.
📝 查看答案
答案：across from
解析：across from 表示"在……对面"。
基础 ★☆☆
Go ____ and turn left. The hotel is on your right. (straight / straightly)
📝 查看答案
答案：straight
解析：go straight 是固定搭配，straight 既是形容词也是副词。

🥈 提升关 · 语境运用

提升 ★★☆
改错：There have a restaurant near here.
📝 查看答案
答案：There is a restaurant near here.
解析：表达"某地有某物"用 There be，不用 have。
提升 ★★☆
连词成句：there / near / a / is / here / post office
📝 查看答案
答案：Is there a post office near here?
解析：There be 的一般疑问句：Is/Are there + 名词 + 地点。
提升 ★★☆
用 between 改写句子：The library is on Center Street. The bank is also on Center Street. The hotel is in the middle.
📝 查看答案
答案：The hotel is between the library and the bank.
解析：between A and B 表示在 A 和 B 之间。

🥇 挑战关 · 综合运用

挑战 ★★★
汉译英：这附近有图书馆吗？有，它在公园对面，医院旁边。
📝 查看答案
答案：Is there a library near here? Yes, there is. It's across from the park and next to the hospital.
解析：先用 Is there... 提问；然后描述位置 across from / next to。
挑战 ★★★
补全对话：
A: Excuse me. ____ there a supermarket ____ here?
B: Yes, ____ is. Go straight and ____ left at the crossing.
A: Is it ____ from here?
B: No, it's not far.
📝 查看答案
答案：Is; near; there; turn; far
解析：Is there...near here? / Yes, there is. / turn left / Is it far from here?

✍️ 微写作：My neighborhood

用 There be 句型和方位介词描述你学校附近或家附近的街区，写5-6句话。

I live near a big park. There are many trees and flowers in the park.

There is a supermarket across from the park. My mom goes shopping there every day.

The library is next to the supermarket. I often read books on weekends.

My school is between the park and the bank. I walk to school every day.

I like my neighborhood very much. It's clean and quiet.

我住在一个大公园附近。公园里有很多树和花。超市在公园对面。我妈妈每天去那里购物。图书馆在超市旁边。我周末经常去看书。我的学校在公园和银行之间。我每天走路去上学。我非常喜欢我的街区。它又干净又安静。
