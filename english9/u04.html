Our Memory

记忆与回忆 · 定语从句 · 情感表达

📖 话题：Memories and past experiences💬 功能：Share memories, express emotions about the past📚 语法：定语从句 (who/which/that) · used to📝 写作：最难忘的记忆

🎯 我能做到

📖 课文精讲 · Memories in Photos
场景：Emma和Jack在翻看旧照片，回忆过去的人、地方和事情。
Emma:Look at this old photo! Do you remember the park we used to go to?
Jack:Of course! That's the park which had the big slide. We used to spend hours there.
Emma:And here's Mrs. Chen, the teacher who taught us how to plant flowers.
Jack:I remember planting sunflowers with her. They were the flowers that grew the tallest!
Emma:Do you remember the treehouse we used to play in? It was a place which felt like magic.
Jack:Yes! And I remember climbing up the ladder that my grandpa built. Those were the days!
Emma:It's funny how memories can make you smile. They are treasures that never fade.
Jack:You're right. Let's take more photos — the ones that we'll look back on in the future!
📖 查看翻译

Emma: 看这张老照片！你还记得我们以前常去的那个公园吗？

Jack: 当然！就是那个有大滑梯的公园。我们以前在那儿一玩就是好几个小时。

Emma: 这是陈老师，她教我们怎么种花。

Jack: 我记得和她一起种向日葵。它们是长得最高的花！

Emma: 你还记得我们以前玩的那个树屋吗？那是个感觉像魔法一样的地方。

Jack: 记得！我还记得爬我爷爷做的梯子。那些日子真美好！

Emma: 回忆总能让人微笑，这真有趣。它们是不会褪色的宝藏。

Jack: 你说得对。我们多拍些照片吧——那些将来我们会回顾的照片！

🔑 重点讲解：

定语从句：用 who 指人，which 指物，that 可指人也可指物。

used to + 动词原形：表示过去常常做某事（现在不再做）。

I remember + V-ing：表示记得做过某事。

🔤 重点句型 · Sentence Patterns
定语从句 — who (指人)
用于修饰人，who 在从句中作主语或宾语。
She is the teacher who taught me English. / The boy who sits next to me is my best friend.
定语从句 — which (指物)
用于修饰事物，which 在从句中作主语或宾语。
This is the book which changed my life. / I love the park which has a beautiful lake.
定语从句 — that (指人/物)
可替代 who 或 which，既指人也指物。
The cake that you made is delicious. / He is the person that I admire most.
used to + 动词原形
表示过去常常做某事（现在不再做）。否定：didn't use to
We used to play together every day. / I didn't use to like vegetables.
I remember + V-ing
表示记得做过某事。
I remember visiting the museum. / I remember meeting you before.
📚 语法聚焦 · 定语从句 & used to

定语从句 (Relative Clauses)

关系代词指代对象用法例句
who人在从句中作主语/宾语I know a girl who speaks five languages.
which物在从句中作主语/宾语This is the house which my father built.
that人/物可替代 who/whichShe is the one that helps me most.

used to 结构

形式用法例句
主语 + used to + V过去常常（现在不）I used to live in that small town.
主语 + didn't use to + V过去不常She didn't use to like reading.
Did + 主语 + use to + V?疑问形式Did you use to play the piano?

I remember + V-ing

表示"记得做过某事"，后接动名词形式。

I remember visiting my grandparents every summer. / I remember hearing that song before.

定语从句中，先行词是物时，who 不能使用，必须用 which 或 that。

先行词既有人又有物时，只能用 that。

used to 只用于过去时，后面接动词原形。

I remember to do（记得要做某事）≠ I remember doing（记得做过某事）。

Memories are the treasures of the heart. 记忆是心中的宝藏。

📖 阅读专项 · The Gift of Memory

Have you ever looked at an old photo and felt a warm feeling inside? That is the power of memory. Memories are like treasures that we keep in our hearts. They connect us to our past and help us understand who we are.

Scientists say that memories are stored in different parts of our brain. Some memories are short — they last for a few seconds. But other memories stay with us for a lifetime. These are the memories that shape our character and influence our choices.

The people who have had a positive impact on our lives are often the ones we remember best. A teacher who encouraged us, a friend who stood by us, or a family member who loved us unconditionally — these are the people that make our memory book rich and colorful.

Memories also teach us lessons. The mistakes which we made in the past help us grow. The successes that we achieved give us confidence. Every experience, whether happy or sad, is a gift that helps us become the person we are meant to be.

✍️ 写作进阶 · My Most Unforgettable Memory
📋 写作题目
描述你最难忘的一段记忆。可以是与家人、朋友或老师之间的故事。写一篇80-100词的短文，尽量使用定语从句和 used to 结构。
开头：引出你要讲述的记忆
描述记忆中的场景（人、地方、事情）
说明这件事为什么难忘
结尾：这段记忆对你的影响或意义
One of the most unforgettable memories that I have is from my first school trip. I remember visiting a science museum with my classmates. The guide who showed us around was very kind and patient. He explained everything in a way that was easy to understand. There was a robot which could draw pictures, and it amazed all of us. We used to think science was boring, but that trip changed our minds. It was an experience that opened my eyes to the wonders of science. I will always treasure that day.
我最难忘的一段记忆来自我的第一次学校旅行。我记得和同学们参观了一个科学博物馆。带我们参观的向导非常和蔼耐心。他用一种容易理解的方式解释一切。有一个会画画的机器人，让我们所有人都惊叹不已。我们以前觉得科学很无聊，但那次旅行改变了我们的想法。那是一次让我大开眼界、领略微科学奥秘的经历。我会永远珍惜那一天。
