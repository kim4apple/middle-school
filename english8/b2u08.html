Making a Difference

公益行动 · 现在完成时 · 社会贡献

📖 话题：Social responsibility and volunteering💬 功能：Talk about volunteer work, express future goals📚 语法：现在完成时复习 · be going to/will · 定语从句 (who/that) 入门📝 写作：志愿活动计划

🎯 我能做到

📖 课文精讲 · Making a Difference
场景：几位同学在讨论自己做过的志愿活动和未来的计划。
Li Hua:Have you ever done any volunteer work?
Wang Fang:Yes, I have. I've helped at a local charity shop twice.
Li Hua:That's great! I've never done that, but I've collected money for homeless people.
Zhang Lin:I've volunteered at a community clean-up. We planted trees and cleaned the park.
Wang Fang:Wow! I'm going to join a beach clean-up next month. Do you want to come?
Li Hua:Sure! I think volunteering is something that makes the world better.
📖 查看翻译

Li Hua: 你做过志愿活动吗？

Wang Fang: 是的，做过。我在一家本地慈善商店帮过两次忙。

Li Hua: 太棒了！我没做过那个，但我为无家可归的人筹过款。

Zhang Lin: 我参加过社区清扫活动。我们种了树，打扫了公园。

Wang Fang: 哇！我下个月准备参加海滩清理活动。你们想来吗？

Li Hua: 当然！我觉得志愿服务是让世界变得更美好的事情。

🔑 重点讲解：

1. Have you ever done any volunteer work? → 现在完成时：Have/Has + 过去分词，表示"曾经做过……"。

2. I've helped at a local charity shop twice. → 现在完成时表示经历，twice "两次"。

3. I've never done that. → never 用于现在完成时否定句中，表示"从未"。

4. I'm going to join a beach clean-up next month. → be going to 表示计划好的志愿活动。

5. Volunteering is something that makes the world better. → that 引导的定语从句修饰 "something"。

🔤 重点句型 · Sentence Patterns
Have you ever + 过去分词 …?
询问志愿经历
Have you ever volunteered at a hospital?
… who/that …（定语从句）
描述某类人或事物
She is a person who always helps others. / It's something that makes me happy.
be going to + 动词原形
计划做某件志愿活动
I am going to join a clean-up event this weekend.
It's + adj. + for + 人 + to + 动词
评价某事
It's important for us to help people in need.
📚 语法聚焦 · 现在完成时 & 定语从句

现在完成时 — 谈论经历

句式结构示例
肯定have/has + 过去分词I have helped at a charity shop.
否定haven't/hasn't + 过去分词I haven't done volunteer work before.
疑问Have/Has + 主语 + 过去分词?Have you ever collected money for the poor?

定语从句 — who / that

关系词指代示例
who人She is a volunteer who teaches children.
that人或物This is a project that helps the environment.

be going to — 未来的志愿计划

句式结构示例
肯定am/is/are going to + 动词I am going to plant trees next week.
否定am/is/are not going to + 动词We are not going to give up.
疑问Am/Is/Are + 主语 + going to + 动词?Are you going to join the clean-up?

It's + adj. + for + 人 + to + 动词

结构含义示例
It's + adj. + for sb. + to do sth.对某人来说做某事是……的It's meaningful for us to help others.

❌ I have went to a charity shop.（go 的过去分词是 gone）

✅ I have gone to a charity shop.

❌ She is a teacher which helps children.（指人用 who）

✅ She is a teacher who helps children.

❌ I will going to volunteer next week.（be going to 已有 will 含义）

✅ I am going to volunteer next week.

📖 阅读专项 · Small Actions, Big Changes

Have you ever thought that one person can make a difference? Meet three young people who are changing the world through small actions.

Chen Jie, 15, started a "Book Sharing" project in her neighborhood. She collected over 200 books from her friends and set up a small library in the community center. "Reading is something that opens our minds. I want everyone to enjoy it," she says.

Tom, 14, from the UK, noticed that many elderly people in his town lived alone. He visits them every weekend to chat and help with shopping. "They have stories that inspire me. It's a small thing, but it means a lot to them," he says.

Li Wei, 16, is a young environmentalist. He organized a river clean-up campaign and invited his classmates to join. In just one day, they collected 30 bags of trash. "We are going to make this a monthly event. The environment is something that we must protect," he says.

These young people show us that you don't need to be rich or famous to make a difference. Small actions, when done with love, can create big changes. What are you going to do to help?

🏆 中考题型专练 · 完形填空

阅读短文，选择最佳选项。

Have you ever (1)____ about becoming a volunteer? Many young people today are making a difference (2)____ their communities. Zhang Lin is a student (3)____ volunteers at a local hospital every weekend. "I have (4)____ many people who need help. It's meaningful (5)____ me to bring them comfort," she says. Some students are going to (6)____ a charity walk next month to raise money for sick children. Small actions can (7)____ a big difference. It's never too late to start!

1. A. think B. thought C. thinking D. thinks
2. A. in B. on C. at D. for
3. A. which B. who C. what D. when
4. A. meet B. met C. meets D. meeting
5. A. to B. of C. for D. with
6. A. organize B. organized C. organizing D. organizes
7. A. do B. take C. make D. give
答案
1. B (thought)；2. A (in)；3. B (who)；4. B (met)；5. C (for)；6. A (organize)；7. C (make)
✍️ 写作进阶 · My Volunteer Plan
📋 写作题目
请以"My Volunteer Plan"为题，写一篇 70 词左右的短文，说说你打算做什么志愿活动来改变世界。用到 be going to、定语从句和 It's + adj. + for + 人 + to + 动词。
开头：我想成为一名志愿者，为什么。
主体：具体计划（用 be going to 描述）。
结尾：志愿活动的意义（用定语从句或 It's…for…to…结构）。
I am going to be a volunteer who helps protect the environment. Next month, I am going to join a beach clean-up with my friends. We are going to collect plastic bottles and trash. Also, I am going to plant trees in our community. It's important for us to take care of our planet. Small actions can make a big difference. I hope more people will join us!
我打算成为一名保护环境的志愿者。下个月，我准备和朋友们参加海滩清理活动。我们会收集塑料瓶和垃圾。我还打算在社区种树。保护我们的星球对我们来说很重要。小行动能带来大改变。希望更多人加入我们！

Volunteer Fair
小组策划一场"志愿活动展览会"：每个小组选择一项公益事业，设计海报向全班介绍，号召大家参加。
1选择一项公益主题（环保、教育、养老等）。
2设计海报：活动名称、时间、地点、内容。
3用英语准备 2 分钟介绍（用到现在完成时和 be going to）。
4在展览会上展示并呼吁同学参加。

4.展示有号召力和感染力
