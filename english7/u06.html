Unit 6

A Day in the Life

日常作息 · 时间表达 · 一般现在时

📖 话题：Daily routines 💬 功能：Talk about daily schedules & time 📚 语法：一般现在时 · 时间表达法 (at/in/on) 📝 写作：我的一天

🎯 我能做到

📖 课文精讲 · Morning before school
场景：早上在家，妈妈叫李明起床，问他一整天的时间安排。
Mum: Li Ming, it's time to get up! It's seven o'clock.
Li Ming: OK, Mum. I'm coming!
Mum: What time do you have breakfast?
Li Ming: I have breakfast at 7:15.
Mum: And what time do you go to school?
Li Ming: At 7:30. I don't want to be late!
Mum: Good. When do you come back home?
Li Ming: I come back at 5:00 in the afternoon.
Mum: OK. Have a nice day!
📖 查看翻译

逐句翻译：

妈妈: 李明，该起床了！现在是七点钟。

李明: 好的，妈妈。我来了！

妈妈: 你几点吃早餐？

李明: 我七点十五分吃早餐。

妈妈: 那你几点去上学？

李明: 七点半。我可不想迟到！

妈妈: 好的。你几点回家？

李明: 我下午五点回来。

妈妈: 好的。祝你过得愉快！

🔑 重点讲解：

1. It's time to ... — "该做……了"
e.g. It's time to get up. / It's time to go to school.

2. What time ... ? / When ... ? — 询问时间。What time 问具体时刻，When 问的时间范围更广。

3. at + 具体时间: at 7 o'clock, at 7:30。在 morning/afternoon/evening 前用 in。

4. 日常活动短语: get up（起床）, have breakfast（吃早餐）, go to school（上学）, come back home（回家）。

5. Have a nice day! — "祝你过得愉快！" 告别时的常用表达。

📖 Reading: Tom's Day

Tom is a student from the UK. He gets up at 7:00 every morning.

He has breakfast at 7:30. Then he goes to school at 8:00.

School starts at 8:30. He has four classes in the morning.

He has lunch at 12:00 at school. In the afternoon, he has two classes.

After school, he plays football with his friends. He goes home at 5:00.

He does his homework at 7:00. He goes to bed at 9:30.

What a busy but happy day!

🔤 重点句型 · Sentence Patterns
It's time to + 动词原形
"该做……了"，催促或提醒某人做某事。
✅ It's time to get up.
✅ It's time to go to school.
✅ It's time to have breakfast.
➡ It's time (for + 人) + to do sth.
What time do you + 动词原形?
询问对方做某事的具体时间。
✅ What time do you get up?
✅ What time do you have lunch?
➡ 回答：I + 动词 + at + 时间.
➡ 第三人称：What time does he get up?
at / in / on + 时间
区分时间介词用法。
✅ at 7 o'clock / 7:30 / night
✅ in the morning / the afternoon / the evening
✅ on Monday / Sunday morning / June 1st
➡ at + 具体时刻 / in + 一天时段 / on + 具体某天
When do you + 动词原形?
When 与 What time 意思相近，但 When 回答范围更广。
✅ When do you come back home?
✅ When does she go to bed?
➡ When = 什么时候（可回答日期或时刻）
➡ What time = 几点（只回答具体时刻）
📚 语法聚焦 · Simple Present & Time Expressions

1️⃣ 时间表达法

表达方式写法读法
整点7:00seven o'clock
半点7:30half past seven
一刻 (过去)7:15a quarter past seven
三刻 (差一刻)7:45a quarter to eight
顺读法7:15seven fifteen
顺读法7:45seven forty-five

📌 口诀：顺读法：小时 + 分钟直接读；逆读法：分钟不超过30用 past，超过30用 to。

2️⃣ 时间介词：at / in / on

介词用法示例
at具体时刻 / 时间点at 7 o'clock, at noon, at night
in一天中的时段 / 月 / 年 / 季节in the morning, in May
on具体某天 / 星期 / 日期on Monday, on June 1st

📌 对比：
at 7:00 in the morning（早上七点）→ 小时间 at 大时段 in
on Monday morning（周一早上）→ 具体某天的早/下午/晚上用 on

3️⃣ 一般现在时 (Simple Present Tense)

表示经常发生的动作或习惯。

主语动词形式示例
I / You / We / They动词原形I get up at 7:00.
He / She / It动词 + s / esHe gets up at 7:00.
💡 动词第三人称单数变化规则：
一般情况 + s: get → gets, play → plays
以 s/x/sh/ch/o 结尾 + es: go → goes, watch → watches
辅音+y 变 y 为 i + es: study → studies, fly → flies
特殊：have → has
⚠️ 常见错误：
❌ He get up at 7:00.（三单动词忘了加 s）
✅ He gets up at 7:00.

❌ I goes to school at 8:00.（I 后用动词原形）
✅ I go to school at 8:00.

用 What time 或 When 来问时间：

问句回答

📝 三级闯关 · 实战练习

🥉 基础关 · 概念识记

基础 ★☆☆
What time do you get up? — I get up ____ 7:00.
📝 查看答案
答案：at
解析：具体时刻前用 at。
基础 ★☆☆
I go to bed ____ 9:30 ____ the evening.
📝 查看答案
答案：at; in
解析：具体时刻 at，时间段 in。
基础 ★☆☆
He ____ (go) to school at 8:00 every day.
📝 查看答案
答案：goes
解析：主语 He 是第三人称单数，go 加 es 变为 goes。

🥈 提升关 · 语境运用

提升 ★★☆
改错：She get up at 6:30 every morning.
📝 查看答案
答案：She gets up at 6:30 every morning.
解析：She 是第三人称单数，get 应改为 gets。
提升 ★★☆
连词成句：time / what / you / do / breakfast / have / ?
📝 查看答案
答案：What time do you have breakfast?
解析：What time + do + 主语 + 动词原形?
提升 ★★☆
用 does 改写：What time do you go to bed?
📝 查看答案
答案：What time does he/she go to bed?
解析：第三人称单数用 does，后面动词用原形 go。

🥇 挑战关 · 综合运用

挑战 ★★★
汉译英：我每天早上六点半起床。我弟弟七点起床。
📝 查看答案
答案：I get up at 6:30 every morning. My brother gets up at 7:00.
解析：注意 my brother 是第三人称单数，get 要加 s。
挑战 ★★★
补全对话：
A: What time ____ you ____ (have) lunch?
B: I ____ lunch at 12:00.
A: ____ your brother have lunch at school?
B: No, he ____. He ____ (go) home for lunch.
📝 查看答案
答案：do; have; have; Does; doesn't; goes
解析：B 句的 brother 是第三人称单数，疑问用 Does，否定用 doesn't，肯定用 goes。
