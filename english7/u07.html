Unit 7

Happy Birthday!

日期表达 · 月份 · 序数词 · 庆祝活动

📖 话题：Birthdays and celebrations 💬 功能：Talk about dates and birthday activities 📚 语法：序数词 (first~thirty-first) · 月份 · When 问句 📝 写作：生日邀请或介绍

🎯 我能做到

📖 课文精讲 · Planning a birthday party
场景：Chen Jie 的生日快到了，她和朋友们商量开生日派对。
Chen Jie: Hi, everyone! My birthday is coming!
Wang Fang: Really? When is your birthday?
Chen Jie: It's on March 15th.
Li Ming: That's next Saturday! Let's have a party!
Wang Fang: Great idea! What do you want to do?
Chen Jie: I want to have a party at my home. We can sing, dance and eat cake!
Li Ming: Can I bring some balloons?
Chen Jie: Sure! And please come at 5 o'clock.
Wang Fang: Happy birthday, Chen Jie!
Chen Jie: Thank you! See you on Saturday!
📖 查看翻译

逐句翻译：

Chen Jie: 嗨，大家！我的生日要到了！

Wang Fang: 真的吗？你的生日是什么时候？

Chen Jie: 在3月15日。

Li Ming: 那是下周六！我们开派对吧！

Wang Fang: 好主意！你想做什么？

Chen Jie: 我想在我家开派对。我们可以唱歌、跳舞、吃蛋糕！

Li Ming: 我能带些气球吗？

Chen Jie: 当然！请5点钟来。

Wang Fang: 生日快乐，Chen Jie！

Chen Jie: 谢谢！周六见！

🔑 重点讲解：

1. When is your birthday? → "你的生日是什么时候？" When 引导特殊疑问句询问时间。
回答：It's on + 日期 (月份+序数词)

2. on + 具体日期（on March 15th）/ in + 月份（in March）

3. 序数词：1st (first), 2nd (second), 3rd (third), 4th-20th (一般加th)...

4. Let's have a party! → "我们开派对吧！" Let's + 动词原形 表示提议。

5. bring — "带来" (bring sth.) ；come — "来" (come to...)

📖 Reading: Birthday Around the World

Birthdays are special days!

In China, people often eat birthday noodles and eggs. Long noodles mean long life.

In the UK, children have birthday parties with cake and balloons. They sing "Happy Birthday" and make a wish.

In the US, people eat birthday cake and open gifts. Some people have a big party with family and friends.

🔤 重点句型 · Sentence Patterns
When is + 某人的 birthday?
询问生日日期，回答用 It's on + 月份 + 序数词。
✅ When is your birthday?
➡ It's on March 15th.
✅ When is his birthday?
➡ It's on May 2nd.
Let's + 动词原形
表示提议"我们一起…吧"。
✅ Let's have a party!
✅ Let's sing Happy Birthday!
➡ 否定：Let's not be late.
Happy + 节日!
节日祝福固定搭配。
✅ Happy Birthday!
✅ Happy New Year!
✅ Merry Christmas!
in + 月份 / on + 具体日期
时间介词的基本搭配。
✅ in March （在三月）
✅ on March 15th （在3月15日）
✅ on Saturday （在周六）
➡ in + 月份/年；on + 具体日期/星期

序数词表示顺序"第几"，常与 the 连用。

3️⃣ 时间介词 in / on / at

介词用法示例
in+ 月份 / 年份 / 季节in March · in 2024

at+ 具体钟点at 5 o'clock · at noon
💡 对比：
My birthday is in March. （只说月份）
My birthday is on March 15th. （具体到哪一天）

📝 三级闯关 · 实战练习

🥉 基础关 · 概念识记

基础 ★☆☆
____ is your birthday? — It's on June 5th.
📝 查看答案
答案：When
解析：When 问时间。回答为具体日期，用 on + 日期。
基础 ★☆☆
My birthday is ____ March. (in / on / at)
📝 查看答案
答案：in
解析：in + 月份；on + 具体日期。
基础 ★☆☆
three 的序数词是 ____。
📝 查看答案
答案：third (3rd)
解析：three → third，特殊变化。
基础 ★☆☆
____ (十二月) is the twelfth month of the year.
📝 查看答案
答案：December
解析：十二月是 December，首字母大写。

🥈 提升关 · 语境运用

提升 ★★☆
选择：My birthday is on ____. (June 5th / June)
📝 查看答案
答案：June 5th
解析：on + 具体日期，June 是月份，不能说 on June。
提升 ★★☆
连词成句：your / when / birthday / is / ?
📝 查看答案
答案：When is your birthday?
解析：特殊疑问句：When + is + 主语？
提升 ★★☆
改错：My birthday is at May 10th.
📝 查看答案
答案：My birthday is on May 10th.
解析：具体日期前用 on，不是 at。
提升 ★★☆
写出 twelve 的序数词形式。
📝 查看答案
答案：twelfth (12th)
解析：twelve → twelfth，ve 变 f 加 th。

🥇 挑战关 · 综合运用

挑战 ★★★
汉译英：我的生日在 5 月 10 日。我通常在家里开派对。
📝 查看答案
答案：My birthday is on May 10th. I usually have a party at my home.
解析：on + 日期；have a party 表示开派对。
挑战 ★★★
补全对话：
A: ____ is your birthday?
B: It's ____ March 22nd. Let's ____ a party!
A: Great! Can I ____ some balloons?
B: Sure!
📝 查看答案
答案：When; on; have; bring
解析：When 问日期；on + 具体日期；Let's have a party；bring 带来。
