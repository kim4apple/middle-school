The Changing World

世界变化 · 现在完成时 · 社会变迁

📖 话题：Social change and development💬 功能：Describe changes, compare past and present📚 语法：现在完成时 (延续性 vs 非延续性动词) · since/for📝 写作：对比过去和现在

🎯 我能做到

📖 课文精讲 · The Changing Town
场景：爷爷 (Grandpa) 和 小明 (Xiao Ming) 在公园里看老照片，谈论小镇的变化。
Grandpa:Look at this old photo, Xiao Ming. This was our town thirty years ago.
Xiao Ming:Wow, it looks so different! Has the town changed a lot?
Grandpa:Yes, it has changed a great deal. There weren't any tall buildings back then.
Xiao Ming:How long have you lived here, Grandpa?
Grandpa:I have lived here for over sixty years. I was born in this town.
Xiao Ming:What was life like in the past?
Grandpa:The streets were narrow and there were few cars. Now the city has developed rapidly.
Xiao Ming:Have people's lives improved?
Grandpa:Certainly! We have better transport, hospitals, and schools. Life has become much more convenient.
Xiao Ming:Has the environment changed too?
Grandpa:Yes. The river was cleaner before, but the government has already taken steps to protect it.
Xiao Ming:I hope our town will become even better in the future!
📖 查看翻译

爷爷： 看这张老照片，小明。这是我们小镇三十年前的样子。

小明： 哇，看起来太不一样了！小镇变化很大吗？

爷爷： 是的，变化非常大。那时候还没有高楼大厦呢。

小明： 爷爷，您在这儿住了多久了？

爷爷： 我在这儿住了六十多年了。我就出生在这个镇上。

小明： 过去的生活是什么样的？

爷爷： 街道很窄，汽车很少。现在城市发展得很快。

小明： 人们的生活改善了吗？

爷爷： 当然！我们有更好的交通、医院和学校。生活变得方便多了。

小明： 环境也变了吗？

爷爷： 是的。以前河水更干净些，但政府已经采取措施保护它了。

小明： 希望我们的小镇未来会变得更好！

🔑 重点讲解：

现在完成时：have/has + 过去分词 — The city has developed rapidly.

How long have you + 过去分词？询问持续多久 — How long have you lived here?

for + 时间段，since + 时间点 — for over sixty years / since 1960

already 用于肯定句，yet 用于否定/疑问句 — has already taken / Has it changed yet?

🔤 重点句型 · Key Sentence Patterns
have/has + 过去分词
现在完成时基本结构，表示过去发生且与现在有关的动作。
The city has changed a lot. / I have lived here for ten years.
How long have you + 过去分词?
询问某个状态或动作持续了多久。
How long have you studied English? / How long has she lived in this city?
for + 时间段 / since + 时间点
for 接一段时间，since 接具体时间点或从句。
He has worked here for five years. / She has been here since 2020.
already / yet / just / ever / never
already 肯定句中，yet 否定/疑问句末，just 刚，ever 曾经，never 从未。
I have already finished. / Have you finished yet? / I have just arrived. / Have you ever been there? / I have never seen that.
📚 语法聚焦 · 现在完成时

基本结构

句式结构例句
肯定句主语 + have/has + 过去分词I have lived here for 10 years.
否定句主语 + haven't/hasn't + 过去分词She hasn't finished her homework yet.
疑问句Have/Has + 主语 + 过去分词?Have you ever been to Beijing?

for / since 用法对比

介词接什么例句
for时间段 (two years, a month, 3 days)I have studied English for three years.
since时间点 (2020, last week, Monday)He has worked here since 2021.

延续性动词 vs 非延续性动词

非延续性 (短暂)→ 延续性 (持续)例句
buyhaveI have had this book for a week.
borrowkeepHe has kept the book for two weeks.
leavebe awayShe has been away for three days.
joinbe in / be a member ofHe has been in the club since last year.
diebe deadHis grandpa has been dead for five years.
openbe openThe shop has been open for six hours.

already / yet / just / ever / never

副词位置用法例句
alreadyhave/has 后肯定句，表示「已经」I have already finished.
yet句末否定/疑问句，表示「还(没)」Have you finished yet?
justhave/has 后肯定句，表示「刚刚」I have just arrived.
everhave/has 后疑问句，表示「曾经」Have you ever been there?
neverhave/has 后否定句，表示「从未」I have never seen that.

for + 时间段 (for two hours)，since + 时间点 (since 8 o'clock)

非延续性动词不能与 for/since 连用，必须换成延续性动词。

Have you ever been to...? 问「去过某地」；Have you gone to...? 问「去了某地(还没回来)」

📖 阅读专项 · A World in Motion

Have you ever wondered how much the world has changed? In the past few decades, technology has transformed almost every part of our lives.

Transport has improved dramatically. People used to travel by horse or on foot. Today, high-speed trains and airplanes have made the world smaller. We can travel to another city in just a few hours. The development of public transport has also reduced pollution.

Communication has changed beyond recognition. In the past, people wrote letters to stay in touch. Now we have smartphones and the Internet. We can video call our friends on the other side of the world instantly. Social media has connected people like never before.

Technology has changed the way we work and learn. Students no longer rely only on textbooks. They have already started using computers and tablets in the classroom. Online learning has become an important part of modern education. Many people now work from home thanks to digital tools.

These changes have brought many benefits, but they have also created new challenges. How has your life changed? What changes do you hope to see in the future? The world is always in motion, and the pace of change is only getting faster.

My hometown is a small town in the south. It 1 (change) a lot over the past twenty years. I 2 (live) here since I was born. In the past, the roads were narrow and dirty. Now the government 3 (build) new roads and hospitals. Many new schools 4 (appear). People's lives 5 (become) better and better. I have 6 (never / ever) felt so proud of my hometown.

1 A. changes B. has changed C. changed D. is changing
2 A. have lived B. lived C. live D. was living
3 A. builds B. built C. has built D. is building
4 A. appear B. appeared C. have appeared D. appearing

✍️ 写作进阶 · 对比过去和现在
📋 写作题目
以 "The Biggest Change in My Life" 或 "Changes in My Hometown" 为题，写一篇 80-100 词的短文。要求：使用现在完成时，对比过去和现在，表达你的感受。
开头：引出话题 — 生活和过去相比发生了很大变化
主体1：描述过去的情况 (used to be / was / were)
主体2：描述现在的变化 (have/has + 过去分词)
主体3：具体例子支撑 (for / since 时间状语)
结尾：总结感受 — I think / I feel
The Biggest Change in My Life My life has changed a lot in the past few years. When I was in Grade 7, I used to be shy and afraid to speak in class. I have become much more confident since I joined the English club. I have made many friends and improved my speaking skills. I have also learned to express my ideas clearly. I have never enjoyed learning so much before. I believe change is a good thing, and I am ready for more changes in the future!
我生活中的最大变化 在过去的几年里，我的生活发生了很大变化。七年级时，我很害羞，不敢在课堂上发言。自从加入英语俱乐部以来，我变得自信多了。我交了很多朋友，提高了口语能力。我还学会了清晰地表达自己的想法。我以前从未如此享受学习。我相信变化是好事，我已经准备好迎接未来更多的变化！
