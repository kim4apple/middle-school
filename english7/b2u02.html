七下 · Unit 2

What time do you go to school?

日常作息 · 时间表达 · 频度副词

📖 话题：Daily routines 💬 功能：Ask and tell time · Talk about daily schedules 📚 语法：What time / When questions · 频率副词 always/usually/often/sometimes 📝 写作：描述自己或他人一天的生活

🎯 我能做到

📖 课文精讲 · A Busy Morning
场景：大课间时，同学们在聊每天几点起床、几点上学。
Anna: Hi Tom! You look sleepy today. What time do you usually get up?
Tom: I usually get up at 6:30. But this morning I got up at 5:45.
Anna: Wow, so early! What time do you eat breakfast?
Tom: I eat breakfast at 6:45. Then I go to school at 7:00.
Anna: You are always on time. I usually get up at 7:00. I eat breakfast at 7:15 and go to school at 7:30.
Tom: Oh, so you never eat a big breakfast? I always eat well in the morning.
Anna: Sometimes I eat an apple on the way to school. I want to sleep more!
Tom: Haha! Maybe you should go to bed earlier.
📖 查看翻译

逐句翻译：

安娜：嗨 Tom！你看上去很困。你通常几点起床？

Tom：我通常6:30 起床。但今天早上我 5:45 就起来了。

安娜：哇，这么早！你几点吃早餐？

Tom：我 6:45 吃早餐，然后 7:00 去上学。

安娜：你总是很准时。我通常7:00 起床，7:15 吃早餐，7:30 去上学。

Tom：哦，所以你从不吃丰盛的早餐？我早上总是吃得很饱。

安娜：有时我在上学的路上吃个苹果。我想多睡会儿！

Tom：哈哈！也许你应该早点睡觉。

🔑 重点讲解：

1. What time do you usually get up? → 用 What time 问具体时间点，回答用 at + 时间。

2. at 6:30 → 时间前面用介词 at。

3. always / usually / often / sometimes / never → 频率副词，放在实义动词之前、be 动词之后。

4. go to bed → "去睡觉"，不是 "go to sleep"（入睡）。

📖 Reading: Rick's Day

Hi, I'm Rick. I have a busy but happy daily life.

I usually get up at 6:15. Then I brush my teeth and take a shower.

I eat breakfast at 6:45. I always have milk and bread for breakfast.

School starts at 8:00. I often walk to school with my friend Jack.

In the evening, I sometimes watch TV for half an hour. I go to bed at 9:30.

🔤 重点句型 · Sentence Patterns
What time do you + 动词短语?
询问对方做某事的具体时间点。
✅ What time do you get up?
✅ What time does she go to school?
➡ 回答：I/She + 动词 + at + 时间
When do you + 动词短语?
When 可以代替 What time，但范围更广（可回答时间段）。
✅ When do you eat lunch?
➡ I eat lunch at 11:50.
✅ When does he take a shower?
➡ He takes a shower in the morning.
时间表达法
整点、半点、刻钟的时间表达方式。
✅ 整点：7:00 → seven o'clock
✅ 半点：6:30 → half past six
✅ 刻钟：7:15 → a quarter past seven
✅ 差一刻：7:45 → a quarter to eight
频率副词位置
频率副词在句子中的位置。
✅ 主语 + always/usually/often/sometimes + 动词
✅ 主语 + be + always/usually/often/sometimes
➡ I always eat breakfast.
➡ He is sometimes late.
📚 语法聚焦 · Time & Frequency

1️⃣ What time / When 问句

询问时间：What time 问具体时间点（几点几分），When 既可问具体时间也可问时间段。

疑问词句式回答
What timeWhat time do you get up?At 6:30.
WhenWhen do you eat lunch?At 11:50.

📌 注意：第三人称单数用 does（What time does he get up?），动词恢复原形。

2️⃣ 频率副词对比

频率副词含义大概频率示例
always总是100%I always get up at 6 o'clock.
usually通常~80%I usually eat breakfast at home.
often经常~60%I often play basketball after school.
sometimes有时~40%Sometimes I watch TV in the evening.
never从不0%I am never late for class.
⚠️ 中式英语纠错：
❌ I go always to school at 7.（频率副词应放在实义动词之前）
✅ I always go to school at 7.

❌ What time you get up?（缺少助动词 do）
✅ What time do you get up?

❌ I at 6:30 get up.（时间状语不可放在主语和动词之间）
✅ I get up at 6:30.

3️⃣ 一般现在时的第三人称单数

当主语是 he/she/it 等第三人称时，动词要加 -s / -es。

规则示例
一般情况加 -sHe gets up at 6 o'clock.
以 ch/sh/s/x/o 结尾加 -esShe goes to school at 7.
以辅音+y 结尾变 y 为 i 加 -esHe studies English every day.
📝 三级闯关 · 实战练习

🥉 基础关 · 概念识记

基础 ★☆☆
____ time do you get up? — At 6:30. (What / When / How)
📝 查看答案
答案：What
解析：问具体时间点用 What time。
基础 ★☆☆
She ____ (go) to school at 7:30 every day.
📝 查看答案
答案：goes
解析：第三人称单数，go → goes。
基础 ★☆☆
I ____ (通常) eat lunch at 12 o'clock.
📝 查看答案
答案：usually
解析：频率副词 usually 放在动词 eat 之前。

🥈 提升关 · 语境运用

提升 ★★☆
改错：What time does Tom goes to school?
📝 查看答案
答案：What time does Tom go to school?
解析：does 后动词要用原形，goes → go。
提升 ★★☆
连词成句：do / what / you / time / eat / dinner / ?
📝 查看答案
答案：What time do you eat dinner?
解析：特殊疑问句：What time + do + 主语 + 动词原形?
提升 ★★☆
He eats breakfast at 7:00. (改为一般疑问句)
📝 查看答案
答案：Does he eat breakfast at 7:00?
解析：第三人称用 Does 开头，动词恢复原形 eats → eat。

✍️ 微写作：My Daily Routine

用 What time / When / 频率副词描述你一天的生活（50-70词），包括起床、吃饭、上学、睡觉等活动。

My Daily Routine

I usually get up at 6:30. Then I brush my teeth and take a shower.

I eat breakfast at 6:50. I always have milk and eggs for breakfast. I go to school at 7:10.

School starts at 8:00. I have four classes in the morning. I eat lunch at school at 12:00.

In the evening, I sometimes watch TV. I go to bed at 9:30. This is my busy but happy day!
