Unit 4

My Favourite Subject

学科名称 · 表达喜好 · 原因 · 连词 because/and/but

📖 话题：School subjects and preferences 💬 功能：Talk about favourite subjects and give reasons 📚 语法：连词 because/and/but · 特殊疑问句 (what/why/who) 📝 写作：介绍我喜欢的学科

🎯 我能做到

📖 课文精讲 · After class
场景：课间，李明和王芳在讨论喜欢的学科。
Li Ming: Hi, Wang Fang! What's your favourite subject?
Wang Fang: My favourite subject is English. I like it because it's interesting. What about you?
Li Ming: I like maths. It's fun and useful.
Wang Fang: Who is your maths teacher?
Li Ming: Mr Zhang. He is very nice. His class is great!
Wang Fang: I like English because Ms Chen is kind and her class is fun.
Li Ming: I also like PE because I like sports.
Wang Fang: We have PE on Wednesday and Friday!
📖 查看翻译 + 重点讲解

逐句翻译：

李明：嗨，王芳！你最喜欢的科目是什么？

王芳：我最喜欢的科目是英语。我喜欢它因为它很有趣。你呢？

李明：我喜欢数学。它有趣又有用。

王芳：你的数学老师是谁？

李明：张老师。他非常和蔼。他的课很棒！

王芳：我喜欢英语，因为陈老师很和蔼，而且她的课很有趣。

李明：我也喜欢体育，因为我喜欢运动。

王芳：我们周三和周五有体育课！

🔑 重点讲解：

1. What's your favourite subject? → "你最喜欢的科目是什么？" What 询问事物。

2. I like it because it's interesting. → because 引导原因状语从句，回答 Why 的问句。

3. What about you? → "你呢？" 口语常用，相当于 And you？

4. Who is your maths teacher? → Who 询问人的身份。

5. and (并列/递进) / but (转折) 连接对等的词或句子。

6. 星期：Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday, Friday

📖 Reading: My Subjects

I have many subjects at school.

My favourite subject is Chinese. I like it because it is beautiful and interesting.

My Chinese teacher is Ms Wang. She is very kind.

I also like English. It is useful.

But I don't like maths. It is difficult for me.

My friend Tom likes maths because he thinks it is fun.

We all like PE because we can play sports.

Every day, I go to school happily!

🔤 重点句型 · Sentence Patterns
What's your favourite subject?
询问对方最喜欢的学科。What 是特殊疑问词，"什么"。
✅ What's your favourite subject?
✅ What's your favourite sport?
✅ What's your favourite colour?
➡ 回答：My favourite ... is ...
I like ... because ...
用 because 说明喜欢的原因。
✅ I like English because it's interesting.
✅ I like PE because I like sports.
✅ I like music because it's fun.
➡ 用 because 连接原因
and / but
and 表示并列/递进；but 表示转折。
✅ It's fun and useful.
✅ I like English but I don't like maths.
✅ Ms Chen is kind and her class is fun.
➡ and = "和，而且"；but = "但是"
Who is your ... teacher?
用 Who 询问教某科的老师是谁。
✅ Who is your maths teacher?
✅ Who is your English teacher?
➡ 回答：Mr Zhang / Ms Chen.
➡ Who = "谁"，询问人的身份或姓名
📚 语法聚焦 · Conjunctions & Question Words

1️⃣ 连词：because / and / but

连词作用例句
because表示原因（"因为"）I like it because it's fun.
and连接并列成分（"和/而且"）It's fun and useful.
but表示转折（"但是"）I like English but I don't like maths.

📌 口诀：because 表原因，and 表并列，but 表转折。

2️⃣ 特殊疑问词：What / Why / Who / When

疑问词含义例句
What什么（问事物/事情）What's your favourite subject?
Why为什么（问原因）Why do you like English?
Who谁（问人）Who is your maths teacher?
When什么时候（问时间）When do we have PE?
💡 回答方式：
What → 回答事物：My favourite subject is English.
Why → 回答原因：Because it's interesting.
Who → 回答人：Mr Zhang.
When → 回答时间：On Wednesday and Friday.

3️⃣ 形容词性物主代词：my / your / his / her / our / their

人称单数复数
第一人称my 我的our 我们的
第二人称your 你的your 你们的
第三人称(男)his 他的their 他/她/它们的
第三人称(女)her 她的
第三人称(物)its 它的

📌 形容词性物主代词后必须跟名词：my book, your teacher, his class.

⚠️ 中式英语纠错：
❌ I like he.（代词宾格错误）
✅ I like him.

❌ I favourite subject is English.（用 I 替代 My）
✅ My favourite subject is English.
📝 三级闯关 · 实战练习

🥉 基础关 · 概念识记

基础 ★☆☆
What's ____ favourite subject? — My favourite subject is English.
📝 查看答案
答案：your
解析：问"你最喜欢的科目"，用 your。
基础 ★☆☆
I like maths ____ it is fun. (because / and / but)
📝 查看答案
答案：because
解析："it is fun" 是喜欢数学的原因，用 because。
基础 ★☆☆
____ is your Chinese teacher? — Ms Wang. (What / Who / When)
📝 查看答案
答案：Who
解析：问"谁"，用 Who。

🥈 提升关 · 语境运用

提升 ★★☆
改错：Why do you like English? — And it's useful.
📝 查看答案
答案：Because it's useful.
解析：回答 Why 问句用 Because，不是 And。
提升 ★★☆
连词成句：your / Who / teacher / maths / is / ?
📝 查看答案
答案：Who is your maths teacher?
解析：特殊疑问句：Who + is + 形容词性物主代词 + 名词 + ?
提升 ★★☆
I like English ____ I don't like maths. (because / and / but)
📝 查看答案
答案：but
解析：前后是转折关系——"我喜欢英语，但是我不喜欢数学"。

🥇 挑战关 · 综合运用

挑战 ★★★
汉译英：我最喜欢的科目是科学。它很有趣。我的科学老师是张老师。他很和蔼。
📝 查看答案
答案：My favourite subject is science. It is very interesting. My science teacher is Mr Zhang. He is very kind.
解析：favourite subject + be + 学科；用 because 可以连接原因。
挑战 ★★★
补全对话：
A: What's ____ favourite subject?
B: My favourite subject is ____. I like it ____ it's fun.
A: ____ is your music teacher?
B: ____ Li. She is very nice.
📝 查看答案
答案：your; music; because; Who; Ms
解析：your 问对方；music 是答案线索；because 表原因；Who 问人；Ms 女士称呼。
