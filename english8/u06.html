📖 话题：Future Plans & Dream Jobs💬 功能：Talk about future plans and dream jobs📚 语法：be going to 表示未来计划 · what/where/how 引导特殊疑问句📝 写作：My Dream Job

🎯 我能做到

📖 课文精讲 · I'm going to study computer science.
场景：班会上，同学们在分享自己的职业梦想和实现计划。
Teacher:What do you want to be when you grow up?
Wang Lin:I'm going to be a computer programmer. I'm going to study computer science.
Teacher:That sounds cool! What about you, Li Mei?
Li Mei:I'm going to be a doctor. I'm going to study medicine at a famous university.
Teacher:Zhang Wei, what are you going to be?
Zhang Wei:I'm not sure yet. Maybe I'm going to be a teacher like you.
Teacher:That's great! How are you going to do that?
Zhang Wei:I'm going to study hard and learn teaching skills.
📖 查看翻译

Teacher: 你们长大了想做什么？

Wang Lin: 我想当程序员，准备学计算机科学。

Teacher: 听起来很酷！你呢，李梅？

Li Mei: 我想当医生，准备上一所名牌大学学医。

Teacher: 张伟，你打算做什么？

Zhang Wei: 还不确定。也许当老师，像您一样。

Teacher: 很好！你打算怎么做？

Zhang Wei: 我打算努力学习并学习教学技能。

🔑 重点讲解：

1. What do you want to be when you grow up? → want to be "想成为" + 职业名词。when you grow up "当你长大"。

2. I'm going to be a computer programmer. → be going to be + 职业 表示想成为什么。

3. How are you going to do that? → 用 How 问方法：How + be + 主语 + going to + 动词?

4. I'm going to study computer science. → 接具体行动：be going to + 动词原形。

📖 阅读专项 · Dreams and Actions

Everyone has a dream. But how can we make our dreams come true?

First, find out what you are good at. If you love drawing, maybe you are going to be an artist. If you enjoy helping others, maybe you are going to be a doctor.

Second, make a plan. What are you going to do this year? What subjects are you going to study hard?

Third, take action. A dream without action is just a wish. Start today!

🏆 中考题型专练 · 语法填空

用括号内单词的适当形式填空。

My brother (1)____ (be) going to be a pilot. He (2)____ (study) hard to get into a good university. He (3)____ (practice) his English every day because pilots need good English. I (4)____ (be) going to be a scientist. We (5)____ (have) big dreams! What are you going to (6)____ (be)?

答案
(1) is；(2) studies；(3) practices；(4) am；(5) have；(6) be
✍️ 写作进阶 · My Dream Job
📋 写作题目
请以"My Dream Job"为题，写 60-80 词短文，说说你梦想的职业和你打算如何实现它。
开头：我梦想的职业是什么。
主体：为什么选择这个职业+我打算怎么做。
结尾：表达信心和期待。
When I grow up, I'm going to be a doctor. I want to help sick people. I'm going to study science and biology hard. I'm going to go to a good medical university. I know becoming a doctor is not easy, but I'm not going to give up. I'm going to work hard every day to make my dream come true.
等我长大了，我打算当一名医生。我想帮助病人。我打算学好科学和生物。我打算上一所好的医科大学。我知道当医生不容易，但我不会放弃。我打算每天努力，实现梦想。
