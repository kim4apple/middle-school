U7

Teenagers should be allowed to choose their own clothes.

规则与许可

📖 话题：Rules and Permission💬 功能：Talk about what you are allowed to do; agree and disagree📚 语法：Modal verbs + passive voice: should be + V-ed📝 写作：Write about rules and express opinions

🎯 我能做到

📖 课文精讲 · Teenagers should be allowed to choose their own clothes.
场景：Anna和妈妈在讨论学校的着装规定和课外活动。
Anna:Mom, can I buy a new dress for the party?
Mom:Sure, but I think teenagers should be allowed to choose their own clothes.
Anna:I agree! Some of the school rules are too strict. We should be allowed to wear our own clothes at school.
Mom:Well, I think students should be required to wear uniforms. It saves time and money.
Anna:Maybe you're right. But I think we should be allowed to choose our hairstyles.
Mom:I can agree with that. Also, teenagers should be allowed to make their own decisions sometimes.
Anna:Thanks Mom. Oh, and I think I should be allowed to study with my friends this weekend.
Mom:Fine, as long as you finish your homework first.
📖 查看翻译

Anna: 妈妈，我能买一件新裙子参加派对吗？

Mom: 当然，但我认为青少年应该被允许自己选择衣服。

Anna: 我同意！有些校规太严格了。我们应该被允许在学校穿自己的衣服。

Mom: 嗯，我认为学生应该被要求穿校服。这样省时省钱。

Anna: 也许你是对的。但我认为我们应该被允许选择自己的发型。

Mom: 这个我同意。而且，青少年有时候应该被允许自己做决定。

Anna: 谢谢妈妈。哦，还有，我认为我这个周末应该被允许和朋友一起学习。

Mom: 行，只要你先完成作业。

🔑 重点讲解：

should be allowed to do 表示「应该被允许做某事」— Teenagers should be allowed to choose their own clothes.

should not be allowed to do 表示「不应该被允许做某事」— Students should not be allowed to use phones in class.

情态动词 + be + V-ed 构成情态动词被动语态。

agree/disagree 表达同意或不同意的常用句型。

🔤 重点句型 · Sentence Patterns
主语 + should (not) be allowed to + V
情态动词被动语态，表示应该（不应该）被允许做某事。
Teenagers should be allowed to choose their own clothes. / Students should not be allowed to use phones.
主语 + can/may/must be + V-ed
其他情态动词的被动语态。
This work must be finished today. / The problem can be solved easily.
I agree / disagree with...
表达同意或不同意。
I agree with you. / I disagree with that rule. / I don't think it's a good idea.
主语 + should (not) be required to V
表示「应该/不应该被要求做某事」。
Students should be required to wear uniforms. / We should not be required to attend extra classes.
📚 语法聚焦 · 情态动词的被动语态

情态动词被动语态结构

情态动词主动语态被动语态
canWe can solve this problem.This problem can be solved.
mustWe must finish the work.The work must be finished.
shouldWe should allow students to choose.Students should be allowed to choose.
mayWe may hold the meeting.The meeting may be held.

常见表达

结构含义例句
should be allowed to应该被允许Kids should be allowed to play.
should not be allowed to不应该被允许Teens shouldn't be allowed to drive.
must be + V-ed必须被Homework must be done.
can be + V-ed可以被The problem can be solved.

情态动词 + be + 过去分词，注意 be 不能变为 am/is/are。

否定形式在情态动词后加 not：should not be allowed

疑问形式把情态动词提到主语前：Should teenagers be allowed to...?

Rules are made to be broken. 规矩是用来打破的。（谚语，常用于娱乐场景）

📖 阅读专项 · Should Teenagers Be Allowed to Make Their Own Decisions?

Many teenagers have hobbies. Sometimes these hobbies can get in the way of schoolwork. Some parents might worry about their children's success at school. They think that teenagers should not be allowed to spend too much time on hobbies.

But teenagers believe that they should be allowed to practice their hobbies as much as they want. For example, Liu Yu, a fifteen-year-old boy from Shandong, wants to be a professional runner. He is a running star at his school. He wants to practice running every day.

However, his parents won't allow him to train as much as he would like. They think he should spend more time on his schoolwork. They say, «He needs to think about what will happen if he doesn't become a professional runner.»

Liu Yu disagrees. He says, «I think I should be allowed to make decisions for myself. My dream is to be a professional runner. I understand that my parents care about me, but I believe I can manage both running and schoolwork.»

📋 写作题目
写一篇短文讨论「青少年是否应该被允许自己选择衣服」。给出你的观点和理由。
开头：引出话题，提出不同看法
正方观点：支持自己选衣服的理由
反方观点：穿校服的好处
你的立场：你认为应该如何
结尾：总结你的观点
Should teenagers be allowed to choose their own clothes? Different people have different opinions. Some people think teenagers should be allowed to choose their own clothes. They say it helps teens express themselves and build confidence. However, others think students should wear uniforms. They say uniforms save time and reduce comparison among students. In my opinion, teenagers should be allowed to choose their own clothes on weekends, but they should wear uniforms to school. This way, they can express themselves while keeping discipline at school.
青少年是否应该被允许自己选择衣服？不同的人有不同的看法。有些人认为青少年应该被允许自己选择衣服。他们说这有助于青少年表达自己和建立自信。然而，其他人认为学生应该穿校服。他们说校服节省时间，减少学生之间的比较。在我看来，青少年在周末应该被允许选择自己的衣服，但上学应该穿校服。这样，他们既可以表达自己，又能保持学校纪律。
