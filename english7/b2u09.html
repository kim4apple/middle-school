七下 · Unit 9

What does he look like?

外貌描述 · 身高体型 · 发型配饰

📖 话题：Physical appearance 💬 功能：Describe people's looks · Ask "What does he look like?" 📚 语法：has/have 描述外貌 · be + 形容词描述整体形象 📝 写作：描述一个人（朋友/家人）的外貌

🎯 我能做到

📖 课文精讲 · Describing people
场景：Mike 和 Sarah 在谈论新来的同学 David，相互描述他的长相。
Sarah: Mike, do you know the new student David?
Mike: No, I don't. What does he look like?
Sarah: Well, he's tall and thin. He has curly brown hair.
Mike: Does he wear glasses?
Sarah: Yes, he does. He also has a round face. And he often wears a blue T-shirt.
Mike: Oh, I think I saw him! He's sitting in the front row. Is he of medium height?
Sarah: No, he's quite tall. He's about 1.8 meters.
Mike: I see. I'll say hello to him later.
📖 查看翻译

逐句翻译：

Sarah: Mike，你认识新来的同学 David 吗？

Mike: 不认识。他长什么样？

Sarah: 嗯，他又高又瘦。他有一头棕色的卷发。

Mike: 他戴眼镜吗？

Sarah: 是的，他戴。他还有一张圆脸。他经常穿蓝色 T 恤。

Mike: 哦，我想我看到他了！他坐在前排。他是中等身高吗？

Sarah: 不，他挺高的。他大概 1.8 米。

Mike: 知道了。我待会儿去跟他打个招呼。

🔑 重点讲解：

1. What does he look like? → "他长什么样？" 固定句型，询问外貌。

2. He's tall and thin. → 用 be + 形容词描述整体形象（身高、体型）。

3. He has curly brown hair. → 用 has/have 描述具体特征（头发、面部特征）。

4. Does he wear glasses? → 用 wear 表示"穿戴（眼镜、衣服等）"。

📖 Reading: My Best Friend

My best friend is Li Ming. He is of medium height but a little heavy.

He has short straight black hair and big eyes. He always wears a smile.

He likes to wear sports clothes and sneakers. He looks very cool in them.

Li Ming is very kind and helpful. He is the most popular boy in our class.

Everyone likes him because he is funny and friendly.

🔤 重点句型 · Sentence Patterns
What does he/she look like?
询问人的外貌特征。
✅ What does he look like?
→ He's tall and thin. He has short curly hair.
✅ What do they look like?
→ They're of medium height.
He is + (height / build) / He has + (hair / face)
be + 形容词描述整体（高矮胖瘦）
has/have + 名词描述具体特征（头发、眼睛等）。
✅ He is tall / short / of medium height / thin / heavy.
✅ She has long black hair / big eyes / a round face.
✅ They have curly hair / glasses.
Does he/she have ...? / Does he/she wear ...?
一般疑问句，询问是否有某个特征或是否穿戴某物。
✅ Does he have a beard? — Yes, he does. / No, he doesn't.
✅ Does she wear glasses? — Yes, she does. / No, she doesn't.
✅ Do they have curly hair? — Yes, they do.
描述顺序：整体 → 局部 → 具体细节
描述一个人时，先整体再局部：
身高→体型→头发→五官→着装。
✅ She is of medium height, a little thin.
✅ She has long straight hair and a small nose.
✅ She usually wears a red dress.
📚 语法聚焦 · Describing Appearance

1️⃣ be 描述 vs. have 描述

be + 形容词have/has + 名词
用途整体特征（身高、体型、年龄）具体特征（头发、眼睛、胡须）
身高He is tall.——
体型She is thin.——
头发——He has curly hair.
脸型——She has a round face.
眼镜——He wears glasses.

2️⃣ 形容词顺序修饰名词

用多个形容词修饰名词时顺序：大小/长短 → 形状 → 颜色

正确顺序示例
大小/长短 + 形状 + 颜色 + 名词long straight brown hair
short curly blonde hair
big round black eyes
⚠️ 中式英语纠错：
❌ He is tall and has long hair. ✅ （正确）

❌ He has hair long black.（形容词顺序错误）
✅ He has long black hair.

❌ She is has long hair.（is 和 has 重复）
✅ She has long hair.
📝 三级闯关 · 实战练习

🥉 基础关 · 概念识记

基础 ★☆☆
What ____ the new teacher look like? (do / does / is)
📝 查看答案
答案：does
解析：the new teacher 是第三人称单数，用 does。
基础 ★☆☆
She ____ (have / has) long straight hair.
📝 查看答案
答案：has
解析：She 是第三人称单数，have 变为 has。
基础 ★☆☆
He ____ (be) of medium height and ____ (have) a round face.
📝 查看答案
答案：is; has
解析：be 和 have 两个动词同时出现，各自独立使用。

🥈 提升关 · 语境运用

提升 ★★☆
改错：Does she has a beard?
📝 查看答案
答案：Does she have a beard?
解析：Does 后面用动词原形 have，不是 has。
提升 ★★☆
连词成句：does / what / look / he / like / ?
📝 查看答案
答案：What does he look like?
解析：特殊疑问句：What + does + 主语 + look like?
提升 ★★☆
排列正确的形容词顺序：她有一头棕色的短卷发。 (short / curly / brown / hair)
📝 查看答案
答案：She has short curly brown hair.
解析：形容词顺序：长短→形状→颜色。

🥇 挑战关 · 综合运用

挑战 ★★★
汉译英：你妈妈长什么样？她中等身高，有点瘦。她有一头黑色的卷发。
📝 查看答案
答案：What does your mother look like? She is of medium height and a little thin. She has curly black hair.
解析：问外貌用 What does...look like；整体描述用 be；具体特征用 has。
挑战 ★★★
补全对话：
A: Do you know that girl ____ a red hat?
B: The tall girl ____ long blonde hair?
A: Yes, that's her!
B: Oh, she's my cousin Lucy. She ____ (wear) glasses, but today she ____ (not wear) them.
📝 查看答案
答案：with; with; wears; isn't wearing
解析："with + 穿着/特征" 作定语修饰名词；wear 表示平常习惯，isn't wearing 表示今天没戴。

✍️ 微写作：Describe a friend

选一位你的朋友或家人，用英语写一段描述其外貌的话。注意先用 be 描述整体，再用 has 描述细节。

I want to tell you about my best friend, Wang Fang.

She is of medium height and a little thin. She has long straight black hair.

She has big bright eyes and a small mouth. She always wears a nice smile.

She likes to wear a white T-shirt and blue jeans. She looks very pretty.

Everyone says she is kind and friendly. I'm lucky to have her as my friend.

我想向你介绍我最好的朋友王芳。她中等身高，有点瘦。她有一头黑色的长直发。她有一双明亮的大眼睛和一张小嘴。她总是带着甜甜的微笑。她喜欢穿白 T 恤和蓝色牛仔裤。她看起来非常漂亮。每个人都说她很善良友好。我很幸运有她做我的朋友。
