U5

Power of Ideas

发明创新 · 被动语态 · 创造表达

📖 话题：Inventions and creative ideas💬 功能：Describe how things are made / have been invented📚 语法：被动语态 (一般过去/现在/将来) · be made of/from/by/in📝 写作：Introduce a useful invention

🎯 我能做到

📖 课文精讲 · Power of Ideas
场景：学生们在创客空间 (makerspace) 展示自己的创意项目。
Ms. Chen:Welcome to our makerspace, everyone! Please show your projects and explain how they are made.
Li Ming:I designed a solar-powered fan. It is made of recycled plastic and small solar panels. The fan is powered by sunlight.
Amy:Wow! How is it made?
Li Ming:First, the plastic is cut into shape. Then the motor and solar panels are connected. Finally, everything is put together.
Wang Fang:Cool! I created an automatic plant waterer. It was made from a plastic bottle and some tubes. It can water plants when nobody is home.
Ms. Chen:Great ideas, everyone! Many useful things will be invented by young minds like yours in the future.
📖 查看翻译

陈老师： 欢迎大家来到创客空间！请展示你们的项目，并解释它们是如何制作的。

李明： 我设计了一个太阳能风扇。它是由回收塑料和小型太阳能板制成的。风扇由阳光供电。

Amy： 哇！它是怎么制作的？

李明： 首先，塑料被切割成形状。然后马达和太阳能板被连接起来。最后，所有部件被组装在一起。

王芳： 太酷了！我制作了一个自动植物浇水器。它是用塑料瓶和一些管子做的。家里没人时它可以给植物浇水。

陈老师： 大家的主意都很棒！未来许多有用的东西将由像你们这样的年轻人发明。

🔑 重点讲解：

be made of + 材料（看得出原材料）— It is made of recycled plastic.

be made from + 材料（看不出原材料）— It was made from a bottle.

be made by + 人 — It was made by Li Ming.

be powered by + 能源 — The fan is powered by sunlight.

will be invented — 一般将来时被动语态

🔤 重点句型 · Sentence Patterns
be made of / from / by / in
表示「某物由……制成/在……制造」。
The model is made of wood. / Paper is made from wood. / It was made by my brother in China.
be used for + V-ing
表示「某物被用来做……」。
This tool is used for cutting paper. / Solar panels are used for generating electricity.
It is said / known that ...
表示「据说/众所周知……」。
It is said that the wheel was invented 5,000 years ago. / It is well known that China invented paper.
will be + V-ed (将来被动)
表示「某物将被……」。
More robots will be developed in the future. / The new technology will be used in schools.
📚 语法聚焦 · 被动语态 (一般现在/过去/将来)

被动语态的结构

时态结构例句
一般现在时am/is/are + V-edThe shirts are made of cotton.
一般过去时was/were + V-edThe telephone was invented in 1876.
一般将来时will be + V-edNew energy cars will be produced next year.

主动变被动

主动句被动句说明
Edison invented the light bulb.The light bulb was invented by Edison.宾语变主语
People use bamboo to make furniture.Bamboo is used to make furniture.by people 常省略
They will build a new school here.A new school will be built here.将来时被动

be made of / from / by / in 辨析

短语用法例句
be made of看得出原材料The chair is made of wood.
be made from看不出原材料Wine is made from grapes.
be made by由某人/方式The cake was made by my mom.
be made in在某地制造This car is made in China.

重要句型

be used for + V-ing — This machine is used for cutting metal.

be used to do — Bamboo is used to make furniture.

It is said that... — It is said that the first computer was huge.

It is known that... — It is known that paper was invented in China.

只有及物动词（后接宾语）才能用于被动语态。

be 动词要根据主语的人称、数和时态进行变化。

如果不想提及动作的执行者，可以省略 by 短语。

Necessity is the mother of invention. 需要是发明之母。

📖 阅读专项 · Ideas That Changed the World

Throughout history, great ideas have changed the way we live. Some inventions are so important that the world was completely transformed by them.

Paper was invented in China around 2,000 years ago. Before paper was invented, people wrote on bamboo, silk, or clay. Writing was difficult and expensive. With the invention of paper, knowledge could be shared more easily. Today, paper is still used widely around the world.

Electricity changed everything. Light bulbs were invented by Thomas Edison in 1879. Before that, people used oil lamps and candles. Electricity is now used for lighting, heating, communication, and so much more. It is said that without electricity, modern life would be impossible.

The Internet is one of the most important inventions of modern times. It was first developed in the 1960s, but it was not widely used until the 1990s. Today, the Internet is used by billions of people. Information can be found in seconds, and people from different countries can communicate instantly. The way we work, learn, and socialize has been completely changed by the Internet.

Great ideas come from curiosity and creativity. New inventions will be developed in the future, and many problems will be solved by creative minds like yours!

✍️ 写作进阶 · Introduce a Useful Invention
📋 写作题目
写一篇短文介绍一项你认为最有用的发明。说明它是由谁、在什么时候发明的，它由什么材料制成，它是如何改变人们生活的。
开头：介绍你要写的发明及其重要性
历史：谁发明的？什么时候发明的？
材料/构造：它是由什么制成的？
用途：它能用来做什么？
影响：它如何改变了人们的生活？
The smartphone is one of the most useful inventions in modern times. The first smartphone was invented in the 1990s, but it has been greatly improved since then. A smartphone is made of glass, metal, and plastic. It is used for calling, taking photos, searching the Internet, and much more. It is said that over six billion people use smartphones today. The way we communicate, shop, and learn has been completely changed by this amazing invention.
智能手机是现代最有用的发明之一。第一款智能手机发明于20世纪90年代，但此后它得到了极大的改进。智能手机由玻璃、金属和塑料制成。它被用来打电话、拍照、搜索互联网等。据说现在有60多亿人使用智能手机。我们沟通、购物和学习的方式已经被这项惊人的发明彻底改变了。
