Beyond Earth

太空探索 · 情态动词推测 · 未来表达

📖 话题：Space exploration and the universe💬 功能：Express possibility/certainty, talk about space📚 语法：情态动词推测 (must/can't/might/could) · 将来时复习📝 写作：我的太空梦想

🎯 我能做到

📖 课文精讲 · Beyond Earth
场景：Tom和Linda在观星，讨论外星生命和未来太空任务的可能性。
Tom:Look at all those stars! There must be life on other planets.
Linda:Maybe. But it might be very different from life on Earth.
Tom:Do you think humans will ever live on Mars?
Linda:I believe so. Scientists are going to send more astronauts there soon.
Tom:Wow! That can't be easy. It must be very difficult to survive in space.
Linda:You're right. But with better technology, we could build space stations on the moon.
Tom:If we find water on Mars, it might be possible to grow food there.
Linda:Exactly! The future of space exploration is exciting.
📖 查看翻译

Tom: 看那些星星！其他星球上一定有生命。

Linda: 也许吧。但它可能和地球上的生命很不一样。

Tom: 你觉得人类将来会住在火星上吗？

Linda: 我相信会的。科学家们很快就要送更多宇航员去那里了。

Tom: 哇！那肯定不容易。在太空中生存一定非常困难。

Linda: 你说得对。但有了更好的技术，我们可以在月球上建造空间站。

Tom: 如果在火星上找到水，就有可能在那里种食物。

Linda: 正是！太空探索的未来令人兴奋。

🔑 重点讲解：

情态动词推测：must be（肯定推测）、can't be（否定推测）、might/could be（可能性推测）

将来时：will + 动词原形（预测未来）、be going to + 动词原形（计划）

if条件句（Type 1）：If + 一般现在时, 主句 + will/might + 动词原形

太空探索词汇：explore, rocket, astronaut, space station, Mars, moon

🔤 重点句型 · Sentence Patterns
主语 + must be + 名词/形容词
表示肯定推测（非常确信）。
There must be life on other planets. / It must be very cold on Mars.
主语 + can't be + 名词/形容词
表示否定推测（不可能）。
That can't be easy. / It can't be true without proof.
主语 + might/could be + 名词/形容词
表示可能性推测（不太确定）。
It might be possible. / There could be aliens in the universe.
If + 一般现在时, 主句 + will/might + 动词原形
Type 1 条件句，表示对未来的预测。
If we find water, we will survive on Mars. / If you study hard, you might become an astronaut.
📚 语法聚焦 · 情态动词推测 + 将来时复习

情态动词推测用法

情态动词推测程度例句
must be非常肯定（一定是）There must be life somewhere in the universe.
can't be非常否定（不可能是）That can't be a real UFO.
could be有可能（也许是）It could be a planet or a star.
might be不太确定（可能是）They might be from another galaxy.

将来时对比

结构用法例句
will + V预测未来 / 临时决定Humans will live on Mars one day.
be going to + V计划 / 有迹象Scientists are going to launch a new rocket.
If…, will…条件预测 (Type 1)If we try, we will succeed.

情态动词 + be 表示对现在情况的推测，不随主语变化。

must / can't / might / could 后面都跟动词原形。

must 的否定推测用 can't（不是 mustn't）。

The sky is not the limit — it's just the beginning. 天空不是极限——这只是开始。

📖 阅读专项 · The Final Frontier

For thousands of years, humans have looked up at the stars and wondered what lies beyond Earth. In 1969, Neil Armstrong became the first person to walk on the moon. He said, "That's one small step for man, one giant leap for mankind." Since then, space exploration has developed rapidly.

Today, many countries have space programs. China's Chang'e missions have explored the moon, and the Tianwen-1 probe successfully reached Mars in 2021. The International Space Station (ISS) orbits the Earth, where astronauts from different countries live and work together.

The next big goal is to send humans to Mars. Scientists believe that if we can solve the problems of gravity, atmosphere, and water, it might be possible to build a colony on the Red Planet. However, space travel is still very expensive and dangerous. There must be many challenges ahead. But as one astronaut said, "Space is the final frontier, and we are just getting started."

frontier 前沿；边界 Neil Armstrong 尼尔·阿姆斯特朗 giant leap 巨大飞跃 Chang'e 嫦娥（中国探月工程） Tianwen-1 天问一号 ISS 国际空间站 colony 殖民地；定居点

✍️ 写作进阶 · My Space Dream
📋 写作题目
Would you like to travel to space? Write about your dream of space exploration. Include what you think space is like, what you would do there, and what might happen in the future of space travel.
开头：表达你对太空的兴趣和梦想
描述：你认为太空是什么样的（使用情态动词推测）
计划：你在太空中会做什么
预测：未来太空探索的发展
结尾：你的感受和期待
Ever since I was a child, I have dreamed of traveling to space. I think it must be an amazing experience to see the Earth from above. The stars might look much brighter in space, and there must be no sound at all because there is no air. If I become an astronaut, I will explore the moon and maybe even Mars. I think humans are going to discover many new things in the universe. There might be aliens out there — who knows? Space is the final frontier, and I want to be part of its exploration.
从小我就梦想去太空旅行。我认为从上面看地球一定是一种奇妙的体验。太空中的星星可能看起来更亮，而且因为没有空气，一定完全没有声音。如果我成为宇航员，我将探索月球甚至火星。我认为人类将会在宇宙中发现许多新事物。外面可能有外星人——谁知道呢？太空是最后的边疆，我想成为探索它的一部分。
