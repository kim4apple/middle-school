U5

Do you want to watch a game show?

媒体偏好

📖 话题：TV Shows & Movies💬 功能：Express preferences and make plans📚 语法：不定式作宾语（want/plan/hope to do）· 动词 + to do 结构📝 写作：My Favorite TV Show

🎯 我能做到

📖 课文精讲 · Do you want to watch a game show?
场景：几个朋友在讨论今晚看什么电视节目。
Jenny:What do you want to watch tonight?
Mike:I plan to watch the game show. It's very exciting.
Jenny:I don't like game shows. I hope to watch a sitcom. They make me laugh.
Mike:But sitcoms are boring. How about a talk show?
Jenny:Talk shows are OK. But I would like to watch a talent show.
Mike:Good idea! I hope to see some great singers.
📖 查看翻译

Jenny: 今晚你想看什么？

Mike: 我打算看游戏类节目，特别刺激。

Jenny: 我不喜欢游戏节目。我希望看情景喜剧，能让我笑。

Mike: 但情景喜剧很无聊。脱口秀呢？

Jenny: 脱口秀还行，但我想看才艺秀。

Mike: 好主意！我希望能看到一些好歌手。

🔑 重点讲解：

1. What do you want to watch? → want to do：想要做某事。to 后跟动词原形。

2. I plan to watch the game show. → plan to do：计划做某事。

3. I hope to watch a sitcom. → hope to do：希望做某事。

4. I would like to watch a talent show. → would like to do：想要（礼貌表达）。

5. 电视节目类型：game show 游戏类 / sitcom 情景喜剧 / talk show 脱口秀 / talent show 才艺秀 / sports show 体育节目 / news 新闻

📝 重点词汇 · 媒体偏好
sitcom🔊 /ˈsɪtkɑːm/n.
情景喜剧
I like watching sitcoms.我喜欢看情景喜剧。
talent🔊 /ˈtælənt/n.
才能；天赋
She has a talent for singing.她有唱歌天赋。
plan🔊 /plæn/v.
计划
We plan to go hiking this weekend.我们计划周末去远足。
hope🔊 /hoʊp/v.
希望
I hope to visit Beijing one day.我希望有一天去北京。
educational🔊 /ˌedʒuˈkeɪʃənl/adj.
教育的；有教育意义的
Documentaries are educational.纪录片很有教育意义。
meaningless🔊 /ˈmiːnɪŋləs/adj.
毫无意义的
I think some ads are meaningless.我觉得有些广告毫无意义。
enjoyable🔊 /ɪnˈdʒɔɪəbl/adj.
令人愉快的
The movie was very enjoyable.这部电影非常好看。
expect🔊 /ɪkˈspekt/v.
期待；预料
I expect to learn a lot from this show.我期待从这个节目中学到很多。
famous🔊 /ˈfeɪməs/adj.
著名的
She wants to be a famous singer.她想成为著名的歌手。
appear🔊 /əˈpɪr/v.
出现；露面
Many stars appear on this show.很多明星在这个节目中出现。
🔤 重点句型 · Sentence Patterns
want / plan / hope + to do
表达意愿和计划
I want to watch a movie. / She plans to visit her grandma.
would like + to do
礼貌表达意愿
I would like to go shopping. / Would you like to join us?
expect / decide + to do
其他接 to do 的动词
I decided to learn English. / I expect to finish it today.
📚 语法聚焦 · 不定式作宾语（动词 + to do）

常见后接 to do 的动词

动词含义示例
want想要I want to be a teacher.
plan计划We plan to have a party.
hope希望I hope to see you soon.
would like想要I would like to drink coffee.
decide决定She decided to join the club.
expect期待I expect to pass the exam.
learn学习He is learning to swim.

否定形式

结构示例
动词 + not + to doI decided not to go.
would like + not + to doI would like not to be late.

❌ I want watch TV.（动词 want 后需加 to）

✅ I want to watch TV.

❌ He doesn't want to eats.（to 后动词用原形）

✅ He doesn't want to eat.

📖 阅读专项 · TV Habits of Chinese Teens

What do Chinese teenagers like to watch on TV? A survey gives us the answer.

Game shows are the most popular. About 35% of teens choose to watch them. Next are talent shows. Many teens hope to see amazing performances.

Sitcoms are also popular. Teens want to relax and laugh after a long day at school. Talk shows are less popular, but some teens find them educational.

Interestingly, many teens also choose to watch the news. They want to know what is happening around the world.

🏆 中考题型专练 · 完形填空

阅读短文，选择最佳选项。

I (1)____ to be a TV reporter one day. That's why I like to watch different TV shows. I (2)____ to learn from them. I love news because it's educational. I also like game shows because they are exciting. I don't (3)____ to watch sitcoms. I think they are (4)____. Do you want (5)____ watch TV with me this weekend?

1. A. want B. wants C. wanted D. wanting
2. A. hope B. hopes C. hoping D. to hope
3. A. likes B. like C. liked D. liking
4. A. educational B. enjoyable C. meaningless D. exciting
5. A. at B. in C. to D. for
答案
1. A (want)；2. A (hope)；3. B (like)；4. C (meaningless)；5. C (to)
✍️ 写作进阶 · My Favorite TV Show
📋 写作题目
请以"My Favorite TV Show"为题，写 60-80 词介绍你最喜欢的电视节目，说明喜欢的原因。
开头：我最喜欢的节目是什么。
主体：节目内容+喜欢理由（用到 want/hope/enjoy）。
结尾：推荐大家去看。
My favorite TV show is "China's Got Talent". I want to watch it every week. The performers are amazing. Some singers make me want to cry, and some dancers make me laugh. I hope to learn from them. This show also teaches me to follow my dreams. I think everyone should watch it!
我最喜欢的节目是《中国达人秀》。我每周都想看。表演者很精彩。有些歌手让我感动，有些舞者让我开心。我希望向他们学习。这个节目也教会我追梦。我觉得每个人都应该看看！
