七下 · Unit 5

Why do you like pandas?

动物世界 · 描述形容词 · 原因问答

📖 话题：Animals in a zoo 💬 功能：Describe animals · Express preferences · Give reasons 📚 语法：Why / Because 问答 · 描述性形容词 📝 写作：描述最喜欢的动物

🎯 我能做到

📖 课文精讲 · At the Zoo
场景：周末同学们一起去动物园，大家在讨论各自喜欢的动物。
Amy: Look at the pandas! They are so cute! I love pandas!
Tom: Why do you like pandas?
Amy: Because they are very cute and kind of shy. They are black and white.
Tom: Yeah! Pandas are from China. They are a symbol of China.
Amy: What animals do you like, Tom?
Tom: I like lions. They are really cool and strong!
Amy: But lions are kind of scary. Why do you like them?
Tom: Because they are brave and beautiful! They are the king of animals.
📖 查看翻译

逐句翻译：

Amy: 看那些熊猫！它们好可爱！我爱熊猫！

Tom: 你为什么喜欢熊猫？

Amy: 因为它们很可爱，而且有点害羞。它们是黑白相间的。

Tom: 是的！大熊猫来自中国。它们是中国的象征。

Amy: Tom，你喜欢什么动物？

Tom: 我喜欢狮子。它们真的很酷很强大！

Amy: 但狮子有点吓人。你为什么喜欢它们？

Tom: 因为它们勇敢又美丽！它们是百兽之王。

🔑 重点讲解：

1. Why do you like pandas? → Why 问原因，回答用 Because。

2. kind of → "有点儿"，相当于 a little，后面跟形容词。例如：kind of cute / kind of scary。

3. Where are ... from? → "来自哪里" = be from。

4. What animals do you like? → 问"你喜欢什么动物"。

🔤 重点句型 · Sentence Patterns
Why do you like + 动物?
询问对方喜欢某种动物的原因。
✅ Why do you like pandas?
✅ Why does she like cats?
➡ 回答：Because + 原因
Why don't you like + 动物?
询问对方不喜欢某种动物的原因。
✅ Why don't you like snakes?
➡ Because they are scary.
What animals do you like?
询问对方喜欢什么动物。
✅ What animals do you like?
➡ I like dogs.
➡ I like elephants because they are smart.
be + 形容词 + 描述动物
用形容词来描述动物的特征。
✅ Pandas are cute and shy.
✅ Lions are strong and brave.
✅ Giraffes are tall and beautiful.
➡ 程度词：very / really / kind of / a little
📚 语法聚焦 · Why / Because & Adjectives

1️⃣ Why / Because 问答

疑问词用法例句回答
Why问原因Why do you like pandas?Because they are cute.
Why问原因（第三人称）Why does Tom like dogs?Because they are friendly.
Why not问不喜欢的原因Why don't you like cats?Because I think they are lazy.

⚠️ 中式英语纠错：
❌ Why you like pandas?（疑问句缺少助动词 do）
✅ Why do you like pandas?

❌ I like pandas because they are very cute.
✅ I like pandas because they are very cute.

❌ Pandas are very cute animal.（主语是复数 Pandas，表语也要用复数）
✅ Pandas are very cute animals.

3️⃣ 程度副词：very / really / kind of / a little

程度副词用来修饰形容词，表达不同的程度。

程度词程度例句
very非常（程度最强）Pandas are very cute.
really真的、很Lions are really strong.
kind of有点儿Koalas are kind of lazy.
a little一点点The dog is a little shy.
📝 三级闯关 · 实战练习

🥉 基础关 · 概念识记

基础 ★☆☆
____ do you like pandas? — Because they are cute. (Why / What / How)
📝 查看答案
答案：Why
解析：问原因用 Why，回答用 Because。
基础 ★☆☆
Koalas are from ____. (Australia / China / Africa)
📝 查看答案
答案：Australia
解析：考拉来自澳大利亚。
基础 ★☆☆
Pandas are ____ (有点儿) shy.
📝 查看答案
答案：kind of
解析："有点儿" = kind of。

🥈 提升关 · 语境运用

提升 ★★☆
改错：Why you like elephants?
📝 查看答案
答案：Why do you like elephants?
解析：疑问句要加助动词 do。
提升 ★★☆
连词成句：do / why / like / you / koalas / ?
📝 查看答案
答案：Why do you like koalas?
解析：Why + do + 主语 + 动词原形 + 其他?
提升 ★★☆
I like dogs because they are friendly. (对划线部分提问)
📝 查看答案
答案：Why do you like dogs?
解析：对 because 从句提问用 Why。

🥇 挑战关 · 综合运用

挑战 ★★★
汉译英：你为什么喜欢大象？因为它们很聪明而且有点可爱。
📝 查看答案
答案：Why do you like elephants? Because they are very smart and kind of cute.
解析：Why 问原因，Because 回答。
挑战 ★★★
补全短文：I like ____ (熊猫) best. They are ____ and ____. They are from ____. I think they are very ____ (可爱的).
📝 查看答案
答案：pandas; black; white; China; cute
解析：根据动物特征填写。

✍️ 微写作：My Favorite Animal

用 Why / Because 和描述性形容词写一段话介绍你最喜欢的动物（50-70词）。

My Favorite Animal

My favorite animal is the elephant. Elephants are from Africa and Asia.

They are very smart and friendly. They can remember things for a long time.

Elephants are kind of big and strong, but they are also gentle.

Why do I like elephants? Because they are amazing animals! I want to protect them.

我最喜欢的动物是大象。大象来自非洲和亚洲。它们非常聪明和友好。它们能记住事情很久。大象有点大和强壮，但也很温和。我为什么喜欢大象？因为它们是很棒的动物！我想保护它们。
