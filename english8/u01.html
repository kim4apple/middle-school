Happy Holiday!

假期经历与计划 · be going to · 一般过去时复习

📖 话题：Holiday plans and experiences💬 功能：Talk about holidays, make plans📚 语法：be going to + 动词原形 · 一般过去时复习 · wh-疑问词📝 写作：My Holiday Plan

🎯 我能做到

📖 课文精讲 · Happy Holiday!
场景：开学后，Li Hua 和 Mike 分享暑假经历，并讨论即将到来的国庆节计划。
Li Hua:Hi, Mike! How was your summer vacation?
Mike:It was great! I went to Chengdu with my family.
Li Hua:Sounds fun! What did you do there?
Mike:We visited the panda base and ate hot pot. It was delicious!
Li Hua:Wow! I stayed at home and practiced English. I'm going to improve my speaking this term.
Mike:That's a good plan! What are you going to do for National Day?
Li Hua:I'm going to visit my grandparents in the countryside. I'm going to help them with the farm work.
Mike:That sounds meaningful! I'm going to take a trip to Xi'an with my cousins.
📖 查看翻译

Li Hua: 嗨，Mike！暑假过得怎么样？

Mike: 太棒了！我和家人去了成都。

Li Hua: 听起来很有趣！你们在那做了什么？

Mike: 我们参观了熊猫基地，还吃了火锅。美味极了！

Li Hua: 哇！我待在家里练习英语。这学期我打算提高口语能力。

Mike: 好计划！你国庆节打算做什么？

Li Hua: 我打算去乡下看望爷爷奶奶，帮他们干农活。

Mike: 听起来很有意义！我打算和表弟表妹去西安旅行。

🔑 重点讲解：

1. How was your summer vacation? → 一般过去时询问他人经历。答语用 It was + 形容词。

2. What did you do there? → 一般过去时特殊疑问句：What + did + 主语 + 动词原形?

3. I'm going to improve my speaking. → be going to + 动词原形 表示打算、计划做某事。

4. What are you going to do for National Day? → be going to 特殊疑问句：疑问词 + be + 主语 + going to + 动词原形?

5. That sounds meaningful! → sound + 形容词 听起来……。类似：look（看起来）、taste（尝起来）、feel（感觉）。

🔤 重点句型 · Sentence Patterns
How was…?
询问过去经历
How was your summer vacation? — It was great.
be going to + do
表示计划打算
I am going to visit my grandparents.
What did you do…?
询问过去行为
What did you do on your holiday?
That sounds + adj.
表达评价
That sounds meaningful! / That sounds fun!
📚 语法聚焦 · be going to & 一般过去时

1. be going to 结构（表示计划/打算）

主语肯定句否定句疑问句
II am going to + v.I am not going to + v.Am I going to + v.?
He / She / ItHe is going to + v.He isn't going to + v.Is he going to + v.?
We / You / TheyThey are going to + v.They aren't going to + v.Are they going to + v.?

2. wh-疑问词

疑问词用法例句

How询问方式How are you going to get there?

4. 顺序词 Sequencing Words

顺序词含义例句
First首先First, I'm going to visit my grandparents.
Then / Next然后；接着Then, I'm going to help on the farm.
After that之后After that, I'm going to read books.
Finally最后Finally, I'm going to relax at home.

❌ I go to Beijing last week.（一般过去时需用 went）

✅ I went to Beijing last week.

❌ I am go to visit my grandparents.（be going to 后动词用原形）

✅ I am going to visit my grandparents.

❌ Where you are going?（be going to 疑问句需调整语序）

✅ Where are you going?

📖 阅读专项 · A Meaningful Holiday

Last National Day, I had a meaningful holiday. I went to the countryside to visit my grandparents.

First, I helped my grandpa pick apples in the orchard. It was hard work, but I felt very happy. Then, my grandma taught me how to make dumplings. After that, I read storybooks to my little cousin. Finally, I sat with my grandparents under the big tree and listened to their stories about the old days.

I learned a lot from this trip. Helping others is not only enjoyable but also meaningful. I'm going to visit them again during the Spring Festival!

orchard n. 果园 hard work n. 辛苦的工作 not only…but also… 不仅……而且…… Spring Festival n. 春节

🏆 中考题型专练 · 完形填空

阅读下面短文，选择最佳选项填入空白处。

My family and I (1)____ a trip to Hangzhou last month. We (2)____ there by high-speed train. The (3)____ was clear and sunny. We (4)____ West Lake and ate some delicious food. Next time, I (5)____ going to bring my grandparents with us!

1. A. take B. took C. taking D. takes
2. A. get B. got C. gets D. getting
3. A. weather B. food C. trip D. holiday
4. A. visit B. visits C. visited D. visiting
5. A. am B. is C. are D. be
答案
B (took)；B (got)；A (weather)；C (visited)；A (am)
✍️ 写作进阶 · My Holiday Plan
📋 写作题目
请以 "My Holiday Plan" 为题，写一篇 60-80 词的短文，使用 be going to 和顺序词 first / then / after that / finally。
开头：引出即将到来的假期。
主体：用顺序词描述具体计划——先去哪？然后做什么？之后呢？
结尾：表达期待或感受。
My winter holiday is coming. First, I'm going to finish my homework early. Then, I'm going to visit my grandparents in the countryside. After that, I'm going to read two English storybooks. Finally, I'm going to have a family trip to Harbin to see the ice and snow. I'm really looking forward to it!
我的寒假要来了。首先我打算早点完成作业。然后去乡下看望爷爷奶奶。之后读两本英语故事书。最后全家去哈尔滨看冰雪。我真的很期待！
