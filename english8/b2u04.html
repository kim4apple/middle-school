Why don't you talk to your parents?

人际沟通

📖 话题：Interpersonal Communication💬 功能：Give advice and talk about problems📚 语法：Why don't you…? / Why not…? 提建议 · until / although / so that 连词📝 写作：Letter to Give Advice

🎯 我能做到

📖 课文精讲 · Why don't you talk to your parents?
场景：Li Ming 最近和好朋友闹矛盾了，向心理辅导员求助。
Li Ming:I have a problem. My best friend is angry with me, but I don't know why.
Counselor:I'm sorry to hear that. Why don't you talk to him directly?
Li Ming:I tried, but he wouldn't listen. He didn't talk to me until yesterday.
Counselor:Although he was angry, communication is important. Why don't you write him a letter?
Li Ming:That's a good idea. I'll try it so that we can make up.
Counselor:You could also invite him to play basketball. Sports can help you relax and reconnect.
📖 查看翻译

Li Ming: 我遇到一个问题。我最好的朋友生我的气了，但我不知道为什么。

Counselor: 听到这个我很难过。你为什么不直接和他谈谈呢？

Li Ming: 我试过，但他不听。他直到昨天才和我说话。

Counselor: 虽然他在生气，但沟通很重要。你为什么不给他写封信呢？

Li Ming: 好主意。我试试，这样我们能和好。

Counselor: 你也可以邀请他打篮球。运动能帮助放松和重新联系。

🔑 重点讲解：

1. Why don't you talk to him directly? → Why don't you + 动词原形? "你为什么不……？" 提建议。

2. He didn't talk to me until yesterday. → not…until "直到……才"。

3. Although he was angry, communication is important. → Although + 从句 "虽然……但是"（不和 but 连用）。

4. I'll try it so that we can make up. → so that "以便" 表目的。make up "和好"。

提建议

not…until…

📚 语法聚焦 · 提建议 & until/although/so that

提建议的多种方式

表达结构示例
Why don't you…Why don't you + v.Why don't you ask your teacher?
Why not…Why not + v.Why not have a rest?
You should…You should + v.You should talk to your parents.
You could…You could + v.You could write a letter.

连词 until/although/so that

连词含义示例
until直到Wait until I come back.
not…until直到……才He didn't sleep until 11 p.m.
although虽然Although he was tired, he kept working.
so that以便I got up early so that I could catch the bus.

❌ Although he is busy, but he helps me.（although 和 but 不能连用）

✅ Although he is busy, he helps me.

❌ Why don't you to ask him?（Why don't you 后加动词原形）

✅ Why don't you ask him?

📖 阅读专项 · How to Deal with Friendship Problems

Friendship is important, but problems can happen. Here are some tips to handle them.

First, stay calm. Don't argue when you're angry. Take a deep breath and wait until you feel better.

Second, talk about your feelings. Use "I" sentences. Say "I felt sad when you ignored me" instead of "You always ignore me."

Third, try to understand your friend's point of view. Although you may disagree, respect each other's feelings.

Finally, why not do something fun together? Playing sports or watching a movie can help you reconnect.

deal with 处理；应对 handle v. 处理 calm adj. 冷静的 point of view 观点；立场 reconnect v. 重新建立联系

🏆 中考题型专练 · 语法填空

用括号内连词或所给词的适当形式填空。

(1)____ (Although / Because) my sister is younger, she often gives me good advice. Last week, I (2)____ (argue) with my best friend. My sister said, "Why (3)____ you call her?" I followed her advice. (4)____ (Although / Until) I was nervous, I made the call. My friend and I talked (5)____ (until / unless) we understood each other. We made up in the end.

答案
(1) Although；(2) argued；(3) don't；(4) Although；(5) until
✍️ 写作进阶 · Letter to Give Advice
📋 写作题目
你的朋友和妈妈吵架了，很伤心。请给他/她写一封 70 词的建议信，用到 Why don't you 和 although。
开头：表达理解和关心。
主体：给出 2 条具体建议（用 Why don't you）。
结尾：鼓励和祝福。
Dear friend, I'm sorry to hear about your problem. Although your mom was angry, I believe she loves you. Why don't you talk to her when she is calm? You could say sorry first. Why not help with some housework to show you care? Good communication can solve many problems. I hope you and your mom can make up soon!
亲爱的朋友：听说你的问题我很难过。虽然你妈妈在生气，但我相信她爱你。你为什么不趁她冷静的时候和她谈谈？你可以先道歉。为什么不帮她做些家务来表现你的关心？好的沟通能解决很多问题。希望你们能尽快和好！
