家务劳动 · 邀请与回应 · Could you please ...?

📖 话题：Home and chores💬 功能：Talk about chores, make polite requests📚 语法：Could you please ...? · 情态动词 can/could · 礼貌请求📝 写作：邀请与回复

🎯 我能做到

📖 课文精讲 · Could you please ...?
场景：Amy 和 Tom 在家谈论家务活，Amy 请 Tom 帮忙。
Amy:Could you please help me with the dishes?
Tom:Sure, no problem! I'll wash and you can dry.
Amy:Thanks! And could you please take out the garbage?
Tom:Sorry, I have to finish my homework first. Can I do it later?
Amy:Of course. Could you please sweep the floor tomorrow?
Tom:I'm afraid I can't. I need to fold the laundry after school.
Amy:OK. Could you at least feed the cat?
Tom:Sure, I can do that!
📖 查看翻译

Amy: 你能帮忙洗碗吗？

Tom: 当然，没问题！我洗，你擦干。

Amy: 谢谢！那你能把垃圾拿出去吗？

Tom: 抱歉，我得先做完作业。我能晚点做吗？

Amy: 当然可以。你明天能扫地吗？

Tom: 恐怕不行。放学后我得叠衣服。

Amy: 好吧。那你能至少喂一下猫吗？

Tom: 当然，这我能做！

🔑 重点讲解：

1. Could you please help me with the dishes? → Could you please + 动词原形? 表示礼貌请求。比 Can you 更客气。

2. 接受请求：Sure / Of course / No problem。拒绝时给出理由：Sorry, I have to ... / I'm afraid I can't。

3. take out the garbage → 把垃圾拿出去。短语动词，宾语放在副词前后均可。

4. Can I do it later? → Can 在此表示请求许可，语气比 Could 更随意。

📚 语法聚焦 · 礼貌请求

1. Could you please + 动词原形?（礼貌请求）

结构示例语气
Could you please + 动词原形?Could you please close the window?非常礼貌
Can you + 动词原形?Can you help me?较随意
Would you please + 动词原形?Would you please wait a moment?正式礼貌

2. 接受与拒绝

功能表达
接受Sure. / Of course. / No problem. / Certainly. / With pleasure.
拒绝（有理）Sorry, I have to ... / I'm afraid I can't. / I'd love to, but ...

3. can vs could 的用法区别

cancould
能力I can swim.（我会游泳。）I could swim when I was five.（五岁时就会了。）
许可Can I use your pen?（我能用你的笔吗？）Could I borrow your book?（可以借你的书吗？更客气）
请求Can you help me?（你能帮我吗？）Could you please help me?（您能帮我一下吗？更礼貌）

❌ Could you please sweeping the floor?（please 后跟动词原形）

✅ Could you please sweep the floor?

❌ Sorry, I can not sorry.（拒绝要说明理由）

✅ Sorry, I have to finish my homework first.

📖 阅读专项 · Helping at Home

There are many chores to do at home. Some people sweep the floor. Others wash the dishes or do the laundry. Many families divide the chores among family members.

Helping at home is important. When everyone helps, the house stays clean and tidy. The work gets done quickly and easily. Children learn to be responsible and independent.

It is also important to cooperate with each other. If you are busy, ask someone for help. You can say, "Could you please help me?" If someone asks you, you can say, "Sure, no problem!" or "Sorry, I have to do my homework."

Helping at home makes our family happy. Let's all do our part!

🏆 中考题型专练 · 语法填空

用所给词的适当形式填空。

Dear Tom,

I (1)____ (need) your help. Could you please come to my house this Saturday? We have a school project. I (2)____ (can) draw, but I am not good at writing. Could you please (3)____ (help) me write the report?

I also (4)____ (tidy) my room yesterday. It looks much better now. And I (5)____ (water) the plants every day. (6)____ (Could) you please bring some pictures? We can use them for the poster.

Please let me (7)____ (know) if you can come. I really (8)____ (appreciate) your help.

Yours, Amy

答案
(1) need；(2) can；(3) help；(4) tidied；(5) water；(6) Could；(7) know；(8) appreciate
✍️ 写作进阶 · An Invitation Email
📋 写作题目
假设你在做一个学校项目，需要请一位朋友帮忙。写一封邀请邮件，至少用 3 次 "Could you please ...?" 表达请求。
开头：问候 + 说明写邮件的原因。
主体：提出请求（至少 3 个 Could you please ...?）。
结尾：询问能否参加 + 表达感谢。
Dear Jack,
How are you? I am writing to ask for your help. We have a school project about our hometown.
Could you please help me draw a map? I am not good at drawing. Could you please bring some photos of our town? And could you please come to my house on Sunday? We can work together. I will prepare the snacks.
Could you please let me know if you can come? I really appreciate your help. I hope we can make a great project together!
Yours, Amy
你好 Jack！你好吗？我写信想请你帮忙。我们有一个关于家乡的学校项目。你能帮我画一张地图吗？我不擅长画画。你能带一些我们小镇的照片吗？还有你能周日来我家吗？我们可以一起做。我会准备零食。请告诉我你能不能来。非常感谢你的帮助。希望我们能一起做一个很棒的项目！
