七下 · Unit 6

I'm watching TV.

现在进行时 · 日常活动 · 电话用语

📖 话题：Everyday activities 💬 功能：Talk about what people are doing · Make phone calls 📚 语法：现在进行时 (am/is/are + doing) 📝 写作：描述家人正在做什么

🎯 我能做到

📖 课文精讲 · What Are You Doing?
场景：周末晚上，Jenny 打电话给 Laura，聊各自正在做什么。
Jenny: Hello? This is Jenny. May I speak to Laura?
Laura: Hi Jenny! This is Laura speaking.
Jenny: Hi Laura! What are you doing right now?
Laura: I'm watching TV. There's a really interesting show on.
Jenny: Sounds fun! What is your brother doing?
Laura: He's playing computer games in his room. He's not doing homework.
Jenny: What about your parents? Are they watching TV too?
Laura: No, they aren't. My mom is cooking dinner and my dad is reading a newspaper.
📖 查看翻译

逐句翻译：

Jenny: 喂？我是 Jenny。我找 Laura。

Laura: 嗨 Jenny！我就是 Laura。

Jenny: 嗨 Laura！你现在在做什么？

Laura: 我在看电视。有个很有意思的节目。

Jenny: 听起来不错！你哥哥在做什么？

Laura: 他在房间里打游戏。他没在做作业。

Jenny: 哈哈。你爸妈呢？他们也在看电视吗？

Laura: 不，他们没有。我妈妈在做晚饭，我爸爸在看报纸。

🔑 重点讲解：

1. What are you doing? → 现在进行时的特殊疑问句：What + am/is/are + 主语 + doing?

2. I'm watching TV. → 现在进行时的肯定句：主语 + am/is/are + 动词-ing

3. 电话用语：This is ... / This is ... speaking. → 打电话时用 this 表示"我"。

4. May I speak to ...? → "我可以找……接电话吗？" 礼貌用语。

📖 Reading: A Busy Sunday Evening

It's 7 o'clock on Sunday evening. My family is at home.

My father is reading a book in the living room. My mother is washing the dishes in the kitchen.

My sister is doing her homework at her desk. She is very quiet.

My brother is playing with the cat. The cat is running after a ball.

And me? I'm sitting on the sofa and listening to music. What a relaxing evening!

🔤 重点句型 · Sentence Patterns
What are you doing?
询问对方正在做什么。
✅ What are you doing?
➡ I'm watching TV.
✅ What is he doing?
➡ He's playing soccer.
Are you + doing?
现在进行时的一般疑问句。
✅ Are you reading a book?
➡ Yes, I am. / No, I'm not.
✅ Is he doing homework?
➡ Yes, he is. / No, he isn't.
电话用语
打电话和接电话的常用表达。
✅ 打电话者：This is Jenny. May I speak to Laura?
✅ 接电话者：This is Laura speaking.
✅ 问别人：Is Tom there?
I'm not + doing (否定)
现在进行时的否定句：在 be 动词后加 not。
✅ I'm not doing homework.
✅ He isn't watching TV.
✅ They aren't playing outside.
📚 语法聚焦 · Present Continuous Tense

1️⃣ 现在进行时：结构

现在进行时表示此刻正在进行的动作。结构为：主语 + am/is/are + 动词-ing

句式结构示例
肯定句主语 + am/is/are + doingI am watching TV.
否定句主语 + am/is/are + not + doingShe isn't sleeping.
一般疑问句Am/Is/Are + 主语 + doing?Are you reading?
特殊疑问句Wh-word + am/is/are + 主语 + doing?What is he doing?

2️⃣ 动词-ing 形式变化规则

规则动词原形-ing 形式
一般情况 + ingplay / read / watchplaying / reading / watching
以不发音 e 结尾，去 e + ingtake / write / cometaking / writing / coming
重读闭音节，双写末尾字母 + ingswim / run / shopswimming / running / shopping
以 ie 结尾，变 ie 为 y + ingdie / lie / tiedying / lying / tying
⚠️ 中式英语纠错：
❌ I watching TV now.（缺少 be 动词）
✅ I am watching TV now.

❌ He is swiming.（swim 重读闭音节，要双写 m）
✅ He is swimming.

❌ What are you do?（进行时要用 doing，不是 do）
✅ What are you doing?

3️⃣ 现在进行时 vs 一般现在时

现在进行时一般现在时
用法此刻正在发生的动作经常发生的习惯性动作
关键词now, right now, at the moment, Look!usually, always, every day, sometimes
例句She is reading now.She reads every day.
📝 三级闯关 · 实战练习

🥉 基础关 · 概念识记

基础 ★☆☆
What ____ you doing? (am / is / are)
📝 查看答案
答案：are
解析：主语 you 用 are。
基础 ★☆☆
He is ____ (watch) TV now.
📝 查看答案
答案：watching
解析：watch + ing → watching。
基础 ★☆☆
They ____ (be) playing basketball right now.
📝 查看答案
答案：are
解析：They 复数，be 动词用 are。

🥈 提升关 · 语境运用

提升 ★★☆
改错：He is swiming in the pool.
📝 查看答案
答案：He is swimming in the pool.
解析：swim 是重读闭音节，双写 m 再加 ing。
提升 ★★☆
连词成句：doing / what / you / are / ?
📝 查看答案
答案：What are you doing?
解析：现在进行时特殊疑问句：What + are + 主语 + doing?
提升 ★★☆
She is reading a book. (改为否定句)
📝 查看答案
答案：She is not / isn't reading a book.
解析：在 is 后加 not。

🥇 挑战关 · 综合运用

挑战 ★★★

📝 查看答案
答案：What is your brother doing? He is playing computer games in his room.
解析：Is + 主语 + doing; 回答用 He is + doing。
挑战 ★★★
补全对话：
A: Hello! ____ I speak to Mary?
B: This is Mary ____. Hi!

📝 查看答案
答案：May; speaking; are; doing; cleaning
解析：May I speak to...? / This is ... speaking. / What are you doing? / cleaning。
