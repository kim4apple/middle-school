📕 八年级上册 8 单元

U1 Happy Holiday! — 假期经历与计划 🔥 中考高频
▾

🎯 核心考点：一般过去时 × be going to 将来时

结构规则例句
规则动词过去式动词 + ed
辅音+y → ied
重读闭音节双写+ed		 visited / watched
studied / carried
stopped / planned
不规则动词过去式特殊变化，需单独记忆 go—went / eat—ate / see—saw / buy—bought / take—took / have—had
一般过去时否定didn't + 动词原形 I didn't go to the beach.
一般过去时疑问Did + 主语 + 动词原形? Did you have a good time?
be going toam/is/are + going to + do I am going to visit my grandparents.
be going to 否定be + not + going to + do I am not going to stay at home.
🚨 易错点 1：did 后动词忘还原
❌ I didn't went to Beijing.
✅ I didn't go to Beijing.

🚨 易错点 2：be going to 的 be 易遗漏
❌ I going to visit my grandparents.
✅ I am going to visit my grandparents.

📌 中考提示：不规则动词过去式是中考语法填空必考题。go/went/gone, eat/ate/eaten, see/saw/seen 是最高频三组。
U2 How often do you exercise? — 频率表达 🔥 中考高频
▾

🎯 核心考点：频率副词 · How often 问句 · 频度表达

频率副词含义大致频率在句中位置
always总是100%be 之后 / 实义动词之前
usually通常80%同上
often经常60%同上
sometimes有时40%句首/句中/句尾
hardly ever几乎从不~5%同上副词位置
never从不0%同上

How often do you exercise?
— Three times a week. / Every day. / Once a month.

🚨 易错点 1：How often vs How long vs How soon
疑问词含义答语特点例句
How often多久一次频率（once/week/every day）How often do you swim? — Twice a week.
How long多长时间时长（for two hours）How long did you stay? — For a week.
How soon多久以后in + 时间段How soon will you come back? — In two days.

🚨 易错点 2：hardly ever ≠ hard ever
❌ I hard ever exercise. ← 不知道 hardly 是副词
✅ I hardly ever exercise.

📌 中考提示：频率副词辨析是完形填空常客。always > usually > often > sometimes > hardly ever > never 这个梯度和词义匹配必须记牢。
U3 I'm more outgoing than my sister. — 比较级 🔥 中考高频
▾

🎯 核心考点：形容词/副词比较级 + as…as 结构

比较级构成规则例词
单音节 + er直接加 -ertall → taller, fast → faster
辅音+y → ier改 y 为 i + erheavy → heavier, friendly → friendlier
重读闭音节双写+er双写尾字母 + erbig → bigger, hot → hotter
多音节 + moremore + 原级outgoing → more outgoing, beautiful → more beautiful
特殊变化单独记忆good/well → better, bad/badly → worse
as + 原级 + as "和……一样"：Tom is as tall as Mike.
not as/so + 原级 + as "不如……"：I'm not as outgoing as her.
than 标志词：She runs faster than me.
🚨 易错点 1：more + 形容词 ≠ 形容词 + er 混用
❌ She is more taller than me.（more 与 -er 不共用）
✅ She is taller than me.

🚨 易错点 2：than 后的人称代词
❌ He runs faster than I.（语法正确但口语别扭）
✅ He runs faster than me.（口语自然，中考也接受）
注：正式语法认为 than 后跟主格(I)，但中考完形/写作中 than me 更常见。

📌 中考提示：比较级常与 "than", "of the two", "much/even/a little + 比较级" 搭配。"much taller" ✓、"even better" ✓。
🔍 辨析：more + 多音节   vs   -er 单音节
单音节 -er tall→taller, fast→faster, big→bigger, nice→nicer
双音节需区分 easy→easier, happy→happier
boring→more boring, tired→more tired
三音节+ more beautiful→more beautiful
expensive→more expensive
U4 What's the best movie theater? — 最高级 🔥 中考高频
▾

🎯 核心考点：形容词/副词最高级

最高级构成规则例词
单音节 + est加 -estcheap → the cheapest
辅音+y → iest改 y 为 i + esthappy → the happiest
重读闭音节双写+est双写尾字母 + estbig → the biggest
多音节 + mostthe most + 原级popular → the most popular
特殊变化单独记忆good → the best, bad → the worst
最高级标志词：
in + 范围（单人/事物）：the tallest boy in our class
of + 范围（多人/事物）：the best of all
one of + 最高级 + 可数名词复数：one of the best movies
🚨 易错点 1：最高级前必须加 the
❌ She is most popular girl in our class.
✅ She is the most popular girl in our class.

🚨 易错点 2：比较级和最高级的范围搞混
✅ 两者比较用比较级：She is the taller of the two.
✅ 三者及以上用最高级：She is the tallest in our class.

📌 中考提示："one of the + 最高级 + 名词复数" 是写作句型模板，背下来就能用：
This is one of the most beautiful places I have ever visited.
U5 Do you want to watch a game show? — 表达喜好与看法 📌 中考中频
▾

🎯 核心考点：喜好表达（want/love/like/don't mind/can't stand）+ 动词形式

表达强度搭配例句
love非常喜欢love + (to do)/doingI love watching talk shows.
like喜欢like + (to do)/doingI like game shows.
don't mind不介意don't mind + doingI don't mind watching the news.
don't like不喜欢don't like + (to do)/doingI don't like soap operas.
can't stand无法忍受can't stand + doingI can't stand watching them.
want想要want + to doI want to watch a game show.
hopes/expects希望/期望hope/expect + to doHe hopes to find out what's going on.
询问喜好句式：
— Do you want to watch a game show?
— What do you think of talk shows? / How do you like talk shows?
— I don't mind them.
🚨 易错点 1：can't stand + to do vs + doing
❌ I can't stand watch soap operas.
✅ I can't stand watching soap operas.
注意：can't stand 后必须接 doing (或名词)，不接动词原形。

🚨 易错点 2："What do you think of…?" 答语引导词
❌ I think it's funny. / I like it.
✅ I think it's funny. / I like it.
注意：反问对方的表达 "I don't, either" 中的 either 表示"也不"。
U6 I'm going to study computer science. — 职业规划与计划 🔥 中考高频
▾

🎯 核心考点：be going to 结构（计划/意图用法）

句式结构例句
肯定句主语 + be + going to + doI am going to study computer science.
否定句主语 + be + not + going to + doHe is not going to be a teacher.
一般疑问句Be + 主语 + going to + do?Are you going to find a job?
特殊疑问句Wh- + be + 主语 + going to + do?What are you going to be when you grow up?
职业名词派生规律：
teach → teacher / write → writer / act → actor / art → artist / science → scientist
注意：study → student（不规则）
🚨 易错点 1：be going to 和 will 混用
be going to（计划/意图） 事先考虑好的打算：
I'm going to be a doctor.（我早就想好了）
will（临时/意愿） 当场决定的：
I'll help you.（哦，我来帮你）

🚨 易错点 2：be 动词的省略
❌ He going to be a pilot.
✅ He is going to be a pilot.

📌 中考提示：be going to 与 will 的区别是初三和中考单选题的常客。记住关键词：打算 → be going to；临时 → will。
U7 Will people have robots? — 对未来预测 🔥 中考高频
▾

🎯 核心考点：will 将来时 · more/less/fewer 数量区别

句式结构例句
肯定句will + 动词原形People will have robots in their homes.
否定句won't + 动词原形Kids won't go to school in the future.
一般疑问句Will + 主语 + 动词原形?Will there be more pollution?
There will beThere will be + 名词There will be more people.
🔍 more / less / fewer 区分（中考必考！）
more — 更多 可数/不可数皆可：
more people, more pollution
less — 更少（不可数） less water, less time, less pollution
fewer — 更少（可数） fewer cars, fewer people, fewer trees
🚨 易错点 1：There will be → There will have ❌
❌ There will have more robots.
✅ There will be more robots.
注意：表示"有"用 there be，不要和 have 混用。

🚨 易错点 2：less/fewer 混淆
❌ There will be less cars.（cars 可数）
✅ There will be fewer cars.

📌 中考提示：less/fewer 辨析是完形填空必考题。记口诀：可数 fewer 不可数 less。
U8 Let's Communicate! — 沟通与表达 📌 中考中频
▾

🎯 核心考点：祈使句 · 提建议 · 请求帮助

功能句式例句
建议（共同行动）Let's + 动词原形Let's communicate openly.
建议（询问对方）How about / What about + doing?How about making a plan?
礼貌请求Could you please + 动词原形?Could you please give me some advice?
祈使句（肯定）动词原形开头Talk to him.
祈使句（否定）Don't + 动词原形Don't shout.
🚨 易错点 1：Let's 的否定形式
❌ Let's don't argue.
✅ Let's not argue. / Let's not be angry.

🚨 易错点 2：How about + 动词用 doing
❌ How about take a break?
✅ How about taking a break?

📌 中考提示：祈使句 + and/or + 一般将来时的结构（Study hard, and you will pass the exam.）是中考写作加分句型。

📗 八年级下册 8 单元

U1 Time to Relax! — 放松与休息 🔥 中考高频
▾

🎯 核心考点：不定式用法 · 反身代词

不定式结构例句
It's + adj. + to doIt's important to relax.
want / hope / need to doI want to take a break.
It's + adj. + (for/of sb.) + to doIt's kind of you to help.
🔍 反身代词总表
第一人称 myself / ourselves
第二人称 yourself / yourselves
第三人称 himself / herself / itself / themselves
常用短语：enjoy oneself（玩得开心）、by oneself（独自）、teach oneself（自学）、hurt oneself（伤到自己）
🚨 易错点：反身代词与人称一致
❌ He enjoyed myself at the party.（反身代词需与主语一致）
✅ He enjoyed himself at the party.

📌 中考提示："It's + adj. + for/of sb. + to do" 中 for 和 of 的区别：
of — 形容人的品质（kind, nice, smart, clever, foolish）
for — 形容事物特性（important, necessary, easy, difficult）
U2 Let's clean up! — 志愿服务与社区 📌 中考中频
▾

🎯 核心考点：动词短语（短语动词）

短语含义例句
clean up打扫干净Let's clean up the park.
help out帮忙I want to help out.
give away赠送Let's give away old clothes.
put up张贴；搭建Let's put up signs.
call up打电话I'll call up my friends.
cheer up使振奋Let's cheer up the sick kids.
🚨 易错点：代词作宾语时位置
✅ 名词可放中间或后面：clean up the park = clean the park up
✅ 代词只能放中间：clean it up ✓ / clean up it ❌
❌ Let's clean up it.
✅ Let's clean it up.

📌 中考提示：动词短语是中考完形填空的常客。注意区分可拆与不可拆短语——像 look after（照顾）不能拆成 look him after ❌。
U3 Could you please clean your room? — 家务与请求 📌 中考中频
▾

🎯 核心考点：礼貌请求 · Could you please / Could I

句式用法例句
Could you please + 动词原形?请求对方做Could you please clean your room?
Could I + 动词原形?请求自己做Could I go out tonight?
肯定回答同意Sure. / Of course. / No problem.
否定回答拒绝（需理由）Sorry, I'm afraid I can't. I need to…
🚨 易错点：否定回答用 I'm afraid → 不用 I couldn't
❌ Could I go out? — No, you couldn't.（语气太生硬）
✅ Could I go out? — Sorry, I'm afraid you can't.

📌 中考提示：Could you please 中 could 不是过去时，而是礼貌语气。回答时用 can/can't，不用 could/couldn't。
U4 Why don't you talk to your parents? — 沟通与建议 🔥 中考高频
▾

🎯 核心考点：提建议句式 + until / although / so that 连词

提建议句型

句式例句
Why don't you + 动词原形?Why don't you talk to him?
Why not + 动词原形?Why not ask for help?
You should / could + 动词原形You could write a letter.
What/How about + doing?How about having a talk?

连词辨析（中考语法填空常考）

连词含义用法特点例句
until直到主句动词通常为 延续性/瞬间否定Wait until she calms down.
although虽然不与 but 连用Although he's angry, he cares.
so that以便引导目的状语从句Talk nicely so that she can understand.
🚨 易错点：although 和 but 不连用
❌ Although he was tired, but he kept working.
✅ Although he was tired, he kept working.
✅ He was tired, but he kept working.
though 同理——句中虽有"虽然/但是"的语义，英语不能同时使用 although/though 和 but。

📌 中考提示：until 在否定句中的用法值得注意：
He didn't leave until his mom came back.（直到妈妈回来他才离开）
not…until = "直到……才……" 是必考句型。
U5 What were you doing when the rainstorm came? — 过去进行时 🔥🔥 中考必考
▾

🎯 核心考点：过去进行时 + when/while 区别

语法点结构例句
过去进行时was/were + doingI was watching TV at 8 pm.
when + 从句when + 一般过去时（打断）I was sleeping when the phone rang.
while + 从句while + 过去进行时（伴随）While I was sleeping, my mom came home.
🔍 when vs while 的本质区别（完形填空必杀题）
when "当……的时候"
后接 一般过去时（短动作）
也可接过去进行时
I was reading when he came in.
while "在……期间"
后接 过去进行时（长动作）
侧重两个动作同时持续
While I was reading, he was watching TV.
🚨 易错点 1：过去进行时的否定和疑问
❌ What did you doing at that time?
✅ What were you doing at that time?

📌 中考提示：过去进行时与一般过去时的对比是完形填空和写作高频考点：
I was taking a shower when the doorbell rang.
（长动作 shower → 过去进行时，短动作 rang → 一般过去时）
U6 An old man tried to move the mountains. — 连词与故事 🔥 中考高频
▾

🎯 核心考点：unless · as soon as · so…that

连词含义等价结构例句
unless除非= if…notYou won't pass unless you study.
as soon as一……就……同义：the momentAs soon as he saw her, he smiled.
so…that如此……以至于so + 形/副 + thatThe mountain was so high that few could climb it.
🚨 易错点：so…that 与 such…that 区别
so + 形容词 + that so tall that…
so + 形/副
such + 名词短语 + that such a tall boy that…
such + (a/an) + 形 + 名
❌ It was so a hot day that we didn't go out.
✅ It was such a hot day that we didn't go out.
✅ It was so hot that we didn't go out.

📌 中考提示：unless 引导条件状语从句，遵循"主将从现"原则：
You won't pass the exam unless you study hard.（从句用一般现在时表将来）
U7 What's the highest mountain in the world? — 地理比较 🔥 中考高频
▾

🎯 核心考点：数字+比较级/最高级（地理数据表达）

结构例句
最高级 + in/of 范围Qomolangma is the highest mountain in the world.
比较级 + than any other + 单数The Caspian Sea is deeper than any other lake.
数字 + 形容词It's 8,844 meters high.
数字 + 比较级It's much higher than any other mountain.
one of + 最高级 + 复数名词It's one of the most dangerous places.
数字 + 形容词的顺序：数字 + 量词 + 形容词
eight kilometers long / 8848 meters high / 100 square meters wide
🚨 易错点 1："any other + 单数"
❌ It's bigger than any others mountain.
✅ It's bigger than any other mountain.

🚨 易错点 2："in the world" vs "of all"
✅ the highest mountain in the world（用 in + 范围）
✅ the highest of all the mountains（用 of + 群体）
❌ in all the world（少见）

📌 中考提示：本单元常以补全句子出现，关键要写出正确语序：
"The Yangtze River is about 6,300 kilometers long." ✓
U8 Making a Difference — 改变与影响 🔥🔥 中考必考（过渡到初三）
▾

🎯 核心考点：现在完成时（基础入门）

句式结构例句
肯定句have/has + 过去分词I have visited the museum.
否定句haven't/hasn't + 过去分词She hasn't read the book yet.
一般疑问句Have/Has + 主语 + 过去分词?Have you ever been to Beijing?
ever / never曾经 / 从未I have never seen such a beautiful place.
already / yet已经 / 还没有I have already done it. / Have you done it yet?
🔍 一般过去时 vs 现在完成时（八年级最核心辨析！）
一般过去时 — 过去动作，现已结束 I visited Beijing last year.
强调"去年"去的，现在不在北京。
时间标志词：yesterday, last…, …ago, in 2022
现在完成时 — 过去动作，与现在有关 I have visited Beijing.
强调"去过"，现在知道北京什么样。
时间标志词：already, yet, ever, never, just, so far
🚨 易错点 1：have been to vs have gone to（必考！）
✅ have been to Beijing = 去过北京（已回）
✅ have gone to Beijing = 去了北京（还没回来 / 在路上）
❌ He has been to Shanghai for two days.（been to 是来往经历，不能接时间段）
✅ He has been in Shanghai for two days.（been in 表示"在某地待了多久"）

🚨 易错点 2：过去分词不规则变化
原形过去式过去分词
gowentgone
eatateeaten
seesawseen
writewrotewritten
readreadread（拼写相同，读音不同）

📌 中考提示：现在完成时是初三中考语法填空和写作的重中之重。八下只是入门，但"have been to / have gone to"辨析是中考单选题铁打的考点。
