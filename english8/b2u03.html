Growing Up

成长变化 · 过去进行时 · 时间状语从句

📖 话题：Growth and change💬 功能：Talk about past experiences and changes📚 语法：过去进行时 (was/were + doing) · when/while 从句📝 写作：我的成长故事

🎯 我能做到

📖 课文精讲 · Growing Up
场景：两个朋友翻看旧照片，回忆当时正在做什么。
Amy:Look at this photo! What were you doing here?
Tom:I was learning to ride a bike. I fell off many times!
Amy:Haha! I remember that day. Your dad was running after you.
Tom:Yes! While I was trying to balance, he was shouting 'You can do it!'
Amy:That's so sweet. I remember when I was learning to swim, I was really scared.
Tom:But look at you now! You're a great swimmer. We've all grown so much.
📖 查看翻译

Amy: 看这张照片！你当时在做什么？

Tom: 我在学骑自行车。摔了好多次！

Amy: 哈哈我记得！你爸爸当时在后面追着你跑。

Tom: 是啊！我在努力保持平衡的时候，他一直在喊"你能行！"

Amy: 真暖心。我记得我学游泳的时候，特别害怕。

Tom: 看看现在的你！游得那么好。我们都成长了好多。

🔑 重点讲解：

1. What were you doing? → 过去进行时：was/were + doing，表示过去某个时刻正在做的事。

2. While I was trying to balance, he was shouting. → while + 过去进行时，表示两个同时进行的动作。

3. I remember when I was learning to swim. → when + 过去进行时，表示"当……的时候"。

4. I fell off many times! → fall off "从……摔下来"，一般过去时表示过去发生的事。

🔤 重点句型 · Sentence Patterns
was/were + V-ing (过去进行时)
描述过去某时正在做的事
I was reading at 8 p.m. yesterday. / They were playing soccer at that time.
when + 一般过去时 / while + 过去进行时
时间状语从句
I was sleeping when the phone rang. / While I was reading, my mom came in.
used to + 动词原形
过去常常（现在不再）
I used to be shy, but now I'm confident. / She used to live in Beijing.
📚 语法聚焦 · 过去进行时

过去进行时 (Past Continuous)

句式结构示例
肯定was/were + V-ingI was reading at 9 p.m. last night.
否定wasn't/weren't + V-ingShe wasn't sleeping when I called.
疑问Was/Were + 主语 + V-ing?What were you doing at that time?

when / while 时间状语从句

连词用法示例
when+ 一般过去时（短暂动作）I was watching TV when the phone rang.
while+ 过去进行时（持续动作）While I was walking home, it started to rain.
while+ 过去进行时（两个同时进行）While I was cooking, my sister was reading.

used to 的用法

句式结构示例
肯定used to + 动词原形I used to be afraid of the dark.
否定didn't use to + 动词原形I didn't use to like vegetables.
疑问Did + 主语 + use to + 动词?Did you use to play the piano?

过去进行时 vs 一般过去时

时态用法示例
过去进行时过去某时正在进行的动作I was doing my homework at 7 p.m.
一般过去时过去完成的动作I did my homework last night.

❌ I was go to school at 8 yesterday.（过去进行时要用 V-ing）

✅ I was going to school at 8 yesterday.

❌ While I did my homework, my mom cooked dinner.（while 后接过去进行时）

✅ While I was doing my homework, my mom was cooking dinner.

❌ I used to played the piano.（used to 后接动词原形）

✅ I used to play the piano.

📖 阅读专项 · A Story of Growth

When I was ten years old, I was afraid of speaking in public. I used to hide behind my mom whenever someone asked me a question.

One day, my teacher asked me to give a short speech in front of the class. I was so nervous! While I was preparing for it, my mom was helping me practice every night. She said, "You are braver than you think."

On the day of the speech, I was shaking when I stood up. But while I was talking, I felt more and more confident. My classmates were listening carefully. When I finished, everyone clapped!

That day changed me. I learned that challenges help us grow. Now I am not afraid to speak in public. I have become stronger and more confident. Growing up means facing difficulties and becoming a better person.

hide v. 躲藏 nervous adj. 紧张的 clap v. 鼓掌 difficulty n. 困难 face v. 面对

When I was a child, I (1)____ to be very shy. I (2)____ talking to new people. But one summer, everything changed. I (3)____ a summer camp. While I (4)____ activities with other kids, I started to feel more confident. I (5)____ new friends and learned to work in a team. Now I am much more outgoing. 1. A. use B. used C. using D. uses
2. A. hate B. hated C. hating D. hates
3. A. join B. joined C. joining D. joins
4. A. do B. did C. was doing D. does
5. A. make B. made C. making D. makes
