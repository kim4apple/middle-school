七下 · Unit 8

Once upon a Time

故事讲述 · 一般过去时 · 叙事顺序

📖 话题：Stories and fables 💬 功能：Tell a simple story in sequence 📚 语法：一般过去时综合 · 叙事连接词 (first/then/next/finally) 📝 写作：讲述一个小故事

🎯 我能做到

📖 课文精讲 · Story Time
场景：Ms Wang 和同学们在图书馆讲故事。
Ms Wang: Good afternoon, children! Let's tell stories today.
Li Ming: Great! I love stories!
Ms Wang: Once upon a time, there was a clever rabbit and a slow turtle...
Chen Jie: I know this story! The rabbit and the turtle had a race!
Ms Wang: Yes! First, the rabbit ran very fast. Then he stopped to rest.
Zhang Wei: What happened next?
Ms Wang: Next, the turtle walked slowly but he never stopped. Finally, the turtle won!
Li Ming: The story tells us: Slow and steady wins the race!
Ms Wang: Very good! Now can you tell your own story?
📖 查看翻译

逐句翻译：

王老师：下午好，孩子们！我们今天讲故事吧。

李明：太好了！我喜欢故事！

王老师：很久以前，有一只聪明的兔子和一只慢乌龟……

陈洁：我知道这个故事！兔子和乌龟赛跑了！

王老师：是的！首先，兔子跑得非常快。然后他停下来休息。

张伟：接下来发生了什么？

王老师：接下来，乌龟慢慢走但他从没停下。最后，乌龟赢了！

李明：这个故事告诉我们：稳扎稳打才能赢！

王老师：非常好！现在你能讲你自己的故事吗？

🔑 重点讲解：

1. Once upon a time — "很久以前"，经典故事开头用语。

2. 叙事顺序词：first → then → next → finally。

3. 过去时动词：was (is→was), had (have→had), ran (run→ran), walked, stopped, won (win→won)。

4. Slow and steady wins the race. — "稳扎稳打才能赢。" (谚语)。

5. Tell a story — "讲故事"；用英语讲故事的实用表达。

📖 Reading: The Fox and the Grapes

Once upon a time, a hungry fox saw some grapes.

The grapes were big and purple. They looked delicious!

The fox wanted to eat them. He jumped up, but the grapes were too high.

He jumped again and again, but he still couldn't reach them.

Finally, the fox gave up. As he walked away, he said, "Those grapes are probably sour anyway!"

The lesson: It's easy to dislike what you cannot have.

🔤 重点句型 · Sentence Patterns
Once upon a time, there was/were...
"很久以前，有……" — 经典故事开头。was + 单数名词，were + 复数名词。
✅ Once upon a time, there was a clever rabbit.
✅ Once upon a time, there were three little pigs.
叙事顺序词: first → then → next → finally
用顺序词使故事有条理。
✅ First, the rabbit ran fast.
✅ Then, he stopped to rest.
✅ Next, the turtle kept walking.
✅ Finally, the turtle won.
主语 + 动词过去式 ...
用一般过去时讲述已经发生的故事。
✅ The fox saw some grapes.
✅ He jumped up and down.
✅ He gave up.
✅ He said, "They are sour."
What happened next?
"接下来发生了什么？" — 用于推进故事叙述或提问。
✅ What happened next?
✅ Then what? （口语化）
✅ What happened at the end? （结尾发生了什么？）
📚 语法聚焦 · 一般过去时 & 叙事顺序

1️⃣ 一般过去时 (Simple Past Tense)

表示过去发生的动作或状态。动词用过去式形式。

类型规则示例
规则动词动词 + -edwalk → walked, jump → jumped, stop → stopped
不规则动词特殊变化see → saw, run → ran, win → won
be 动词am/is → was / are → wereThe fox was hungry. / The grapes were purple.
否定句didn't + 动词原形He didn't stop.
疑问句Did + 主语 + 动词原形?Did the fox eat the grapes?

2️⃣ 叙事顺序词 (Sequence Words)

用顺序词让故事更有条理。

顺序词含义示例
Once upon a time很久以前Once upon a time, there was a fox.
First首先First, the rabbit ran very fast.
Then然后Then, he stopped to rest.
Next接下来Next, the turtle walked slowly.
Finally最后Finally, the turtle won.
💡 提示：也可以用 at first (起初), after that (之后), in the end (最终) 来替换。

3️⃣ 常用不规则动词过去式

原形过去式例句
seesawThe fox saw some grapes.
runranThe rabbit ran very fast.
winwonThe turtle won the race.
givegaveThe fox gave up.
saysaidHe said they were sour.
eatateThe fox ate nothing that day.
havehadThey had a race.
telltoldMs Wang told a story.
⚠️ 中式英语纠错：
❌ Once upon a time, there is a rabbit.（讲故事用过去时）
✅ Once upon a time, there was a rabbit.

❌ First, he run very fast.（run 的过去式是 ran）
✅ First, he ran very fast.

❌ He don't stop.（否定过去时用 didn't + 动词原形）
✅ He didn't stop.
📝 三级闯关 · 实战练习

🥉 基础关 · 概念识记

基础 ★☆☆
Once upon a time, there ____ a clever rabbit. (is / was / were)
📝 查看答案
答案：was
解析：讲故事用过去时，a clever rabbit 是单数，所以用 was。
基础 ★☆☆
The turtle walked slowly but he never stopped. ____, he won the race! (First / Then / Finally)
📝 查看答案
答案：Finally
解析：最后乌龟赢了比赛，用 Finally 表示"最后"。
基础 ★☆☆
The fox ____ (see / saw) some grapes and ____ (want / wanted) to eat them.
📝 查看答案
答案：saw; wanted
解析：讲过去的故事用过去式：see→saw, want→wanted。

🥈 提升关 · 语境运用

提升 ★★☆
用正确的过去式填空：First, the rabbit ____ (run) very fast. Then he ____ (stop) to rest.
📝 查看答案
答案：ran; stopped
解析：run→ran（不规则）；stop→stopped（双写 p + ed）。
提升 ★★☆
给故事排序（填 1-4）：
____ The turtle won the race.
____ The rabbit ran very fast.
____ The rabbit stopped to rest.
____ The turtle kept walking.
📝 查看答案
答案：4, 1, 2, 3
解析：首先兔子跑(1)→然后停下(2)→乌龟继续走(3)→最后乌龟赢(4)。
提升 ★★☆
改错：The fox see some grapes and want to eat them.
📝 查看答案
答案：The fox saw some grapes and wanted to eat them.
解析：叙述过去的故事，动词要用过去式：see→saw, want→wanted。

🥇 挑战关 · 综合运用

挑战 ★★★
汉译英：很久以前，有一只饥饿的狐狸。他看到一些葡萄。他跳啊跳，但够不到它们。
📝 查看答案
答案：Once upon a time, there was a hungry fox. He saw some grapes. He jumped and jumped, but he couldn't reach them.
解析：用 Once upon a time 开头，所有动词用过去式。
挑战 ★★★
用正确的过去式补全故事：A little bird ____ (want) to fly. She ____ (try) and ____ (try). Finally, she ____ (learn) to fly. She ____ (is) so happy!
📝 查看答案
答案：wanted; tried; tried; learned; was
解析：want→wanted, try→tried (y→ied), learn→learned, is→was。
挑战 ★★★
选词填空 (once upon a time / first / then / next / finally)：
____ ____, there was a little duck. ____, he could not swim. ____, he tried and tried. ____, his mother helped him. ____, he learned to swim!
📝 查看答案
答案：Once upon a time; First; Then; Next; Finally
解析：故事开头用 Once upon a time，然后按顺序用叙事连接词。

✍️ 微写作：Write a Short Story

用 once upon a time / first / then / next / finally 和一般过去时写一个小故事（50-70 词）。

Once upon a time, there was a little bird. She could not fly.

First, she tried to fly. She jumped up, but she fell down.

Then she tried again. She flapped her wings very hard.

Next, her mother showed her how to fly.

Finally, she learned to fly. She was so happy!

很久以前，有一只小鸟。她不会飞。首先，她试图飞翔。她跳起来，但摔倒了。然后她又试了一次。她用力拍打翅膀。接下来，她的妈妈教她如何飞翔。最后，她学会了飞翔。她太开心了！
