七下 · Unit 11

How was your school trip?

学校旅行 · 过去经历 · 过去式

📖 话题：School trip & Past events 💬 功能：Describe people's looks · Ask "How was your school trip?" 📚 语法：Simple past tense (was/were) · Regular & irregular past verbs 📝 写作：用过去时描述一次学校旅行

🎯 我能做到

📖 课文精讲 · Describing people
场景：Tom、Sarah 和老师在学校组织的农场展览后回忆过去的经历。
Teacher: How was your school trip, everyone?
Tom: It was great! I went to the farm with my classmates.
Teacher: What did you do there?
Tom: I rode a horse and milked a cow. It was so much fun!
Sarah: I went to the museum. I saw some old things. They were cool.
Teacher: Did you have a good time?
Tom: Yes, I did. I learned a lot about farming!
Sarah: Well... I got lost in the museum. It was a little scary!
📖 查看翻译

逐句翻译：

Sarah: Mike，你认识新来的同学 David 吗？

Mike: 不认识。他长什么样？

Sarah: 嗯，他又高又瘦。他有一头棕色的卷发。

Mike: 他戴眼镜吗？

Sarah: 是的，他戴。他还有一张圆脸。他经常穿蓝色 T 恤。

Mike: 哦，我想我看到他了！他坐在前排。他是中等身高吗？

Sarah: 不，他挺高的。他大概 1.8 米。

Mike: 知道了。我待会儿去跟他打个招呼。

🔑 重点讲解：

1. How was your school trip? → "他长什么样？" 固定句型，询问外貌。

2. He's tall and thin. → 用 be + 形容词描述整体形象（身高、体型）。

3. He has curly brown hair. → 用 has/have 描述具体特征（头发、面部特征）。

4. Does he wear glasses? → 用 wear 表示"穿戴（眼镜、衣服等）"。

📖 Reading: A Great Day at the Farm

Last Monday, our class went to a farm for a school trip. The weather was warm and sunny.

We arrived at the farm at nine o'clock. First, my friend Mike and I rode horses. It was exciting!

Then we milked cows and fed chickens. The farmer taught us how to grow vegetables.

We also picked some apples and tomatoes. They were very fresh and delicious.

At noon we had lunch on the grass. I ate a big sandwich and drank some orange juice.

In the afternoon we visited the cowshed and took many photos. I learned a lot about farm life.

I think the school trip was really wonderful. I want to go there again!

📘 went /went/ v. (go的过去式) 去 📘 milk /mɪlk/ v. 撠奶 📘 ride /raɪd/ v. 骑（马、自行车） 📘 feed /fiːd/ v. 喂食 📘 farm /fɑːrm/ n. 农场 📘 heavy /ˈhevi/ adj. 重的；胖的 📘 sneakers /ˈsniːkərz/ n. 运动鞋 📘 popular /ˈpɒpjʊlər/ adj. 受欢迎的 📘 friendly /ˈfrendli/ adj. 友好的

🔤 重点句型 · Sentence Patterns
What does he/she look like?
询问人的外貌特征。
✅ What does he look like?
→ He's tall and thin. He has short curly hair.
✅ What do they look like?
→ They're of medium height.
He is + (height / build) / He has + (hair / face)
be + 形容词描述整体（高矮胖瘦）
has/have + 名词描述具体特征（头发、眼睛等）。
✅ He is tall / short / of medium height / thin / heavy.
✅ She has long black hair / big eyes / a round face.
✅ They have curly hair / glasses.
Does he/she have ...? / Does he/she wear ...?
一般疑问句，询问是否有某个特征或是否穿戴某物。
✅ Does he have a beard? — Yes, he does. / No, he doesn't.
✅ Does she wear glasses? — Yes, she does. / No, she doesn't.
✅ Do they have curly hair? — Yes, they do.
描述顺序：整体 → 局部 → 具体细节
描述一个人时，先整体再局部：
身高→体型→头发→五官→着装。
✅ She is of medium height, a little thin.
✅ She has long straight hair and a small nose.
✅ She usually wears a red dress.
📚 语法聚焦 · Describing Appearance

1️⃣ be 描述 vs. have 描述

be + 形容词have/has + 名词
用途整体特征（身高、体型、年龄）具体特征（头发、眼睛、胡须）
身高He is tall.——
体型She is thin.——
头发——He has curly hair.
脸型——She has a round face.
眼镜——He wears glasses.

2️⃣ 形容词顺序修饰名词

用多个形容词修饰名词时顺序：大小/长短 → 形状 → 颜色

正确顺序示例
大小/长短 + 形状 + 颜色 + 名词long straight brown hair
short curly blonde hair
big round black eyes
⚠️ 中式英语纠错：
❌ He is tall and has long hair. ✅ （正确）

❌ He has hair long black.（形容词顺序错误）
✅ He has long black hair.

❌ She is has long hair.（is 和 has 重复）
✅ She has long hair.
📝 三级闯关 · 实战练习

🥉 基础关 · 概念识记

基础 ★☆☆
How ____ your school trip? — It was great! (is / was / were)
📝 查看答案
答案：was
解析：How was...? 询问对某事的看法，回答用 It was...
基础 ★☆☆
I ____ to the farm yesterday. (go / goes / went)
📝 查看答案
答案：went
解析：yesterday 表示过去，用 go 的过去式 went。
基础 ★☆☆
Did you ____ a horse? — Yes, I did. (rode / ride / riding)
📝 查看答案
答案：ride
解析：Did 后动词用原形。

🥈 提升关 · 语境运用

提升 ★★☆
改错：I didn't went to the museum yesterday.
📝 查看答案
答案：I didn't go to the museum yesterday.
解析：didn't 后动词用原形 go，不能用过去式 went。
提升 ★★☆
连词成句：did / you / what / do / on / farm / the
📝 查看答案
答案：What did you do on the farm?
解析：疑问句：What + did + 主语 + do + 其他？
提升 ★★☆
用过去式填空：We ____ (see) a lot of cows and ____ (feed) the chickens.
📝 查看答案
答案：saw; fed
解析：see→saw（不规则），feed→fed（不规则）。

🥇 挑战关 · 综合运用

挑战 ★★★
汉译英：你的学校旅行怎么样？太棒了！我骑了马还摘了苹果。
📝 查看答案
答案：How was your school trip? It was excellent! I rode a horse and picked apples.
解析：综合过去时表达：was 描述感受，过去式动词描述具体活动。
挑战 ★★★
编写对话：
A: ____ was your ____?
B: It was ____.
A: What ____ you ____?
B: I ____ a horse and ____ a cow.
📝 查看答案
答案：How; trip; wonderful; did; do; rode; milked
解析：茶茶式提问：How was...? What did you do? 逐步询问细节。

✍️ 微写作：Describe a friend

选一位你的朋友或家人，用英语写一段描述其外貌的话。注意先用 be 描述整体，再用 has 描述细节。

Last Friday, I went on a school trip to the science museum with my classmates.

The bus ride was long but very interesting. We saw many tall buildings on the way.

At the museum, I saw a robot that could play chess. It was so cool!

I also tried a 3D game. The experience was amazing and I learned a lot.

We took many photos and bought some souvenirs. The gift shop was not expensive.

I think the trip was really educational and fun. I want to visit again.

上周五，我和同学们去科学博物馆参观。座公共汽车很长时间但很有趣。我们在路上看到了许多高楼。在博物馆里，我看到了一个会下棋的机器人，非常酷！我还尝试了 3D 游戏，那种体验太惊人了，我学到了很多。我们拍了很多照片，买了纪念品。禮品店也不贵。我觉得这次旅行既有教育意义又很有趣，我还想再去一次。
