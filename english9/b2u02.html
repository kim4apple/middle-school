A Look into the Past

回顾历史 · 情态动词推测 · 历史事件

📖 话题：History and historical events💬 功能：Talk about past events, express speculation📚 语法：情态动词推测 (must/can't/might/could/may) · 过去时复习📝 写作：介绍一个历史事件

🎯 我能做到

📖 课文精讲 · A Visit to the Museum
场景：Tom、Li Ming 和 Sarah 正在参观历史博物馆，他们对展出的文物和考古发现充满了好奇和推测。
Tom:Look at this ancient bronze vessel! It must be over two thousand years old.
Li Ming:You're right. It could have been used for cooking or ceremonies. The patterns are amazing.
Sarah:Look at this display about the Terracotta Army. The emperor must have spent years building his tomb.
Tom:I can't believe it! These artifacts can't be real — they look so well preserved.
Li Ming:Actually, they are real. Archaeologists have discovered thousands of them in Xi'an.
Sarah:What's that broken pottery over there? It might have belonged to an ancient civilization.
Tom:This is fascinating. I wonder what life was like in those times. People must have lived very differently.
Li Ming:Yes, but their discoveries help us understand our own history. The past is a treasure.
📖 查看翻译

Tom： 看这个古代青铜器！这一定有两千多年了。

李明： 你说得对。它可能被用来做饭或举行仪式。那些图案太棒了。

Sarah： 看这个关于兵马俑的展览。皇帝一定花了多年修建他的陵墓。

Tom： 真不敢相信！这些文物不可能是真的——它们看起来保存得太好了。

李明： 其实它们是真的。考古学家已经在西安发现了数千件。

Sarah： 那边那个破陶器是什么？它可能属于某个古代文明。

Tom： 太迷人了。我想知道那些时代的生活是什么样的。人们一定生活得非常不同。

李明： 是的，但他们的发现帮助我们理解自己的历史。过去是一笔财富。

🔑 重点讲解：

must be + 名词/形容词 — 肯定推测 — It must be over two thousand years old.

can't be + 名词/形容词 — 否定推测 — These artifacts can't be real.

might / may / could + 动词原形 — 可能推测 — It might have belonged to an ancient civilization.

must / can't + have + 过去分词 — 对过去的推测 — The emperor must have spent years building his tomb.

🔤 重点句型 · Key Sentence Patterns
must be + 名词/形容词
肯定推测，表示「一定是」。
It must be very old. / This must be the right place.
can't be + 名词/形容词
否定推测，表示「不可能是」。
That can't be true. / He can't be the thief.
might / may / could + 动词原形
可能推测，表示「也许/可能」。
It might belong to the king. / She could be at home.
must / can't + have + 过去分词
对过去的推测，表示「一定已经/不可能已经」。
They must have arrived already. / He can't have forgotten.
📚 语法聚焦 · 情态动词推测

情态动词 + 动词原形（对现在/将来的推测）

情态动词程度含义例句
must95% 肯定一定；肯定This must be a Ming Dynasty vase.
may50% 可能也许；可能She may know the answer.
might30% 可能也许（语气弱）He might be at the museum.
could40% 可能可能；可以It could be a valuable artifact.
can't95% 否定不可能That can't be a real dinosaur bone.

情态动词 + have + 过去分词（对过去的推测）

结构含义例句
must have done一定已经（肯定推测）They must have lived here for centuries.
may/might have done也许已经（可能推测）She might have seen the artifact before.
could have done本来可能（过去可能但未发生）He could have left earlier.
can't have done不可能已经（否定推测）They can't have destroyed the evidence.

一般过去时 vs 现在完成时

时态用法例句
一般过去时过去某个时间发生的动作，与现在无关The emperor built the Great Wall.
现在完成时过去发生的动作与现在有联系Archaeologists have discovered many tombs.
一般过去时 + 时间点有明确的过去时间The dynasty fell in 1644.
现在完成时 + 时间段持续到现在的状态This tradition has lasted for centuries.

must 表示肯定推测，can't 表示否定推测（不是 mustn't）。

might 比 may 语气更弱，可能性更低。

对已经发生的事情推测用 must have done / can't have done。

Those who do not learn history are doomed to repeat it. 以史为鉴，可以知兴替。

📖 阅读专项 · Secrets of the Past

Have you ever wondered what secrets lie beneath the ground? Archaeology is the science that helps us uncover these mysteries. Every discovery tells a story about how people lived thousands of years ago.

One of the most amazing discoveries in China is the Terracotta Army. In 1974, farmers were digging a well when they found clay fragments. Archaeologists soon realized that they had uncovered one of the greatest archaeological sites in the world — the tomb of Emperor Qin Shi Huang. The army consists of thousands of life-sized soldiers, each with a unique face. The emperor must have spent decades building his tomb. These soldiers were meant to protect him in the afterlife.

Another incredible discovery is the ancient city of Pompeii in Italy. In 79 AD, Mount Vesuvius erupted and buried the city under ash. For centuries, the city lay hidden. When archaeologists excavated the site, they found buildings, paintings, and even the remains of people exactly as they had been when the disaster struck. The ash had preserved everything. It's as if time had stopped.

In Egypt, the discovery of Tutankhamun's tomb in 1922 revealed treasure beyond imagination. The tomb had remained untouched for over 3,000 years. Inside, archaeologists found gold, jewelry, furniture, and the king's mummy. The treasures must have belonged to one of the wealthiest kings of ancient Egypt.

These discoveries remind us that history is not just about the past. It connects us to our ancestors and helps us understand who we are. Every artifact, every ruin, and every fossil holds a secret waiting to be discovered. What secrets might still be hidden beneath our feet?

🏆 中考题型专练 · 语法选择 — 情态动词推测

阅读短文，选择最佳答案。

Last week, our class visited the History Museum. The guide told us about an ancient tomb that archaeologists 1 (discover / have discovered) recently. "This 2 be one of the most important finds of the century," she said. Inside the tomb, there was a bronze mirror. It 3 (might have belonged / must belong) to a noblewoman because of the fine craftsmanship. Some pottery was broken, so it 4 (can't have been / must have been) damaged during the excavation. The guide also showed us a jade bracelet that 5 be over 3,000 years old. Our ancestors 6 (must have been / can't have been) very skilled at making such beautiful objects. I have never seen anything so amazing!

1 A. discover B. have discovered C. discovered D. had discovered
2 A. can't B. must C. might D. could
3 A. might have belonged B. must belong C. can't have belonged D. must have belonged
4 A. can't have been B. must have been C. might have been D. could be
5 A. must B. can't C. may D. should
6 A. must have been B. can't have been C. might have been D. could be
答案
1. B；2. B；3. A；4. A；5. C；6. A
✍️ 写作进阶 · 介绍一个历史事件
📋 写作题目
选择一个你感兴趣的历史事件或人物 ("An Important Event in History" 或 "A Historical Figure I Admire")，写一篇 80-100 词的短文。要求：使用情态动词推测，对比过去与现在的理解，表达你的感受。
开头：引出历史事件/人物 — One of the most important events in history was ...
主体1：描述事件/人物的基本事实 (过去时)
主体2：用情态动词推测当时的情况 (must have / might have)
主体3：告诉我们今天可以学到什么
结尾：总结感受 — I believe / I think ...
A Historical Figure I Admire I admire Sima Qian, a great historian from the Han Dynasty. He wrote the famous book "Records of the Grand Historian." Life in ancient times must have been difficult for him. After offending the emperor, he could have given up, but he didn't. He must have been very determined to finish his work in prison. His book has influenced Chinese history for over two thousand years. We can learn from his courage that no matter what happens, we should never give up. I think he is one of the greatest writers in Chinese history.
我敬仰的历史人物 我敬仰司马迁，汉朝的一位伟大史学家。他写了著名的《史记》。古代的生活对他来说一定很艰难。触怒皇帝后，他本可以放弃，但他没有。他一定非常坚定，在狱中完成了自己的著作。他的书影响中国历史两千多年。我们可以从他的勇气中学到：无论发生什么，都不应该放弃。我认为他是中国历史上最伟大的作家之一。

History Detective — Research a Discovery
研究一个考古发现或历史遗址，准备一份简短的报告，向全班展示你的发现。

#评价标准

3.内容具体，有真实细节支撑
4.结构清晰，有开头、主体、结尾
5.能阐述该发现对今天的意义
