七下 · Unit 3

Keep Fit

运动与健康 · 频率副词 · How often

📖 话题：Sports and healthy lifestyle 💬 功能：Talk about exercise habits & frequency 📚 语法：频率副词 (always/usually/often/sometimes/never) · How often 📝 写作：我的运动习惯

🎯 我能做到

📖 课文精讲 · In the PE Class
场景：体育课上，同学们在聊运动习惯。
Wang Fang: Li Ming, you run very fast! How often do you run?
Li Ming: I run every morning. I always run for 30 minutes.
Wang Fang: That's great! I usually play badminton. Sometimes I swim.
Li Ming: How often do you swim?
Wang Fang: I swim twice a week. My brother plays football every weekend.
Li Ming: Good! Sports are good for our health.
Wang Fang: Yes! Let's keep fit together!
Li Ming: I agree. A healthy body is important!
📖 查看翻译

逐句翻译：

王芳：李明，你跑得好快！你多久跑一次步？

李明：我每天早上都跑。我总是跑 30 分钟。

王芳：太好了！我通常打羽毛球。有时我游泳。

李明：你多久游一次泳？

王芳：我每周游两次。我哥哥每周都踢足球。

李明：好！运动对……有益健康。

王芳：是的！我们一起保持健康吧！

李明：我同意。健康的身体很重要！

🔑 重点讲解：

1. How often do you run? → How often 问频率（多久一次）。

2. always / usually / sometimes → 频率副词，放在实义动词之前、be 动词之后。

3. twice a week → "每周两次"，频率表达：次数 + a + 时间单位。

4. be good for → "对……有益"；keep fit → "保持健康"。

📖 Reading: Healthy Habits for Everyone

Do you want to keep fit? Here are some tips.

First, do sports regularly. You can run, swim or play ball games. Exercise makes you strong.

Second, eat healthy food. Eat more vegetables and fruit. Don't eat too much junk food.

Third, get enough sleep. Go to bed early and get up early.

Fourth, drink lots of water. Water is good for your body.

Remember: A healthy lifestyle makes a happy life!

🔤 重点句型 · Sentence Patterns
How often do you + 动词原形?
询问频率（多久一次）。
✅ How often do you run?
✅ How often does she swim?
➡ 回答：I run every day. / I swim twice a week.
频率副词 + 动词
频率副词的句中位置：实义动词前，be 动词后。
✅ I always get up early.
✅ He usually plays football.
✅ She often reads books.
✅ They sometimes swim.
✅ I never eat junk food.
every + 时间 / 次数 + a + 时间
频率表达方式汇总。
✅ every day / every morning / every weekend
✅ once a week 每周一次
✅ twice a week 每周两次
✅ three times a month 每月三次
be good for / be bad for
表达"对……有益/有害"。
✅ Exercise is good for your health.
✅ Junk food is bad for your body.
✅ Running is good for your heart.
➡ keep fit = 保持健康
📚 语法聚焦 · Frequency Adverbs & How often

1️⃣ 频率副词用法对比

副词频率例句中文
always100%I always drink water.总是
usually80%I usually run.通常
often60%I often play basketball.经常
sometimes40%Sometimes I swim.有时
never0%I never eat junk food.从不
💡 注意：频率副词放在实义动词之前，be 动词之后。
✅ I always run. / He is always happy.
❌ I run always. / He is happy always.

2️⃣ How often 问句

结构例句回答
How often + do/does + 主语 + 动词原形?How often do you exercise?Every day.
How often does he play football?Twice a week.
How often do you eat fruit?Every day.

3️⃣ 频率表达汇总

表达方式结构举例
every + 时间单位every + day/morning/week/weekendevery day
次数 + a + 时间单位once/twice/三次以上用基数词+times + a + week/monththree times a week
频率副词always/usually/often/sometimes/neverI often run.
⚠️ 中式英语纠错：
❌ I every day run.（频率副词不能放在主语和动词之间）
✅ I run every day.

❌ He usually is happy.（频率副词应放在 be 动词之后）
✅ He is usually happy.

❌ I go sometimes swimming.（sometimes 不能随便放）
✅ Sometimes I go swimming. / I sometimes go swimming.
📝 三级闯关 · 实战练习

🥉 基础关 · 概念识记

基础 ★☆☆
____ do you go swimming? (How often / How long / How far)
📝 查看答案
答案：How often
解析：问频率用 How often。
基础 ★☆☆
I ____ (总是) get up at 6:00 every morning.
📝 查看答案
答案：always
解析："总是" = always。
基础 ★☆☆
She swims ____ (每周两次).
📝 查看答案
答案：twice a week
解析："每周两次" = twice a week。
基础 ★☆☆
Exercise is ____ for your health. (good / bad)
📝 查看答案
答案：good
解析：be good for = 对……有益。

🥈 提升关 · 语境运用

提升 ★★☆
改错：He always is late for school.
📝 查看答案
答案：He is always late for school.
解析：频率副词放在 be 动词之后。
提升 ★★☆
连词成句：often / how / do / you / exercise / ?
📝 查看答案
答案：How often do you exercise?
解析：How often + do + 主语 + 动词原形 + ?
提升 ★★☆
She always gets up early. (改为否定句)
📝 查看答案
答案：She doesn't always get up early.
解析：第三人称用 doesn't + 动词原形。

🥇 挑战关 · 综合运用

挑战 ★★★
汉译英：你多久游一次泳？我每周游三次。游泳对健康有益。
📝 查看答案
答案：How often do you swim? I swim three times a week. Swimming is good for your health.
解析：How often 问频率，three times a week，be good for 对……有益。
挑战 ★★★
补全对话：
A: ____ do you play basketball?
B: I play it ____ (每周两次).
A: Do you ____ (总是) win?
B: Not always. Sometimes I lose.
📝 查看答案
答案：How often; twice a week; always
解析：How often 问频率，twice a week，always 总是。
挑战 ★★★
用频率副词填空（always / usually / often / sometimes / never）：
1. I ____ eat junk food. It's bad for health.
2. He ____ gets up at 6:00. He is never late.
3. We ____ play football on weekends, but not every weekend.
📝 查看答案
答案：1. never 2. always 3. sometimes
解析：never 从不；always 总是；sometimes 有时。
