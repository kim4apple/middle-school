How often do you exercise?

生活习惯

📖 话题：Daily Habits & Free Time Activities💬 功能：Talk about how often you do things📚 语法：频率副词（always/usually/often/sometimes/never）· How often 疑问句📝 写作：My Healthy Habit Report

🎯 我能做到

📖 课文精讲 · How often do you exercise?
场景：老师让同学们互相采访各自的课外活动频率。
Claire:Hi, Tom! How often do you exercise?
Tom:I exercise every day. I run for 30 minutes in the morning.
Claire:Great! How often do you watch TV?
Tom:I hardly ever watch TV. I'm too busy with homework.
Claire:What about reading?
Tom:I often read books before bed. I read about two books a month.
Claire:You have good habits! I always stay up late. I need to change.
Tom:How often do you stay up late?
Claire:Almost every day. I'm going to go to bed earlier from next week.
📖 查看翻译

Claire: 嗨 Tom！你多久锻炼一次？

Tom: 我每天锻炼。早上跑 30 分钟步。

Claire: 很好！你看电视的频率呢？

Tom: 我几乎不看电视，作业太忙了。

Claire: 那阅读呢？

Tom: 我经常睡前看书，一个月大概读两本。

Claire: 你习惯真好！我总是熬夜，得改改。

Tom: 你多久熬夜一次？

Claire: 几乎每天都熬。我打算下周起早点睡。

🔑 重点讲解：

1. How often do you exercise? → How often 问频率，回答用频率副词或次数表达。

2. 频率副词顺序：always(100%) > usually(80%) > often(60%) > sometimes(40%) > hardly ever(5%) > never(0%)

3. I hardly ever watch TV. → hardly ever 表示"几乎不"，含否定意义。

4. I read about two books a month. → 次数 + a + 时间单位：once a week / twice a month / three times a year

🔤 重点句型 · Sentence Patterns
How often + 一般疑问句?
询问频率
How often do you exercise? — Every day.
频率副词 + 实义动词
频率副词放在行为动词前
I always get up early. / She hardly ever watches TV.
次数 + a + 时间单位
表达频率数量
Once a week / Three times a month.
📚 语法聚焦 · 频率副词 & How often 疑问句

频率副词用法

副词频率示例
always100%I always brush my teeth.
usually80%I usually get up at 6:30.
often60%I often go to the park.
sometimes40%I sometimes watch TV.
hardly ever5%I hardly ever eat junk food.
never0%I never smoke.

How often 疑问句结构

结构示例
How often + do/does + 主语 + 动词?How often do you read books?
How often + be + 主语 + 形容词?How often are you late for school?

❌ I every day exercise.（频率词不放在句首/句末修饰动词）

✅ I exercise every day.

❌ How often you go shopping?（疑问句需助动词 do/does）

✅ How often do you go shopping?

We did a survey on teen health habits. Here are the results.

Only 25% of teens exercise every day. Most teens exercise once or twice a week. 45% of teens spend more than two hours on screens every day.

Teens who exercise more sleep better and feel happier. Experts say teens should exercise at least three times a week.

Good habits start small. Try to walk for 20 minutes every day. Your body and mind will thank you!

🏆 中考题型专练 · 语法填空

用所给词的适当形式填空。

My aunt is a doctor. She works very hard. She (1)____ (usual) gets up at 6 a.m. and goes running. She (2)____ (exercise) for 30 minutes every morning. She (3)____ (hard) ever stays up late. She says good habits help her (4)____ (stay) healthy. I think I (5)____ (should) learn from her.

答案
(1) usually；(2) exercises；(3) hardly；(4) stay；(5) should
✍️ 写作进阶 · My Healthy Habit Report
📋 写作题目
请写一份"My Healthy Habit Report"，描述你一周的健康习惯，至少用到 4 个频率副词。
开头：引出话题——我的一周习惯。
主体：分饮食、运动、睡眠三方面描述。
结尾：总结哪些习惯好，哪些需要改进。
Let me report my healthy habits. I always get up at 6:30 and go to bed before 10 p.m. I usually eat three meals a day. I often eat fruit and vegetables. I exercise three times a week. But I hardly ever drink milk. I'm going to drink milk every day from now on.
我来汇报一下我的健康习惯。我总是 6:30 起床，晚上 10 点前睡觉。我通常一日三餐。我经常吃水果蔬菜，每周锻炼三次。但我几乎不喝牛奶。我打算从今以后每天喝牛奶。
🎯 Project · Class Habit Survey
Class Habit Survey
在班里做一个小调查，收集 5 位同学的运动、阅读和屏幕时间数据，用英语写一份调查报告。
1设计 3 个频率问题（运动/阅读/屏幕时间）。
2采访 5 位同学，记录数据。
3计算百分比并写报告。
4在班里展示调查结果。
#评价标准
1.问题设计合理
2.数据准确完整
3.报告使用了频率副词和百分比表达
4.展示清晰
