Amazing Plants and Animals

动植物 · 最高级 · 被动语态入门

📖 话题：Nature and living things💬 功能：Describe plants/animals, give facts📚 语法：形容词/副词最高级 · 被动语态 is/are + past participle📝 写作：介绍一种动物

🎯 我能做到

📖 课文精讲 · Amazing Animals and Plants
场景：几个同学在自然历史博物馆参观。
Li Ming:Wow, look at this blue whale! It's the biggest animal in the world.
Emma:Yes, it's the largest animal that has ever lived on Earth. It can grow up to 30 meters long.
Li Ming:And the giraffe is the tallest land animal. What's the fastest animal on land?
Emma:The cheetah! It's the fastest land animal. It can run at 120 kilometers per hour.
Li Ming:Amazing! What about plants? Which plant is the tallest?
Emma:The tallest trees are redwoods. They are found in California. Some are over 100 meters tall.
Li Ming:I'd love to visit a rainforest. I hear the Amazon is the most amazing place.
Emma:Right! Many rare plants are grown there. It's one of the most important ecosystems on Earth.
📖 查看翻译

Li Ming: 哇，看这只蓝鲸！它是世界上最大的动物。

Emma: 是的，它是地球上有史以来最大的动物。可以长到 30 米长。

Li Ming: 长颈鹿是最高的陆地动物。陆地上最快的动物是什么？

Emma: 猎豹！它是跑得最快的陆地动物。时速可达 120 公里。

Li Ming: 太神奇了！那植物呢？哪种植物最高？

Emma: 最高的树是红杉。它们在加利福尼亚被发现。有些超过 100 米高。

Li Ming: 我好想去热带雨林看看。听说亚马逊是最神奇的地方。

Emma: 对！很多稀有植物都在那里生长。它是地球上最重要的生态系统之一。

🔑 重点讲解：

1. It's the biggest animal in the world. → 最高级：the + 最高级 + 范围

2. What's the fastest animal on land? → What/Which + be + the + 最高级? 提问最高级

3. They are found in California. → 被动语态：are + found (过去分词)，表"被发现于"

4. Many rare plants are grown there. → 被动语态：are + grown (过去分词)，表"被种植"

5. one of the most important ecosystems → one of the + 最高级 + 复数名词

🔤 重点句型 · Sentence Patterns

📚 语法聚焦 · 最高级 & 被动语态入门

一、形容词/副词最高级

规则构成方法示例
单音节the + adj./adv. + esttall → the tallest, fast → the fastest
以 e 结尾the + adj./adv. + stlarge → the largest, late → the latest
辅音+y变 y 为 i + esthappy → the happiest, early → the earliest
重读闭音节双写尾字母 + estbig → the biggest, hot → the hottest
多音节the most + 原级amazing → the most amazing, beautiful → the most beautiful
不规则特殊变化good → the best, bad → the worst, many → the most

二、最高级常用句型

句型示例
the + 最高级 + 范围The cheetah is the fastest animal on land.
one of the + 最高级 + 复数It is one of the rarest plants in the world.
Which/Who + be + the + 最高级?Which animal is the tallest?

三、被动语态入门（一般现在时）

主动语态被动语态
People find this plant in Africa.This plant is found in Africa.
Farmers grow tea in China.Tea is grown in China.
People protect these animals.These animals are protected.

结构：主语 + is/are + 过去分词 (+ by + 执行者)

❌ Which is the most biggest animal?（big 是单音节，不加 most）

✅ Which is the biggest animal?

❌ Tea grows in China.（表被动应用 is grown，不是主动 grows）

✅ Tea is grown in China.

❌ Rare plants are grow here.（过去分词不用原形）

✅ Rare plants are grown here.

📖 阅读专项 · The Amazon Rainforest

The Amazon rainforest is one of the most amazing places on Earth. It is the largest rainforest in the world. It covers about 5.5 million square kilometers.

The Amazon is home to millions of species. It has the highest biodiversity of any place on Earth. Some of the most beautiful birds, such as toucans and parrots, are found there. Rare plants are grown in the forest. Many of these plants are used for medicine.

The Amazon is often called "the lungs of the Earth" because it produces a large amount of oxygen. Many different animals live here, including jaguars, monkeys, and snakes.

However, the rainforest is in danger. Many trees are cut down every year. We need to protect this important ecosystem. Conservation efforts are needed to save the Amazon for future generations.

🏆 中考题型专练 · 语法填空

There are many (1)____ (amaze) animals in the world. The cheetah is the (2)____ (fast) land animal. The blue whale is the (3)____ (big) of all. Some animals (4)____ (find) only in one place. ✍️ 写作进阶 · Fact File About an Animal
📋 写作题目
请写一篇短文"Fact File: My Favorite Animal"，介绍一种你最喜欢的动物。包括名称、外形、栖息地、食物、有趣事实和保护现状。
开头：动物名称和基本介绍。
中间：外形特征、栖息地、食物。
结尾：有趣事实和保护现状。
Fact File: Giant Panda

Giant pandas are one of the most famous animals in the world. They are only found in China.

Pandas have black and white fur. They look very cute. Most pandas are found in the bamboo forests of Sichuan, Shaanxi, and Gansu. They eat about 12–38 kg of bamboo every day!

Giant pandas are the rarest bears in the world. They are endangered and are protected by Chinese law. Conservation efforts have helped increase their population. I hope these amazing animals will always be with us.
动物档案：大熊猫

大熊猫是世界上最著名的动物之一。它们只在中国被发现。

熊猫有黑白相间的皮毛，看起来非常可爱。大多数熊猫生活在四川、陕西和甘肃的竹林里。它们每天吃大约 12–38 公斤竹子！

大熊猫是世界上最稀有的熊类。它们是濒危动物，受中国法律保护。保护措施帮助增加了它们的数量。希望这些神奇的动物能永远陪伴我们。
