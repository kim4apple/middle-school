七下 · Unit 7

A Day to Remember

过去经历 · 一般过去时 · 特殊日子

📖 话题：Memorable days and past experiences 💬 功能：Talk about past events & special days 📚 语法：一般过去时 (was/were · 规则动词过去式) 📝 写作：描述难忘的一天

🎯 我能做到

📖 课文精讲 · How was your weekend?
场景：周一早上，李明和王芳在学校聊周末经历。
Wang Fang: How was your weekend, Li Ming?
Li Ming: It was great! I visited my grandparents with my family.
Wang Fang: Sounds fun! What did you do there?
Li Ming: We cooked together and played chess. My grandpa told me stories.
Wang Fang: That was a wonderful day. My weekend was exciting too!
Li Ming: What did you do?
Wang Fang: I went to the amusement park with my friends. We played games and watched a show.
Li Ming: Wow! That sounds amazing. I want to go there next time!
📖 查看翻译

逐句翻译：

王芳：你周末怎么样，李明？

李明：太棒了！我和家人去拜访了我的祖父母。

王芳：听起来很有趣！你们做了什么？

李明：我们一起做饭、下棋。我爷爷给我讲故事。

王芳：那是一个精彩的一天。我的周末也很令人兴奋！

李明：你做了什么？

王芳：我和朋友去了游乐园。我们玩了游戏、看了表演。

李明：哇！听起来太棒了。下次我也想去！

🔑 重点讲解：

1. How was your weekend? → "你周末怎么样？" was 是 is 的过去式。

2. was/were → be 动词的过去式。I/He/She/It + was，You/We/They + were。

3. 规则动词过去式 → visit→visited, play→played, cook→cooked, watch→watched。

4. 不规则动词 → tell→told, go→went, do→did。

5. last weekend / yesterday / last night → 过去时间状语。

📖 Reading: An Unforgettable Day

Last Sunday was a special day for me. It was my first time to fly a kite!

In the morning, my father and I went to the park. The weather was sunny but a little windy — perfect for flying kites!

My father showed me how to fly a kite. At first, the kite didn't fly. I tried again and again.

Finally, the kite flew high in the sky! I was so happy and excited.

I learned an important lesson: Never give up!

📘 unforgettable /ˌʌnfərˈɡetəbl/ adj. 难忘的 📘 kite /kaɪt/ n. 风筝 📘 show /ʃoʊ/ v. 展示 (showed) 📘 try /traɪ/ v. 尝试 (tried) 📘 lesson /ˈlesn/ n. 教训；课

🔤 重点句型 · Sentence Patterns
How was your ...?
"你的……怎么样？" 用 was (is/am 过去式) 询问过去的情况。
✅ How was your weekend?
✅ How was your day?
✅ How was the party?
➡ 回答：It was great / wonderful / exciting!
What did you do?
"你做了什么？" did 帮助构成一般过去时的疑问句，后面动词用原形。
✅ What did you do yesterday?
✅ What did she do last night?
✅ What did they do last weekend?
主语 + 动词过去式
一般过去时陈述句：主语 + 动词过去式 + 其他。
✅ I visited my grandparents.
✅ We played chess.
✅ She watched a movie.
✅ He went to the park.
过去时间状语
常用过去时间状语，帮助判断时态。
✅ yesterday 昨天
✅ last night 昨晚
✅ last weekend 上周末
✅ last week 上周
✅ ... ago ……前 (two days ago)
📚 语法聚焦 · 一般过去时 (Simple Past Tense)

1️⃣ 一般过去时概述

表示过去某个时间发生的事，常与过去时间状语连用（yesterday, last night/week, ... ago）。

2️⃣ Be 动词过去式: was / were

主语be 过去式例句
I / He / She / ItwasI was happy yesterday.
You / We / TheywereThey were at the park last Sunday.

3️⃣ 规则动词过去式变化

规则原形过去式
直接 + edplayplayed
以 e 结尾 + dlikeliked
辅音 + y → iedtrytried
重读闭音节，双写末字母 + edstopstopped

4️⃣ 常见不规则动词

原形过去式例句
gowentI went to the park.
dodidI did my homework.
havehadWe had a great time.
saysaidHe said hello to me.
telltoldShe told me a story.

5️⃣ 一般疑问句与否定句

一般疑问句：Did + 主语 + 动词原形？

否定句：主语 + didn't + 动词原形

句式结构例句
肯定句主语 + 动词过去式I played chess.
否定句主语 + didn't + 动词原形I didn't play chess.
一般疑问句Did + 主语 + 动词原形?Did you play chess?
⚠️ 中式英语纠错：
❌ I am go to park yesterday.（be + 原形 + 过去时间）
✅ I went to the park yesterday.

❌ She didn't went to school.（didn't 后动词用原形）
✅ She didn't go to school.

❌ I visit my grandparents yesterday.（过去时间用过去式）
✅ I visited my grandparents yesterday.
📝 三级闯关 · 实战练习

🥉 基础关 · 概念识记

基础 ★☆☆
How ____ (was / were) your weekend? — It was great!
📝 查看答案
答案：was
解析：your weekend 是第三人称单数，用 was。
基础 ★☆☆
They ____ (was / were) at the park yesterday.
📝 查看答案
答案：were
解析：They 是复数主语，用 were。
基础 ★☆☆
I ____ (play) basketball with my friends yesterday.
📝 查看答案
答案：played
解析：yesterday 提示用过去式，play → played。
基础 ★☆☆
She ____ (go) to the park last Sunday.
📝 查看答案
答案：went
解析：go → went (不规则动词变化)。

🥈 提升关 · 语境运用

提升 ★★☆
改错：What did you did yesterday?
📝 查看答案
答案：What did you do yesterday?
解析：did 已经是助动词，后面的动词用原形 do。
提升 ★★☆
连词成句：last / visited / I / my / weekend / grandparents
📝 查看答案
答案：I visited my grandparents last weekend.
解析：主语 + 动词过去式 + 其他 + 过去时间状语。
提升 ★★☆
She cooked dinner. (改为否定句)
📝 查看答案
答案：She didn't cook dinner.
解析：否定句用 didn't + 动词原形。

🥇 挑战关 · 综合运用

挑战 ★★★
汉译英：你昨天做了什么？我和朋友去了游乐园。我们玩得很开心！
📝 查看答案
答案：What did you do yesterday? I went to the amusement park with my friends. We had a great time!
解析：What did you do? 问过去的事；go → went；have → had。
挑战 ★★★
补全对话：
A: How ____ your last weekend?
B: It ____ wonderful! I ____ (visit) the museum.
A: What ____ you ____ (do) there?
B: I ____ (see) many interesting things.
📝 查看答案
答案：was; was; visited; did; do; saw
解析：How was your last weekend? / It was wonderful. I visited. / What did you do? / I saw (see→saw)。
挑战 ★★★
用所给词的过去式填空：
1. We ____ (walk) to school yesterday.
2. He ____ (tell) us a funny story.
3. They ____ (have) a party last night.
4. She ____ (watch) a show with her friends.
📝 查看答案
答案：1. walked 2. told 3. had 4. watched
解析：walk→walked (规则); tell→told (不规则); have→had (不规则); watch→watched (规则)。

✍️ 微写作：A Memorable Day

用一般过去时写一段 60-80 词的话，描述你难忘的一天。包括：什么时候、在哪里、做了什么、感受如何。

A Memorable Day

Last Sunday was a special day. I went to the park with my family.

We flew a kite and had a picnic. The weather was sunny and warm.

I played games with my sister. We all felt very happy.

It was a day to remember!

上周日是一个特别的日子。我和家人去了公园。我们放了风筝、野餐了。天气晴朗又温暖。我和妹妹玩了游戏。我们都感到很开心。那是难忘的一天！
