What a Delicious Meal!

烹饪 · 食物制作 · 祈使句 · 量词

📖 话题：Cooking and recipes💬 功能：Describe cooking steps, talk about food📚 语法：祈使句用于指令 · 可数/不可数 · 量词 (a cup of / two spoons of)📝 写作：写一份食谱

🎯 我能做到

📖 课文精讲 · Making a Fruit Salad
场景：Amy 和 Ben 一起做水果沙拉，Amy 看食谱，Ben 动手做。
Amy:Let's make a fruit salad!
Ben:Great! What ingredients do we need?
Amy:We need some apples, bananas, and yogurt.
Ben:OK. First, peel the bananas and cut them into small pieces.
Amy:Next, wash and cut the apples. Then mix the fruit together.
Ben:How much yogurt do we need?
Amy:Add two spoons of yogurt. Then mix everything again.
Ben:Finally, pour it into a bowl. Wow, it looks delicious!
Amy:Let's share it with our friends!
📖 查看翻译

Amy: 我们做水果沙拉吧！

Ben: 太棒了！需要什么材料？

Amy: 我们需要一些苹果、香蕉和酸奶。

Ben: 好的。先剥香蕉皮，把它们切成小片。

Amy: 然后洗苹果、切苹果。再把水果拌在一起。

Ben: 需要多少酸奶？

Amy: 加两勺酸奶，再搅拌一次。

Ben: 最后倒进碗里。哇，看起来很好吃！

Amy: 我们跟朋友一起分享吧！

🔑 重点讲解：

1. Let's make a fruit salad! → Let's + 动词原形：提议一起做某事。

2. First / Next / Then / Finally → 描述过程的顺序词。

3. How much yogurt do we need? → How much + 不可数名词，询问数量。

4. Add two spoons of yogurt. → 量词 + of + 名词：two spoons of, a cup of, a bowl of。

5. 祈使句：Peel / Cut / Mix / Add / Pour — 动词原形开头，表指令。

📚 语法聚焦 · 祈使句 & 量词

1. 祈使句用于食谱和指令

结构示例
肯定祈使句（动词原形开头）Cut the vegetables.
Add two cups of water.
Mix all the ingredients.
否定祈使句（Don't + 动词原形）Don't cook too long.
Don't add too much salt.

2. 常用量词

量词搭配示例
a cup of液体 / 粉末状a cup of water / milk / flour
a spoon of粉末 / 细小材料a spoon of sugar / salt
a piece of片 / 块状a piece of bread / cake / meat
a bowl of盛在碗里的食物a bowl of rice / soup / salad

3. First / Next / Then / Finally 过程顺序词

First, wash the fruit.首先，洗水果。
Next, cut them into pieces.接下来，把它们切块。
Then, mix them in a bowl.然后，在碗里混合。
Finally, enjoy your salad.最后，享用沙拉。

4. How many vs How much

How manyHow much
搭配名词可数名词复数不可数名词
示例How many apples?How much water?
How many eggs?How much milk?

❌ Peels the bananas.（祈使句用动词原形，不加 s）

✅ Peel the bananas.

❌ How many water do we need?（water 不可数，用 how much）

✅ How much water do we need?

📖 阅读专项 · A Special Family Recipe

My grandmother makes the best chicken soup in the world. It's a family recipe passed down for four generations.

Last weekend, I asked Grandma to teach me. "First, choose fresh ingredients," she said. "Fresh chicken, fresh ginger, and fresh vegetables."

"Next, boil the chicken in a pot of water. Then add the ginger. After 30 minutes, add the vegetables. Finally, add some salt."

I followed every step carefully. When the soup was ready, I tasted it. It was delicious! Grandma smiled and said, "Now the recipe is yours. Pass it down to your children one day."

I learned more than a recipe that day. I learned that food connects us to our family and our past.

generation n. 代；一代人 ginger n. 生姜 taste v. 尝起来 connect v. 连接；联系

🏆 中考题型专练 · 完形填空

阅读短文，选择最佳选项。

Do you want to make a sandwich? It's easy! (1)____, take two pieces of bread. Next, (2)____ some butter on the bread. Then, add some lettuce and two slices of (3)____. (4)____, put the other piece of bread on top. Now you have a (5)____ sandwich! How many sandwiches can you make?

1. A. Finally B. First C. Then D. Never
2. A. put B. puts C. putting D. to put
3. A. milk B. bread C. chicken D. water
4. A. Next B. First C. Finally D. Then
5. A. delicious B. boring C. expensive D. educational
答案
1. B (First)；2. A (put)；3. C (chicken)；4. C (Finally)；5. A (delicious)
✍️ 写作进阶 · Write a Recipe
📋 写作题目
请写一份食谱，介绍你最喜欢的一道菜。60–80 词，用祈使句和量词，包含步骤顺序词（First / Next / Then / Finally）。
开头：介绍菜名和需要哪些材料。
主体：用 First/Next/Then/Finally 写清楚步骤，使用祈使句和量词。
结尾：总结菜品特色或享用方式。
How to Make Tomato and Egg Soup

First, wash two tomatoes and cut them into small pieces. Next, beat two eggs in a bowl. Then, pour two cups of water into a pot and boil it. Add the tomatoes and cook for five minutes. Finally, pour the eggs into the pot and add a little salt. Enjoy your healthy soup!
如何制作番茄蛋汤

首先，洗两个番茄并切成小块。接下来，在碗里打两个鸡蛋。然后，往锅里倒两杯水烧开，加入番茄煮五分钟。最后，把蛋液倒进锅里加一点盐。享用你的健康汤吧！
