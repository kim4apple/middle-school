🎯 我能做到

📖 课文精讲 · Happy Holiday!
场景：开学后，同学们互相分享暑假经历并讨论即将到来的国庆假期计划。
Li Hua:Hi, Mike! How was your summer vacation?
Mike:It was wonderful! I went to Beijing with my family.
Li Hua:Sounds great! What did you do there?
Mike:We visited the Great Wall and ate Beijing duck. What about you?
Li Hua:I stayed at home and practiced English every day. I'm going to improve my speaking this term.
Mike:That's a good plan! What are you going to do for National Day?
Li Hua:I'm going to visit my grandparents in the countryside. I'm going to help them with the farm work.
Mike:That sounds meaningful. I'm going to take a short trip to Hangzhou with my cousins.
📖 查看翻译

Li Hua: 嗨，Mike！暑假过得怎么样？

Mike: 棒极了！我和家人去了北京。

Li Hua: 听起来不错！你们在那里做了什么？

Mike: 我们参观了长城，还吃了北京烤鸭。你呢？

Li Hua: 我待在家里每天练英语。这学期我打算提高口语。

Mike: 好计划！你国庆节打算做什么？

Li Hua: 我打算去乡下看望爷爷奶奶，帮他们干农活。

Mike: 听起来很有意义。我打算和表弟表妹去杭州短途旅行。

🔑 重点讲解：

1. How was your summer vacation? → 询问过去经历，用一般过去时。回答用 It was + adj.

2. What did you do there? → 一般过去时的特殊疑问句：What + did + 主语 + do?

3. I'm going to improve my speaking. → be going to + 动词原形 表示打算/计划做某事。

4. What are you going to do for National Day? → be going to 的特殊疑问句：What + be + 主语 + going to do?

5. That sounds meaningful. → sound（听起来）+ adj.，类似的还有 look（看起来）、taste（尝起来）、feel（感觉）。

🔤 重点句型 · Sentence Patterns
How was your…?
询问过去经历
How was your summer vacation? — It was wonderful.
be going to + do
表示计划打算
I am going to visit my grandparents.
What did you do?
询问过去具体行为
What did you do last weekend? — I visited the museum.
That sounds + adj.
表达对某事的评价
That sounds great! / That sounds meaningful.
📚 语法聚焦 · 一般过去时 & be going to 将来时

一般过去时——规则动词变化

构成规则示例变化
一般情况 +edplay → playedwork → worked
以 e 结尾 +dlike → likedlive → lived
辅音+y 变 iedstudy → studiedtry → tried
重读闭音节双写+edstop → stoppedplan → planned

be going to 结构

人称肯定句疑问句
II am going to + v.Am I going to + v.?
He/She/ItHe is going to + v.Is he going to + v.?
We/You/TheyThey are going to + v.Are they going to + v.?

❌ I go to Beijing last week.（一般过去时需用 went）

✅ I went to Beijing last week.

❌ I am go to visit my grandparents.（be going to 后动词用原形）

✅ I am going to visit my grandparents.

📖 阅读专项 · Holidays Around the World

People around the world celebrate holidays in different ways.

In China, the Spring Festival is the most important holiday. Families get together, eat dumplings, and watch the Spring Festival Gala. Many children get red packets from their elders.

In the United States, Thanksgiving is a time for family reunions. People eat turkey, mashed potatoes, and pumpkin pie.

No matter where you are, holidays are a time to relax, have fun, and be with the people you love.

🏆 中考题型专练 · 完形填空

阅读下面短文，选择最佳选项填入空白处。

Last summer, I (1)____ to Qingdao with my parents. We (2)____ there by train. The weather was sunny. We (3)____ on the beach and swam. We also (4)____ fresh seafood. Next year, I (5)____ going to visit again!

1. A. go B. went C. going D. goes
2. A. get B. got C. gets D. getting
3. A. relax B. relaxed C. relaxing D. relaxes
4. A. eat B. eats C. ate D. eating
5. A. am B. is C. are D. be
答案
B (went)；B (got)；B (relaxed)；C (ate)；A (am)
