七下 · Unit 6

Rain or Shine

天气 · 气候 · 现在进行时综合

📖 话题：Weather and seasons 💬 功能：Describe weather & what people are doing 📚 语法：How's the weather? · 现在进行时综合 📝 写作：描述今天的天气

🎯 我能做到

📖 课文精讲 · Video Call
场景：Chen Jie（北京）跟 Tom（伦敦）视频通话，聊天气。
Chen Jie: Hello, Tom! How's the weather in London today?
Tom: It's rainy and cold. I'm wearing a coat. How about Beijing?
Chen Jie: It's sunny and warm! I'm wearing a T-shirt.
Tom: Lucky you! What are you doing today?
Chen Jie: I'm going to the park with my family. We are having a picnic!
Tom: Sounds great! In London, people are staying at home because of the rain.
Chen Jie: What are you doing right now?
Tom: I'm drinking hot tea and reading a book. Very cozy!
Chen Jie: Enjoy your day! Rain or shine, every day is a good day!
📖 查看翻译

逐句翻译：

Chen Jie: 你好 Tom！今天伦敦天气怎么样？

Tom: 下雨又冷。我穿着外套。北京呢？

Chen Jie: 晴朗又温暖！我穿着 T 恤。

Tom: 真羡慕你！你今天在做什么？

Chen Jie: 我和家人要去公园。我们在野餐！

Tom: 听起来不错！在伦敦，因为下雨人们都待在家里。

Chen Jie: 你现在在做什么？

Tom: 我在喝热茶、看书。很惬意！

Chen Jie: 享受你的一天！无论雨天还是晴天，每一天都是好日子！

🔑 重点讲解：

1. How's the weather? = What's the weather like? → "天气怎么样？"

2. 描述天气：It is + 天气形容词（rainy / sunny / windy）

3. 穿着表达：I'm wearing + 服装 → "我穿着……"

4. sounds + 形容词 → "听起来……"（sounds great / sounds fun）

5. because of + 名词 → "因为……"

📖 Reading: The Four Seasons

There are four seasons in a year: spring, summer, autumn and winter.

In spring, the weather is warm and windy. People fly kites and go on picnics.

In summer, it is hot and sunny. People go swimming and eat ice cream.

In autumn, the weather is cool and dry. Leaves turn yellow. People go hiking.

In winter, it is cold and snowy. People make snowmen and go skating.

Every season is beautiful!

🔤 重点句型 · Sentence Patterns
How's the weather?
询问天气的两种方式。
✅ How's the weather? = What's the weather like?
➡ It's sunny / rainy / cloudy.
✅ How's the weather in Beijing?
➡ It's windy and cold.
I'm wearing + 服装
描述穿着。
✅ I'm wearing a coat.
✅ He is wearing a T-shirt.
✅ She is wearing a dress.
Sounds + 形容词
"听起来……" 用于表达感受。
✅ Sounds great!
✅ Sounds fun!
✅ That sounds interesting.
because of + 名词
"因为……" 后接名词短语。
✅ People stay at home because of the rain.
✅ We can't go out because of the snow.
✅ The picnic is cancelled because of the weather.

📝 三级闯关 · 实战练习

🥉 基础关 · 概念识记

基础 ★☆☆
How's the weather? = What's the weather ____?
📝 查看答案
答案：like
解析：How's the weather? = What's the weather like? 都是询问天气的句型。
基础 ★☆☆
It's ____ (sun) today. Let's go to the park!
📝 查看答案
答案：sunny
解析：sun 是名词，描述天气用形容词 sunny。
基础 ★☆☆
People ____ (stay) at home because of the rain.
📝 查看答案
答案：are staying
解析：描述当下正在进行的动作，用现在进行时：are staying。
基础 ★☆☆
In winter, it is ____ (snow) and cold.
📝 查看答案
答案：snowy
解析：snow 加 y 变成形容词 snowy，描述天气。

🥈 提升关 · 语境运用

提升 ★★☆
改错：How's the weather like today?
📝 查看答案
答案：What's the weather like today? 或 How's the weather today?
解析：How 和 like 不能同时用。How's the weather? = What's the weather like?
提升 ★★☆
He is ____ a coat because it's cold. (wear / wearing)
📝 查看答案
答案：wearing
解析：is + wearing 现在进行时，表示"穿着"的状态。
提升 ★★☆
连词成句：weather / how's / London / in / the / ?
📝 查看答案
答案：How's the weather in London?
解析：How's the weather in + 地点？询问某地的天气。

🥇 挑战关 · 综合运用

挑战 ★★★
汉译英：今天北京天气怎么样？晴朗又温暖。我穿着一件 T 恤。
📝 查看答案
答案：How's the weather in Beijing today? It's sunny and warm. I'm wearing a T-shirt.
解析：How's the weather in + 地点；描述天气用 It's + 形容词；穿着用 I'm wearing。
挑战 ★★★
补全对话：
A: Hello! How's the weather in Sydney?
B: It's ____ (rain). I'm ____ (stay) at home.
A: What ____ you ____?
B: I'm ____ (read) a book.
📝 查看答案
答案：rainy; staying; are; doing; reading
解析：描述天气用 rainy；现在进行时：am/is/are + doing。
挑战 ★★★
阅读理解：In spring, the weather is warm. People fly kites. In summer, it's hot. People go swimming. Which season do people fly kites?
📝 查看答案
答案：Spring.
解析：原文 "In spring, the weather is warm. People fly kites." 所以春天放风筝。
