📖 话题：Personal Traits & Comparisons💬 功能：Compare people using comparative adjectives📚 语法：形容词比较级（-er / more）· than · as…as 同级比较📝 写作：My Best Friend and I

🎯 我能做到

📖 课文精讲 · I'm more outgoing than my sister.
场景：班会课上，同学们讨论自己和家人的性格差异。
Anna:Mom, am I more like you or Dad?
Mom:You look like your father, but you're more outgoing than him.
Anna:Really? Dad seems pretty outgoing too.
Mom:Well, your dad is funnier than me. But I'm more serious than him.
Anna:What about my sister?
Mom:She's quieter than you. But she's more hard-working. You two are very different.
Anna:I think we're both smart and kind. That's what matters!
📖 查看翻译

Anna: 妈妈，我更像你还是爸爸？

Mom: 你长得像你爸爸，但性格比他外向。

Anna: 是吗？爸爸看起来也挺外向的。

Mom: 嗯，你爸爸比我幽默。但我比他认真。

Anna: 那姐姐呢？

Mom: 她比你安静，但她更勤奋。你俩很不一样。

Anna: 我觉得我俩都聪明善良，这才是重要的！

🔑 重点讲解：

1. You're more outgoing than him. → 多音节形容词比较级：more + adj. + than

2. Your dad is funnier than me. → 辅音+y 结尾改 y 为 i + er：funny → funnier

3. She's quieter than you. → quiet → quieter（双音节以 e 结尾 +r）

4. We're both smart and kind. → both 意为"两者都"，放在 be 动词后、实义动词前。

📚 语法聚焦 · 形容词比较级

比较级构成规则

规则构成方法示例
单音节/部分双音节+ertall → taller; short → shorter
以 e 结尾+rlarge → larger; nice → nicer
辅音+y 结尾变 y 为 i +erfunny → funnier; easy → easier
重读闭音节双写尾字母 +erbig → bigger; hot → hotter
多音节/部分双音节more + 原级outgoing → more outgoing; beautiful → more beautiful

不规则比较级

原级比较级中文
good/wellbetter更好的
bad/badlyworse更差的
many/muchmore更多的
littleless更少的
farfarther/further更远的

❌ She is more taller than me.（taller 已是比较级，不加 more）

✅ She is taller than me.

❌ He is more smart than his brother.（smart 单音节用 smarter）

✅ He is smarter than his brother.

📖 阅读专项 · Twin Brothers, Different Personalities

Leo and Sam are twin brothers. They look the same, but they are very different.

Leo is more outgoing. He loves to talk and make friends. He is also more athletic and plays basketball every day. Sam is quieter and more serious. He enjoys reading and drawing.

Although they are different, they get on well. Leo helps Sam practice basketball, and Sam helps Leo with math.

Leo says, "Sam is smarter at school, but I'm better at sports. We learn from each other!"

🏆 中考题型专练 · 完形填空

阅读短文，选择最佳选项。

My best friend Lucy is different from me. She is (1)____ (outgoing) than me, and she is (2)____ (funny) than anyone I know. I am (3)____ (quiet) and more serious. But we have one thing (4)____ common—we are both kind. A good friend (5)____ like a mirror. You can see yourself in your friend.

1. A. outgoing B. more outgoing C. most outgoing D. outgoinger
2. A. funny B. funnier C. funniest D. more funny
3. A. quiet B. quieter C. quietest D. more quiet
4. A. in B. on C. at D. for
5. A. is B. are C. be D. am
答案
1. B (more outgoing)；2. B (funnier)；3. B (quieter)；4. A (in)；5. A (is)
✍️ 写作进阶 · My Best Friend and I
📋 写作题目
请以"My Best Friend and I"为题，从外貌、性格、爱好三方面比较你和你的好朋友。
开头：介绍你的好朋友是谁。
主体：从三方面比较（外貌/性格/爱好）。
结尾：总结虽然不同，但友情深厚。
My best friend is Li Ming. He is taller than me, but I am heavier than him. He is more outgoing. He likes to talk and play with others. I am quieter and I enjoy reading. However, we both like sports. He is better at basketball, and I am better at running. We learn from each other. Good friends don't need to be the same!
我最好的朋友是李明。他比我高，但我比他重。他更外向，喜欢和别人聊天玩耍。我比较安静，喜欢阅读。不过我们都喜欢运动。他篮球更好，我跑步更好。我们互相学习。好朋友不需要一模一样！
