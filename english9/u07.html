🎯 我能做到

📖 课文精讲 · Sharing Music We Love
场景：Lin Tao 和 Mei Ling 在分享各自喜欢的音乐，讨论不同音乐类型和乐器。
Lin Tao:What kind of music do you like, Mei Ling?
Mei Ling:I love music that has a strong beat. Pop and rock are my favorites.
Lin Tao:Nice! I prefer music which is relaxing. Jazz is great for studying.
Mei Ling:Do you play any instruments?
Lin Tao:I play the guitar. I'm in a band. We have a concert next month!
Mei Ling:That's cool! Who's your favorite singer?
Lin Tao:I like singers who write their own songs. They express real feelings.
Mei Ling:I agree. Compared with pop stars, singer-songwriters are more authentic.
Lin Tao:Want to come to our concert? It's going to be amazing!
Mei Ling:I'd love to! I'm a fan of live music.
📖 查看翻译

Lin Tao: 你喜欢什么类型的音乐，梅玲？

Mei Ling: 我喜欢节奏感强的音乐。流行和摇滚是我的最爱。

Lin Tao: 不错！我更喜欢让人放松的音乐。爵士乐适合学习的时候听。

Mei Ling: 你会弹什么乐器吗？

Lin Tao: 我会弹吉他。我在一个乐队里。我们下个月有一场音乐会！

Mei Ling: 太酷了！你最喜欢的歌手是谁？

Lin Tao: 我喜欢自己写歌的歌手。他们表达真实的感受。

Mei Ling: 我同意。和流行明星相比，唱作人更真实。

Lin Tao: 想来看我们的音乐会吗？会很棒的！

Mei Ling: 我很想去！我是现场音乐的粉丝。

🔑 重点讲解：

定语从句修饰音乐：music that has a strong beat / music which is relaxing

定语从句修饰人：singers who write their own songs

compared with/to 表示「与……相比」

be a fan of 表示「是……的粉丝」

🔤 重点句型 · Sentence Patterns
I like / love / enjoy music that / which ...
用定语从句描述喜欢的音乐。
I like music that has a strong beat. / She enjoys songs which are relaxing.
a singer who / that ...
用定语从句修饰歌手。
I like singers who write their own songs. / He is a musician who plays the guitar.
prefer A to B
表达偏好：比起 B 更喜欢 A。
I prefer pop to rock. / She prefers classical music to jazz.
be into / be a fan of / compared with
表达热爱和比较。
I'm into hip-hop. / I'm a big fan of Jay Chou. / Compared with pop, jazz is more relaxing.
📚 语法聚焦 · 定语从句 (who/that/which)

定语从句基本结构

关系词指代例句
that人 / 物I like music that makes me happy.
which物She likes songs which have good lyrics.
who人He is a singer who writes his own songs.

定语从句在表达喜好中的应用

结构含义例句
music that makes me ...让我……的音乐I like music that makes me feel happy.
songs which have ...有……的歌曲She enjoys songs which have a strong beat.
singers who sing about ...唱……的歌手I admire singers who sing about real life.
prefer ... to ...比起……更喜欢……I prefer jazz to rock.

that 既可以指人也可以指物；which 只能指物；who 只能指人。

关系词在从句中作宾语时可以省略：The song (that) I like is ...

从句的动词要和先行词（被修饰的名词）的单复数保持一致。

📖 阅读专项 · The Language of Music

Music is often called the universal language. No matter where you come from, music can touch your heart. It connects people across cultures, ages, and backgrounds. A happy melody can make you smile. A sad song can make you cry. The rhythm of a drum can make you want to dance.

There are many different genres of music around the world. In China, traditional folk music uses instruments like the erhu and guzheng. In Africa, drumming is an important part of celebrations. In Western countries, classical music has a long history, while pop and rock are popular among young people.

Music also helps us express feelings that words cannot describe. When you are happy, you might sing a joyful song. When you are sad, you might listen to a gentle melody that comforts you. Music is a bridge that brings people together. It is a gift that everyone can enjoy.

✍️ 写作进阶 · My favorite song/musician
📋 写作题目
写一篇短文，介绍你最喜欢的歌曲或音乐人（歌手/乐队）。说说你为什么喜欢，以及它如何影响你。要求使用定语从句。
开头：介绍你最喜欢的歌曲或音乐人
音乐类型：用定语从句描述它的风格
喜欢原因：这首歌/歌手的特别之处
感受：它给你带来怎样的感受
结尾：总结，推荐给别人
My favorite singer is Jay Chou. He is a singer who writes his own songs. His music combines pop, R&B, and Chinese traditional elements. I like songs that have meaningful lyrics, and his songs are full of beautiful poetry. For example, "Qing Hua Ci" is a song which describes Chinese porcelain in a romantic way. Compared with other pop singers, Jay's music is more creative and unique. His melodies always make me feel relaxed and inspired. I am a big fan of his music, and I hope more people can enjoy it.
我最喜欢的歌手是周杰伦。他是一位自己写歌的歌手。他的音乐融合了流行、R&B和中国传统元素。我喜欢歌词有意义的歌曲，他的歌曲充满了优美的诗意。比如《青花瓷》是一首以浪漫方式描述中国瓷器的歌曲。和其他流行歌手相比，周杰伦的音乐更有创意和独特。他的旋律总是让我感到放松和受到启发。我是他音乐的忠实粉丝，我希望更多人能欣赏它。
