📖 话题：Future Life & Predictions💬 功能：Make predictions about the future📚 语法：will 将来时 · there will be · 一般将来时的各种句式📝 写作：Life in 20 Years

🎯 我能做到

📖 课文精讲 · Will people have robots?
场景：科技课上，同学们在讨论 50 年后世界会是什么样子。
Mr. Chen:Will people have robots in their homes in 50 years?
Liu Wei:Yes, I think every family will have a robot. Robots will do housework.
Lin Tao:Will kids still go to school?
Liu Wei:Maybe not. They will study at home on computers.
Anna:I think there will be more trees and less pollution.
Mr. Chen:That sounds great! Will there be flying cars?
Anna:Yes, I think there will be. People will travel faster.
📖 查看翻译

Mr. Chen: 50 年后人们家里会有机器人吗？

Liu Wei: 我觉得每个家庭都会有机器人。机器人会做家务。

Lin Tao: 孩子们还会去上学吗？

Liu Wei: 也许不会了。他们会在家通过电脑学习。

Anna: 我觉得会有更多树，更少污染。

Mr. Chen: 听起来不错！会有飞行汽车吗？

Anna: 我觉得会有。人们出行会更快。

🔑 重点讲解：

1. Will people have robots? → will + 动词原形 表示未来预测。Will + 主语 + 动词? 一般疑问句。

2. There will be more trees. → There will be + 名词 "将有……"，be 不随后面名词变化。

3. Kids will study at home on computers. → will 肯定句：主语 + will + 动词原形。

4. People will travel faster. → will 后接比较级，表示未来会更快。

🔤 重点句型 · Sentence Patterns
will + 动词原形
未来预测
People will have robots. / I will be a doctor.
Will + 主语 + 动词?
一般疑问句未来时
Will there be flying cars? / Will kids go to school?
There will be + 名词
描述未来的存在
There will be more trees. / There will be less pollution.
📚 语法聚焦 · will 将来时

will 句式总结

句式结构示例
肯定句主语 + will + 动词I will travel to the moon.
否定句主语 + won't + 动词People won't need cars.
一般疑问句Will + 主语 + 动词?Will everyone have a robot?
特殊疑问句Wh- + will + 主语 + 动词?Where will people live?

there will be

句式示例
肯定There will be more people.
否定There won't be any wars.
疑问Will there be flying cars?

❌ People will has robots.（will 后动词用原形）

✅ People will have robots.

❌ There will have more trees.（固定搭配 there will be）

✅ There will be more trees.

🏆 中考题型专练 · 完形填空

阅读短文，选择最佳选项。

What (1)____ life be like in 50 years? I think there (2)____ be great changes. People (3)____ live longer. Many people (4)____ work from home. Robots will (5)____ us with housework. But there (6)____ be some problems too. What will you do to make the future better?

1. A. will B. is C. are D. was
2. A. is B. are C. will D. was
3. A. is B. are C. will D. won't
4. A. will B. are C. is D. be
5. A. help B. helping C. helped D. helps
6. A. will B. won't C. is D. are
答案
1. A (will)；2. C (will)；3. C (will)；4. A (will)；5. A (help)；6. A (will)
