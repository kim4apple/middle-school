Let's clean up!

环保行动

📖 话题：Environmental Protection💬 功能：Talk about environmental issues and volunteer work📚 语法：情态动词 should / shouldn't ·动词短语（clean up, put up, give out）📝 写作：A Letter About Protecting the Environment

🎯 我能做到

📖 课文精讲 · Let's clean up!
场景：班级讨论如何参与学校和社区的环保活动。
Teacher:Our city is having a "Clean Up Day" this Saturday. What can we do?
Li Lei:We should clean up the park near our school. There is a lot of trash.
Wang Fang:Good idea! We could also put up signs to ask people not to litter.
Zhang Wei:I think we should give out leaflets to tell people about recycling.
Li Lei:We shouldn't use plastic bags. Let's bring our own bags when shopping.
Teacher:Great suggestions! Let's meet at the school gate at 8 a.m. on Saturday. Let's clean up together!
📖 查看翻译

Teacher: 这周六是城市清洁日。我们能做什么？

Li Lei: 我们应该打扫学校附近的公园。那里有很多垃圾。

Wang Fang: 好主意！我们还可以张贴标语，请人们不要乱扔垃圾。

Zhang Wei: 我觉得应该发传单告诉大家关于回收的知识。

Li Lei: 我们不应该用塑料袋。购物时自带袋子。

Teacher: 好建议！周六早上 8 点校门口集合。一起行动！

🔑 重点讲解：

1. We should clean up the park. → should + 动词原形 "应该做"，表建议。

2. We could also put up signs. → could + 动词原形 "可以做"，语气比 should 委婉。

3. We shouldn't use plastic bags. → shouldn't + 动词原形 "不应该做"。

4. 动词短语：clean up 打扫干净 / put up 张贴 / give out 分发 / throw away 扔掉。

📚 语法聚焦 · should / shouldn't + 动词短语

should 的用法

句式结构示例
肯定should + 动词原形We should plant more trees.
否定shouldn't + 动词原形You shouldn't waste water.
疑问Should + 主语 + 动词?Should we clean the room?

📖 阅读专项 · Teens Who Make a Difference

Many teenagers around the world are helping to protect the environment.

In China, a group of students started a "No Plastic" campaign at school. They asked students to bring their own water bottles. They also put up posters about plastic pollution.

In Australia, a 14-year-old girl organized beach clean-ups every month. She and her friends picked up trash and recycled what they could.

These teens show us that everyone can help. You don't need to be an adult to make a difference. Start small—clean up your neighborhood or plant a tree!

🏆 中考题型专练 · 完形填空

阅读短文，选择最佳选项。

Our school had a "Green Week" last month. We (1)____ up the campus. We also (2)____ up posters about recycling. Students (3)____ throw away plastic bottles. Instead, they should (4)____ them. The best part was the tree-planting activity. We (5)____ 50 trees near the playground. Everyone felt happy. Small actions (6)____ make a big difference!

1. A. clean B. cleaned C. cleaning D. cleans
2. A. put B. puts C. putting D. putted
3. A. should B. shouldn't C. can D. will
4. A. recycle B. throw C. buy D. make
5. A. plant B. planted C. planting D. plants
6. A. can B. can't C. should D. shouldn't
答案
1. B (cleaned)；2. A (put)；3. B (shouldn't)；4. A (recycle)；5. B (planted)；6. A (can)
✍️ 写作进阶 · A Letter About Protecting the Environment
📋 写作题目
请以"Protecting Our Environment"为题，写一封 70 词的倡议信，号召同学们从身边小事做起保护环境。
开头：指出环境问题的严重性。
主体：提出 3 条具体建议（用 should / shouldn't）。
结尾：号召大家一起行动。
Dear classmates, Our environment is in danger. We should do something to help. First, we shouldn't litter. Please put trash in the bin. Second, we should recycle paper and plastic. Third, we should plant more trees. Let's start from small things. Remember, a clean environment means a healthy life. Let's work together!
亲爱的同学们：我们的环境正面临威胁。我们应该做些事情来帮忙。第一，不要乱扔垃圾，请扔进垃圾桶。第二，我们应该回收纸张和塑料。第三，我们应该多种树。从小事做起。记住，干净的环境意味着健康的生活。一起行动吧！
