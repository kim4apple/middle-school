Making a Difference

社会责任

📖 话题：Social Responsibility & Volunteer Work💬 功能：Talk about volunteer work and social issues📚 语法：动词不定式作目的状语（to do / in order to）· make / let / help 使役动词📝 写作：How I Can Make a Difference

🎯 我能做到

📖 课文精讲 · Making a Difference
场景：学校举办"青年公益论坛"，几位同学分享自己做志愿者的经历。
Host:Welcome to our Youth Volunteer Forum. Let's hear from our speakers.
Chen Wei:I volunteer at an animal shelter to help stray dogs. I walk them every Saturday.
Liu Na:I teach children in a village school to make a difference. I go there every summer.
Chen Wei:That's amazing! How does that make you feel?
Liu Na:It makes me feel useful. I help them learn English, and they teach me to be patient.
Host:Small actions can make a big difference. Let's all try to help others!
📖 查看翻译

Host: 欢迎来到青年志愿论坛。来听听大家的分享。

Chen Wei: 我在动物收容所做志愿者，帮助流浪狗。每周六带它们散步。

Liu Na: 我在一所乡村学校教书来改变现状。我每年暑假都去。

Chen Wei: 太棒了！那让你感觉如何？

Liu Na: 让我觉得自己很有用。我教他们英语，他们教我耐心。

Host: 小行动能带来大改变。让我们一起帮助他人！

🔑 重点讲解：

1. I volunteer at an animal shelter to help stray dogs. → to do 不定式表目的："为了……"。

2. I teach children in a village school to make a difference. → make a difference "有所作为；带来改变"。

3. It makes me feel useful. → make + 人 + 动词原形 "让某人做/感到……"。

4. They teach me to be patient. → teach + 人 + to do "教某人做……"。

📝 重点词汇 · 社会责任
volunteer🔊 /ˌvɑːlənˈtɪr/v./n.

shelter🔊 /ˈʃeltər/n.

🔤 重点句型 · Sentence Patterns
…to do…（不定式表目的）
为了……
I went there to help the children.
make / let / help + 人 + 动词
使役动词
It makes me feel happy. / Let me help you.
make a difference
有所作为；带来改变
Small things can make a big difference.
📚 语法聚焦 · 不定式表目的 & 使役动词

不定式作目的状语

结构含义示例
to + 动词原形为了……I called to invite you.
in order to + 动词为了……（更正式）He left early in order to catch the bus.

使役动词 make / let / help

动词结构示例
makemake + 人 + 动词原形It makes me cry.
letlet + 人 + 动词原形Let me try.
helphelp + 人 + (to) 动词She helped me (to) clean.

❌ It makes me to feel happy.（make 后接动词原形，不加 to）

✅ It makes me feel happy.

❌ I went there for help him.（表目的用 to do，不用 for do）

✅ I went there to help him.

📖 阅读专项 · Teens Who Make a Difference

Many young people around the world are making a difference. Here are two inspiring stories.

Lin Tao, 14, noticed that many elderly people in his community lived alone. He started a "Weekend Companion" program. Every Saturday, he visits an elderly neighbor to chat and help with housework. "It makes me feel useful to bring them joy," he says.

Megan, 13, from the UK, loves reading. She collected hundreds of books and sent them to children in poor areas. "I hope to open a window to the world for them," she said.

These teens show us that age doesn't matter. You can make a difference starting today!

🏆 中考题型专练 · 完形填空

阅读短文，选择最佳选项。

Last summer, I went to a village school (1)____ teach English. The children there made me (2)____ warm. They were so eager (3)____ learn. I taught them songs and games. They taught me (4)____ be strong. This experience made me (5)____ that small actions can make a big difference. I plan to go back next summer!

1. A. to B. for C. at D. in
2. A. feels B. feel C. felt D. feeling
3. A. to B. for C. at D. in
4. A. to B. for C. at D. how
5. A. realize B. realized C. realizing D. to realize
答案
1. A (to)；2. B (feel)；3. A (to)；4. A (to)；5. A (realize)
✍️ 写作进阶 · How I Can Make a Difference
📋 写作题目
请以"How I Can Make a Difference"为题，写 70 词短文说说你打算通过什么行动为社会做贡献。
开头：我想在哪个领域做出改变。
主体：具体行动（用 to do 表达目的）。
结尾：呼吁大家一起行动。
I want to make a difference by helping stray animals. I plan to volunteer at an animal shelter every weekend to take care of cats and dogs. I will also raise money to buy food for them. I hope my actions can make more people care about animal welfare. Making a difference doesn't need to be big—start with small things!
我想通过帮助流浪动物来做出改变。我打算每周末在动物收容所做志愿者，照顾猫狗。我还会筹钱为它们买食物。我希望我的行动能让更多人关注动物福利。做出改变不需要做大事——从小事开始！

Community Action Plan
小组发现社区中存在一个问题（如环境差、老人孤独、流浪动物等），设计一份行动计划并用英语展示。

2讨论解决方案（至少 3 条）。
3用英语写出行动计划（用到 to do / make / help）。
4在全班展示。
