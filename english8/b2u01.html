U1

Time to Relax

休闲娱乐 · 建议 · 情态动词 should/might

📖 话题：Hobbies and Free Time💬 功能：Give Suggestions for Free Time📚 语法：should / might / How about ...? / Why not ...?📝 写作：推荐活动

🎯 我能做到

📖 课文精讲 · Time to Relax
场景：周末下午，几个朋友在一起讨论怎么打发空闲时间——Tom 觉得无聊，大家纷纷给出建议。
Tom:I'm so bored. There's nothing to do this weekend.
Amy:How about going to the park? We could play basketball or fly kites.
Tom:I don't feel like it. I'm too tired.
Jack:You should rest first. Then why not try something new? You might enjoy painting or photography.
Amy:Good idea! It's a good way to relax your mind. I prefer reading, but I also want to try camping.
Jack:We should go for a picnic together! The weather forecast says it'll be sunny.
Tom:That sounds great! Let's do that.
📖 查看翻译

Tom: 我好无聊。这周末没什么事可做。

Amy: 去公园怎么样？我们可以打篮球或者放风筝。

Tom: 我不太想。我太累了。

Jack: 你应该先休息。然后为什么不试试新东西？你可能会喜欢绘画或者摄影。

Amy: 好主意！那是放松心情的好方法。我更喜欢阅读，但我也想试试露营。

Jack: 我们应该一起去野餐！天气预报说明天是晴天。

Tom: 听起来太棒了！就这么办。

🔑 重点讲解：

1. How about going to the park? → How about + V-ing? 用于提出建议。

2. Why not try something new? → Why not + 动词原形? 也是提出建议。

3. You should rest first. → should + 动词原形 表达建议。

4. You might enjoy painting or photography. → might + 动词原形 表示可能性。

5. I prefer reading. → prefer + V-ing 表达偏好。

🔤 重点句型 · Sentence Patterns

📚 语法聚焦 · 建议与可能性

1. 情态动词 should / shouldn't

用于提出建议或劝告，后接动词原形。

结构示例中文
You should + verb.You should try yoga.你应该试试瑜伽。
You shouldn't + verb.You shouldn't stay up too late.你不应该熬夜太晚。
Should I/we + verb?Should we go for a walk?我们应该去散步吗？

2. 情态动词 might (可能性)

表示不太确定的推测或可能性，语气比 may 更弱。

结构示例中文
主语 + might + verbYou might enjoy gardening.你可能会喜欢园艺。
主语 + might not + verbHe might not like camping.他可能不喜欢露营。

3. 提出建议的句型

句型示例中文
How about + V-ing?How about going to the park?去公园怎么样？
Why not + verb?Why not try something new?为什么不试试新东西？
It's a good way to + verbIt's a good way to relax.这是放松的好方法。
prefer + V-ingI prefer reading to watching TV.比起看电视我更喜欢阅读。

❌ You should to rest.（should 后接动词原形，不加 to）

✅ You should rest.

❌ How about go out?（How about 后接 V-ing）

✅ How about going out?

❌ Why not to take a walk?（Why not 后接动词原形）

✅ Why not take a walk?

📖 阅读专项 · The Power of Rest

Do you know why rest is so important? Many students think they should study all the time. But taking breaks is just as important as studying!

When you rest, your brain gets a chance to recharge. After a good rest, you can focus better and learn faster. That's why a short break after studying can really help.

There are many relaxing activities you can try. Reading a book, taking a walk, or doing yoga are peaceful ways to rest. Some people enjoy gardening or painting. These activities help you feel calm and happy.

Outdoor activities are also great for relaxation. How about going camping or having a picnic with family? Traveling to a new place might be exciting too!

Remember: don't feel guilty about taking breaks. Rest is not wasting time — it's taking care of yourself. So why not relax and enjoy your free time?

🏆 中考题型专练 · 语法填空

用所给单词的适当形式填空。

There are many ways (1)____ (relax) after a busy week. Some people enjoy (2)____ (do) outdoor activities. (3)____ (Camp) in the mountains is a great choice. Others prefer (4)____ (stay) at home and read a book. You (5)____ (should) try different hobbies to find what you like. Why not (6)____ (start) with something simple? A short walk in the park is a good way (7)____ (feel) better. Remember, rest is important for everyone!

答案
(1) to relax；(2) doing；(3) Camping；(4) staying / to stay；(5) should；(6) start；(7) to feel
✍️ 写作进阶 · Recommend a Free-Time Activity
📋 写作题目
你的朋友最近觉得很累，想找一个放松的方式。请你推荐一项休闲活动，用 60-80 词说明该活动是什么、为什么推荐它，以及它带来的好处。
开头：推荐的活动名称 + 简单说明。
主体：用 should / might / How about / Why not 等给出建议理由。
结尾：鼓励朋友尝试。
I'd like to recommend gardening to you. Why not try growing some flowers or vegetables? It's a good way to relax after a stressful day. You might enjoy watching plants grow day by day. Gardening is quiet and peaceful, but it also keeps you active. You should give it a try — I'm sure you'll love it!
我想向你推荐园艺。为什么不试试种些花或蔬菜呢？这是压力大的一天过后放松的好方法。你可能会喜欢每天看植物生长。园艺安静又祥和，但也能让你活跃起来。你应该试试——我相信你会喜欢的！
