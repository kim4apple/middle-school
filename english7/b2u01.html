Animal Friends

动物名称 · 描述动物 · 喜好原因

📖 话题：Animals and nature 💬 功能：Describe animals & express likes/dislikes 📚 语法：Why 问句 · because · 形容词描述动物 📝 写作：介绍最喜欢的动物

🎯 我能做到

📖 课文精讲 · At the Zoo
场景：Tom 和 Chen Jie 在动物园里看动物。
Tom: Wow! Look at the pandas! They are so cute!
Chen Jie: Yes! They are black and white. I like pandas because they are lovely.
Tom: Why do you like pandas?
Chen Jie: Because they are gentle and cute. What animals do you like?
Tom: I like elephants. They are very smart and strong.
Chen Jie: Look! There are lions over there. They look scary!
Tom: Yes, but they are also beautiful. Let's see the tigers!
Chen Jie: OK! I also want to see the monkeys. They are very funny!
📖 查看翻译

逐句翻译：

Tom：哇！看那些熊猫！它们太可爱了！

陈洁：是的！它们是黑白色的。我喜欢熊猫因为它们很可爱。

Tom：你为什么喜欢熊猫？

陈洁：因为它们温顺又可爱。你喜欢什么动物？

Tom：我喜欢大象。它们非常聪明和强壮。

陈洁：看！那边有狮子。它们看起来很吓人！

Tom：是的，但它们也很漂亮。我们去看老虎吧！

陈洁：好的！我还想去看猴子。它们非常有趣！

🔑 重点讲解：

1. Why do you like ...? → "你为什么喜欢……？" Why 是对原因提问的特殊疑问词。
回答用 Because ...，表示"因为……"。

2. 动物描述形容词 → cute, lovely, smart, strong, scary, funny, beautiful
结构：主语 + be + 形容词 / 主语 + look + 形容词

3. There are lions over there. → "那边有狮子"
There be 句型表示"某地有某物"，复数用 There are。

4. look + 形容词 → "看起来……"，表示感官的系动词用法。
如：look scary / look beautiful

📖 Reading: Animals Are Our Friends

Animals are our good friends. Pandas live in China. They are black and white. They eat bamboo. They are very cute.

Elephants are big and strong. They can help people carry things. Monkeys are clever and funny. They like eating bananas.

I like dogs best. Dogs are loyal. They can help blind people. We should love and protect animals!

🔤 重点句型 · Sentence Patterns
Why do you like ...?
用 why 询问喜欢某事物的原因，用 because 回答。
✅ Why do you like pandas?
➡ Because they are cute.
✅ Why do you like cats?
➡ Because they are lovely.
主语 + be + 形容词
用 be 动词加形容词描述动物特征。
✅ Pandas are cute.
✅ Elephants are strong.
✅ The tiger is beautiful.
主语 + look + 形容词
用 look 表示"看起来……"。
✅ Lions look scary.
✅ The monkeys look funny.
✅ The pandas look cute.
There are + 复数名词
There be 句型表示"某地有某物"。
✅ There are lions over there.
✅ There are many animals in the zoo.
➡ 单数用 There is + 单数名词
📚 语法聚焦 · Why & Because · Adjectives

1️⃣ Why 问句与 Because 回答

Why 是特殊疑问词，用于询问原因。回答必须用 because 引导原因状语从句。

句式结构示例
问句Why + do/does + 主语 + 动词原形?Why do you like pandas?
回答Because + 主语 + 动词 ...Because they are cute.

2️⃣ 形容词描述动物

用形容词说明动物的特征。常用结构：

结构说明例句
主语 + be + adj.表示某物具有某种特征Pandas are cute.
主语 + look + adj.表示某物看起来……Lions look scary.
主语 + sound + adj.表示某物听起来……That sounds fun.

3️⃣ 第三人称单数用法

当主语是 he/she/it 或单数名词时，动词要用第三人称单数形式。

主语动词例句
The pandalikes / eatsThe panda likes bamboo.
Itlives / eatsIt lives in China.
⚠️ 中式英语纠错：
❌ I like panda because it cute.（缺少be动词）
✅ I like pandas because they are cute.

❌ Why you like cats?（缺少助动词 do）
✅ Why do you like cats?

❌ Lions look is scary.（look后不能再用be动词）
✅ Lions look scary.
📝 三级闯关 · 实战练习

🥉 基础关 · 概念识记

基础 ★☆☆
____ do you like pandas? (Why / What / How)
📝 查看答案
答案：Why
解析：询问原因用 Why。
基础 ★☆☆
I like dogs ____ they are loyal. (so / because / but)
📝 查看答案
答案：because
解析：表示原因用 because。
基础 ★☆☆
Pandas ____ (is / are) cute.
📝 查看答案
答案：are
解析：Pandas 是复数，用 are。

🥈 提升关 · 语境运用

提升 ★★☆
改错：Why do you likes pandas?
📝 查看答案
答案：Why do you like pandas?
解析：do 后面的动词用原形，like 不加 s。
提升 ★★☆
连词成句：like / why / you / do / cats / ?
📝 查看答案
答案：Why do you like cats?
解析：特殊疑问句：Why + do + 主语 + 动词原形?
提升 ★★☆
The lion looks ____ (scary / scarily).
📝 查看答案
答案：scary
解析：look 是系动词，后面跟形容词，不是副词。
提升 ★★☆
There ____ (is / are) a tiger in the zoo.
📝 查看答案
答案：is
解析：a tiger 是单数，用 There is。

🥇 挑战关 · 综合运用

挑战 ★★★
汉译英：你为什么喜欢大象？因为它们很聪明。
📝 查看答案
答案：Why do you like elephants? Because they are clever.
解析：Why 问句 + Because 回答。
挑战 ★★★
补全对话：
A: I like pandas.
B: ____ ____ you like pandas?
A: ____ they are very cute.
📝 查看答案
答案：Why do; Because
解析：询问原因用 Why do...，回答用 Because。
挑战 ★★★
翻译句子：那只老虎看起来很漂亮。它生活在森林里。
📝 查看答案
答案：That tiger looks beautiful. It lives in the forest.
解析：look + 形容词；tiger 是第三人称单数，live → lives。

My Favourite Animal

My favourite animal is the panda. Pandas are black and white. They are very cute. They eat bamboo. They live in China. I love pandas!
