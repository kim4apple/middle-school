七下 · Unit 4

Don't eat in class.

校规班规 · 祈使句 · 情态动词 have to / must

📖 话题：School rules 💬 功能：Talk about rules · Express what you must / have to do 📚 语法：祈使句 (Don't...) · 情态动词 can't / have to / must 📝 写作：制定班规或校规

🎯 我能做到

📖 课文精讲 · School Rules
场景：新学期班会，班长在给大家宣读校规。
Monitor: Welcome back, everyone! Let's go over the school rules.
Monitor: First, don't eat or drink in the classroom. Please eat in the dining hall.
Tom: Can we eat in the hallways?
Monitor: No, you can't. Don't eat in the hallways either.
Mary: What about wearing a hat in class?
Monitor: Don't wear hats in class. Also, don't arrive late for class. You have to be on time.
Tom: Do we have to wear school uniforms every day?
Monitor: Yes, you do. And you must keep the classroom clean.
📖 查看翻译

逐句翻译：

班长：欢迎回来！我们来说说校规。

班长：首先，不要在教室里吃东西或喝饮料。请在食堂吃。

Tom：走廊里可以吃吗？

班长：不，不可以。走廊里也不能吃。

Mary：上课戴帽子呢？

班长：上课不要戴帽子。另外，不要迟到。你们必须准时。

Tom：我们必须每天穿校服吗？

班长：是的。而且你们必须保持教室整洁。

🔑 重点讲解：

1. Don't eat in class. → 祈使句否定形式：Don't + 动词原形。

2. have to → "必须、不得不"，强调客观要求。注意：第三人称用 has to。

3. must → "必须"，强调主观义务，比 have to 语气更强。must 没有人称变化。

4. Can we eat in the hallways? → 用 Can 询问是否允许做某事。

📖 Reading: Our Class Rules

Hi, I'm Lucy. There are many rules in our school.

First, don't run in the hallways. It is dangerous.

Second, don't listen to music in class. We must listen to the teacher carefully.

Third, don't fight with classmates. We have to be friendly to each other.

We also have to wear school uniforms from Monday to Friday.

Rules help us learn better and keep safe!

🔤 重点句型 · Sentence Patterns
Don't + 动词原形！
祈使句的否定形式，用来表示"不要做某事"。
✅ Don't eat in class.
✅ Don't be late for school.
✅ Don't run in the hallways.
We can't + 动词原形
用 can't 表达"不允许、不能做某事"。
✅ We can't wear hats in class.
✅ We can't listen to music in class.
We have to + 动词原形
have to 表示"必须、不得不"（客观要求）。
✅ We have to wear uniforms.
✅ She has to do homework first.
➡ 否定：don't / doesn't have to（不必）
You must + 动词原形
must 表示"必须"（主观义务）。语气强于 have to。
✅ You must keep the classroom clean.
✅ You must be quiet in the library.
➡ 否定：mustn't（禁止做某事）
📚 语法聚焦 · Imperatives & Modal Verbs

1️⃣ 祈使句 (Imperatives)

祈使句用来表达命令、请求、建议。主语通常省略，动词用原形。

句式结构示例
肯定祈使句动词原形 + 其他Sit down, please.
否定祈使句Don't + 动词原形 + 其他Don't run in the hallways.
Let's 祈使句Let's + 动词原形Let's go to the library.

2️⃣ 情态动词对比：can't / have to / must

情态动词含义特点例句
can't不可以（禁止）表达不允许You can't eat in class.
have to不得不（客观）有人称变化 (has to)He has to wear a uniform.
must必须（主观）无人称变化I must finish my homework.
⚠️ 中式英语纠错：
❌ Not eat in class.（祈使句否定不能用 Not 开头）
✅ Don't eat in class.

❌ He have to wear school uniform.（第三人称用 has to）
✅ He has to wear a school uniform.

❌ We must to follow the rules.（must 后直接跟动词原形，不加 to）
✅ We must follow the rules.

3️⃣ have to 的疑问句和否定句

句式结构示例
一般疑问句Do/Does + 主语 + have to + 动词原形?Do we have to wear uniforms?
否定句主语 + don't/doesn't have to + 动词原形You don't have to come on Sunday.
💡 提示：don't have to = "不必"（≠ mustn't "禁止"）。mustn't 语气更强。
📝 三级闯关 · 实战练习

🥉 基础关 · 概念识记

基础 ★☆☆
____ eat in class! (Don't / Not / No)
📝 查看答案
答案：Don't
解析：祈使句否定用 Don't + 动词原形。
基础 ★☆☆
We ____ (必须) clean the classroom every day.
📝 查看答案
答案：must
解析：must + 动词原形，表达必须做某事。
基础 ★☆☆
He ____ (have to) do his homework first.
📝 查看答案
答案：has to
解析：第三人称单数用 has to。

🥈 提升关 · 语境运用

提升 ★★☆
改错：Not arrive late for school.
📝 查看答案
答案：Don't arrive late for school.
解析：否定祈使句用 Don't，不是 Not。
提升 ★★☆
连词成句：eat / in / don't / the / library / please / ,
📝 查看答案
答案：Don't eat in the library, please.
解析：祈使句否定：Don't + 动词 + 其他。
提升 ★★☆
We have to wear uniforms. (改为否定句)
📝 查看答案
答案：We don't have to wear uniforms.
解析：have to 的否定用 don't have to = "不必"。

🥇 挑战关 · 综合运用

挑战 ★★★
汉译英：不要在教室里跑。我们必须保持安静。我们必须按时完成作业。
📝 查看答案
答案：Don't run in the classroom. We must keep quiet. We must finish homework on time.
解析：祈使句 Don't + 动词原形；must + 动词原形。
挑战 ★★★
补全短文：Our school has many ____. We ____ arrive late. We ____ listen to the teacher. We ____ wear uniforms.
📝 查看答案
答案：rules; can't / mustn't; must; have to
解析：rules 规则；can't 不可以；must 必须；have to 不得不。

✍️ 微写作：Our Class Rules

用 Don't... / We can't... / We have to... / We must... 制定 5-6 条班规（50-70词）。

Our Class Rules

Our class has some rules. First, don't eat or drink in the classroom.

Second, don't be late for class. We have to arrive on time.

Third, we can't listen to music in class. We must listen to the teacher carefully.

We also have to keep the classroom clean and tidy. Rules make our class better!

我们班有一些规则。首先，不要在教室里吃东西或喝饮料。第二，上课不要迟到。我们必须准时到校。第三，我们不能在课堂上听音乐。必须认真听老师讲课。我们还要保持教室整洁。规则让我们的班级更好！

✅ 章节自查清单

🧑‍🏫 费曼挑战：用英语向新同学介绍我们学校的规则。练习 Don't... / We have to... / We must... 等表达。"Don't eat in class. We have to wear uniforms. We must keep the classroom clean."
