Unit 3

My School

学校设施 · 方位介词 · there be 句型

📖 话题：School buildings and locations 💬 功能：Describe school & ask for directions 📚 语法：there be 句型 · 方位介词 (in/on/under/behind/next to) 📝 写作：描述你的学校

🎯 我能做到

📖 课文精讲 · New Student Tours the School
场景：新生李明参观学校，张伟做导游。
Li Ming: Hi, Zhang Wei! Our school is big!
Zhang Wei: Yes! Let me show you around. This is our classroom building.
Li Ming: It's tall! How many classrooms are there?
Zhang Wei: There are 24 classrooms. And there is a big library behind our building.
Li Ming: Where is the library? Is it next to the playground?
Zhang Wei: No, it's behind the classroom building. There are many books in it.
Li Ming: Great! I love reading. Is there a computer room?
Zhang Wei: Yes, there is. It's on the second floor.
Li Ming: Our school is wonderful!
📖 查看翻译

逐句翻译：

李明: 嗨，张伟！我们学校好大啊！

张伟: 是啊！让我带你参观。这是我们的教学楼。

李明: 好高啊！有多少间教室？

张伟: 有24间教室。我们教学楼后面还有一个大图书馆。

李明: 图书馆在哪里？它在操场旁边吗？

张伟: 不，它在教学楼后面。里面有很多书。

李明: 太棒了！我喜欢阅读。有电脑室吗？

张伟: 有的。它在二楼。

李明: 我们学校真棒！

🔑 重点讲解：

1. This is ... — "这是……"，用来介绍人或物。如 This is our classroom building.

2. Let me show you around. — "让我带你参观"。let + 人 + do 表示"让某人做某事"。

3. How many ... are there? — "有多少……？" How many 后面必须跟 复数名词。

4. There is/are ... — "有……"。There is + 单数名词，There are + 复数名词。

5. Where is ... ? — "……在哪里？" 用来询问位置。

6. 方位介词：behind (在……后面)、next to (在……旁边)、in (在……里面)、on (在……上面)、under (在……下面)

7. on the ... floor — "在……楼(层)"。如 on the second floor (在二楼)。

🔤 重点句型 · Sentence Patterns
There is / There are + 某物 + 地点
"有……" 表示某处存在某物。is + 单数，are + 复数。
✅ There is a library behind the building.
✅ There are 30 classrooms in our school.
➡ 否定：There isn't / aren't
➡ 疑问：Is / Are there ...?
Where is / are + 主语?
询问某人或某物的位置。
✅ Where is the library?
➡ It's behind the classroom building.
✅ Where are the books?
➡ They are in the library.
How many + 复数 + are there?
询问数量，How many 后必须跟复数名词。
✅ How many classrooms are there?
➡ There are 24.
✅ How many buildings are there?
➡ There are 6.
This is / That is ...
用来介绍人或事物。This 指近处，That 指远处。
✅ This is our classroom building.
✅ That is the dining hall.
➡ 复数：These are / Those are
📚 语法聚焦 · There be & Prepositions

1️⃣ There be 句型

"某处有某物" 用 There is / are 表示。

形式用法例句
There is+ 单数名词 / 不可数名词There is a library in our school.
There are+ 复数名词There are 24 classrooms.
否定isn't / aren'tThere isn't a swimming pool.
疑问Is/Are ... ?Is there a computer room?
💡 提示：be 动词用 is 还是 are，取决于后面紧跟着的第一个名词。
如：There is a library and three buildings.（a library 是单数，用 is）
如：There are three buildings and a library.（three buildings 是复数，用 are）

2️⃣ 方位介词

表示位置关系的介词，常放在 be 动词之后。

📦⬆️ in 在……里面
📦⬆️ on 在……上面
📦⬇️ under 在……下面
📦⬅️ behind 在……后面
📦🤝📦 next to 在……旁边
📦➡️ in front of 在……前面
介词含义例句
in在……里面The books are in the library.
on在……上面The book is on the desk.
under在……下面The ball is under the chair.
behind在……后面The library is behind the building.
next to在……旁边The dining hall is next to the playground.
in front of在……前面The playground is in front of the building.

3️⃣ Where 问句

Where 用来询问位置，回答时用方位介词短语。

问句回答
Where is the library?It's behind the building.
Where are the students?They are in the classroom.
Where is the computer room?It's on the second floor.

📌 结构：Where + is/are + 主语？→ 主语 + be + 方位介词短语。

4️⃣ How many ... are there?

How many 用来询问可数名词的数量，后面必须跟 复数名词。

问句回答
How many classrooms are there?There are 24.
How many buildings are there?There are 6.
How many students are there in your class?There are 45.
⚠️ 中式英语纠错：
❌ How many book are there?（book 必须是复数 books）
✅ How many books are there?

❌ There have 24 classrooms.（"有" 不用 have，用 there be）
✅ There are 24 classrooms.
📝 三级闯关 · 实战练习

🥉 基础关 · 概念识记

基础 ★☆☆
There ____ a library in our school. (is / are / am)
📝 查看答案
答案：is
解析：a library 是单数名词，用 There is。
基础 ★☆☆
The playground is ____ the classroom building. (in front of / under / on)
📝 查看答案
答案：in front of
解析：in front of 表示"在……前面"，操场通常在教学楼前面。
基础 ★☆☆
____ there any books in the library? (Is / Are / Am)
📝 查看答案
答案：Are
解析：books 是复数，所以用 Are there ...?
基础 ★☆☆
How many ____ are there in your school? (classroom / classrooms)
📝 查看答案
答案：classrooms
解析：How many 后面必须跟复数名词。

🥈 提升关 · 语境运用

提升 ★★☆
改错：There are a computer room on the second floor.
📝 查看答案
答案：There is a computer room on the second floor.
解析：a computer room 是单数，用 There is。
提升 ★★☆
连词成句：is / where / the / library / ?
📝 查看答案
答案：Where is the library?
解析：Where 特殊疑问句：Where + is/are + 主语？
提升 ★★☆
用 There be 句型改写：A big playground is behind the building.
📝 查看答案
答案：There is a big playground behind the building.
解析：There be 句型 = There is/are + 名词 + 地点。

🥇 挑战关 · 综合运用

挑战 ★★★
汉译英：我们学校有30间教室。图书馆在教学楼后面。
📝 查看答案
答案：There are 30 classrooms in our school. The library is behind the classroom building.
解析：第一句用 There are + 复数；第二句用方位介词 behind。
挑战 ★★★
补全对话：
A: Excuse me. ____ is the library?
B: It's ____ the classroom building.
A: ____ there a computer room?
B: Yes, there ____. It's on the second floor.
📝 查看答案
答案：Where; behind; Is; is
解析：Where 问位置；behind 方位介词；Is there ... ? 一般疑问句。

There is a tall classroom building in my school.

There are 20 classrooms in it.
