Let's Communicate!

沟通技巧 · 建议 · 情态动词 should

📖 话题：Communication skills💬 功能：Give advice, express opinions📚 语法：should/shouldn't · Why don't you …? · 提建议句型📝 写作：如何有效沟通

🎯 我能做到

📖 课文精讲 · A Communication Problem
场景：Amy 和 Tom 产生了误会，Li Ming 居中调解并给出沟通建议。
Li Ming:Hey Amy, you look upset. What's wrong?
Amy:I had a fight with Tom. He didn't talk to me today. I don't know why.
Li Ming:Why don't you talk to him and find out what happened? You should communicate openly.
Amy:I tried, but he just said "It's nothing." In my opinion, he's still angry.
Tom:Hi Li Ming. Hi Amy. I'm sorry I was quiet today. I was just stressed about the exam.
Li Ming:See? You should tell her how you feel. How about studying together this weekend?
Amy:That's a good idea! I agree. It's a good idea to talk about problems instead of hiding them.
Tom:You're right. Let's communicate better from now on!
📖 查看翻译

Li Ming: 嘿 Amy，你看起来不开心。怎么了？

Amy: 我和 Tom 吵架了。他今天没跟我说话。我不知道为什么。

Li Ming: 你为什么不跟他谈谈，弄清楚发生了什么？你应该坦诚沟通。

Amy: 我试过了，但他只说"没事"。在我看来，他还在生气。

Tom: 嗨 Li Ming，嗨 Amy。抱歉我今天没说话。我只是因为考试压力大。

Li Ming: 看吧？你应该告诉她你的感受。这个周末一起学习怎么样？

Amy: 好主意！我同意。有问题就谈，而不是藏着掖着，这是个好办法。

Tom: 你说得对。从现在起我们更好地沟通！

🔑 重点讲解：

1. Why don't you talk to him? → Why don't you + 动词原形 "你为什么不……？" 表建议。

2. You should communicate openly. → should + 动词原形 "应该"，表建议和义务。

3. In my opinion, he's still angry. → In my opinion "在我看来"，表达个人观点。

4. How about studying together? → How about + V-ing "……怎么样？" 提建议。

5. It's a good idea to talk about problems. → It's a good idea to + 动词 "做……是个好主意"。

🔤 重点句型 · Sentence Patterns
should / shouldn't + 动词原形
应该 / 不应该
You should talk to her. / You shouldn't shout at others.
Why don't you + 动词原形?
你为什么不……？(提建议)
Why don't you ask your teacher? / Why don't you try again?
How / What about + V-ing?
……怎么样？
How about saying sorry first? / What about writing a letter?
Let's + 动词原形
让我们……吧
Let's have a talk. / Let's find a solution together.
It's a good idea to + 动词
……是个好主意
It's a good idea to listen first. / It's a good idea to share your feelings.
I think / In my opinion
我认为 / 在我看来
I think you should apologize. / In my opinion, honesty is very important.
📚 语法聚焦 · should / 提建议句型

1. 情态动词 should / shouldn't

句式结构示例
肯定should + 动词原形You should say sorry to her.
否定shouldn't + 动词原形You shouldn't ignore your friends.
疑问Should + 主语 + 动词? Should I call him first?

2. 提建议常用句型

句型结构示例
Why don't you…?Why don't you + 动词?Why don't you talk to your parents?
How/What about…?How/What about + V-ing?How about going for a walk together?
Let's…Let's + 动词Let's discuss this later.
It's a good idea to…It's a good idea to + 动词It's a good idea to listen first.
I think you should…I think + 主语 + shouldI think you should be honest.
In my opinion,…In my opinion, + 句子In my opinion, communication is key.

3. 表达同意与不同意

功能表达
同意I agree. / You're right. / That's a good point. / I think so too.
不同意（委婉）I disagree. / I don't think so. / I'm not sure about that. / That's a good point, but…

❌ You should to talk to him.（should 后跟动词原形，不加 to）

✅ You should talk to him.

❌ Why don't you to ask for help?（Why don't you 后跟动词原形）

✅ Why don't you ask for help?

❌ How about go shopping?（How about 后跟 doing）

✅ How about going shopping?

📖 阅读专项 · Better Communication, Better Life

Good communication makes life better. Here are some tips to help you communicate well with others.

Listen actively. When someone is talking, look at them and listen carefully. Don't look at your phone. Show you care by nodding or saying "I see."

Use polite words. Say "please" and "thank you." Don't interrupt when others are speaking. It's rude to cut in.

Express your feelings, don't blame. Say "I feel sad when you don't talk to me" instead of "You never talk to me." "I" sentences help others understand you better.

Respect different opinions. It's OK to disagree. You can say "I don't think so" politely. You don't have to win every argument.

Talk face to face. When you have something important, talk in person. Texts and messages can be misunderstood.

Practice these tips every day. You will have better relationships and a happier life!

🏆 中考题型专练 · 任务型阅读

阅读短文，完成以下任务。

Dear Dr. Know,
I have a problem. My best friend Amy is always on her phone when we hang out. (A) I think she should pay more attention to our conversation. I feel ignored. (B) Why don't you talk to her about it honestly? Let her know how you feel. (C) How about suggesting "phone-free time" when you meet? Good friends listen to each other. Respect starts with communication. — Mike

任务
任务1：判断下列说法正误（T/F）：
1. Mike's friend Amy talks a lot on the phone. ( )
2. Dr. Know gives advice in the letter. ( )

任务2：从文中找出与下列表达意思相近的建议句：
"你可以和她好好谈谈" → ____

任务3：你还有别的建议吗？用英语写一条。→ ____
答案
任务1：1. F (She is on her phone); 2. T
任务2：Why don't you talk to her about it honestly?
任务3：自由回答，如：Let's do fun activities together without phones.
✍️ 写作进阶 · Give Advice to a Friend
📋 写作题目
你的朋友 Tom 最近总是和同桌吵架，因为他觉得同桌不听他说话。请你给他写一封建议信（不少于 60 词），用上 should / Why don't you / How about 等方式给他建议。
开头：表明写信目的（安慰 + 表示理解）。
主体：2–3 条具体建议，使用不同句型。
结尾：鼓励 + 表达关心。
Dear Tom, I understand how you feel. Good communication takes practice. You should listen to your deskmate first and let him finish talking. Why don't you ask him about his hobbies? This can start a nice conversation. How about having lunch together and talking face to face? In my opinion, respect is the most important thing in a friendship. Let's try to be better communicators! Yours, Li Ming
亲爱的 Tom：我理解你的感受。良好的沟通需要练习。你应该先听听同桌的话，让他把话说完。你为什么不问问他有什么爱好？这样可以开始愉快的对话。一起吃午饭、面对面聊聊怎么样？在我看来，尊重是友谊中最重要的事。让我们一起努力成为更好的沟通者吧！你的朋友，李明
🎯 Project · Communication Advice Cards
Communication Advice Cards
和小组一起制作一套"沟通建议卡"：每人写出一个常见沟通问题，组内讨论后用不同句型给出一条建议。
1每人写一个生活中遇到的沟通问题（与父母、老师或朋友之间）。
2轮流分享问题，小组讨论解决建议。
3用 should / Why don't you / How about 等句型写出建议卡。
4装饰建议卡并张贴在班级"沟通角"。
#评价标准
1.问题真实具体
2.建议使用 3 种以上句型
3.建议切实可行
4.团队合作与分享
