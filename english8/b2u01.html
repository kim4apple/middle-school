U1

Time to Relax!

放松与娱乐

📖 话题：Leisure Activities & Hobbies💬 功能：Talk about leisure activities and express preference📚 语法：动词-ing 作主语和宾语 · enjoy/like/love/hate + doing📝 写作：My Favorite Way to Relax

🎯 我能做到

📖 课文精讲 · Time to Relax!
场景：考试结束后，同学们讨论周末怎么放松。
Amy:Finally, the exams are over! What do you like to do in your free time?
Ben:I love listening to music. It helps me relax. What about you?
Amy:I enjoy reading novels. Reading is a great way to escape from stress.
Ben:My sister hates staying at home on weekends. She always goes out with friends.
Amy:Going out is fun, but staying home can be relaxing too. I like watching movies at home.
Ben:That sounds nice. Maybe I'll try reading a book this weekend for a change!
📖 查看翻译

Amy: 终于考完了！你空闲时间喜欢做什么？

Ben: 我喜欢听音乐，能帮我放松。你呢？

Amy: 我喜欢读小说。阅读是逃避压力的好方式。

Ben: 我妹妹讨厌周末待在家，她总是和朋友出去玩。

Amy: 出去玩很有趣，但待在家里也很放松。我喜欢在家看电影。

Ben: 听起来不错。也许这周末我换换口味，读本书！

🔑 重点讲解：

1. I love listening to music. → love / like / enjoy / hate + doing 表达喜好。

2. Reading is a great way to escape. → 动词-ing 作主语，谓语动词用三单：Reading is…

3. Going out is fun, but staying home can be relaxing too. → 两个动词-ing 短语作主语对比。

4. I like watching movies at home. → like doing (习惯性爱好) = like to do (偶尔想做)。

🔤 重点句型 · Sentence Patterns
like / love / enjoy + doing
表达喜好
I enjoy reading books. / She loves dancing.
动词-ing 作主语
把动作当话题
Swimming is good for health. / Playing chess is fun.
hate / dislike + doing
表达厌恶
I hate getting up early. / He dislikes watching TV.
📚 语法聚焦 · 动词-ing 形式作主语和宾语

动词-ing 作主语

结构示例中文
动词-ing + is/are…Running is good exercise.跑步是好的锻炼。
It is + adj. + to do = Doing + is + adj.It is fun to swim. = Swimming is fun.游泳很有趣。

后接 doing 的常见动词

动词含义示例
enjoy享受I enjoy playing basketball.
like喜欢She likes singing.
love热爱He loves traveling.
hate讨厌They hate waiting.
finish完成I finished doing my homework.
keep持续Keep trying! Don't give up!

❌ I enjoy play basketball.（enjoy 后接 doing）

✅ I enjoy playing basketball.

❌ Play chess is interesting.（动词原形不能作主语）

✅ Playing chess is interesting.

📖 阅读专项 · How Teens Relax Around the World

Different teens have different ways to relax. Here are some examples.

In Japan, many teens enjoy reading manga. It's a popular hobby. Some like drawing anime characters in their free time.

In the UK, playing sports is very common. Teens love playing football, tennis, or swimming. Going to the park with friends is also a favorite activity.

In China, listening to music and playing video games are popular. Some teens enjoy practicing calligraphy. It helps them relax and focus.

No matter how you relax, taking a break is important for everyone!

🏆 中考题型专练 · 语法填空

用括号内单词的适当形式填空。

Everybody needs (1)____ (relax). In my family, we all have different ways. My father enjoys (2)____ (fish). My mother loves (3)____ (cook). (4)____ (Read) newspapers is her favorite. My sister likes (5)____ (draw). As for me, (6)____ (play) the guitar is the best way to relax.

答案
(1) to relax；(2) fishing；(3) cooking；(4) Reading；(5) drawing；(6) playing
