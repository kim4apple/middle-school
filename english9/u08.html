U8

It must belong to Carla.

推测与判断

📖 话题：Speculation and Judgment💬 功能：Make inferences and guesses about things and people📚 语法：Modal verbs for deduction: must, might, could, can't📝 写作：Write a story using reasoning and deduction

🎯 我能做到

📖 课文精讲 · It must belong to Carla.
场景：Linda和Tom在教室里发现了一个无人认领的书包，在推测书包的主人。
Linda:Look! There's a schoolbag on the desk. Whose is it?
Tom:I'm not sure. It could be Maria's. She has a pink schoolbag.
Linda:No, it can't be hers. Her schoolbag is on her desk. I saw it just now.
Tom:Then it must belong to a girl. There's a hair band in it.
Linda:It might be Lucy's. She has long hair and wears hair bands.
Tom:But Lucy is absent today. Wait, there's a science magazine in it. It could be Bob's.
Linda:It can't be Bob's. He doesn't like science. I think it must be Tony's. He loves science magazines.
Tom:You're right! And look, there's a name tag inside. It IS Tony's!
📖 查看翻译

Linda: 看！桌子上有一个书包。是谁的？

Tom: 我不确定。可能是玛丽亚的。她有一个粉色的书包。

Linda: 不，不可能是她的。她的书包在她桌子上。我刚才看到了。

Tom: 那它一定是一个女生的。里面有一个发带。

Linda: 可能是露西的。她留着长发，戴发带。

Tom: 但露西今天缺席。等等，里面有一本科学杂志。可能是鲍勃的。

Linda: 不可能是鲍勃的。他不喜欢科学。我认为一定是托尼的。他喜欢科学杂志。

Tom: 你说得对！看，里面有一个名字标签。的确是托尼的！

🔑 重点讲解：

must + 动词原形 表示「一定……」（非常肯定的推测）— It must belong to Carla.

might/could + 动词原形 表示「可能是……」（不太肯定的推测）— It could be Maria's.

can't + 动词原形 表示「不可能是……」（否定推测）— It can't be hers.

belong to + 人 表示「属于某人」— It must belong to Carla.

🔤 重点句型 · Sentence Patterns
主语 + must + 动词原形
表示非常肯定的推测（≈ 一定是）。
The schoolbag must belong to Carla. / She must be at home. I saw her car.
主语 + could/might + 动词原形
表示可能性较小的推测（≈ 可能是）。
It could be Maria's bag. / He might be in the library.
主语 + can't + 动词原形
表示否定的推测（≈ 不可能是）。
It can't be Bob's. He doesn't like science. / She can't be at school. She's sick.
主语 + must/could/might + be + V-ing
推测正在进行的动作。
They must be studying. The light is on. / He might be sleeping.
📚 语法聚焦 · 情态动词表推测

推测的程度

情态动词可能性含义
must100% (非常肯定)一定是……
can't0% (非常肯定否定)不可能是……
could约30-50%可能是……
might约10-30%也许是……

不同时态的推测

时态结构例句
对现在的推测must/could/might/can't + VHe must be tired.
对正在进行的推测must/could/might + be V-ingThey must be sleeping.
对过去的推测must/could/might + have V-edShe must have left.

must 表推测只用于肯定句，否定句只能用 can't。

can't be 和 must not be 的区别：can't be = 不可能是；must not be = 禁止是。

belong to 后接人称代词时要用宾格（me/him/her）。

Where there is smoke, there is fire. 无风不起浪。

📖 阅读专项 · The Mystery of the Strange Noise

Our neighborhood used to be very quiet. But recently, strange things have been happening. People in the neighborhood hear noises in the night. They don't know what is happening.

Some people think it must be teenagers having fun. They say, «Teens must be playing tricks.» But others disagree. They say, «It can't be teenagers. The noises are too loud.»

One woman said she saw something running in the dark. She thought it could be a bear. But another neighbor said, «It can't be a bear. There are no bears near our town.»

A group of neighbors decided to find out what it was. They stayed up one night. They heard the noise again and followed it. Guess what? It was just a big dog! The dog was looking for food. Everyone felt silly, but they were also relieved.

✍️ 写作进阶 · Write a story using reasoning and deduction
📋 写作题目
想象你在一个旧房间里发现了一个神秘的箱子。用推测结构（must/can't/might/could）描述它的主人可能是什么样的人。
开头：描述你在哪里发现了什么
对物品的描述（颜色、材质、形状等）
对主人的推测（年龄、性别、职业等）
推理的依据（物品中的线索）
结尾：谜底揭晓或留下悬念
Yesterday, I found an old box in the attic. It was covered in dust. It must have been there for a long time. The box was blue and had a lock. It might belong to a child because there were stickers on it. Inside, I found some old letters and photos. The person in the photos could be my grandmother when she was young. The letters were written in English, so the owner must have studied English. I asked my grandmother, and she said the box was hers! She used it to keep her treasures when she was a girl.
昨天，我在阁楼上发现了一个旧箱子。上面布满了灰尘。它一定在那里很久了。箱子是蓝色的，有一把锁。它可能属于一个孩子，因为上面有贴纸。在里面，我找到了一些旧信和照片。照片中的人可能是我祖母年轻的时候。信是用英文写的，所以箱子的主人一定学过英语。我问了祖母，她说箱子是她的！她小时候用它来保存她的宝贝。
🎯 Project · Whose Is It? — A Classroom Mystery Game
Whose Is It? — A Classroom Mystery Game
举办一场「失物招领」推理游戏。每位同学从家里带一件物品放到箱子里，大家轮流推测物品的主人。
1每人带一件不常用的个人物品放进神秘箱
2随机抽取一件物品，描述它
3用 must/could/might/can't 推测它的主人
4说出你的推理过程（物品的特征→可能的用途→主人）
5猜测三次后揭晓答案
6记录猜对的数量，评出最佳侦探
#评价标准
1.描述物品清晰、准确
2.正确使用情态动词表推测
3.推理过程合理、有逻辑
4.积极参与、尊重他人
5.能分析自己猜对/错的原因
