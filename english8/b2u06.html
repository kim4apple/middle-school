Crossing Cultures

文化交流 · 宾语从句 · 文化对比

📖 话题：Culture and cultural exchange💬 功能：Compare cultures, express opinions📚 语法：宾语从句 (that/if/whether) · I think / I believe / I wonder📝 写作：介绍中国文化

🎯 我能做到

📖 课文精讲 · Crossing Cultures
场景：来自英国的交换生 Tom 和中国学生 Li Hua 在讨论文化差异。
Tom:Hi Li Hua! I'm really excited to learn about Chinese culture.
Li Hua:I'm glad you're interested! I think that Chinese culture is very rich and colorful.
Tom:I believe that every culture has something special. In the UK, we have afternoon tea. Is it similar in China?
Li Hua:That's interesting! In China, we have tea culture too. People drink tea and chat with friends. I wonder if you have a festival like our Spring Festival?
Tom:We have Christmas! It's a big family celebration. I wonder whether your customs are different from ours.
Li Hua:Yes, I think that understanding cultural differences helps us respect each other more.
📖 查看翻译

Tom: 你好李华！我很想了解中国文化。

Li Hua: 我很高兴你有兴趣！我认为中国文化很丰富多彩。

Tom: 我相信每种文化都有特别之处。在英国我们有下午茶。中国有类似的吗？

Li Hua: 真有趣！中国也有茶文化。人们喝茶和朋友聊天。我想知道你们有没有像春节一样的节日？

Tom: 我们有圣诞节！是个盛大的家庭庆祝活动。我想知道你们的习俗是否和我们不同。

Li Hua: 是的，我认为理解文化差异有助于我们更加互相尊重。

🔑 重点讲解：

1. I think that Chinese culture is very rich. → I think + that 宾语从句，that 可省略。

2. I believe that every culture has something special. → believe + that 从句，表达信念。

3. I wonder if you have a festival like our Spring Festival? → I wonder if/whether "我想知道是否……"。

4. I wonder whether your customs are different from ours. → whether 在正式语境中可代替 if。

🔤 重点句型 · Sentence Patterns
I think that + 从句
我认为……（表达观点）
I think that cultural exchange is wonderful.
I wonder if / whether + 从句
我想知道是否……（礼貌提问）
I wonder if you celebrate Christmas in your country.
It's + adj. + to + V
做某事是……的
It's interesting to learn about different cultures.
used to / be used to
过去常常 / 习惯于
I used to think chopsticks were hard. Now I am used to using them.
📚 语法聚焦 · 宾语从句

宾语从句 (Object Clauses)

引导词用法示例
that陈述句（可省略）I think (that) Chinese culture is fascinating.
if / whether一般疑问句I wonder if / whether he likes Chinese food.
疑问词特殊疑问句Do you know what she thinks about our culture?

that 引导的宾语从句

主句宾语从句合并
I thinkCulture is important.I think (that) culture is important.
I believeWe can learn from each other.I believe (that) we can learn from each other.
She saysShe loves Chinese food.She says (that) she loves Chinese food.

if / whether 引导的宾语从句

主句疑问句→从句合并
I wonderDoes he like tea?I wonder if / whether he likes tea.
I'm not sureIs this custom different?I'm not sure if this custom is different.

It's + adj. + to do 结构

结构示例
It's + adj. + to + VIt's important to respect other cultures.
It's + adj. + for sb + to + VIt's easy for us to understand.

used to vs be used to

结构含义示例
used to + V过去常常（现在不）I used to eat with a fork.
be used to + V-ing习惯于I am used to eating with chopsticks now.

❌ I think is a good culture.（宾语从句缺少主语）

✅ I think that Chinese culture is wonderful.

❌ I wonder if is he from the UK.（从句用陈述语序）

✅ I wonder if he is from the UK.

❌ I used to eat with chopsticks now.（used to 表过去习惯）

✅ I am used to eating with chopsticks now.

📖 阅读专项 · Bridging Cultures

Last summer, I took part in a cultural exchange program. A student from France came to stay with my family. At first, I was a little nervous. I wondered if we could communicate well.

When Pierre arrived, I thought that he was very friendly. He brought a gift for my family. I believe that giving gifts is a common custom in both China and France. However, I noticed some differences. In France, it is polite to kiss on the cheek when greeting friends. In China, we usually shake hands or nod.

During dinner, Pierre used a knife and fork. I used chopsticks. He was curious about Chinese food. I think that Chinese cuisine is one of the best ways to introduce our culture. He tried dumplings, and he loved them!

By the end of the week, we became good friends. I learned that although cultures are different, people's hearts are the same. Cultural exchange bridges the gap between us.

exchange program n. 交换项目 curious adj. 好奇的 bridge v. 架桥；消除 gap n. 差距；鸿沟

🏆 中考题型专练 · 语法填空

用括号内所给词的适当形式填空。

Do you know that China (1)____ (have) 56 ethnic groups? Each group has its own culture and (2)____ (tradition). I think that (3)____ (learn) about different cultures is exciting. Many foreign friends are interested (4)____ Chinese culture. I wonder (5)____ they find Chinese customs interesting. It's important for us (6)____ (respect) cultural differences. I used (7)____ (think) that all cultures were the same, but now I know they are different and wonderful. The more we understand, the (8)____ (much) we grow.

答案
(1) has；(2) traditions；(3) learning；(4) in；(5) if / whether；(6) to respect；(7) to think；(8) more
