Stay Healthy

健康习惯 · 建议表达 · 情态动词 should/must

📖 话题：Health and wellness💬 功能：Talk about healthy habits, give advice📚 语法：should/ought to · must/have to · had better📝 写作：健康建议

🎯 我能做到

📖 课文精讲 · Stay Healthy
场景：朋友们讨论健康习惯——运动、饮食、睡眠。
Tom:I feel tired all the time. What should I do?
Lucy:You should exercise more. It can give you energy.
Mike:And you ought to eat a balanced diet. Don't eat too much junk food.
Tom:I know. I must get enough sleep too. I usually go to bed late.
Lucy:You'd better go to bed before 10 p.m. Sleep is important for your health.
Mike:Exactly. A healthy body leads to a healthy mind!
Tom:You're right. I'll start taking better care of myself!
📖 查看翻译

Tom: 我总觉得累。该怎么办？

Lucy: 你应该多运动。运动能给你能量。

Mike: 还应该吃均衡饮食。别吃太多垃圾食品。

Tom: 我知道。我还必须保证充足睡眠。我通常睡很晚。

Lucy: 你最好在 10 点前睡觉。睡眠对健康很重要。

Mike: 说得对。健康身体造就健康心灵！

Tom: 你们说得对。我要开始更好地照顾自己了！

🔑 重点讲解：

1. You should exercise more. → should + 动词原形 "应该"，表建议。

2. You ought to eat a balanced diet. → ought to + 动词原形 "应该"，与 should 基本相同。

3. I must get enough sleep. → must + 动词原形 "必须"，表必要性。

4. You'd better go to bed before 10 p.m. → had better + 动词原形 "最好"，强烈推荐。

5. Sleep is important for your health. → It's important to ... 句型。

📚 语法聚焦 · 情态动词 & 常用句型

1. should / ought to 提建议

句式结构示例
肯定should / ought to + 动词原形You should drink more water.
否定should not (shouldn't) / ought not to + 动词原形You shouldn't eat too much sugar.
疑问Should + 主语 + 动词原形?Should I see a doctor?

2. must / have to 表必要性

句式结构示例
必须（主观）must + 动词原形You must take this medicine.
不得不（客观）have to + 动词原形I have to go to the hospital.
没人称变化must (无三单变化)She must rest. / He has to rest.

3. had better 强烈推荐

句式结构示例
肯定had better + 动词原形You'd better get some sleep.
否定had better not + 动词原形You'd better not stay up late.

4. It's important to ...

结构示例
It's + adj. + to doIt's important to eat a balanced diet.

5. enough 的用法

结构示例
enough + 名词Get enough sleep every night.
形容词 + enoughYou are strong enough to do it.

❌ You should to drink water.（should 后不用 to）

✅ You should drink water.

❌ He musts take medicine.（must 没有三单）

✅ He must take medicine.

❌ You had better don't smoke.（否定是 had better not）

✅ You had better not smoke.

🔤 重点句型 · Sentence Patterns
should / ought to + V
提建议
You should exercise more. / You ought to eat healthy food.
must / have to + V
表必要性
I must rest. / She has to take medicine.
had better (not) + V
强烈推荐
You 'd better sleep early. / You 'd better not stay up.
It's important / good to ...
评价行为
It's important to exercise. / It's good to eat vegetables.
enough + N / adj. + enough
足够
Get enough sleep. / You are healthy enough.
📖 阅读专项 · Healthy Body, Healthy Mind

Do you know that your body and mind are connected? When you take care of your body, your mind feels better too.

Exercise is a great way to keep both your body and mind healthy. When you run, swim or play sports, your body produces chemicals that make you feel happy. That's why people often feel good after exercise!

What you eat also matters. Foods rich in vitamins, like fruits and vegetables, give you energy and help your brain work well. On the other hand, too much junk food can make you feel tired and slow.

Sleep is just as important. A good night's sleep helps your body recover and your brain organise what you learned during the day. Without enough sleep, it's hard to focus in class.

Finally, don't forget to rest and relax. Taking breaks helps reduce stress. A healthy body and a healthy mind work together. So eat well, stay active, sleep enough, and take time to relax!

🏆 中考题型专练 · 完形填空

阅读短文，选择最佳选项。

Staying healthy is important for everyone. First, you (1)____ eat a balanced diet. (2)____ is important to eat fruits and vegetables. They give you (3)____. Second, you (4)____ exercise regularly — at least three times a week. You'd (5)____ not sit in front of the computer for too long. (6)____, don't forget to get enough sleep. A good sleep helps you grow and stay active.

1. A. should B. mustn't C. shouldn't
2. A. That B. It C. This
3. A. medicine B. energy C. hospital
4. A. ought B. should C. must to
5. A. better B. worse C. best
6. A. Finally B. Firstly C. However
答案
1. A (should)；2. B (It)；3. B (energy)；4. B (should)；5. A (better)；6. A (Finally)
✍️ 写作进阶 · 5 Tips for Staying Healthy
📋 写作题目
请写一篇 70 词左右的短文，给出 5 条保持健康的建议。使用 should/ought to、must/have to 或 had better 中的至少三种结构。
开头：总述健康的重要性。
主体：5 条具体建议（每条用不同的表达）。
结尾：总结鼓励。
Staying healthy is very important. Here are five tips for you. First, you should eat a balanced diet with lots of fruits and vegetables. Second, you ought to exercise for at least 30 minutes every day. Third, you must get enough sleep — about 8 to 9 hours each night. Fourth, you'd better drink plenty of water instead of sugary drinks. Finally, you had better not stay up late. Take care of your body, and you will feel great!
保持健康非常重要。这里有五条建议。第一，你应该吃均衡的饮食，多吃水果蔬菜。第二，你应该每天至少锻炼 30 分钟。第三，你必须保证充足睡眠——每晚 8 到 9 小时。第四，你最好多喝水，少喝含糖饮料。最后，最好不要熬夜。照顾好你的身体，你会感觉很棒！
