I like music that I can dance to.

偏好与定从

📖 话题：Preferences and Attributive Clauses💬 功能：Express preferences and talk about music/movies📚 语法：Attributive clauses with that/who/which📝 写作：Write about your favorite music, movie, or book

🎯 我能做到

📖 课文精讲 · I like music that I can dance to.
场景：Jenny和Mike在学校音乐节上讨论自己喜欢的音乐类型。
Jenny:What kind of music do you like, Mike?
Mike:I like music that I can dance to. It makes me feel happy.
Jenny:I prefer music that has great lyrics. Songs that tell a story are my favorite.
Mike:Do you like the band playing on stage right now?
Jenny:Yes, I love them! They are a band that plays different styles of music.
Mike:Who is the singer that is wearing a red jacket?
Jenny:That's Amy. She is a singer who writes her own songs.
Mike:Wow, that's cool! I like musicians who are creative.
📖 查看翻译

Jenny: 你喜欢什么类型的音乐，迈克？

Mike: 我喜欢能跟着跳舞的音乐。它让我感到快乐。

Jenny: 我更喜欢有好歌词的音乐。能讲故事的歌是我的最爱。

Mike: 你喜欢现在在台上表演的乐队吗？

Jenny: 是的，我很喜欢！他们是一个演奏不同风格音乐的乐队。

Mike: 那个穿红色夹克的歌手是谁？

Jenny: 那是艾米。她是一个自己写歌的歌手。

Mike: 哇，太酷了！我喜欢有创造力的音乐人。

🔑 重点讲解：

定语从句修饰名词或代词，相当于形容词的作用。

that 可以指人或物 — I like music that I can dance to.

who 只指人 — She is a singer who writes her own songs.

which 只指物 — I prefer movies which are funny.

🔤 重点句型 · Sentence Patterns
主语 + 谓语 + 名词 + that + 从句 (that 指人或物)
用 that 引导定语从句修饰名词。
I like music that I can dance to. / She likes movies that are funny.
主语 + 谓语 + 名词 + who + 从句 (who 指人)
用 who 引导定语从句修饰人。
I prefer singers who write their own songs. / He is a teacher who loves his job.
主语 + 谓语 + 名词 + which + 从句 (which 指物)
用 which 引导定语从句修饰物。
The book which I borrowed is interesting. / The song which she sang is beautiful.
Prefer A to B / Prefer to do A rather than do B
表示偏好。
I prefer pop music to rock. / She prefers to read at home.
📚 语法聚焦 · 定语从句（Attributive Clauses）

关系代词的用法

关系代词指代在从句中的作用例句
that人/物主语/宾语The music that I like is pop.
who人主语/宾语The girl who sings well is Amy.
which物主语/宾语The movie which I saw was great.

关系代词的省略

情况说明例句
作宾语时可省略关系代词在从句中作宾语时可以省略The song (that) she sang was beautiful.
作主语不可省略关系代词在从句中作主语时不可省略The girl who is singing is my friend.

that 可以指人也可以指物，更口语化。

which 只能指物，不能指人。

who 只能指人，不能指物。

关系代词在从句中作宾语时可以省略（即从句中已有主语）。

Music is the medicine of the mind. 音乐是心灵的良药。

📖 阅读专项 · What Kind of Music Do You Like?

Music is an important part of our lives. Different people like different kinds of music. Some people like music that is loud and energetic. Others prefer music that is quiet and peaceful.

I have a friend named Sarah. She likes music that she can dance to. She says dancing makes her feel free and happy. Her favorite band is one that plays upbeat pop music.

My brother, on the other hand, prefers music that tells a story. He loves folk music and songs that have deep lyrics. He especially likes musicians who sing about life and nature.

As for me, I like all kinds of music. I believe that every song has something special. Music that moves our hearts is always good music, no matter what style it is. After all, music is a language that everyone can understand.

🏆 中考题型专练 · 短文填空 — 关系代词

用 that, who, which 填空。

I have a friend 1 loves movies. She only watches movies 2 make her laugh. She says comedies are the best. Her favorite actor is one 3 is very funny. Last week, she recommended a movie 4 I should watch. It was the funniest movie 5 I have ever seen! I now understand why she likes comedies.

1 A. who B. which C. what D. when
2 A. who B. that C. what D. whose
3 A. which B. who C. whom D. what
4 A. who B. whom C. that D. what
5 A. that B. what C. who D. whom
答案
1. A；2. B；3. B；4. C；5. A
