A Taste of Art

艺术鉴赏 · 不定式用法 · 艺术表达

📖 话题：Art and artistic expression💬 功能：Express opinions about art; describe artworks📚 语法：不定式作宾语/状语/宾语补足语 (to-infinitive as object/adverbial/object complement)📝 写作：介绍一件艺术作品

🎯 我能做到

📖 课文精讲 · At the Art Gallery
场景：Sarah、Mike和Emma一起参观美术馆，讨论他们喜欢的艺术作品。
Sarah:Wow! This painting is amazing. I want to learn more about abstract art.
Mike:I agree. The artist used bright colors to express strong emotions.
Emma:I prefer realistic paintings. They teach me how to see beauty in everyday life.
Sarah:That's true. But I think art is about expressing yourself. Everyone can decide what art means to them.
Mike:Look at this sculpture! The artist used stone to create a sense of movement.
Emma:I asked the guide to explain the meaning of this piece. She said it represents freedom.
Sarah:Art makes me think. I hope to visit more galleries in the future.
📖 查看翻译

Sarah: 哇！这幅画太棒了。我想多了解一些抽象艺术。

Mike: 我同意。艺术家用明亮的色彩来表达强烈的情感。

Emma: 我更喜欢写实画。它们教会我如何在日常生活中发现美。

Sarah: 没错。但我认为艺术是关于表达自我。每个人都可以决定艺术对他们意味着什么。

Mike: 看这个雕塑！艺术家用石头来创造一种动感。

Emma: 我请导游解释这件作品的含义。她说它代表着自由。

Sarah: 艺术让我思考。我希望将来能参观更多美术馆。

🔑 重点讲解：

I want to learn — 不定式作宾语，want + to do

to express strong emotions — 不定式作目的状语

how to see beauty — 疑问词 + 不定式结构

I asked the guide to explain — 不定式作宾语补足语，ask sb to do

I hope to visit — 不定式作宾语，hope + to do

📚 语法聚焦 · 不定式 (To-Infinitive)

一、不定式作宾语 (Object)

动词结构例句
wantwant + to doI want to learn more about art.
decidedecide + to doShe decided to become an artist.
hopehope + to doWe hope to visit the museum tomorrow.
planplan + to doThey plan to hold an exhibition next month.

二、不定式作状语 (Adverbial)

类型结构例句
目的状语to do / in order to doHe used bright colors to express emotions.
She practiced every day in order to improve her technique.
结果状语so as to doHe studied the painting carefully so as to understand its meaning.

三、不定式作宾语补足语 (Object Complement)

动词结构例句
askask sb + to doI asked the guide to explain the painting.
telltell sb + to doThe teacher told us to draw a self-portrait.
wantwant sb + to doMy parents want me to study art history.
would likewould like sb + to doI would like you to see this sculpture.

四、疑问词 + 不定式

结构用法例句
how + to do方式、方法She taught me how to mix colors.
what + to do做什么I don't know what to paint next.
when + to do时间Can you tell me when to start the drawing?
where + to do地点He showed me where to buy art supplies.

不定式 (to-infinitive) 是"to + 动词原形"的结构，在句中可作宾语、状语和宾语补足语。

作宾语时，常用在 want, decide, hope, plan, wish, expect 等动词之后。

作目的状语时，可以放在句尾或句首，还可用 in order to / so as to 来强调目的。

作宾语补足语时，结构为"动词 + 宾语 + to do"，常见的动词有 ask, tell, want, would like, expect 等。

疑问词 (how/what/when/where) + 不定式 = 简化的宾语从句，如：I know how to draw → I know how I should draw.

📖 阅读专项 · The Language of Art

Have you ever looked at a painting and felt something, even before you understood what it was about? That is the power of art. Art is a universal language that speaks to people across different cultures and time periods.

Long before written language existed, early humans used cave paintings to communicate. They drew animals, hunting scenes, and symbols on cave walls. These paintings tell us stories about how people lived thousands of years ago. In a way, art was the first form of human communication.

Today, art continues to bring people together. A painting from China can be understood and appreciated by someone in France. A sculpture from Africa can inspire an artist in Brazil. Art crosses borders and connects hearts. It allows us to share emotions and ideas without using words.

Art also changes as society changes. Different art movements — from Renaissance to Impressionism to Pop Art — reflect the values and concerns of their time. The mediums artists use have also evolved, from stone and paint to photography and digital media.

So next time you visit a museum or gallery, take your time. Let the artwork "speak" to you. You might be surprised by what you discover. After all, art is not just something to look at — it is something to feel.

✍️ 写作进阶 · My Favorite Artwork
📋 写作题目
选择一件你最喜欢的艺术作品（绘画、雕塑或建筑），写一篇短文介绍它。请包括：作品的名称和艺术家、作品的材质和风格、你对它的感受和评价。要求使用至少三个不定式结构。
开头：介绍你最喜欢的艺术作品名称和艺术家
主体1：描述作品的外观（颜色、形状、风格等）
主体2：表达你对作品的感受和看法
结尾：总结为什么这件作品对你很重要
My favorite artwork is "Starry Night" by Vincent van Gogh. He used thick brushstrokes and bright colors to show a night sky full of movement. I decided to learn more about this painting after seeing it in a book. The artist wanted people to feel the energy of the night. Every time I look at it, I feel calm and inspired. My teacher told me to pay attention to how the lines flow like waves. I plan to visit the museum in New York to see the real painting one day. Art like this teaches us how to see beauty in ordinary moments.
我最喜欢的艺术作品是文森特·梵高的《星月夜》。他用厚重的笔触和明亮的色彩来表现充满动感的夜空。我在书中看到这幅画后决定了解更多。艺术家希望人们感受到夜晚的能量。每次看到它，我都感到平静和受到启发。我的老师告诉我要注意线条如水波般流动。我计划有一天去纽约的博物馆看真迹。这样的艺术教会我们如何在平凡时刻发现美。
