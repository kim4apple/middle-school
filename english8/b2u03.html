Could you please clean your room?

家务劳动

📖 话题：Housework & Polite Requests💬 功能：Make polite requests and ask for permission📚 语法：Could you please…? 礼貌请求 · Could I…? 请求许可 · 祈使句 vs 请求句📝 写作：A Note to a Family Member

🎯 我能做到

📖 课文精讲 · Could you please clean your room?
场景：周末早上，妈妈和 Mike 之间的对话。
Mom:Mike, could you please clean your room? It's a mess!
Mike:Sure, Mom. I'll do it after breakfast. Could I play computer games after I finish?
Mom:Yes, you can. But could you also take out the trash?
Mike:Of course. And could I invite Tom over this afternoon?
Mom:I'm sorry, but we're going out this afternoon. How about this weekend?
Mike:OK. Could you please help me with my math homework later?
Mom:Sure, I'd be happy to. Let's finish the chores first!
📖 查看翻译

Mom: Mike，请打扫下你的房间好吗？太乱了！

Mike: 好的妈妈。我吃完早饭就做。做完可以玩电脑游戏吗？

Mom: 可以。但你能顺便倒个垃圾吗？

Mike: 没问题。那我能邀请 Tom 下午来家里吗？

Mom: 抱歉，我们下午要出门。这周末怎么样？

Mike: 好吧。那一会儿你能帮我辅导数学作业吗？

Mom: 好的，很乐意。我们先做完家务！

🔑 重点讲解：

1. Could you please clean your room? → Could you please + 动词原形? 礼貌请求别人做某事。

2. Could I play computer games after I finish? → Could I + 动词原形? 请求许可做某事。

3. 肯定回答：Sure / Of course / Certainly. 否定回答：Sorry, but…

4. Could I invite Tom over? → invite…over "邀请……来家里"。

📚 语法聚焦 · Could 礼貌请求与许可

Could 请求句式对比

句式用途示例
Could you please + v.?请求对方做某事Could you please open the window?
Could I + v.?请求许可自己做Could I borrow your pen?
Can I + v.?非正式请求许可Can I have a glass of water?

📖 阅读专项 · Sharing Housework Makes a Happy Family

In many families, only parents do the housework. But sharing chores is important for everyone.

Doing chores teaches children responsibility. When kids help with housework, they learn to take care of themselves and their home. It also makes parents feel less tired.

In my family, everyone has a job. I sweep the floor and take out the trash. My brother does the dishes. My parents do the cooking and shopping.

Sometimes I ask: "Could I help with dinner?" I enjoy learning to cook. Sharing housework is not just about cleaning—it's about caring for each other.

responsibility n. 责任 take care of 照顾；负责 share v. 分享；分担 caring adj. 关爱的

🏆 中考题型专练 · 补全对话

从方框中选择合适的句子补全对话。

A: Mom, (1)________?
B: Sure, what is it?
A: (2)________ My friends are waiting for me.
B: I'm sorry, but you need to finish your homework first.
A: (3)________ I'll do it right after I come back.
B: OK then. (4)________
A: Thanks, Mom! I promise I'll finish it.

答案
(1) Could I go out to play?；(2) Could you please let me go now?；(3) Could I finish it later?；(4) But don't be too late.
✍️ 写作进阶 · A Note to a Family Member
📋 写作题目
请给家人写一张便条(Note)，礼貌地请求他们帮忙做一件事（打扫房间、买东西等），用 Could you please。
开头：称呼+礼貌请求。
主体：说明需要帮忙的事及原因。
结尾：提前感谢。
Dear Mom, Could you please help me buy some art supplies? I need colored paper and glue for my school project. It's due on Friday. I have basketball practice after school, so I can't go myself. Thank you so much! I'll finish my homework before dinner. Love, Tom
亲爱的妈妈：能帮我买些美术用品吗？学校项目需要彩纸和胶水，周五要交。我放学后有篮球训练，自己去不了。非常感谢！我会在晚饭前写完作业。爱你的 Tom
🎯 Project · Polite Request Role-Play
Polite Request Role-Play
和搭档编写一段 6-8 轮的对话，场景自选（家里/学校/商店），必须用到 Could you please 和 Could I。
1选择一个场景。
2编写对话脚本（6-8 个来回）。
3用不同颜色的笔标出请求句。
4和搭档排练并在班里表演。
#评价标准
1.场景合理
2.包含 3 次以上 Could 请求
3.回应自然
4.表演流利有感情
