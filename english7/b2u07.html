七下 · Unit 7

It's raining!

天气描述 · 正在进行的事 · 电话用语

📖 话题：Weather 💬 功能：Describe weather · Talk about ongoing activities 📚 语法：现在进行时 (be + v-ing) · How's the weather? 📝 写作：描写天气的明信片

🎯 我能做到

📖 课文精讲 · Talking about the weather
场景：妈妈打电话给在外地的女儿，询问当地的天气和正在做的事。
Mom: Hi, Mary! How's it going?
Mary: Hi, Mom! Pretty good. I'm in Beijing now.
Mom: How's the weather in Beijing?
Mary: It's raining! What are you doing, Mom?
Mom: I'm cooking dinner. Is Dad at home?
Mary: No, he's working in the garden. It's sunny there.
Mom: Sounds like you're having a nice time!
Mary: Yeah. I'm visiting the Great Wall tomorrow. Talk to you later!
📖 查看翻译

逐句翻译：

妈妈: 嗨，Mary！最近怎么样？

Mary: 嗨，妈妈！挺好的。我现在在北京。

妈妈: 北京的天气怎么样？

Mary: 正在下雨！妈妈，你在做什么？

妈妈: 我在做饭。爸爸在家吗？

Mary: 不，他在花园里干活。那里天气晴朗。

妈妈: 听起来你玩得很开心！

Mary: 是的。我明天去参观长城。回头聊！

🔑 重点讲解：

1. How's it going? → "最近怎么样？" 问候语，回答用 Pretty good! / Not bad. / Terrible.

2. How's the weather? → 问天气，也可说 What's the weather like? 回答：It's raining/sunny/cloudy/windy.

3. I'm cooking dinner. → 现在进行时 (am/is/are + v-ing)，表示正在进行的动作。

4. Sounds like... → "听起来像……"，省略了 It，口语常用。

📖 Reading: Postcard about the weather

Dear Mom and Dad, I'm writing this postcard from London.

The weather is cloudy and cold. It's going to rain, so I'm staying in my hotel room.

Right now I'm drinking hot tea and looking out the window.

I'm also reading a book about London's history.

I hope the weather gets better tomorrow! Yours, Lily

🔤 重点句型 · Sentence Patterns
How's the weather? / What's the weather like?
两个句型都用来询问天气情况。
✅ How's the weather in Beijing?
✅ What's the weather like in Shanghai?
➡ 回答：It's sunny / cloudy / raining / windy.
What are you doing? (现在进行时)
问某人正在做什么，用现在进行时 am/is/are + v-ing。
✅ What are you doing? — I'm reading a book.
✅ What is she doing? — She's cooking dinner.
✅ What are they doing? — They're playing soccer.
How's it going? 问候用语
打电话或见面时询问近况。
✅ How's it going?
➡ Pretty good! / Not bad. / Terrible! / Just so-so.
✅ How's your study going?
➡ It's going well.
Sounds like + 从句
"听起来……"，省略 It，后跟从句或名词短语。
✅ Sounds like you're having fun.
✅ Sounds like a good idea.
➡ 完整形式：It sounds like...
📚 语法聚焦 · Present Continuous Tense

1️⃣ 现在进行时的构成：am/is/are + v-ing

表示现在（说话的瞬间）正在进行的动作。

主语be 动词v-ing例句
IamreadingI am reading a book.
He / She / ItiscookingShe is cooking dinner.
You / We / TheyareplayingThey are playing soccer.

2️⃣ v-ing 变化规则

规则原形v-ing 形式
大多数 + ingplayplaying
以不发音 e 结尾，去 e + ingtaketaking
重读闭音节(辅+元+辅)，双写末字母 + ingrunrunning
以 ie 结尾，改 ie 为 y + inglielying

3️⃣ 一般疑问句与回答

将 be 动词提到主语前面：

陈述句一般疑问句肯定回答否定回答
He is sleeping.Is he sleeping?Yes, he is.No, he isn't.
They are watching TV.Are they watching TV?Yes, they are.No, they aren't.
⚠️ 中式英语纠错：
❌ He is cook dinner.（be + 原形，缺少 ing）
✅ He is cooking dinner.

❌ She is reading a book yesterday.（现在进行时不能用于过去时间）
✅ She read a book yesterday.（用一般过去时）
📝 三级闯关 · 实战练习

🥉 基础关 · 概念识记

基础 ★☆☆
How's the ____ in Beijing? — It's sunny. (weather / weathers)
📝 查看答案
答案：weather
解析：weather 是不可数名词，没有复数形式。
基础 ★☆☆
My sister ____ (read) a book in her room now.
📝 查看答案
答案：is reading
解析：now 提示用现在进行时，主语 My sister 是第三人称单数，用 is reading。
基础 ★☆☆
It's ____ (snow) outside. Let's make a snowman.
📝 查看答案
答案：snowing
解析：It's + v-ing 表示天气状况，snow → snowing。

🥈 提升关 · 语境运用

提升 ★★☆
改错：What are you do now?
📝 查看答案
答案：What are you doing now?
解析：现在进行时用 v-ing 形式，do → doing。
提升 ★★☆
连词成句：weather / like / what's / the / today / ?
📝 查看答案
答案：What's the weather like today?
解析：What's the weather like? 是询问天气的固定句型。
提升 ★★☆
用现在进行时改写：She reads a magazine every night. (now)
📝 查看答案
答案：She is reading a magazine now.
解析：将动词变为 be + v-ing，删除 every night 添加 now。

🥇 挑战关 · 综合运用

挑战 ★★★
汉译英：北京的天气怎么样？正在下雪。孩子们在堆雪人。
📝 查看答案
答案：How's the weather in Beijing? It's snowing. The children are making a snowman.
解析：天气用 It's snowing；孩子们正在做某事用 are making。
挑战 ★★★
补全对话：
A: Hello! ____ is Tom?
B: Sorry, he's not here. He ____ (swim) at the pool.
A: ____ the weather today?
B: It's sunny and hot.
📝 查看答案
答案：May I speak to; is swimming; What's the weather like / How's
解析：电话用语 May I speak to...；现在进行时 is swimming；询问天气用 How's / What's...like。
