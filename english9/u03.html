Smart Learning

高效学习 · 宾语从句 · 学习方法

📖 话题：Effective Learning Strategies💬 功能：Give advice on learning; express opinions📚 语法：宾语从句 (that/if/whether) · 疑问词 + 不定式📝 写作：学习建议

🎯 我能做到

📖 课文精讲 · Smart Learning
场景：Lucy、Tom和Lily在讨论各自的学习方法。
Lucy:I think that online courses are really helpful for self-study.
Tom:I agree. But I'm not sure if I can stay focused while studying online.
Lily:I prefer group study. I believe that discussing with others helps me understand better.
Lucy:That's a good point. Do you know how to make flashcards for vocabulary?
Tom:Yes! I think that flashcards are great for memorizing words quickly.
Lily:I wonder whether taking notes by hand is more effective than typing.
Lucy:I believe that different methods work for different people. The key is to find what works for you.
📖 查看翻译

Lucy: 我觉得在线课程对自学非常有帮助。

Tom: 我同意。但我不确定在线上课时能否保持专注。

Lily: 我更喜欢小组学习。我相信和别人讨论能帮助我更好地理解。

Lucy: 有道理。你知道怎么做词汇闪卡吗？

Tom: 知道！我觉得闪卡能快速记住单词。

Lily: 我想知道手写笔记是否比打字更有效。

Lucy: 我相信不同的方法适用于不同的人。关键是找到适合自己的方法。

🔑 重点讲解：

I think that... 表示「我认为……」 — I think that online courses are helpful.

I'm not sure if/whether... 表示「我不确定是否……」 — I'm not sure if I can stay focused.

I wonder whether... 表示「我想知道是否……」 — I wonder whether taking notes is effective.

Do you know how to... — 疑问词 + 不定式结构，表示「你知道怎么……吗」

📚 语法聚焦 · 宾语从句 & 疑问词 + 不定式

一、宾语从句 (that/if/whether)

引导词用法例句
that陈述句作宾语，that可省略I think (that) online courses are helpful.
if / whether一般疑问句作宾语，"是否"I'm not sure if I can focus.
He asked whether I studied every day.

宾语从句语序

原问句错误语序正确语序
Does he study hard?I wonder does he study hard?I wonder if he studies hard.
Can I improve?I'm not sure can I improve.I'm not sure if I can improve.
Is this method good?He asked whether is this method good.He asked whether this method is good.

二、疑问词 + 不定式

结构用法例句
how + to do方式、方法Do you know how to make flashcards?
what + to do做什么I don't know what to review first.
when + to do时间Can you tell me when to start?
where + to do地点She showed me where to find the notes.

三、常用表达

结构用法例句
It's + adj. + to do做某事是……的It's important to review regularly.
The more... the more...越……就越……The more you practice, the more you improve.

宾语从句必须用陈述句语序：引导词 + 主语 + 谓语。

if 和 whether 都表示"是否"，但在正式文体中 whether 更常用，且 whether 可与 or not 连用。

疑问词 + 不定式 = 简化的宾语从句，如：I know how to study → I know how I should study.

The more you read, the more you know. 读书越多，知道的越多。

📖 阅读专项 · Learning Smarter, Not Harder

Do you often feel tired after studying for hours but still don't remember what you learned? You are not alone. The key is not to study harder, but to study smarter.

One powerful technique is spaced repetition. Instead of reviewing everything at once, space out your review sessions. For example, after learning new vocabulary, review it after one day, then three days, then one week. This helps move information into your long-term memory.

Another effective method is active recall. Instead of just reading your notes, try to remember the information without looking. Close your book and ask yourself: "What did I just learn?" This forces your brain to work harder and strengthens your memory.

Finally, remember to take breaks. Studying for too long without a break reduces focus and efficiency. Try the Pomodoro Technique — study for 25 minutes, then take a 5-minute break. Your brain needs time to rest and process new information.

By using these techniques, you will learn more in less time. Remember: work smarter, not harder!

✍️ 写作进阶 · My Learning Tips
📋 写作题目
假设你是一名学习经验丰富的学长/学姐，写一段文字向低年级同学分享你的学习建议。请使用至少两个宾语从句和两个"疑问词 + 不定式"结构。
开头：问候并引出话题
主体1：你的学习方法（计划、笔记、复习）
主体2：学习中遇到的困难及解决方法
主体3：鼓励和祝福
Hi everyone! I believe that everyone can become a good learner. Here are some tips for you. First, I think that making a study plan is very important. It helps you know what to do every day. Second, try to take notes in class. Do you know how to take effective notes? Write down the key points, not every word. Third, review regularly. I'm not sure if you like reviewing, but it really helps! Remember: the more you practice, the better you will get. Finally, don't be afraid to ask questions. Learning is a journey, not a race. Keep going and enjoy the process!
大家好！我相信每个人都能成为好的学习者。这里有一些建议给你们。首先，我觉得制定学习计划非常重要。它帮助你每天知道做什么。第二，尽量在课堂上做笔记。你知道如何做有效的笔记吗？写下关键点，而不是每个字。第三，定期复习。我不确定你们是否喜欢复习，但这真的很有帮助！记住：你练习得越多，就会变得越好。最后，不要害怕问问题。学习是一场旅程，不是一场比赛。继续前进，享受这个过程吧！
