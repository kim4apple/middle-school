When Tomorrow Comes

未来预测 · 环保 · will/won't

📖 话题：The future and predictions💬 功能：Make predictions, express opinions📚 语法：will/won't · 条件句 if · 可能/可能不会📝 写作：Predict the future

🎯 我能做到

📖 课文精讲 · When Tomorrow Comes
场景：朋友们在讨论 50 年后生活会是什么样子。
Tom:What do you think life will be like in 50 years?
Li Hua:I think there will be AI robots everywhere. They will help us with everything.
Anna:Will people still drive cars?
Tom:No, they won't. Cars will drive themselves. There will be fewer accidents.
Li Hua:And there will be more electric cars, so pollution will be a thing of the past.
Anna:If we use renewable energy, the planet will be cleaner.
Tom:I hope so. What do you think will be different, Li Hua?
Li Hua:People will live on other planets too! Maybe we will have space colonies.
📖 查看翻译

Tom: 你觉得 50 年后生活会是什么样？

Li Hua: 我觉得会有很多 AI 机器人。它们会帮我们做一切。

Anna: 人们还会开车吗？

Tom: 不，不会了。汽车会自动驾驶。事故会更少。

Li Hua: 而且会有更多电动车，所以污染将成为过去式。

Anna: 如果我们使用可再生能源，地球会更干净。

Tom: 希望如此。Li Hua，你觉得还会有哪些不同？

Li Hua: 人们也会住在其他星球上！也许我们会有太空殖民地。

🔑 重点讲解：

1. What do you think life will be like? → will + be like 询问未来样子。

2. There will be AI robots everywhere. → There will be + 名词 "将有……"。

3. Cars will drive themselves. → themselves 反身代词，"它们自己"。

4. If we use renewable energy, the planet will be cleaner. → if + 现在时，will + 动词 条件句。

🔤 重点句型 · Sentence Patterns
will + 动词原形
对未来做预测
People will travel to space. / AI will change the world.
won't + 动词原形
对未来做否定预测
People won't need cash. / Cars won't pollute the air.
There will be + 名词
描述未来存在
There will be more robots. / There will be no pollution.
If + 现在时, will + 动词
条件句表未来可能
If we recycle, the Earth will be cleaner.
in + 年份
表示未来某个时间点
in 2050 / in 50 years / in the future
📚 语法聚焦 · will/won't 与条件句

will/won't 句式

句式结构示例
肯定句主语 + will + 动词原形Robots will help us.
否定句主语 + won't + 动词原形Robots won't take our jobs.
一般疑问句Will + 主语 + 动词原形?Will people live on Mars?
特殊疑问句Wh- + will + 主语 + 动词?Where will people live?

if 条件句（将来可能）

条件结果示例
If + 现在时will + 动词原形If we plant trees, the air will be cleaner.
If + 现在时won't + 动词原形If we don't act, the planet won't survive.

There will be

句式示例
肯定There will be smart cities.
否定There won't be any trash.
疑问Will there be flying cars?

❌ People will lives on Mars.（will 后动词用原形）

✅ People will live on Mars.

❌ There will have smart homes.（固定搭配 there will be）

✅ There will be smart homes.

❌ If we will recycle, we will save the Earth.（if 后不用 will）

✅ If we recycle, we will save the Earth.

📖 阅读专项 · Our Future Planet

What will our planet be like in 2050? Scientists have some predictions.

First, technology will change a lot. AI will be everywhere. Smart robots will do dangerous jobs. People will have robots at home to help with cleaning and cooking.

Second, the environment will get better. There will be more electric cars on the road. If we use renewable energy like solar and wind power, there will be less pollution. More people will recycle and protect the Earth.

Third, transportation will be very different. There will be flying cars and high-speed trains. People will travel to space for holidays!

The future is exciting. But it's also in our hands. If we make good choices today, tomorrow will be a better place!

🏆 中考题型专练 · 完形填空

阅读短文，选择最佳选项。

What (1)____ life be like in 2050? I think there (2)____ be amazing changes. People (3)____ live on other planets. Robots will (4)____ almost all the housework. If we use clean energy, there (5)____ be less pollution. But there (6)____ be new problems too. What do you think we should do?

1. A. will B. is C. are D. was
2. A. is B. are C. will D. was
3. A. is B. are C. will D. won't
4. A. do B. doing C. did D. does
5. A. will B. won't C. is D. are
6. A. will B. won't C. is D. are
答案
1. A (will)；2. C (will)；3. C (will)；4. A (do)；5. A (will)；6. A (will)

请以"What Will Life Be Like in 2050?"为题，写 60-80 词短文，从科技、环境、交通等方面预测未来。

主体：科技+环境+交通三方面。
结尾：我们的选择决定未来。
What will life be like in 2050? I think there will be great changes. First, AI robots will help us with everything. Every home will have a smart robot. Second, the environment will be cleaner. There will be more electric cars and less pollution. People will use solar and wind energy. Third, transportation will be faster. We will travel in flying cars or high-speed trains. Some people will even take vacations in space! The future will be amazing if we take care of our planet today.
2050 年的生活会是什么样？我觉得会有巨大变化。首先，AI 机器人会帮我们做一切。每个家庭都会有智能机器人。其次，环境会更干净。会有更多电动车，更少污染。人们会使用太阳能和风能。第三，交通会更快。我们会坐飞行汽车或高铁出行。有些人甚至去太空度假！如果我们现在照顾好地球，未来会很美好。
