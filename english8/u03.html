Same or Different?

比较人物 · 形容词/副词比较级 · as ... as

📖 话题：Personal traits and comparisons💬 功能：Compare people and things📚 语法：形容词/副词比较级 (-er/more) · as ... as · both/also📝 写作：比较两个朋友

🎯 我能做到

📖 课文精讲 · Two Friends
场景：课间，李明和王芳正在聊他们各自的性格和习惯。
Li Ming:Wang Fang, do you think we are similar or different?
Wang Fang:I think we're quite different. I'm more outgoing than you.
Li Ming:That's true. You are also more active. But I think I'm more careful.
Wang Fang:Yes, you are. And you are more hard-working than me. I'm a little lazy sometimes!
Li Ming:But we are both friendly and kind. That's why we're good friends.
Wang Fang:I agree. Although we are different, we get along very well.
📖 查看翻译

Li Ming: 王芳，你觉得我们相似还是不同？

Wang Fang: 我觉得我们很不一样。我比你更外向。

Li Ming: 没错。你也更活跃。但我认为我更细心。

Wang Fang: 是的。你更勤奋。我有时候有点懒！

Li Ming: 但我们都很友好、善良。这就是我们做好朋友的原因。

Wang Fang: 我同意。虽然我们不一样，但相处得很好。

🔑 重点讲解：

1. I'm more outgoing than you. → 多音节形容词比较级：more + adj. + than

2. You are more hard-working than me. → more + 多音节形容词 + than

3. We are both friendly and kind. → both 意为"两者都"，放在 be 动词之后。

4. Although we are different, we get along well. → Although 引导让步状语从句，"虽然……但是……"。

🔤 重点句型 · Sentence Patterns
比较级 + than
用比较级比较两个人
She is taller than her brother.
more + adj. + than
多音节形容词的比较级
He is more outgoing than me.
as + 原级 + as
同级比较（肯定）
Tom is as tall as Mike.
not as + 原级 + as
同级比较（否定）
He is not as outgoing as his brother.
📚 语法聚焦 · 形容词比较级

1. 比较级构成规则

规则构成方法示例
单音节/部分双音节+ertall → taller; short → shorter
以 e 结尾+rlarge → larger; nice → nicer
辅音+y 结尾变 y 为 i + erfunny → funnier; heavy → heavier
重读闭音节双写尾字母 + erthin → thinner; big → bigger
多音节/部分双音节more + 原级outgoing → more outgoing; careful → more careful

2. as ... as 同级比较

句式含义例句
as + adj. + as和……一样Tom is as tall as Mike.
not as + adj. + as不如……He is not as outgoing as his brother.

3. both 的用法

both + 名词复数Both students are smart.
both of + 代词Both of them are friendly.
be both / both + 动词They are both kind. / They both like sports.

4. Although 让步状语从句

Although + 从句, 主句Although they are different, they get on well.

❌ She is more taller than me.（taller 已是比较级，不加 more）

✅ She is taller than me.

❌ He is as tall than his brother.（同级比较用 as…as，不用 than）

✅ He is as tall as his brother.

❌ Although they are different, but they are friends.（although 不与 but 连用）

✅ Although they are different, they are friends.

📖 阅读专项 · Best Friends — Same or Different?

Tom and Tim are best friends. They are in the same class, but they are very different from each other.

Tom is tall and thin. He is outgoing and loves to talk. He is active in sports and plays basketball very well. Tim is short and heavy. He is quieter and more serious. He enjoys reading and playing chess.

Although they are different in many ways, they share one thing — they are both honest and kind. They help each other with homework and spend weekends together.

"Good friends don't need to be the same," says Tom. "We learn from each other's differences."

share v. 分享；共有 chess n. 国际象棋 each other 互相 difference n. 不同之处

🏆 中考题型专练 · 完形填空

阅读短文，选择最佳选项。

My best friend Amy is very different (1)____ me. She is (2)____ (outgoing) than me, and I am (3)____ (quiet) than her. However, we have a lot (4)____ common. We are (5)____ kind and honest. A true friend (6)____ for your hand and touches your heart.

1. A. to B. from C. with D. as
2. A. outgoing B. more outgoing C. most outgoing D. outgoinger
3. A. quiet B. quieter C. quietest D. more quiet
4. A. at B. on C. in D. of
5. A. both B. all C. either D. neither
6. A. reach B. reaches C. reaching D. reached
答案
1. B (from)；2. B (more outgoing)；3. B (quieter)；4. C (in)；5. A (both)；6. B (reaches)
✍️ 写作进阶 · Compare Yourself and Your Best Friend
📋 写作题目
请以"My Best Friend and I"为题，用比较级和同级比较描述你和好朋友之间的相同点和不同点（60-80 词）。
开头：介绍你的好朋友是谁。
主体：写 2-3 个不同点（比较级）和 2-3 个相同点（both / as…as）。
结尾：总结虽然不同，但彼此珍惜友情。
My best friend is Li Hua. He is taller and thinner than me, but I am more hard-working than him. He is more outgoing and active in class. I am quieter and more serious about my studies. However, we have a lot in common. We are both kind and honest. He is as smart as me. Although we are different, we help each other and learn from each other. A good friend is like a mirror!
我最好的朋友是李华。他比我高瘦，但我比他更勤奋。他上课更外向活跃。我比较安静，学习更认真。不过，我们有很多共同点。我们都很善良、诚实。他和我一样聪明。虽然我们不同，但我们互相帮助、互相学习。好朋友就像一面镜子！
