Unit 1

You and Me

自我介绍 · 个人信息 · 国籍城市 · be 动词

📖 话题：Getting to know each other 💬 功能：Introduce yourself and ask about others 📚 语法：be 动词 (am/is/are) · 缩写形式 📝 写作：个人信息介绍段落

🎯 我能做到

📖 课文精讲 · You and Me
场景：开学第一周，两位新同学在课间聊天。
Peter: Hi! I'm Peter. What's your name?
Chen Jie: My name is Chen Jie. Nice to meet you, Peter!
Peter: Nice to meet you too, Chen Jie! Where are you from?
Chen Jie: I'm from Beijing. It's the capital of China. Where are you from?
Peter: I'm from London. It's the capital of the UK.
Chen Jie: Cool! How old are you, Peter?
Peter: I'm 12 years old. And you?
Chen Jie: I'm 12 too. We're the same age!
Peter: Great! We're good friends now!
📖 查看翻译

逐句翻译：

Peter: 嗨！我是 Peter。你叫什么名字？

Chen Jie: 我叫陈洁。很高兴认识你，Peter！

Peter: 我也很高兴认识你，陈洁！你来自哪里？

Chen Jie: 我来自北京。它是中国的首都。你来自哪里？

Peter: 我来自伦敦。它是英国的首都。

Chen Jie: 酷！你多大了，Peter？

Peter: 我 12 岁。你呢？

Chen Jie: 我也 12 岁。我们同龄！

Peter: 太棒了！我们现在是好朋友了！

🔑 重点讲解：

1. Where are you from? — 询问"你来自哪里？"回答用 I'm from + 地点。

2. How old are you? — 询问"你多大了？"回答用 I'm + 数字 + years old。

3. be 动词三兄弟: I 用 am，you 用 are，he/she/it 用 is。缩写：I'm, You're, He's, She's, It's。

4. Capital: 首都。Beijing is the capital of China. London is the capital of the UK.

5. Too: "也"，用于肯定句末尾。如：I'm 12 too.

🔤 重点句型 · Introductions & Questions
What's your name? — My name is ... / I'm ...
询问姓名和回答的两种方式。
✅ What's your name?
➡ My name is Chen Jie.
➡ I'm Peter.
Where are you from? — I'm from ...
询问来自哪里，回答用 I'm from + 地点。
✅ Where are you from?
➡ I'm from Beijing.
➡ I'm from London.
➡ 句型：Where + be + 主语 + from?
How old are you? — I'm ... years old.
询问年龄，回答用 I'm + 数字 + years old。
✅ How old are you?
➡ I'm 12 years old.
➡ I'm 13 years old.
➡ 简略回答：I'm 12.
Are you ...? — Yes, I am. / No, I'm not.
用 be 动词构成的一般疑问句。
✅ Are you a student?
➡ Yes, I am. / No, I'm not.
✅ Is he your friend?
➡ Yes, he is. / No, he isn't.
📚 语法聚焦 · Be Verbs & Possessives

1️⃣ Be 动词：am / is / are

人称be 动词缩写例句
I（我）amI'mI am a student. → I'm 12.
You（你/你们）areyou'reYou are my friend.
He / She / It（他/她/它）ishe's / she's / it'sHe is from London.
We（我们）arewe'reWe are in the same class.
They（他们/她们/它们）arethey'reThey are good friends.
💡 口诀：I 用 am，you 用 are，is 用于 he/she/it。单数 is，复数 are。

2️⃣ 缩写形式 (Contractions)

完整形式缩写示例
I amI'mI'm from Beijing.
You areYou'reYou're my friend.
He isHe'sHe's from London.
She isShe'sShe's a student.
It isIt'sIt's the capital.
We areWe'reWe're in the same class.
They areThey'reThey're good friends.
💡 注意：缩写中的撇号 (') 表示省略了字母。如 I'm = I am，You're = You are。

3️⃣ Where are you from? 句型

提问回答
Where are you from?I'm from Beijing.
Where is he from?He is from London.
Where is she from?She is from Sydney.

📌 结构：Where + be + 主语 + from?

4️⃣ How old are you? 句型

提问回答
How old are you?I'm 12 (years old).
How old is he?He is 13 (years old).
How old is she?She is 12 (years old).

📌 结构：How old + be + 主语? (years old 可省略)

5️⃣ 形容词性物主代词：my / your / his / her

人称物主代词例句
第一人称my 我的My name is Chen Jie.
第二人称your 你的；你们的What's your name?
第三人称(男)his 他的His name is Peter.
第三人称(女)her 她的Her name is Chen Jie.

📌 形容词性物主代词 后面必须跟名词：my friend, your class, his name, her book。

⚠️ 中式英语纠错：
❌ I name is Peter.（I 是主格，不能修饰名词）
✅ My name is Peter.

❌ What's he name?（he 是主格，要用 his）
✅ What's his name?
📝 三级闯关 · 实战练习

🥉 基础关 · 概念识记

基础 ★☆☆
I ____ a student. (am / is / are)
📝 查看答案
答案：am
解析：I 后面用 am。I am = I'm。
基础 ★☆☆
She ____ from Beijing. (am / is / are)
📝 查看答案
答案：is
解析：She 是第三人称单数，用 is。
基础 ★☆☆
— ____ are you from? — I'm from China.
📝 查看答案
答案：Where
解析：询问"来自哪里"用 Where。Where are you from? 你来自哪里？

🥈 提升关 · 语境运用

提升 ★★☆
— How old ____ you? — I ____ 12 years old.
📝 查看答案
答案：are; am
解析：You 后面用 are；I 后面用 am。
提升 ★★☆
We ____ in the same class. (am / is / are)
📝 查看答案
答案：are
解析：We（我们）是复数，用 are。
提升 ★★☆
你来自哪里？（翻译）
📝 查看答案
答案：Where are you from?
解析：where 表示"哪里"，be 动词用 are（与 you 搭配），from 表示"来自"。

🥇 挑战关 · 综合运用

挑战 ★★★
短文改错：She are from Beijing. She am 12 years old.
📝 查看答案
答案：She is from Beijing. She is 12 years old.
解析：She 是第三人称单数，be 动词用 is，不能用 are 或 am。
挑战 ★★★
连词成句：from / where / you / are / ?
📝 查看答案
答案：Where are you from?
解析：疑问词 Where 放在句首，be 动词紧随其后，最后加 from 和问号。
挑战 ★★★
连词成句：years / I / 13 / old / am / .
📝 查看答案
答案：I am 13 years old.
解析：主语 I 开头，be 动词 am，数字 + years old 表示年龄。
