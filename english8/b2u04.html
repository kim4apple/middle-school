The Wonders of Nature

自然奇观 · 被动语态 · 数字表达

📖 话题：Natural wonders and geography💬 功能：Describe natural wonders, give facts📚 语法：被动语态 (is/are + pp) · 数字表达 (thousand/million/billion)📝 写作：介绍一个自然奇观

🎯 我能做到

📖 课文精讲 · The Wonders of Nature
场景：同学们在聊想去的自然奇观，分享有趣的地理知识。
Li Wei:I really want to visit the Grand Canyon someday. It's one of the most famous natural wonders.
Wang Fang:Me too! The Grand Canyon is located in Arizona, USA. It's about 446 kilometers long.
Zhang Wei:I've read that the Great Barrier Reef is known for its colorful coral and amazing sea life.
Teacher:That's right. It is said that the reef is over 2,300 kilometers long. It's the largest coral reef system in the world.
Li Wei:I'd love to see the Aurora Borealis too. Those colorful lights in the night sky look magical.
Wang Fang:Yes! The Northern Lights can be seen in countries like Norway and Canada. It's a breathtaking sight.
📖 查看翻译

Li Wei: 我真想有一天去大峡谷看看。它是最有名的自然奇观之一。

Wang Fang: 我也是！大峡谷位于美国亚利桑那州，长约 446 公里。

Zhang Wei: 我读过，大堡礁以色彩斑斓的珊瑚和神奇的海洋生物闻名。

Teacher: 没错。据说这个珊瑚礁系统超过 2300 公里长，是世界上最大的珊瑚礁系统。

Li Wei: 我还想去看北极光。夜空中那些五彩缤纷的光看起来很神奇。

Wang Fang: 是的！北极光在挪威、加拿大等国家可以看到。那景象真是叹为观止。

🔑 重点讲解：

1. The Grand Canyon is located in Arizona. → is located "位于"，被动语态表位置。

2. The reef is known for its colorful coral. → is known for "以……闻名"。

3. It is said that the reef is over 2,300 kilometers long. → It is said that… "据说……"。

4. It's about 446 kilometers long. → 数字 + 单位 + 形容词 表度量。

🔤 重点句型 · Sentence Patterns

📚 语法聚焦 · 被动语态 · 大数字表达

被动语态 (Passive Voice: be + past participle)

主动句被动句
People know the reef for its coral.The reef is known for its coral.
Water carved the canyon.The canyon was carved by water.
People find fossils in the area.Fossils are found in the area.
People say the cave is very deep.It is said that the cave is very deep.

大数字 (Large Numbers)

数字英文
1,000one thousand
10,000ten thousand
1,000,000one million
1,000,000,000one billion
7,800,000,000seven point eight billion

问句回答

📖 阅读专项 · Seven Wonders of the Natural World

Our planet is full of amazing natural wonders. Here are some of the most incredible ones.

The Grand Canyon in the USA is a spectacular sight. It is 446 kilometers long, up to 29 kilometers wide, and over 1,800 meters deep. The canyon was formed by the Colorado River over millions of years.

The Great Barrier Reef in Australia is the largest coral reef system in the world. It is over 2,300 kilometers long and is known for its amazing biodiversity. Thousands of species of fish and coral are found there.

The Aurora Borealis (Northern Lights) is a breathtaking natural light display. It can be seen in the night sky near the Arctic. The colors are created when particles from the sun hit the Earth's atmosphere.

Mount Everest is the highest mountain in the world. It is located in the Himalayas and is 8,848.86 meters high. Every year, climbers from all over the world try to reach the top.

Victoria Falls is one of the largest waterfalls in the world. It is located on the border of Zambia and Zimbabwe. The waterfall is 108 meters high and 1,708 meters wide.

🏆 中考题型专练 · 语法填空

用括号内单词的适当形式填空。

The Grand Canyon (1)____ (know) as one of the greatest natural wonders in the world. It (2)____ (locate) in the USA. The canyon (3)____ (carve) by the Colorado River millions of years ago. It (4)____ (say) that the canyon is over 1,800 meters deep. Many tourists (5)____ (attract) by its beauty every year. Thousands of photos (6)____ (take) by visitors daily.

答案
(1) is known；(2) is located；(3) was carved；(4) is said；(5) are attracted；(6) are taken
✍️ 写作进阶 · Introduce a Natural Wonder
📋 写作题目
选择一个你最喜欢的自然奇观，用被动语态和大数字写一篇 70 词的介绍，包括其位置、大小、特点和意义。
开头：自然奇观的名称和位置。
主体：大小、特征、有趣的事实（用被动语态和大数字）。
结尾：为什么它很特别。
The Great Barrier Reef is located in Australia. It is known as the largest coral reef system in the world. It is said that the reef is over 2,300 kilometers long. Thousands of species of fish and coral are found there. Millions of visitors are attracted by its beauty every year. It is a unique ecosystem that should be protected.
大堡礁位于澳大利亚。它被誉为世界上最大的珊瑚礁系统。据说这个珊瑚礁超过 2300 公里长。那里可以找到数千种鱼类和珊瑚。每年有数百万游客被它的美丽吸引。这是一个需要被保护的独特生态系统。
