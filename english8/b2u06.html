🎯 我能做到

📖 课文精讲 · An old man tried to move the mountains.
场景：语文课上，同学们在讨论中国民间故事《愚公移山》。
Teacher:What do you think of the story "Yu Gong Moves a Mountain"?
Wang Lin:I think it's amazing! The old man never gave up. He kept trying until the end.
Li Mei:I agree. The story tells us: Unless we try, we will never know what we can do.
Zhang Wei:But I think it's a bit silly. Why didn't he just move to a new place?
Teacher:Good point! The story is not about moving mountains. It's about the spirit of never giving up.
Wang Lin:As soon as I heard this story, I thought of my grandpa. He never gives up either.
📖 查看翻译

Teacher: 你们觉得《愚公移山》这个故事怎么样？

Wang Lin: 我觉得很了不起！老人从不放弃，一直坚持到底。

Li Mei: 我同意。这个故事告诉我们：不去尝试就永远不会知道自己的能力。

Zhang Wei: 但我觉得有点傻。他为什么不直接搬家呢？

Teacher: 说得好！这个故事不是关于搬山，而是永不放弃的精神。

Wang Lin: 我一听到这个故事就想起了我爷爷。他也从不放弃。

🔑 重点讲解：

1. Unless we try, we will never know what we can do. → unless = if not "如果不"，引导条件状语从句。

2. As soon as I heard this story, I thought of my grandpa. → as soon as "一……就"。

3. The old man never gave up. → give up "放弃"，代词放中间：give it up。

4. He kept trying until the end. → keep doing "持续做某事"。

🔤 重点句型 · Sentence Patterns
unless + 从句
如果不；除非
Unless you work hard, you won't succeed.
as soon as + 从句
一……就……
As soon as I got home, I started my homework.
so + adj. + that + 从句
如此……以至于
The mountain was so high that few people could climb it.
📚 语法聚焦 · unless / as soon as / so…that

三个连词

连词含义示例
unless如果不，除非You'll be late unless you hurry.
as soon as一……就……I'll call you as soon as I arrive.
so…that…如此……以致The box was so heavy that I couldn't carry it.

unless = if not

unless 句子= if not 句子
You won't pass unless you study.= You won't pass if you don't study.
I'll go unless it rains.= I'll go if it doesn't rain.

❌ Unless you will study, you will fail.（unless 从句用一般现在时表将来）

✅ Unless you study, you will fail.

❌ As soon as I will get home, I will call you.（as soon as 从句用一般现在时表将来）

✅ As soon as I get home, I will call you.

📖 阅读专项 · The Magic Paintbrush

Once upon a time, there was a poor boy named Ma Liang. He loved painting but had no brush.

One night, an old man appeared in his dream and gave him a magic paintbrush. As soon as Ma Liang woke up, he found the brush beside his bed.

He painted a bird and the bird came to life! He was so happy that he painted food for the poor people.

A rich man heard about the brush. "Paint me a gold tree!" he ordered. "Unless you paint it, I will put you in jail."

Ma Liang refused. As soon as the rich man tried to take the brush, Ma Liang painted a big river. The rich man was washed away and never came back.

🏆 中考题型专练 · 语法填空

用括号内所给词或连词的适当形式填空。

Once upon a time, an old man (1)____ (try) to move the mountains. His family (2)____ (help) him every day. A clever man said, "You are (3)____ (silly) old man. (4)____ you stop, you will never finish." The old man replied, "(5)____ I die, my children will continue. (6)____ (unless / as soon as) we keep trying, the mountains will become smaller." Finally, the gods (7)____ (move) by his spirit and took the mountains away.

答案
(1) tried；(2) helped；(3) a silly；(4) Unless；(5) After / When；(6) As long as；(7) were moved
