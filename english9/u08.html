📖 课文精讲 · More than a Game
场景：李明、杨帆和Tom在操场边谈论他们最喜欢的运动以及运动教会了我们什么。
Li Ming:Hey guys! Did you watch the basketball game last night?
Yang Fan:Of course! It was amazing. Basketball is played all over the world now.
Tom:True. Do you know when basketball was invented?
Li Ming:In 1891, I think. It was invented by a Canadian teacher named James Naismith.
Yang Fan:Cool! I think sports teach us a lot. If you practice hard, you will improve.
Tom:Exactly. And if you work as a team, you will achieve more. That's sportsmanship.
Li Ming:Right. It's not just about winning. Sports also build character and discipline.
Yang Fan:I agree. The Olympic Games will be held in Paris next year. I can't wait!
📖 查看翻译

李明: 嘿，大家！昨晚你们看篮球赛了吗？

杨帆: 当然！太精彩了。现在全世界都打篮球。

Tom: 没错。你们知道篮球是什么时候发明的吗？

李明: 我认为是1891年。它是由一位名叫詹姆斯·奈史密斯的加拿大老师发明的。

杨帆: 酷！我觉得运动教会了我们很多。如果你努力练习，你就会进步。

Tom: 确实。而且如果你团队合作，你会取得更多成就。这就是体育精神。

李明: 对。运动不只是关于赢。它还塑造品格和纪律。

杨帆: 我同意。明年奥运会将在巴黎举行。我等不及了！

🔑 重点讲解：

被动语态一般现在时：is/am/are + V-ed — Basketball is played all over the world.

被动语态一般过去时：was/were + V-ed — Basketball was invented in 1891.

被动语态一般将来时：will be + V-ed — The Olympics will be held in Paris.

被动语态 by 短语：It was invented by a Canadian teacher.

条件状语从句 Type 1：If + 一般现在时, 主句 + will + V — If you practice hard, you will improve.

🔤 重点句型 · Sentence Patterns
主语 + is/are + V-ed (被动·一般现在时)
表示一般现在时的被动语态。
Basketball is played all over the world. / Soccer is loved by millions.
主语 + was/were + V-ed (被动·一般过去时)
表示一般过去时的被动语态。
Basketball was invented in 1891. / The game was won by our team.
主语 + will be + V-ed (被动·一般将来时)
表示一般将来时的被动语态。
The Olympics will be held in Paris. / The match will be played tomorrow.
If + 一般现在时, 主语 + will + V
条件状语从句 Type 1（真实条件句）。
If you practice hard, you will improve. / If you work as a team, you will succeed.
📚 语法聚焦 · 被动语态综合复习 + 条件状语从句

被动语态各时态结构

时态主动语态被动语态
一般现在时People play soccer everywhere.Soccer is played everywhere.
一般过去时Naismith invented basketball.Basketball was invented by Naismith.
一般将来时They will hold the match.The match will be held tomorrow.
情态动词You can finish the game.The game can be finished.

条件状语从句 Type 1

从句 (if)主句例句
一般现在时will + VIf you train hard, you will get better.
一般现在时can/may + VIf you try, you can succeed.
一般现在时祈使句If you are tired, take a rest.

被动语态：be 动词随主语的人称和数变化，过去分词不变。

by + 动作执行者，如果不重要可省略 — The game was won (by our team).

条件状语从句「主将从现」：主句用 will，从句用一般现在时。

It's not whether you win or lose, it's how you play the game. 重要的不是输赢，而是你如何比赛。

📖 阅读专项 · More Than a Game

Sports are more than just games. They teach us valuable lessons about life. Whether you win or lose, there is always something to learn.

Take basketball, for example. It was invented more than 100 years ago, but it is still loved by people of all ages. Basketball is not just about scoring points. If you play on a team, you learn to communicate with others. You learn to trust your teammates and work together toward a common goal.

What about individual sports like running or swimming? These sports teach self-discipline. If you want to improve, you must train regularly. No one else can do the work for you. Athletes learn that hard work leads to progress.

The best part of sports is sportsmanship. Good players respect their opponents. They follow the rules and accept the referee's decisions. They know that how you play the game is more important than whether you win or lose. As the saying goes, «It's not whether you win or lose, it's how you play the game.»

📋 写作题目
写一篇短文介绍你最喜欢的运动。可以包括：这项运动怎么玩、它的历史、你为什么喜欢它、它教会了你什么。用上被动语态和条件状语从句。
开头：介绍你最喜欢的运动是什么
描述这项运动怎么玩（规则、人数等）
它的历史和基本规则
你从这项运动中学到了什么
结尾：鼓励他人参与
My favorite sport is basketball. It is played by two teams of five players each. The game was invented by James Naismith in 1891. If you practice shooting every day, you will improve your aim. Basketball has taught me teamwork and persistence. I also love watching NBA games. If I work hard, I believe I can become a good player too. I hope more people will fall in love with this exciting sport!
我最喜欢的运动是篮球。它由两队各五名运动员进行比赛。篮球是詹姆斯·奈史密斯在1891年发明的。如果你每天练习投篮，你的准度就会提高。篮球教会了我团队合作和坚持不懈。我也喜欢看NBA比赛。如果我努力，我相信我也能成为一名好球员。我希望更多人能爱上这项激动人心的运动！
