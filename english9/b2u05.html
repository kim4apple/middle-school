Open to New Beginnings

新的开始 · 时态综合复习 · 毕业展望

📖 话题：Graduation, future plans, and new beginnings💬 功能：Talk about future plans; reflect on past experiences📚 语法：时态综合复习 (8大时态 + 被动语态)📝 写作：我的毕业感言

📖 课文精讲 · Graduation Day Memories
场景：毕业典礼结束后，Sarah、Mike和Lisa在校园里回忆三年时光，畅谈未来。
Sarah:I can't believe we're graduating! It feels like yesterday that we first walked into this school.
Mike:I know! I still remember how nervous I was on the first day of Grade 7.
Lisa:We have learned so much over these three years — not just English, but also how to be better people.
Sarah:What are your plans for senior high, Mike?
Mike:I will study at No.1 High School. I hope to join the science club there. I've always been interested in physics.
Lisa:That's great! I'm going to a language school because I want to become a translator someday.
Sarah:Both of you have such clear goals. I'm still thinking about what I want to do. But I'm sure the future will be bright for all of us!
Mike:Let's stay in touch and support each other. Even though we're going to different schools, our friendship will last forever.
📖 查看翻译

Sarah: 真不敢相信我们就要毕业了！感觉就像昨天才第一次走进这所学校。

Mike: 是啊！我还记得七年级第一天我有多紧张。

Lisa: 这三年来我们学到了很多——不仅仅是英语，还有如何成为更好的人。

Sarah: Mike，你高中有什么计划？

Mike: 我将在第一高中学习。我希望加入那里的科学俱乐部。我一直对物理感兴趣。

Lisa: 太棒了！我打算去一所语言学校，因为我想有一天成为一名翻译。

Sarah: 你们俩都有这么明确的目标。我还在思考我想做什么。但我相信未来对我们所有人都会很美好！

Mike: 让我们保持联系，互相支持。虽然我们要去不同的学校，但我们的友谊会永远持续下去。

🔑 重点讲解：

现在进行时 be + graduating — 表示即将发生的动作（表将来）。

现在完成时 have learned — 表示从过去持续到现在，强调经历和结果。

一般将来时 will study / be going to — 表示将来的打算和预测。

一般现在时 are / want / is — 描述当前的状态和事实。

过去时 walked / was / had — 回忆过去的经历。

📚 语法聚焦 · 时态综合复习 (8大时态 + 被动语态)

一、8大时态对比表

时态结构用法例句 (毕业主题)
一般现在时do/does习惯、事实、状态I study hard every day.
一般过去时did过去的动作/状态I started junior high in 2023.
一般将来时will do / be going to将来的动作/打算I will study at a senior high school.
现在进行时am/is/are + doing正在发生 / 计划将来We are preparing for the exam.
过去进行时was/were + doing过去某时正在发生I was studying when you called.
现在完成时have/has + done从过去持续到现在I have learned a lot in three years.
现在完成进行时have/has been doing从过去持续到现在还在进行I have been studying English for 9 years.
过去完成时had + done过去的过去I had finished my homework before dinner.

二、被动语态

时态被动结构例句
一般现在被动am/is/are + doneEnglish is spoken all over the world.
一般过去被动was/were + doneThe graduation ceremony was held last week.
一般将来被动will be + doneA new school will be built next year.
现在完成被动have/has been + doneThe plan has been made already.

三、时态综合运用 — 毕业主题段落

I started (一般过去) junior high school three years ago. At first, I was shy and nervous. But my teachers encouraged (一般过去) me, and I have made (现在完成) great progress. Right now, I am preparing (现在进行) for the senior high entrance exam. I have been studying (现在完成进行) English for nine years, and I hope (一般现在) to continue in senior high. The graduation ceremony will be held (一般将来被动) next week. I will never forget (一般将来) my time in junior high. A new chapter is beginning (现在进行表将来). I am confident (一般现在) that the future will be (一般将来) bright!

判断时态的关键：看时间状语（yesterday → 过去；tomorrow → 将来；for/since → 完成）。

be going to 表示"打算做"，will 表示"将要做"或"预测"。

现在完成时强调"影响"或"经历"，一般过去时强调"何时发生"。

被动语态中，动作的承受者作主语，by + 动作执行者。

The future belongs to those who believe in the beauty of their dreams. 未来属于那些相信自己梦想之美的人。

📖 阅读专项 · Looking Back, Moving Forward

Three years ago, I walked through the gates of this school with a mixture of excitement and fear. I didn't know what to expect. Would I make friends? Would I do well in my studies? Looking back now, I realize how much I have grown.

I remember my first English class. I was so nervous that I couldn't say a complete sentence. But my teacher, Ms. Li, was patient and encouraging. She told us, "Mistakes are proof that you are trying." Those words have stayed with me ever since. Now I can give a speech in English without fear.

There were challenges, of course. I failed a math test in Grade 8 and felt terrible. But that failure taught me a valuable lesson — success comes not from never falling, but from rising every time you fall. I worked harder, asked more questions, and finally improved.

Junior high has been a journey of growth. I have learned not just knowledge from textbooks, but also the importance of friendship, perseverance, and courage. Every challenge has made me stronger. Every achievement has given me confidence.

Now, as I prepare to say goodbye, I feel both sad and excited. Sad to leave behind familiar faces and places. But excited for the new adventures that await. Graduation is not an end — it is a new beginning. I am ready to open my arms to the future and embrace every opportunity that comes my way.

mixture 混合 proof 证明 perseverance 毅力 familiar 熟悉的 await 等待 embrace 拥抱

✍️ 写作进阶 · My Graduation Speech
📋 写作题目
假设你将在毕业典礼上作为学生代表发言。写一篇80-120词的毕业感言，回顾初中三年的成长经历，表达对老师同学的感谢，并展望未来的高中生活。尽量使用至少三种不同的时态。
开场白：问候和引入
回顾：难忘的经历和成长（过去时 / 现在完成时）
感谢：对老师和同学的感谢
展望：对未来的期待和祝福（现在时 / 将来时）
结束语：激励的话语
Dear teachers and classmates,
Good morning! Today is a special day — we are graduating from junior high school.
Looking back, I still remember the first day I walked into this school. I was shy and nervous. But with the help of my teachers and friends, I have grown so much. I have learned not only knowledge but also how to be a better person.
I want to thank all my teachers. You have inspired me to work hard and never give up. I also want to thank my classmates. We have shared so many happy memories together.
Now, a new chapter is beginning. I believe that all of us will achieve great things in the future. Let's carry the lessons we have learned and move forward with courage and hope.
Thank you, and good luck to everyone!
尊敬的老师、亲爱的同学们，早上好！今天是一个特别的日子——我们即将从初中毕业。回首过去，我依然记得第一次走进这所学校的那一天。那时的我害羞又紧张。但在老师和朋友的帮助下，我成长了许多。我不仅学到了知识，也学会了如何成为一个更好的人。我要感谢所有的老师，你们激励我努力学习，永不放弃。我还要感谢我的同学们，我们一起分享了这么多快乐的回忆。现在，新的篇章即将开启。我相信我们所有人将来都会取得伟大的成就。让我们带着所学到的经验，带着勇气和希望继续前进。谢谢大家，祝大家好运！
