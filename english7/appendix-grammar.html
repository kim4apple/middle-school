1. be 动词 (am / is / are)

相关单元：Starter 1 · U1 · U3
人称主语be 动词例句
第一人称单数IamI am a student.
第二人称单数YouareYou are my friend.
第三人称单数He / She / ItisHe is tall. / It is a pen.
第一人称复数WeareWe are in Class 1.
第二人称复数YouareYou are all right.
第三人称复数TheyareThey are my parents.
💡 顺口溜：I 用 am，you 用 are，is 连着他/她/它。单数 is 复数 are。
⚠️ 常错：He am a teacher. → He is a teacher. （第三人称用 is）

2. 指示代词 (this / that / these / those)

相关单元：Starter 2 · U3 · U4
单数复数
近处（眼前）this 这个these 这些
远处（那边）that 那个those 那些
💡 this/that + 单数名词；these/those + 复数名词。
Is this your pen? → Are these your pens?

3. 物主代词（形容词性 vs 名词性）

相关单元：U3 · U5
人称形容词性（+名词）名词性（单独使用）
第一人称单数my 我的mine 我的（东西）
第二人称your 你的/你们的yours 你的/你们的
第三人称(男)his 他的his 他的
第三人称(女)her 她的hers 她的
第一人称复数our 我们的ours 我们的
第三人称复数their 他们的theirs 他们的
⚠️ 形容词性后面必须跟名词（my book）；名词性独立使用（This book is mine）。
❌ This is mine book. → ✅ This is my book.

4. 人称代词（主格 vs 宾格）

相关单元：U5 · U6
人称主格（作主语）宾格（作宾语）
一单Ime
二单youyou
三单(男)hehim
三单(女)sheher
三单(物)itit
一复weus
二复youyou
三复theythem
💡 主格在动词前（I like it）；宾格在动词/介词后（Give it to me）。

5. 名词复数规则

相关单元：Starter 2 · U3 · U6
规则单数 → 复数
一般 + sbook → books, pen → pens
以 s/x/sh/ch + esbox → boxes, watch → watches
辅音+y → iesfamily → families, dictionary → dictionaries
以 f/fe → vesknife → knives, shelf → shelves
不规则man → men, woman → women, child → children, foot → feet

6. 方位介词

相关单元：U4 · 七下 U8
介词含义例句
in在…里面The pencil is in the pencil case.
on在…上面Your book is on the desk.
under在…下面The cat is under the table.
near在…附近The school is near the park.
behind在…后面The broom is behind the door.
next to紧挨着The library is next to the classroom.
across from在…对面The post office is across from the bank.
between在…之间The park is between the school and the hospital.

7. 情态动词 (can / must / would)

相关单元：七下 U1 · 七下 U4 · 七下 U10
情态动词含义结构例句
can能，会，可以can + 动词原形I can swim. / Can you play the guitar?
must必须must + 动词原形We must be on time.
would想要（委婉）would like + to do / n.I would like some noodles.
💡 情态动词后永远跟动词原形，无人称和数的变化。
⚠️ ❌ He cans sing. → ✅ He can sing.

8. 时态总览

相关单元：U5 · 七下U2 · 七下U6 · 七下U11 · 七下U12

一般现在时

用法结构例句
表示习惯/事实主语 + do/does + ...I get up at 7:00. / He plays soccer every day.
be 动词主语 + am/is/are + ...She is a teacher.
否定don't / doesn't + 动词原形I don't like bananas. / He doesn't have a soccer ball.
一般疑问Do/Does + 主语 + 动词原形?Do you like apples? / Does he play basketball?

现在进行时

用法结构例句
正在进行的动作am/is/are + doingI am watching TV. / They are playing soccer.
否定am/is/are + not + doingHe is not sleeping.
一般疑问Am/Is/Are + 主语 + doing?Is she reading a book?

一般过去时

用法结构例句
过去发生的动作主语 + 动词过去式I visited the museum yesterday.
be 动词过去式was / wereIt was fun. / They were happy.
否定didn't + 动词原形I didn't go to school.
一般疑问Did + 主语 + 动词原形?Did you see the movie?
📌 一般过去时标志词：yesterday, last night/week, ... ago, in 2023

9. 祈使句

相关单元：七下 U4
类型结构例句
肯定动词原形 + ...Sit down, please. / Be quiet.
否定Don't + 动词原形Don't run in the hallways.
Let'sLet's + 动词原形Let's go to the park.

10. There be 句型

相关单元：U4 · 七下 U8
结构用法例句
There is + 单数/不可数存在（单数）There is a book on the desk.
There are + 复数存在（复数）There are two pens in the bag.
否定There is/are + notThere isn't any milk.
一般疑问Is/Are there + ...?Is there a post office near here?
💡 There be 表示"（某地）有某物"，have/has 表示"（某人）拥有"。

11. 疑问句总结

各单元均有涉及

12. 形容词比较级（拓展预览）

八年级内容 · 七年级了解即可
规则原级 → 比较级
单音节 + ertall → taller, small → smaller
以 e 结尾 + rlarge → larger, nice → nicer
辅音+y → ierhappy → happier, easy → easier
重读闭音节双写 + erbig → bigger, hot → hotter
多音节 more +beautiful → more beautiful
不规则good/well → better, bad → worse, many/much → more
