Nature's Temper

自然灾害 · 过去进行时 · 原因描述

📖 话题：Natural disasters and extreme weather💬 功能：Describe disasters, talk about causes📚 语法：过去进行时复习 · because/since/as 原因状语从句📝 写作：灾难经历报道

🎯 我能做到

📖 课文精讲 · Surviving the Storm
场景：暴风雨过后，同学们在教室里分享自己当时的经历。
Sarah:That was the scariest storm I've ever experienced! What were you doing when it hit?
Mike:I was walking home from basketball practice. The wind was blowing so hard that I could barely stand.
Sarah:I was helping my dad prepare the emergency kit. We filled bottles of water and gathered flashlights.
Emma:I was searching for our cat, Lucky. Since she's afraid of thunder, she hid under the bed.
Mike:Were you all safe in the end?
Sarah:Yes! Because we prepared well, we stayed safe. And the storm passed after about three hours.
📖 查看翻译

Sarah: 这是我经历过的最可怕的暴风雨！它来袭时你在做什么？

Mike: 我正从篮球训练走回家。风太大了，我几乎站不稳。

Sarah: 我在帮爸爸准备应急包。我们装了好几瓶水，收集了手电筒。

Emma: 我在找我们的猫 Lucky。因为它怕打雷，所以躲到床底下了。

Mike: 你们最后都安全吗？

Sarah: 是的！因为我们准备充分，所以很安全。暴风雨大约三个小时后就过去了。

🔑 重点讲解：

1. What were you doing when it hit? → 过去进行时问句，when + 一般过去时表示"突然发生"。

2. so...that → so hard that I could barely stand"如此……以至于……"。

3. Since she's afraid of thunder, she hid... → since 引导原因状语从句，表示"因为"。

4. Because we prepared well, we stayed safe. → because 直接说明原因。

🔤 重点句型 · Sentence Patterns
was/were + doing (过去进行时)
描述过去某时正在做的事
I was sleeping when the earthquake hit. / They were running for shelter.
because / since / as + 从句
说明原因（because→直接；since/as→已知原因）
Because we prepared well, we were safe. / Since it was dark, we used flashlights.
so…that… / such…that…
如此……以至于……
The wind was so strong that trees fell. / It was such a big storm that schools closed.
so that (目的状语)
为了；以便
We left early so that we could reach the shelter in time.
📚 语法聚焦 · Disasters & Grammar

一、过去进行时复习 (was/were + doing)

用法结构示例
过去某时正在做was/were + doingI was reading at 8 p.m.
when 打断was doing + when + didI was sleeping when the alarm went off.
while 同时while + was doingWhile I was running, the ground shook.

二、原因状语从句：because / since / as

连词语气位置示例
because最强，直接原因前/后均可Because the road was flooded, we stayed home.
since已知/明显原因常放句首Since the storm is coming, we should prepare.
as较弱，附带原因常放句首As it was getting dark, we turned on the lights.

三、so that / such…that / so…that

结构用法示例
so that + 从句目的（为了）We took a map so that we wouldn't get lost.
so + adj. + that结果The rain was so heavy that we couldn't see the road.
such + n. + that结果It was such a strong wind that it blew off the roof.

❌ Because the storm, we stayed home.（because 接从句，不接名词短语）

✅ Because of the storm, we stayed home.（because of + 名词）

❌ Since I was tired, so I went to bed.（since/so 不连用）

✅ Since I was tired, I went to bed.（只用 since）

❌ It was so heavy rain that we couldn't go out.（so + adj.，不直接修饰名词）

✅ It was such heavy rain that we couldn't go out.（such + n.）

📖 阅读专项 · When Nature Strikes

Nature can be powerful and dangerous. Here are two stories of people who survived natural disasters.

Story 1 — The Earthquake

Lin Tao was doing his homework in his bedroom when the earthquake hit. The building began to shake violently. "Since I had learned about earthquake safety at school, I knew what to do," he said. He quickly hid under his desk. While he was waiting for the shaking to stop, he heard his mother calling his name. After the earthquake, they evacuated the building together. "Because we stayed calm, we got out safely," Lin Tao said.

Story 2 — The Flood

Maria was sleeping when her phone rang. It was a flood warning message. She woke up her family immediately. As they were walking to higher ground, the water was rising fast. They reached a shelter and stayed there for two days. "The volunteers brought us food and water. I'll never forget their kindness," Maria said.

violently adv. 剧烈地 shake v. 摇晃 ground n. 地面 kindness n. 善良；好意

📝 查看答案

🥈 提升关 · 语境运用

提升★★☆
[改错] Because the storm was so strong, so we stayed inside.
📝 查看答案
答案：Because the storm was so strong, we stayed inside. (去掉 so)
提升★★☆
[合并] The wind was strong. It blew down the tree. (用 so…that)
📝 查看答案
答案：The wind was so strong that it blew down the tree.
提升★★☆
[填空] We took an umbrella ____ ____ we wouldn't get wet.
📝 查看答案
答案：so that
提升★★☆
[句型] I was walking home. The tornado came. (用 when 合并)
📝 查看答案
答案：I was walking home when the tornado came.

🏆 中考题型专练 · 完形填空

阅读短文，选择最佳选项。

Last month, a terrible earthquake (1)____ a small town. People (2)____ their daily lives when the ground began to shake. Many people (3)____ breakfast at that time. The earthquake (4)____ many buildings. (5)____ the damage was serious, no one died. The local people quickly (6)____ to safe places. Volunteers arrived soon (7)____ they could provide food and water. (8)____ everyone worked together, the town began to recover quickly.

1. A. hit B. hits C. was hitting D. is hitting
2. A. live B. lived C. were living D. are living
3. A. have B. were having C. are having D. had
4. A. destroy B. destroyed C. destroying D. was destroying
5. A. Because B. Although C. Since D. So
6. A. evacuated B. were evacuating C. evacuate D. evacuating
7. A. so that B. because C. such that D. and
8. A. As B. But C. Although D. Because of
答案
1. A (hit)；2. C (were living)；3. B (were having)；4. B (destroyed)；5. B (Although)；6. A (evacuated)；7. A (so that)；8. A (As)
✍️ 写作进阶 · A News Report About a Natural Event
📋 写作题目
假设你是一名记者，写一篇 80 词左右的新闻报道，报道一场自然灾害（如暴风雨、洪水、地震等），描述灾害发生时人们正在做什么以及灾后救援情况。
开头：标题 + 事件时间地点。
主体：灾害发生时人们在做什么（过去进行时）+ 原因说明（because/since/as）。
结尾：救援和重建情况 + 人们的感受。
Storm Hits Coastal Town

A heavy storm hit Greenville yesterday afternoon. When the storm came, many people were shopping in the supermarket. Some were driving home from work. Since the weather warning had been issued early, most people were prepared. Luckily, the rescue team arrived quickly so that everyone could get help. Because the community worked together, the recovery began right away. "We were scared during the storm," said one resident, "but we felt safe because the volunteers helped us."
《暴风雨袭击沿海小镇》昨日下午，一场强烈的暴风雨袭击了格林维尔。暴风雨来袭时，很多人正在超市购物，有些人正开车下班回家。由于天气预警提前发布，大多数人都做好了准备。幸运的是，救援队迅速到达，以便每个人都能得到帮助。因为社区团结合作，灾后重建立即开始。"暴风雨时我们很害怕，"一位居民说，"但因为有志愿者帮助我们，我们感到很安全。"
🎯 Project · Disaster News Report
Disaster News Report
小组合作模拟一次自然灾害的新闻报道。一人当记者，其他成员扮演受灾者或救援人员。
1选择一种自然灾害（地震/洪水/飓风/暴风雨）。
2记者准备 4-5 个采访问题（用过去进行时和 because/since/as）。
3受灾者描述当时正在做的事，救援人员描述救援行动。
4在班里展示你们的新闻报道。
#评价标准
1.使用过去进行时描述事件
2.正确使用 because/since/as 说明原因
3.新闻结构完整（时间/地点/经过）
4.台风自然，配合默契
