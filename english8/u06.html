U6

Plan for Yourself

未来计划 · be going to / will · 职业理想

📖 话题：Future plans and dreams💬 功能：Talk about future plans, express goals📚 语法：be going to · will + 动词原形 · 时间状语从句 (when/if)📝 写作：我的未来计划

🎯 我能做到

📖 课文精讲 · Friends Share Their Dreams
场景：放学后，几个朋友聚在一起聊未来梦想和计划。
Tom:Hey guys, what are you going to be when you grow up?
Lisa:I'm going to be a scientist. I want to discover new things.
Mike:That's cool! I'm going to be an engineer. I love building things.
Tom:What about you, Amy?
Amy:I'm going to be a writer. I want to write books for children.
Tom:Great dreams, everyone! I'm going to be a musician. I will practice the piano every day.
Lisa:When I grow up, I will work in a lab. If I study hard, I will succeed.
Mike:I hope to go to a good university. And I plan to learn new skills every day.
📖 查看翻译

Tom: 嘿大家，你们长大了想做什么？

Lisa: 我打算当科学家，我想发现新事物。

Mike: 太酷了！我打算当工程师，我喜欢建造东西。

Tom: 你呢，Amy？

Amy: 我打算当作家，我想给孩子们写书。

Tom: 大家的梦想都很棒！我打算当音乐家，我会每天练钢琴。

Lisa: 长大了我将在实验室工作。如果努力学习，我一定会成功。

Mike: 我希望上好大学，计划每天学习新技能。

🔑 重点讲解：

1. What are you going to be when you grow up? → be going to be "打算成为" + 职业。句中 when you grow up 用一般现在时表将来。

2. I will practice the piano every day. → will + 动词原形 表示决心或承诺。

3. When I grow up, I will work in a lab. → When + 现在时 表将来，"当……时，将……"。

4. If I study hard, I will succeed. → If + 现在时 表将来条件，"如果……，就……"。

5. I hope to ... / I plan to ... → 表示"希望做……" / "计划做……"，后接动词原形。

🔤 重点句型 · Sentence Patterns
be going to + 动词原形
表示计划好的未来动作
I am going to study computer science.
will + 动词原形
表示预测或意愿
I will work hard to achieve my dream.
When + 现在时，主句 + will
时间状语从句表将来
When I grow up, I will be a doctor.
If + 现在时，主句 + will
条件状语从句表将来
If I study hard, I will succeed.
want to be / hope to / plan to
表达职业愿望
I want to be a writer. / I hope to become an artist. / I plan to study medicine.
📚 语法聚焦 · be going to & will

1. be going to — 表计划好的动作

人称肯定句否定句疑问句
II'm going to + v.I'm not going to + v.Am I going to + v.?
He/She/ItHe's going to + v.He isn't going to + v.Is he going to + v.?
We/You/TheyThey're going to + v.They aren't going to + v.Are they going to + v.?

2. will — 表预测/意愿/承诺

人称肯定句否定句缩略否定
I/You/He/She/It/We/Theywill + v.will not + v.won't + v.

例：I will help you. / It will be sunny tomorrow. / She won't give up.

3. be going to vs will

比较项be going towill
计划事先计划好的即时决定
预测有迹象表明主观推测
举例I'm going to visit my grandma this weekend.I'll answer the phone. / It will rain soon.

4. when/if + 现在时表将来

when 引导时间状语从句：When I grow up, I will be a pilot.

if 引导条件状语从句：If I work hard, I will achieve my dream.

⚠️ 主句用 will + 动词原形，从句用一般现在时，即"主将从现"。

5. 表达职业愿望

结构例句
want to be + 职业I want to be a doctor.
hope to + 动词I hope to become a scientist.
plan to + 动词I plan to study computer science.

❌ I going to be a doctor.（遗漏 be 动词 am）

✅ I am going to be a doctor.

❌ What you are going to do?（疑问句语序错误）

✅ What are you going to do?

❌ If I will study hard, I will succeed.（if 从句不用 will）

✅ If I study hard, I will succeed.

🏆 中考题型专练 · 语法填空

用括号内单词的适当形式填空。

My sister (1)____ (be) going to be a doctor. She (2)____ (study) hard to get into a good medical school. She (3)____ (take) science classes every term. I (4)____ (be) going to be a teacher. When I (5)____ (grow) up, I will teach in a village school. My brother (6)____ (have) a different plan — he wants to be a musician. If we (7)____ (work) hard, our dreams will come true!

答案
(1) is；(2) studies；(3) takes；(4) am；(5) grow；(6) has；(7) work
✍️ 写作进阶 · My Future Plan
📋 写作题目
请以"My Future Plan"为题，写 5 句话，说说你未来的职业梦想和如何实现它。
开头：我的未来职业梦想是什么。
第二句：为什么选择这个职业。
第三句：我打算怎么做（用 be going to）。
第四句：我的信念或决心（用 will）。
结尾：用 when/if 表达期望。
I have a plan for my future. I am going to be an engineer. I like building things and solving problems. I am going to study math and science very hard. When I grow up, I will go to a good university. If I never give up, I will make my dream come true.
我对未来有计划。我打算当一名工程师。我喜欢建造东西和解决问题。我打算努力学习数学和科学。等我长大了，我会上一所好大学。如果永不放弃，我一定会实现梦想。
