What were you doing when the rainstorm came?

过去进行

📖 话题：Past Continuous Tense💬 功能：Talk about what was happening at a specific past time📚 语法：过去进行时（was/were + doing）· when / while 引导时间状语从句📝 写作：An Unforgettable Moment

🎯 我能做到

📖 课文精讲 · What were you doing when the rainstorm came?
场景：暴风雨过后，同学们分享昨天下午暴风雨来临时他们在做什么。
Lisa:What were you doing when the rainstorm came yesterday?
Tom:I was playing basketball in the gym. We stopped immediately when we heard the thunder.
Lisa:I was doing my homework in my room. While I was writing, the lights went out!
Amy:I was walking home from school. I was carrying an umbrella, but it was no use!
Tom:Were you scared?
Amy:Yes, I was. While I was running, I fell and got wet all over.
📖 查看翻译

Lisa: 昨天暴风雨来的时候你在做什么？

Tom: 我在体育馆打篮球。听到雷声我们立刻停下了。

Lisa: 我在房间写作业。正写着，灯灭了！

Amy: 我正从学校走回家。我带了伞，但根本没用！

Tom: 你害怕吗？

Amy: 是的。我跑的时候摔倒了，全身都湿了。

🔑 重点讲解：

1. What were you doing when the rainstorm came? → 过去进行时问句：What + was/were + 主语 + doing + when…?

2. I was playing basketball when the rainstorm came. → 长动作（was playing）被短动作（came）打断，用 when。

3. While I was writing, the lights went out. → while + 过去进行时 表示"在做某事期间"。

4. While I was running, I fell. → while 从句用过去进行时，主句用一般过去时。

🔤 重点句型 · Sentence Patterns
was/were + doing
过去进行时基本结构
I was reading at 8 p.m. / They were playing soccer then.
…when…（when + 一般过去时）
长动作被短动作打断
I was sleeping when the phone rang.
…while…（while + 过去进行时）
两个动作同时进行
She was cooking while he was watching TV.
📚 语法聚焦 · 过去进行时（was/were + doing）

过去进行时的构成

人称was/were动词形式示例
I / He / She / ItwasdoingI was sleeping.
We / You / TheyweredoingThey were running.

when vs while

连词用法示例
when+ 短暂性动词（一般过去时）I was eating when he came.
when+ 延续性动词（过去进行时）When I was sleeping, someone knocked.
while+ 延续性动词（过去进行时）While I was walking, it started to rain.
while+ 两个同时进行的动作He was singing while she was dancing.

❌ I was play basketball when it rained.（过去进行时要加 -ing）

✅ I was playing basketball when it rained.

❌ While I watched TV, my mom was cooking.（while 后多用进行时）

✅ While I was watching TV, my mom was cooking.

📖 阅读专项 · The Day the Storm Came

Last summer, a terrible storm hit our town. I will never forget that afternoon.

I was doing my homework when dark clouds started to cover the sky. While I was looking out of the window, the wind began to blow heavily. Trees were swaying. Soon, the rain was pouring down.

My mom was cooking in the kitchen. My dad was trying to close all the windows. I felt a little scared.

After the storm, we went outside. There were fallen branches everywhere. But our neighbors were already helping each other clean up. Although the storm was strong, our community was stronger.

hit v. 袭击 cloud n. 云 cover v. 覆盖 sway v. 摇摆 pour v. 倾泻 branch n. 树枝 community n. 社区
阅读★★☆
1. What was the writer doing when dark clouds appeared?
📝 查看答案
答案：Doing homework.

2. What was the writer's mom doing during the storm?
📝 查看答案
答案：Cooking in the kitchen.
阅读★★★
3. What did the writer see after the storm?
📝 查看答案

🏆 中考题型专练 · 完形填空

阅读短文，选择最佳选项。

Yesterday at 6 p.m., a fire alarm (1)____ in our building. I (2)____ my homework. My mom (3)____ dinner in the kitchen. We quickly went outside. While we (4)____ for the firefighters, I saw my neighbor helping an old lady down the stairs. Everyone was safe in the end. While the firefighters (5)____ the fire, we waited outside.

1. A. go off B. went off C. going off D. goes off
2. A. do B. did C. was doing D. am doing
3. A. was cooking B. cooking C. cooks D. cooked
4. A. wait B. waited C. were waiting D. are waiting
5. A. puts out B. putting out C. were putting out D. are putting out
答案
1. B (went off)；2. C (was doing)；3. A (was cooking)；4. C (were waiting)；5. C (were putting out)
✍️ 写作进阶 · An Unforgettable Moment
📋 写作题目
请以"An Unforgettable Moment"为题，写 70 词短文描述一个你难忘的时刻（用过去进行时）。
开头：时间+地点+事件背景。
主体：当时你在做什么（过去进行时）。
结尾：你的感受和收获。
I will never forget the moment I won first prize in the English speech contest. When the host announced my name, I was sitting in my seat and my heart was beating fast. While I was walking to the stage, I could feel everyone's eyes on me. I took a deep breath and started my speech. When I finished, everyone was clapping. It was the best moment of my life!
我永远不会忘记英语演讲比赛得一等奖的那一刻。主持人宣布我的名字时，我正坐在座位上，心跳加速。上台时，我能感到所有人的目光。我深吸一口气开始了演讲。结束时，所有人都在鼓掌。那是我人生中最美好的一刻！

1小组选择一个"新闻事件"。
2一人当记者，三人当目击者。
3记者准备采访问题（用过去进行时）。
4排练并在班里展示。
#评价标准
1.事件设定合理
2.采访使用过去进行时
3.目击者回答详细
4.展示有新闻效果
