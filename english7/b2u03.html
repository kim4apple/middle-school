七下 · Unit 3

How do you get to school?

交通方式 · 距离与时间 · 跨文化出行

📖 话题：Transportation 💬 功能：Talk about how to get to places · Ask about distance and time 📚 语法：How / How long / How far 疑问句 · 交通方式表达 📝 写作：描述上学的交通方式

🎯 我能做到

📖 课文精讲 · How Do You Get to School?
场景：放学路上，同学们在聊各自怎么来上学的。
Lisa: Hi Bob! How do you get to school every day?
Bob: I ride my bike to school. It's good exercise!
Lisa: Cool! How long does it take?
Bob: It takes about 15 minutes. How about you?
Lisa: I take the bus. My home is far from school. It's about 8 kilometers.
Bob: How far is it from your home to school?
Lisa: It's about 8 kilometers. The bus ride takes 30 minutes.
Bob: Wow, that's quite far! Do you want to ride with me tomorrow?
📖 查看翻译

逐句翻译：

Lisa: 嗨 Bob！你怎么去上学？

Bob: 我骑自行车上学。这是很好的锻炼！

Lisa: 酷！需要多长时间？

Bob: 大约需要 15 分钟。你呢？

Lisa: 我坐公交。我家离学校很远。大约 8 公里。

Bob: 从你家到学校有多远？

Lisa: 大约 8 公里。坐公交需要 30 分钟。

Bob: 哇，挺远的！你明天想和我一起骑车吗？

🔑 重点讲解：

1. How do you get to school? → 用 How 询问交通方式（how = 怎样）。

2. How long does it take? → How long 问时间长度（多久）。回答：It takes + 时间。

3. How far is it from ... to ...? → How far 问距离（多远）。

4. take the bus / ride a bike / walk → 常见的交通方式表达。

📖 Reading: How Students Get to School

In our school, students come from different places. They get to school in different ways.

Most students live near school. They usually walk or ride a bike to school.

Some students take the bus. The bus stop is in front of the supermarket.

A few students take the subway. It is fast, and the subway station is not far from school.

For many students, the school trip is always fun because they go together.

🔤 重点句型 · Sentence Patterns
How do you get to + 地点?
询问对方的交通方式。
✅ How do you get to school?
✅ How does she get to work?
➡ 回答：I + take the bus / ride my bike / walk + to school.
How long does it take?
询问做某事需要多长时间。
✅ How long does it take to get to school?
➡ It takes 20 minutes.
✅ How long does it take to go to Beijing by train?
➡ It takes about 5 hours.
How far is it from A to B?
询问两地间的距离。
✅ How far is it from your home to school?
➡ It's about 5 kilometers.
✅ How far is it from Beijing to Shanghai?
➡ It's about 1,200 kilometers.
by + 交通工具 = 乘坐……
by 直接加交通工具名词，不加冠词。
✅ go to school by bus
✅ go to work by bike
✅ go to Beijing by train
➡ 注意：on foot = 步行（by walk ❌）

2️⃣ 交通方式表达对比

方式动词短语 (谓语)by + 名词 (状语)
公交车take the busby bus
自行车ride a bikeby bike
步行walkon foot

地铁take the subwayby subway
火车take the trainby train
⚠️ 中式英语纠错：
❌ I by bus go to school.（方式状语不能放在主语和动词之间）
✅ I go to school by bus.

❌ I by walk to school.（步行不用 by，用 on foot）
✅ I walk to school. / I go to school on foot.

❌ I take bike to school.（缺少冠词或物主代词）
✅ I ride a bike / my bike to school.

🥉 基础关 · 概念识记

基础 ★☆☆
____ do you get to school? (How / What / Where)
📝 查看答案
答案：How
解析：问交通方式用 How。
基础 ★☆☆
I ____ (步行) to school every day.
📝 查看答案
答案：walk
解析：步行 = walk。
基础 ★☆☆
She goes to school ____ bus.
📝 查看答案
答案：by
解析：by + 交通工具，不用冠词。

🥈 提升关 · 语境运用

提升 ★★☆
改错：He go to school by a bus.
📝 查看答案
答案：He goes to school by bus.
解析：第三人称加 -s，by bus 不加冠词。
提升 ★★☆
连词成句：get / school / you / how / do / to / ?
📝 查看答案
答案：How do you get to school?
解析：特殊疑问句：How + do + 主语 + 动词原形 + 其他?
提升 ★★☆
She takes the bus to school. (改为否定句)
📝 查看答案
答案：She doesn't take the bus to school.
解析：第三人称用 doesn't + 动词原形。

🥇 挑战关 · 综合运用

挑战 ★★★
汉译英：你从家到学校有多远？大约 5 公里。你骑自行车需要多长时间？20 分钟。
📝 查看答案
答案：How far is it from your home to school? About 5 kilometers. How long does it take by bike? 20 minutes.
解析：How far 问距离，How long 问时间。
挑战 ★★★
补全对话：
A: ____ do you get to the library?
B: I ____ the subway. It's fast.
A: ____ long does it ____?
B: It ____ about 25 minutes.
📝 查看答案
答案：How; take; How; take; takes
解析：How 问方式，take the subway；How long does it take；It takes + 时间。

✍️ 微写作：How I Get to School

用 How / How long / How far 描述你上学的交通方式（50-70词），包括距离、时间和感受。

How I Get to School

I live about 3 kilometers from my school. I usually ride my bike to school.

It takes about 15 minutes. I think riding a bike is fun and healthy.

Sometimes it rains, and my father drives me to school by car. That takes only 10 minutes.

But I like riding my bike best because I can enjoy the fresh air on the way.

我家离学校大约 3 公里。我通常骑自行车上学，大约需要 15 分钟。我觉得骑自行车既有趣又健康。有时候下雨，爸爸开车送我，只需要 10 分钟。但我最喜欢骑自行车，因为路上可以享受新鲜空气。
