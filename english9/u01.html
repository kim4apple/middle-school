U1

How can we become good learners?

学习方法

📖 话题：Learning Methods💬 功能：Talk about how to study and learn effectively📚 语法：Verb + by + V-ing; 动名词作主语📝 写作：Write about learning methods and strategies

🎯 我能做到

📖 课文精讲 · How can we become good learners?
场景：Mei和Peter在教室里讨论英语学习方法。
Mei:Hi Peter! You look worried. What's wrong?
Peter:I'm having trouble learning English. I can't remember the new words.
Mei:Don't worry! I can help you. How do you study vocabulary?
Peter:I just read the textbook again and again. But it doesn't seem to work.
Mei:You should try making word cards. I study by making flashcards.
Peter:That sounds helpful! What about listening and speaking?
Mei:I improve my listening by watching English movies. And I practice speaking by talking with friends.
Peter:Great ideas! I'll try them. Thanks, Mei!
📖 查看翻译

Mei: 嗨，彼得！你看起来有点担心。怎么了？

Peter: 我学英语有困难。我记不住新单词。

Mei: 别担心！我可以帮你。你怎么学词汇的？

Peter: 我就一遍一遍地读课本。但似乎没什么用。

Mei: 你应该试试做单词卡。我通过制作闪卡来学习。

Peter: 听起来很有帮助！那听力和口语呢？

Mei: 我通过看英文电影来提高听力。我通过和朋友交谈来练习口语。

Peter: 好主意！我试试看。谢谢你，梅！

🔑 重点讲解：

have trouble doing sth 做某事有困难 — Peter said he was having trouble learning English.

by + V-ing 表示「通过……方式」 — I study by making flashcards.

What about...? 用来提建议 — What about listening to English songs?

It doesn't seem to work. 表示「似乎没什么用」 — 用于评价方法的有效性。

🔤 重点句型 · Sentence Patterns
Verb + by + V-ing
表示「通过做某事来……」，回答 how 引导的问句。
I learn English by listening to English songs. / She improved her speaking by practicing every day.
What about + V-ing?
用来提出建议或询问看法。
What about joining an English club? / What about reading English newspapers?
It + adj + to do sth
描述做某事是怎么样的。
It is important to review after class. / It is helpful to read aloud every morning.
The + 比较级, the + 比较级
表示「越……就越……」
The more you read, the faster you will become. / The harder you work, the better results you get.
📚 语法聚焦 · Verb + by + V-ing 结构

基本结构

结构用法例句
by + 动名词表示「通过做某事的方式」I learn English by watching movies.
by + 名词表示「通过某种方式」Please contact me by email.
how 问句 + by 结构问「如何做某事」How do you study for a test? I study by working with a group.

动名词作主语

功能结构例句
作主语V-ing + is/was + ...Reading aloud is very important.
形式主语It is + adj + V-ingIt's no use crying over spilt milk.
否定形式not + V-ingNot reviewing will lead to forgetting.

by 后面只能跟名词或动名词，不能跟动词原形。

动名词作主语时，谓语动词用第三人称单数。

区分 by 和 with：by 表示「通过某种方式」，with 表示「用某种工具」。

Practice makes perfect. 熟能生巧。

📖 阅读专项 · How I Learned to Learn English

Last year, I did not like my English class. Every class was like a bad dream. The teacher spoke too quickly. But I was afraid to ask questions because my pronunciation was very bad. So I just hid behind my textbook and never said anything.

Then one day I watched an English movie called Toy Story. I fell in love with this exciting and funny movie! So I began to watch other English movies as well. My pronunciation improved as well by listening to the conversations in English movies.

I discovered that listening to something interesting is the secret to language learning. I also learned useful sentences like «It's a piece of cake» or «It serves you right». I did not understand these sentences at first. But because I wanted to understand the story, I looked them up in a dictionary.

Now I really enjoy my English class. I want to learn new words and more grammar. Then I can have a better understanding of English movies.

as well 也；同样 at first 起初 look up 查阅 a piece of cake 小菜一碟；很容易 It serves you right 你活该

🏆 中考题型专练 · 阅读理解 — 推理判断

阅读短文，选择最佳答案。

Many students ask for advice about how to improve their English. There are three common questions. The first question is about real English. Watching English films and TV programs is a great way to learn English. The second question is about speaking. The best way to improve speaking is to speak as much as possible. The third question is about vocabulary. The best way to learn new words is to read English newspapers and magazines.

1 A. Watching films is a bad way to learn. B. Watching English films helps learn English. C. Only TV programs help. D. Films are not real English.
2 A. Read more books. B. Speak as much as possible. C. Write more sentences. D. Listen to the teacher.
3 A. By watching films. B. By reading newspapers and magazines. C. By asking teachers. D. By writing diaries.
答案
1. B；2. B；3. B

📋 写作题目
你的朋友李明在学习英语方面遇到了困难。请给他写一封信，提出至少三条学习英语的建议，并说明理由。
开头：问候 + 表示理解对方的困难
主体1：第一条建议 + 理由和例子
主体2：第二条建议 + 如何实施
主体3：第三条建议 + 预期效果
结尾：鼓励 + 祝福
Dear Li Ming, I'm sorry to hear that you are having trouble learning English. Don't worry! I'd like to give you some advice. First, you can improve your English by listening to English songs. This will help you learn new words in a fun way. Second, try to speak English every day by talking with your classmates. Don't be afraid of making mistakes! Third, it's helpful to keep a diary in English. Writing every day can improve your writing skills. I hope these suggestions are helpful. Keep going and you will make progress! Yours, Mei
亲爱的李明： 听说你在学英语方面遇到了困难，我很难过。别担心！我想给你一些建议。 首先，你可以通过听英文歌来提高英语。这会帮助你以有趣的方式学习新单词。其次，试着每天通过和同学交谈来说英语。别害怕犯错误！第三，用英语写日记是很有帮助的。每天写作可以提高你的写作技能。 希望这些建议有用。坚持下去，你会取得进步的！ 你的朋友， 梅
🎯 Project · My English Learning Plan
My English Learning Plan
制定一个为期一个月的英语学习计划，包括每日和每周的学习目标。
1反思你目前的英语水平和薄弱环节
2设定一个具体、可衡量的月目标
3制定每日学习计划（10-15分钟可完成的小任务）
4设计每周复习计划
5和同伴分享并互相评价计划
6开始执行并记录进展
#评价标准
1.目标具体且可衡量
2.计划包含听、说、读、写四个方面
3.有明确的复习安排
4.计划切实可行、可持续
5.与同伴的互评有建设性
