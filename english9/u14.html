🎯 我能做到

📖 课文精讲 · I remember meeting all of you in Grade 7.
场景：Lily和Sam在毕业典礼后翻看毕业纪念册，回忆三年初中时光。
Lily:I can't believe we're graduating! It feels like just yesterday we were in Grade 7.
Sam:I know! I remember meeting all of you on the first day of school. We were all so shy!
Lily:Do you remember our first English class? Ms. Wang asked us to introduce ourselves in English.
Sam:Ha, yes! I was so nervous that I forgot my own name! Everyone laughed.
Lily:I remember scoring the winning goal in the basketball game. That was the best moment of Grade 8!
Sam:And I remember the science fair. Our volcano project won first prize! We stayed up all night working on it.
Lily:We've had so many great memories. I'll never forget these three years.
Sam:Me neither. But I'm also excited about the future! Remember to keep in touch!
📖 查看翻译

Lily: 真不敢相信我们要毕业了！感觉就像昨天我们还在七年级。

Sam: 是啊！我记得第一天上学见到你们的情景。我们当时都好害羞！

Lily: 你还记得我们的第一节英语课吗？王老师让我们用英语自我介绍。

Sam: 哈，记得！我紧张得连自己的名字都忘了！大家都笑了。

Lily: 我记得在篮球比赛中投进了制胜球。那是八年级最棒的时刻！

Sam: 还有科学展。我们的火山项目赢得了一等奖！我们熬夜做那个。

Lily: 我们有太多美好的回忆了。我永远不会忘记这三年。

Sam: 我也是。但我也对未来充满期待！记得保持联系！

🔑 重点讲解：

remember + V-ing 表示「记得做过某事」— I remember meeting you in Grade 7.

remember + to V 表示「记得要做某事」— Remember to keep in touch!

forget + V-ing / to V 也有类似区别。

初中毕业话题常用表达：look back at, be thankful to, learn from, look forward to

🔤 重点句型 · Sentence Patterns
Remember + V-ing (记得做过某事)
表示记得过去曾经做过的事情。
I remember meeting all of you in Grade 7. / I remember scoring the winning goal.
Remember + to V (记得要做某事)
表示记住要去做某事。
Remember to keep in touch! / Remember to bring your camera.
主语 + will never forget + V-ing / n
表示永远不会忘记。
I will never forget these three years. / We will never forget our teachers.
主语 + be thankful to + sb + for + n/V-ing
表示因某事感谢某人。
I am thankful to my teachers for their help. / We are thankful to our parents for their support.
📚 语法聚焦 · Remember + V-ing vs Remember + to V

remember / forget 的两种用法

动词+ V-ing (做过)+ to V (要做)
remember记得做过（已发生）记得要做（未发生）
forget忘记做过（已发生）忘记要做（未发生）
例句I remember meeting her before.Remember to call me.
例句I forgot locking the door.Don't forget to lock the door.

初中三年主要时态复习

时态结构例句
一般现在时V / V-sWe are classmates.
一般过去时V-edI was shy in Grade 7.
现在完成时have/has + V-edI have learned a lot.
一般将来时will + VI will go to senior high.

remember doing 和 remember to do 意思完全不同，注意区分。

stop doing (停止做) vs stop to do (停下来去做) 也有类似区别。

try doing (尝试做) vs try to do (努力做) 同理。

📖 阅读专项 · A Graduation Speech

Good morning, dear teachers, parents, and fellow students. Today is a special day. We are graduating from junior high school. I still remember the first day I walked into this school. I was so nervous that my hands were shaking. But you, my classmates, welcomed me with warm smiles. Thank you.

I remember our English teacher, Ms. Wang, who always encouraged us to speak bravely. I remember our math teacher, Mr. Li, who taught us that there is always more than one way to solve a problem. I remember our class monitor, who organized so many fun activities. I remember all of you.

These three years have been full of challenges and achievements. We have learned not only knowledge from textbooks, but also how to be better people. We have learned to work together, to help each other, and to never give up.

Now we are going to different senior high schools. Some of us may not see each other for a long time. But the memories we share will always be with us. As we move on to the next chapter of our lives, let's remember to be brave, to be kind, and to always believe in ourselves. Congratulations, everyone! We did it!

But my deskmate, Lisa, 3 (smile) at me and said, 「Hi!」 From that day on, we became best friends. We 4 (study) together, laughed together, and even cried together. Now we are 5 (graduate). I will never 6 (forget) the time we spent together. I know we 7 (go) to different high schools, but our friendship will last forever.

1 A. remember B. remembered C. will remember D. am remembering
2 A. am B. was C. were D. be

写一篇毕业演讲稿，回顾初中三年的难忘经历，感谢老师和同学，并展望未来的高中生活。
开场问候：向老师、家长和同学问好
回顾初一：初入校园的感受
回顾初二：最难忘的经历

感恩：感谢老师和同学的帮助
展望：对未来的期待和祝福
Good morning, dear teachers and fellow students. It's a great honor to speak here today. I remember walking into this school for the first time three years ago. I was nervous, but the friendly faces of my classmates quickly made me feel at home. I remember our class trip to the mountain in Grade 8. We helped each other climb to the top. That day, I learned the meaning of teamwork. I remember the late nights we spent studying for exams in Grade 9. We encouraged each other and never gave up. I want to say thank you to our teachers. You have taught us knowledge and showed us how to be good people. Thank you to my classmates for the laughter and friendship. As we go to different high schools, let's remember to stay in touch. I believe our future will be bright. Congratulations, Class of 2025!
亲爱的老师、同学们，早上好。今天能站在这里发言，我感到无比荣幸。我记得三年前第一次走进这所学校的情景。当时我很紧张，但同学们友好的面孔很快让我感到自在。 我记得八年级的班级登山活动。我们互相帮助，一起爬到了山顶。那一天，我懂得了团队合作的意义。我记得九年级我们一起熬夜备考的夜晚。我们互相鼓励，永不放弃。 我想对我们的老师说声谢谢。你们不仅教给我们知识，还教会我们如何成为好人。谢谢我的同学们的欢声笑语和友谊。当我们走向不同的高中时，让我们记得保持联系。我相信我们的未来会一片光明。2025届的同学们，恭喜大家！
