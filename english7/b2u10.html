七下 · Unit 10

I'd like some noodles.

点餐用餐 · 食物种类 · would like 用法

📖 话题：Food & Ordering 💬 功能：Describe people's looks · Ask "I'd like some noodles." 📚 语法：would like (I'd like) · some/any · 可数/不可数名词 📝 写作：餐厅点餐对话或菜单介绍

🎯 我能做到

📖 课文精讲 · Describing people
场景：在一家面馆里，服务员和顾客之间的点餐对话。
Waiter: Good evening! What would you like?
Tom: Good evening. I'd like some noodles, please.
Waiter: What kind of noodles would you like?
Tom: I'd like beef noodles with some vegetables.
Waiter: What size would you like? Large, medium or small?
Tom: A large bowl, please. I'm very hungry.
Waiter: OK. A large bowl of beef noodles. Would you like a drink?
Tom: Yes, I'd like a glass of orange juice, please.
📖 查看翻译

逐句翻译：

Sarah: Mike，你认识新来的同学 David 吗？

Mike: 不认识。他长什么样？

Sarah: 嗯，他又高又瘦。他有一头棕色的卷发。

Mike: 他戴眼镜吗？

Sarah: 是的，他戴。他还有一张圆脸。他经常穿蓝色 T 恤。

Mike: 哦，我想我看到他了！他坐在前排。他是中等身高吗？

Sarah: 不，他挺高的。他大概 1.8 米。

Mike: 知道了。我待会儿去跟他打个招呼。

🔑 重点讲解：

1. I'd like some noodles. → "他长什么样？" 固定句型，询问外貌。

2. He's tall and thin. → 用 be + 形容词描述整体形象（身高、体型）。

3. He has curly brown hair. → 用 has/have 描述具体特征（头发、面部特征）。

4. Does he wear glasses? → 用 wear 表示"穿戴（眼镜、衣服等）"。

🔤 重点句型 · Sentence Patterns
What does he/she look like?
询问人的外貌特征。
✅ What does he look like?
→ He's tall and thin. He has short curly hair.
✅ What do they look like?
→ They're of medium height.
He is + (height / build) / He has + (hair / face)
be + 形容词描述整体（高矮胖瘦）
has/have + 名词描述具体特征（头发、眼睛等）。
✅ He is tall / short / of medium height / thin / heavy.
✅ She has long black hair / big eyes / a round face.
✅ They have curly hair / glasses.
Does he/she have ...? / Does he/she wear ...?
一般疑问句，询问是否有某个特征或是否穿戴某物。
✅ Does he have a beard? — Yes, he does. / No, he doesn't.
✅ Does she wear glasses? — Yes, she does. / No, she doesn't.
✅ Do they have curly hair? — Yes, they do.
描述顺序：整体 → 局部 → 具体细节
描述一个人时，先整体再局部：
身高→体型→头发→五官→着装。
✅ She is of medium height, a little thin.
✅ She has long straight hair and a small nose.
✅ She usually wears a red dress.
📚 语法聚焦 · Describing Appearance

1️⃣ be 描述 vs. have 描述

be + 形容词have/has + 名词
用途整体特征（身高、体型、年龄）具体特征（头发、眼睛、胡须）
身高He is tall.——
体型She is thin.——
头发——He has curly hair.
脸型——She has a round face.
眼镜——He wears glasses.

2️⃣ 形容词顺序修饰名词

用多个形容词修饰名词时顺序：大小/长短 → 形状 → 颜色

正确顺序示例
大小/长短 + 形状 + 颜色 + 名词long straight brown hair
short curly blonde hair
big round black eyes
⚠️ 中式英语纠错：
❌ He is tall and has long hair. ✅ （正确）

❌ He has hair long black.（形容词顺序错误）
✅ He has long black hair.

❌ She is has long hair.（is 和 has 重复）
✅ She has long hair.
📝 三级闯关 · 实战练习

🥉 基础关 · 概念识记

基础 ★☆☆
I'd like some noodles, please. (I'd like / I like / I want)
📝 查看答案
答案：I'd like
解析：I'd like 是 I would like 的缩写，表示“我想要”，礼貌表达。
基础 ★☆☆
____ size would you like? — Large, please. (What / How / Which)
📝 查看答案
答案：What
解析：What size 询问大小。
基础 ★☆☆
Would you like ____ (some / any) tea?
📝 查看答案
答案：some
解析：提出建议希望得到肯定回答时用 some。

🥈 提升关 · 语境运用

提升 ★★☆
改错：I would like eat a bowl of noodles.
📝 查看答案
答案：I would like to eat a bowl of noodles.
解析：would like 后跟 to do，不能直接跟动词原形。
提升 ★★☆
连词成句：kind / what / of / would / noodles / you / like / ?
📝 查看答案
答案：What kind of noodles would you like?
解析：特殊疑问句：What kind of + 名词 + would + 主语 + like?
提升 ★★☆
用 How much 改写：Tell me the price of the beef.
📝 查看答案
答案：How much is the beef?
解析：How much 询问不可数名词的价格。

🥇 挑战关 · 综合运用

挑战 ★★★
汉译英：你想要什么种类的面条？我想要牛肉面，中碗的。
📝 查看答案
答案：What kind of noodles would you like? I'd like a medium bowl of beef noodles.
解析：点餐三部曲：询问种类→询问大小→确定下单。
挑战 ★★★
补全对话：
A: Welcome! ____ can I do ____ you?
B: I'd ____ some dumplings, please.
A: What ____ of dumplings?
B: Pork and cabbage, please.
📝 查看答案
答案：What; for; like; kind
解析：餐厅常用语：What can I do for you? / I'd like... / What kind of?

✍️ 微写作：Describe a friend

选一位你的朋友或家人，用英语写一段描述其外貌的话。注意先用 be 描述整体，再用 has 描述细节。

Waiter: Welcome to Happy Noodle! What would you like?

Customer: I'd like a bowl of noodles, please.

Waiter: What kind of noodles would you like?

Customer: I'd like chicken noodles with some vegetables.

Waiter: What size would you like?

Customer: A small bowl, please. And I'd like a glass of milk, too.

Waiter: OK. A small bowl of chicken noodles and a glass of milk. Anything else?

Customer: No, thanks. That's all.

服务员：欢迎光临快乐面馆！您想吃点什么？顾客：我想要一碗面。服务员：您想要什么样的面？顾客：我想要鸡肉面加蔬菜。服务员：想要什么尺寸？顾客：小碗的。再要一杯牛奶。服务员：好的。一小碗鸡肉面和一杯牛奶。还要别的吗？顾客：不了，谢谢。就这些。
