七下 · Unit 2

No Rules, No Order

校规班规 · 祈使句 · 情态动词 can / must / have to

📖 话题：School rules and discipline 💬 功能：Talk about rules · Give instructions 📚 语法：祈使句 (Don't ... / Be ... / Please ...) · must / have to 📝 写作：制定班级规则

🎯 我能做到

📖 课文精讲 · School Rules
场景：新同学李明向张伟了解学校规定。
Zhang Wei: Welcome to our school! Let me tell you about the rules.
Li Ming: OK. What are the rules?
Zhang Wei: First, don't be late for class. You must arrive on time.
Li Ming: Got it. What else?
Zhang Wei: Please keep the classroom clean. Don't eat or drink in class.
Li Ming: Can I use my phone at school?
Zhang Wei: No, you can't. You must turn off your phone in class.
Li Ming: OK. Can we play sports after class?
Zhang Wei: Yes, you can. But don't run in the hallways!
Li Ming: I see. Thank you!
📖 查看翻译

逐句翻译：

张伟：欢迎来到我们学校！让我告诉你一些规定。

李明：好的。都有什么规定？

张伟：首先，不要上课迟到。你必须准时到。

李明：知道了。还有呢？

张伟：请保持教室干净。上课不要吃东西或喝饮料。

李明：我可以在学校用手机吗？

张伟：不，你不能。上课时你必须关掉手机。

李明：好的。我们下课后可以做运动吗？

张伟：是的，你们可以。但不要在走廊里跑！

李明：明白了。谢谢！

🔑 重点讲解：

1. Don't + 动词原形 → 表示否定祈使句，"不要做某事"。如 Don't be late.

2. Please + 动词原形 → 表示委婉的祈使句，"请做某事"。如 Please keep the classroom clean.

3. must → "必须"，情态动词，后跟动词原形。如 You must arrive on time.

4. can / can't → 表示允许/禁止。如 Can I use my phone? No, you can't.

5. 规则相关短语：be late (迟到), keep clean (保持干净), turn off (关掉).

📖 Reading: Our School Rules

Every school has rules. Our school has many rules too.

You must wear the school uniform. Don't eat in the classroom.

Please keep the library quiet. You must arrive at school before 8:00.

Don't fight with classmates. Please say "hello" to teachers.

Why do we have rules? Rules help us learn better and stay safe.

A school without rules is like a game without rules!

📚 语法聚焦 · Imperatives & Modal Verbs

1️⃣ 祈使句 (Imperatives)

祈使句用来表达命令、请求、建议或规则。主语通常省略。

类型结构示例
肯定祈使句动词原形Sit down, please.
Be 型祈使句Be + 形容词Be quiet.
否定祈使句Don't + 动词原形Don't run.
委婉祈使句Please + 动词原形Please open the door.

📌 注意：否定祈使句一律用 Don't + 动词原形，不管主语是谁。

2️⃣ must / can't 对比

情态动词含义用法示例
must必须表示主观义务或规则要求You must wear the school uniform.
mustn't绝对不能表示禁止（语气最强）You mustn't run in the hallway.
can可以表示允许You can play sports after class.
can't不能表示禁止/不允许You can't use your phone in class.

3️⃣ must vs have to

对比musthave to
含义必须（主观）不得不（客观）
来源说话人自己的意志外界客观要求
时态变化无（只有现在时）有（has to / had to）
否定mustn't（禁止）don't have to（不必）
示例I must finish my homework.I have to wear a uniform at school.

📌 don't have to = "不必"（≠ mustn't "绝对不能"）

⚠️ 中式英语纠错：
❌ Don't running in the hallway.（Don't 后要用动词原形）
✅ Don't run in the hallway.

❌ You must to wear the uniform.（must 后直接跟动词原形）
✅ You must wear the uniform.

❌ No, I don't have to fight.（don't have to ≠ 禁止）
✅ No, you mustn't fight.
📝 三级闯关 · 实战练习

🥉 基础关 · 概念识记

基础 ★☆☆
____ late for class. — Sorry, I won't. (Don't / Not / No)
📝 查看答案
答案：Don't
解析：否定祈使句用 Don't + 动词原形，Don't be late.
基础 ★☆☆
Please ____ (keep) the classroom clean.
📝 查看答案
答案：keep
解析：Please 后跟动词原形。
基础 ★☆☆
You ____ (必须) wear the school uniform.
📝 查看答案
答案：must
解析：must 表示"必须"，后跟动词原形。

🥈 提升关 · 语境运用

提升 ★★☆
改错：Don't running in the hallway.
📝 查看答案
答案：Don't run in the hallway.
解析：Don't 后必须用动词原形，running → run。
提升 ★★☆
连词成句：your / turn / please / phone / off
📝 查看答案
答案：Please turn off your phone.
解析：Please + 动词原形 + 宾语。
提升 ★★☆
You must be quiet. (改为否定祈使句)
📝 查看答案
答案：Don't be noisy.
解析：否定祈使句用 Don't + 动词原形。

🥇 挑战关 · 综合运用

挑战 ★★★
汉译英：上课不要用手机。请保持教室安静。
📝 查看答案
答案：Don't use your phone in class. Please keep the classroom quiet.
解析：否定祈使句 Don't + 动词原形；委婉祈使用 Please + 动词原形。
挑战 ★★★
阅读判断正误 (T/F)：根据 "Our School Rules" 课文判断。1. You can eat in the classroom. 2. You must arrive at school before 8:00. 3. You can fight with classmates.
📝 查看答案
答案：1. F 2. T 3. F
解析：根据课文：Don't eat in the classroom. Arrive before 8:00. Don't fight with classmates.
挑战 ★★★
选择：I ____ finish my homework first. Then I can play. (can / must / mustn't)
📝 查看答案
答案：must
解析：must 表示"必须"，先完成作业才能玩。
挑战 ★★★
用 have to 改写：Please be quiet in the library.（改为 You 开头）
📝 查看答案
答案：You have to be quiet in the library.
解析：have to 表示"不得不"（客观要求）。

3. Don't eat in class.

4. Be quiet in the library.

5. Say "hello" to teachers.
