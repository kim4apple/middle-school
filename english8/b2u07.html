What's the highest mountain in the world?

世界之最

📖 话题：Geographic Facts & Superlatives💬 功能：Talk about geographic features using comparatives and superlatives📚 语法：形容词/副词比较级与最高级的深入运用 · 数字表达（长/宽/高/深等）📝 写作：Amazing Places in China

🎯 我能做到

📖 课文精讲 · What's the highest mountain in the world?
场景：地理课上，老师在用英语介绍世界地理之最。
Teacher:What's the highest mountain in the world?
Li Wei:Mount Qomolangma! It's 8,848.86 meters high.
Teacher:Correct! And what's the longest river in China?
Wang Fang:The Yangtze River. It's about 6,300 kilometers long.
Teacher:Very good. The Caspian Sea is the deepest lake in the world. It's 1,025 meters deep.
Zhang Wei:Is the Sahara the biggest desert?
Teacher:The Sahara is the hottest desert. The largest desert in the world is Antarctica!
📖 查看翻译

Teacher: 世界上最高的山是什么？

Li Wei: 珠穆朗玛峰！高 8848.86 米。

Teacher: 正确！中国最长的河是什么？

Wang Fang: 长江。长约 6300 公里。

Teacher: 很好。里海是世界上最深的湖，深 1025 米。

Zhang Wei: 撒哈拉是最大的沙漠吗？

Teacher: 撒哈拉是最热的沙漠。世界上最大的沙漠是南极洲！

🔑 重点讲解：

1. It's 8,848.86 meters high. → 数字 + 单位 + adj.：meters high / kilometers long / meters deep。

2. What's the longest river in China? → the + 最高级 + 名词 + in + 范围。

3. The Caspian Sea is the deepest lake. → deep → deepest 。

4. 数字读法：8,848.86 → eight thousand eight hundred forty-eight point eight six。

🔤 重点句型 · Sentence Patterns
数字 + 单位 + 形容词
描述度量
The mountain is 8,848 meters high.
the + 最高级 + in + 范围
在一定范围内最…
It's the longest river in China.
比较级 + than any other…
比任何其他都…
It's higher than any other mountain.
📚 语法聚焦 · 比较级与最高级深入运用 · 数字度量

度量描述

度量维度问句回答
高度How high is…?It's … meters high.
长度How long is…?It's … kilometers long.
深度How deep is…?It's … meters deep.
宽度How wide is…?It's … meters wide.
重量How heavy is…?It's … kilos.

最高级 + 范围

结构示例
the + 最高级 + in + 地点the tallest building in the world
the + 最高级 + of + 群体the tallest of the three boys
one of the + 最高级 + 复数one of the longest rivers

❌ The river is 6,000 meters length.（用 long 而不是 length）

✅ The river is 6,000 meters long.

❌ It's the highest mountain of the world.（of 改为 in）

✅ It's the highest mountain in the world.

📖 阅读专项 · Amazing Animals: Pandas

Pandas are one of the most famous animals in the world. They live in the forests of China.

At birth, a baby panda is about 15 cm long and weighs only 0.1 kg. It's so small that you can hold it in one hand! An adult panda weighs about 100 kg and is 1.5 meters long.

Pandas eat bamboo. An adult panda eats about 12 to 38 kilos of bamboo every day. That's a lot!

The panda is one of the most endangered animals in the world. There are only about 1,800 pandas living in the wild. We should do more to protect them.

🏆 中考题型专练 · 语法填空

用括号内单词的适当形式填空。

The Great Wall is one of the (1)____ (great) wonders in the world. It's about 21,196 kilometers (2)____ (long). It's (3)____ (long) than any other wall in the world. The Great Wall is so (4)____ (famous) that millions of tourists visit it every year. It's the (5)____ (popular) tourist attraction in China.
