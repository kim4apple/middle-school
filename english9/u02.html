🎯 我能做到

📖 课文精讲 · I think that mooncakes are delicious!
场景：Wu Ming和Harry在谈论中秋节的经历。
Harry:Hi Wu Ming! How was your trip to your hometown?
Wu Ming:It was great! I went back for the Mid-Autumn Festival.
Harry:I think that mooncakes are delicious! What did you do?
Wu Ming:My family got together and had a big dinner. Then we watched the full moon.
Harry:That sounds wonderful. I wonder if they have mooncakes in other countries.
Wu Ming:I'm not sure if they do. But I know that many countries celebrate harvest festivals.
Harry:I believe that festivals are a time for family and sharing.
Wu Ming:I agree. I think that the best part of a festival is being with family.
📖 查看翻译

Harry: 嗨，吴明！你回老家的旅行怎么样？

Wu Ming: 太棒了！我回去过中秋节了。

Harry: 我觉得月饼很好吃！你们都做什么了？

Wu Ming: 我们家人聚在一起吃了顿大餐。然后我们赏月了。

Harry: 听起来很棒。我想知道其他国家有没有月饼。

Wu Ming: 我不确定。但我知道很多国家都庆祝丰收节。

Harry: 我相信节日是家人团聚和分享的时刻。

Wu Ming: 我同意。我觉得节日最棒的部分就是和家人在一起。

🔑 重点讲解：

I think that... 引导宾语从句，表示「我认为……」 — I think that mooncakes are delicious.

I wonder if/whether... 表示「我想知道是否……」 — I wonder if they have mooncakes in other countries.

宾语从句的语序用陈述语序，不用疑问语序。

that 在口语中常可省略，if/whether 不可省略。

🔤 重点句型 · Sentence Patterns
I think that...
表达个人观点，后跟宾语从句。
I think that mooncakes are delicious. / I think that festivals are important for families.
I wonder if/whether...
礼貌地表达疑问或好奇。
I wonder if he likes mooncakes. / I wonder whether they celebrate Christmas in China.
I believe that...
表达信念或强烈看法。
I believe that festivals bring people together. / I believe that kindness is the best gift.
主语 + 动词 + that 从句
that 引导的宾语从句作动词的宾语。
She knows that I love dumplings. / They said that the festival was amazing.
📚 语法聚焦 · 宾语从句 (Object Clauses)

that 引导的宾语从句

引导词用法例句
thatthat 无实义，可省略I think (that) mooncakes are delicious.
that用于陈述句作宾语She knows (that) I like festivals.
that谓语动词 + that 从句He said (that) he enjoyed the dinner.

if / whether 引导的宾语从句

引导词用法例句
if表示「是否」，用于口语I wonder if he likes mooncakes.
whether表示「是否」，正式用法I wonder whether they celebrate it too.
if/whether从句用陈述语序I'm not sure if she will come.

宾语从句的语序必须是陈述句语序：主语 + 谓语。

如果主句是一般现在时，从句可以用任何需要的时态。

如果主句是一般过去时，从句要用相应的过去时态。

It takes a village to raise a child. 反映了集体主义文化。

📖 阅读专项 · The Story of Chang'e

Chinese people have been celebrating the Mid-Autumn Festival and enjoying mooncakes for centuries. Mooncakes are in the shape of a full moon on the night of the Mid-Autumn Festival.

There are many traditional folk stories about this festival. One of the most famous is the story of Chang'e. Hou Yi, a young man, shot down nine suns and saved the earth. As a reward, he was given a magic medicine. Anyone who took this medicine would live forever.

Hou Yi did not want to take the medicine without his wife, Chang'e. So he asked her to keep it safe. A bad man tried to steal the medicine, but Chang'e refused to give it to him and drank it herself. She flew up to the moon and became the goddess of the moon.

Hou Yi was sad. Every night, he looked at the moon and thought of his wife. The story shows the Chinese people's love for family and peace.

描述除夕的活动（年夜饭、守岁等）
描述春节期间的活动（拜年、红包等）
结尾：邀请对方来中国体验春节
Dear Tom, I'm glad to hear that you are interested in the Spring Festival. Let me tell you something about it. The Spring Festival is the most important festival in China. It usually comes in January or February. Before the festival, people clean their houses and put up couplets on the doors. On New Year's Eve, families get together for a big dinner. We eat dumplings and stay up late. Children love this festival because they can get red packets with money inside! During the festival, people visit relatives and friends. Everyone says «Happy New Year» to each other. I think that the Spring Festival is a time for joy and family reunion. I hope you can come to China and experience it yourself! Yours, Li Hua
